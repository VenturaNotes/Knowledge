## Source [^1]
- a test for folate or vitamin $\mathrm{B}_{12}$ deficiency. A dose of the amino acid histidine, which requires the presence of folate or vitamin $\mathrm{B}_{12}$ for its complete breakdown, is given by mouth. In the absence of these vitamins, formiminoglutamic acid (figlu) $\textendash$ an intermediate product in histidine metabolism $\textendash$ accumulates and can be detected in the urine.
