## Source [^1]
- $n$. A type of cell in the primary visual cortex (Area V1) that responds maximally to a bar (lighter than the surround for some cells, darker than the surround for others) oriented at a particular angle on the retina, the response being strongest for a moving bar-shaped stimulus. See also COMPLEX CELL, END-STOPPED RECEPTIVE FIELD, FEATURE DETECTOR, HYPERCOMPLEX CELL, SIMPLE CELL. Compare EDGE DETECTOR.
