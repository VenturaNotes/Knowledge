## Source [^1]
- adj. within a blood vessel: describing procedures for imaging the circulation or for treating vascular disorders from within the circulation, including angioplasty, the placement of stents or coils in aneurysms (see ENDOVASCULAR ANEURYSM REPAIR; COILING), and embolization. Endovascular procedures are usually performed by an interventional radiologist or a vascular surgeon.
