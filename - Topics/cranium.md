## Source [^1]
- $n$. the part of the skeleton that encloses the brain. It consists of eight bones connected together by immovable joints (see SKULL). $\textemdash$cranial adj.
## Source[^2]
- (brain case) (pl. craniums or crania) The part of the vertebrate skull that encloses and protects the brain. It is formed by the fusion of several flattened bones, which have immovable joints (sutures) between them.
