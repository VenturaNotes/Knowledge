## Source [^1]
- $n$. a corticosteroid drug that is administered by mouth to treat ulcerative colitis, by nasal spray to treat hay fever, as a cream or ointment to treat severe skin infections, and by inhaler for the prevention of asthma. Possible side-effects include nasal irritation and hoarseness.
