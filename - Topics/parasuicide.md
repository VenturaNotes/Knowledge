## Source [^1]
- $n$. A deliberate self-inflicted injury or poisoning resembling a suicide attempt but probably not intended to be successful. The term was introduced by the British psychiatrist Norman Kreitman (1927-2012) and three co-authors in the heading of a letter in the British Journal of Psychiatry in 1969. parasuicidal adj. \[From Greek para beside + English suicide]
