## Source [^1]
- (S&P) A US credit rating agency and financial services company based in New York. S&P rates the bonds issued by companies, public corporations, and sovereign states on a scale from AAA downwards to D: a rating of BBB or above denotes 'investment grade', lower ratings 'speculative grade'. The company also publishes a series of stock-market indexes, the widest being the S&P 500.
