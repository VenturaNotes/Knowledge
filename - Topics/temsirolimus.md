## Source [^1]
- $n$. a protein kinase inhibitor used for the treatment of advanced renal cell carcinoma. It targets a serine threonine kinase that regulates a signaling cascade controlling cell proliferation. Side-effects include hypersensitivity reactions (flushing, chest pain, breathlessness).
