## Source [^1]
- (1) A monetary unit of Costa Rica, worth one hundredth of a colón. 
- (2) A monetary unit of Paraguay, worth one hundredth of a guaraní. 
- (3) A monetary unit of Venezuela, worth one hundredth of a bolívar. 
- (4) A monetary unit of Peru, worth one hundredth of a new sol. 
- (5) A former monetary unit of Spain and Andorra, worth one hundredth of a peseta.
