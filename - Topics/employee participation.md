## Source [^1]
- (1) The encouragement of motivation in a workforce by giving shares in the company to employees. Employee shareholding (see EMPLOYEE SHARE OWNERSHIP PLAN) can be an important factor in improving industrial relations. 
- (2) The appointment to a board of directors of a representative of the employees of a company, to enable the employees to take part in the direction of the company. See PARTICIPATIVE DECISION MAKING.
