## Source [^1]
- A design method in which the system is seen from the functional viewpoint. The design concentrates on isolating high-level functions that can then be decomposed into and synthesized from lower-level functions. Development proceeds as a series of stepwise refinements of functionality. Compare DATA-DRIVEN DESIGN.
