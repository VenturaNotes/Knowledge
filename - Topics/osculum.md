## Source[^2]
- (pl. oscula) 
- (1) The mouthlike aperture in the body wall of a sponge (see PORIFERA) through which water leaves the body cavity. 
- (2) Any of the suckers on the head (scolex) of a tapeworm, by which it attaches itself to the gut wall of its host. 
- (3) (in anatomy) A pore or tiny opening.
