## Source [^1]
- $n$. an enzyme, produced by some bacteria, that is capable of antagonizing the antibacterial action of penicillin and other beta-lactam antibiotics. Purified penicillinase may be used to treat reactions to penicillin. It is also used in diagnostic tests to isolate microorganisms from the blood of patients receiving penicillin.
