## Source [^1]
- $n$. Impairment of learning or performance of a task caused by having previously learnt similar information or a similar task. Also called proactive inhibition. See also Ranschburg inhibition, Skaggs-Robinson paradox. Compare Retroactive interference. PI abbrev.
