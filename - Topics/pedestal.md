## Source [^1]
- A pulse waveform that is ‘flat-topped’, i.e. the amplitude is made constant for a pulse interval large compared to the rise and fall times (see PULSE). A pedestal is combined with a second waveform in order to increase the magnitude of the second waveform by a constant amount.
