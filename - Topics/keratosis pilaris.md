## Source [^1]
- (follicular keratosis, lichen pilaris) a very common autosomal dominant condition characterized by rough horny plugs in the hair follicles, usually on the skin of the lateral and posterior aspects of the arms. They may be skin-colored, red, or brown. The condition typically appears during teenage years and persists into adulthood; it is completely harmless.
