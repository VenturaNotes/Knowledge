## Source [^1]
- A service provided by a bank which automatically transfers funds above a certain level from a current account to a higher-interest earning account. The process can work in reverse when funds in the current account fall below a certain level. The purpose is to provide the customer with the greatest amount of interest, with the minimum personal intervention.
