## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any sample drawn by a technique that does not involve randomization, so that the probabilities of inclusion of members of the population or subgroups within it are not equal. See ACCIDENTAL SAMPLE, CONVENIENCE SAMPLE, OPPORTUNITY SAMPLE, QUOTA SAMPLE, SELF-SELECTED SAMPLE, SNOWBALL SAMPLE. Compare CLUSTER SAMPLE, PROBABILITY SAMPLE, RANDOM DIGIT DIALLING, SIMPLE RANDOM SAMPLE, STRATIFIED RANDOM SAMPLE.
