## Source [^1]
- $n$. A sleep-wake disorder in DSM-5 characterized by hypersomnia and excessive sleepiness, even after a main sleep period of at least seven hours, with recurrent lapses into sleep during the day, prolonged sleep not being experienced as refreshing or restorative, and difficulty becoming fully awake when woken abruptly. \[From Greek hyper over + Latin somnus sleep + -entia indicating a state or condition]
