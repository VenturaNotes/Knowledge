## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any form of sensation that results from stimuli located outside the body and is detected by exteroceptors, including vision, hearing, touch or pressure, heat, cold, pain, smell, and taste. Compare INTEROCEPTION, KINAESTHESIS, PROPRIOCEPTION. exteroceptive adj. \[From Latin exterus exterior + English receptor + -ion indicating an action, process, or state]
