## Source [^1]
- The process of merging a number of overlapping images into one large image. It involves the use of image registration and image rectification techniques, as well as techniques to identify the extent of image overlap, such as feature matching. A common application is to create a panorama image from a series of photographs taken while scanning a camera horizontally.
