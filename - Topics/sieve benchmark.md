## Source [^1]
- A program that calculates the prime numbers within a specified range in order to obtain a benchmark timing to complete execution. It is easy to use and is often employed to tune the optimization algorithms in compilers; however its coverage of language features is limited to arrays, simple variable types, looping, and comparison.
