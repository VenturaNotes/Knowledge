## Source [^1]
- Symbol $K_{d}$. A measure of the affinity of a receptor for a ligand, or of an enzyme for its substrate. It reflects the tendency of the two entities to dissociate under equilibrium conditions. Hence, the lower the value of $K_{\mathrm{d}}$, the greater the affinity between the two entities.
