## Source [^1]
- (PLCC) A rectangular plastic package for integrated circuits that has pins on all four sides, which are folded underneath the body of the carrier to form a 'J' shape. The chips can be soldered directly to the printed circuit board or mounted on a special PLCC socket that is itself soldered in. This is a useful package for chips that need to be replaced often, as in read-only memory.
