## Source [^1]
- $n$. the egg of a louse. The eggs of head lice are firmly cemented to the hair, usually at the back of the head; those of body lice are fixed to the clothing. Nits, $0.8 \times 0.3 \mathrm{~mm}$, are visible as light white specks.
