## Source [^1]
- (personality tests) Tests designed to assess the personalities and abilities of individuals to determine their suitability for a particular job and to make best use of their talents. With the increasing use of computers to analyse information, the tests used in personnel selection have become increasingly complex; choosing the wrong person for a senior job can be costly and have far-reaching effects in a competitive market. See also ASSESSMENT CENTRE; INTEREST FACTORS.
