## Source [^1]
- adj. Pertaining to a molecule that has one hydrophilic end (attracted to water) and one hydrophobic end (repulsed by water), such as are found in the lipid components of cell membranes and in detergents. See also LIPID BILAYER. \[From Greek amphi both + pathos feeling + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
