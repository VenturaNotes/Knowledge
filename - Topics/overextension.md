## Source [^1]
- $n$. The interpretation of a word, usually by a child during the course of language development in the second or third year, to denote more than it normally denotes, as when a child understands the word daddy to denote all men, or cat to denote all animals. See also CONCEPT FORMATION. Compare MISMATCH, OVERGENERALIZATION, UNDEREXTENSION.
