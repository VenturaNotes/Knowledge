## Source [^1]
- a type of T lymphocyte that plays a key role in cell-mediated immunity by recognizing foreign antigen on the surface of antigen-presenting cells when this is associated with the individual's MHC antigens, having been processed by antigen-presenting cells. Helper T cells stimulate the production of cytotoxic T cells, which destroy the target cells.
