## Source [^1]
- Can think of the [[multiplicative principle]] as the number of pairs needed to choose one of each of something
	- For example, if you have 8 appetizers and 14 entrées, there are $8*14 = 112$ choices or pairs that exist where $x$ is an appetizer and $y$ is an entrée.
