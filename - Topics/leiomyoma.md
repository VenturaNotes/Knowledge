## Source [^1]
- $n$. a benign tumor of smooth muscle. Such tumors occur most commonly in the uterus (see FIBROID) but can also arise in the digestive tract, walls of blood vessels, etc. They may undergo malignant change (see LEIOMYOSARCOMA).
