## Source [^1]
- a bone forming the base of the cranium behind the eyes. It consists of a body, containing air spaces continuous with the nasal cavity (see PARANASAL SINUSES); two wings that form part of the orbits; and two pterygoid processes projecting down from the point where the two wings join the body.
