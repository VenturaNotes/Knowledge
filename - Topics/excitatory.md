## Source [^1]
- adj. Tending to cause excitation. In neurophysiology, characteristic of neurons that cause other neurons to fire, or neurotransmitters, the action of which tends to cause neurons to fire, or synapses (1) at which the transmission of nerve impulses tends to cause postsynaptic neurons to fire. See also EXCITATORY POSTSYNAPTIC POTENTIAL. Compare INHIBITORY.
