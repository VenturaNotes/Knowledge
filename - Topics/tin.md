## Source [^1]
- (symbol: Sn) A metal, atomic number 50, that has good resistance to corrosion and alloys readily with other metals, such as copper. It is used as a component in many solders, and is extensively used to make permanent ohmic contacts in electronic circuits, devices, or other equipment.
