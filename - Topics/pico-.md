## Source [^1]
- (symbol: p) A prefix to a unit, indicating a submultiple of a millionth of a millionth, $10^{-12}$, of that unit, as in picosecond.
## Source[^2]
- prefix denoting one million-millionth $\left(10^{-12}\right)$.
## Source[^3]
- Symbol p. A prefix used in the metric system to denote $10^{-12}$. For example, $10^{-12}$ farad $=1$ picofarad $(\mathrm{pF})$.
