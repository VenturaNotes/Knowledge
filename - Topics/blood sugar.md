## Source [^1]
- the concentration of glucose in the blood, normally expressed in millimoles per litre. The normal range is $3.5-5.5 \mathrm{mmol} / \mathrm{l}$. Blood-sugar estimation is an important investigation in a variety of diseases, most notably in diabetes mellitus. See also HYPERGLYCAEMIA; HYPOGLYCAEMIA.
