## Source [^1]
- a research process in which results are reported according to the treatment arm to which a research subject was assigned, rather than the treatment eventually received. Intention to treat analysis is very widely used in clinical research as a method of avoiding bias resulting from nonrandom withdrawal from the trial or crossover between treatment arms.
