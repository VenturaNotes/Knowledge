## Source [^1]
- $n$. painful swallowing. This may be due to severe inflammation of the gullet (see oESOPHAGITIS) or infection as by such as cytomegalovirus, candidiasis, or herpes simplex virus in an immunocompromised patient. Other causes include neuromuscular disease, such as achalasia, foreign bodies, such as impacted fish bones, and malignancy.
