## Source [^1]
- $n$. A usage of language in which an inoffensive word or expression is substituted for one that is considered unpleasant or derogatory, as in pass away for die or sleep with for have sexual intercourse with. Compare DYSPHEMISm. \[From Greek eu good + pheme speech, from phanai to speak]
