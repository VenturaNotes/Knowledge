## Source [^1]
- An integer representation in binary notation in which negative numbers are obtained from positive numbers, and vice versa, by complementing all the bits of a number $\textendash$ converting 1s to 0s and vice versa $\textendash$ and adding 1 (discarding the carry); for example, +3 in 4-bit two's complement is $0011_{2}$ and -3 in 4-bit two's complement is $1100_{2} + 1 = 1101_{2}$ .
