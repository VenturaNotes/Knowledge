## Source [^1]
- Techniques for minimizing the effects of electrical heating on the performance, reliability, and economics of running electronic equipment. These range from designs that incorporate low-power circuitry or provide mitigation techniques, such as thermal protection, to operational procedures that put devices into standby mode when workloads are light.
