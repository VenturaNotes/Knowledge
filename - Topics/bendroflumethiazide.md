## Source [^1]
- (bendrofluazide) $n$. a potent thiazide diuretic used in the treatment of conditions involving retention of fluid, such as congestive heart failure, hypertension, and oedema. Side-effects include a fall in blood pressure on standing up, dizziness, lethargy, and muscle cramps.
