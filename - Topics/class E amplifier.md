## Source [^1]
- A switching amplifier used for radiofrequency amplification; it employs only one transistor, with an output resonator to control the frequency of operation and harmonic content of the output. In this arrangement the output capacitance of the transistor can be incorporated into the load resonator network. Theoretical efficiency of 100% can be obtained with an ideal switching behavior.
