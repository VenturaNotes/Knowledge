## Source [^1]
- A unit of digital information made up of eight [[bit|bits]]

## Source[^2]
- Eight contiguous bits; an eight bit byte. The term is used instead of byte to prevent confusion in cases where the term has pre-existing hardware associations, as in machines with 7-bit bytes, 9-bit bytes, 12-bit bytes.
