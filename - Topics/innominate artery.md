## Source [^1]
- (brachiocephalic artery) a short artery originating as the first large branch of the aortic arch, passing upwards to the right, and ending at the lower neck near the right sternoclavicular joint. Here it divides into the right common carotid and the right subclavian arteries.
## Source[^2]
- A short artery that branches from the aorta to divide into the subclavian artery (the main artery to the arm) and the right carotid artery (which supplies blood to the head).
