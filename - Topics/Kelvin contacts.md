## Source [^1]
- A means for testing or making measurements in electronic circuits or components, particularly when low values are being measured. Two sets of leads are used to each test point, similar with respect to thickness, material, and length; one set carries the test signal and the second set connects with the measuring instrument used. The effect of resistance in the leads is thus eliminated from the measurement.
