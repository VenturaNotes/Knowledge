## Source [^1]
- One of the many extensions (see HAUSDORFF DIMENSION) of the notion of dimension to objects for which the traditional concept of dimension is not appropriate. The fractal dimension may have a non-integer value. The Koch curve has dimension $\log_3 4 \approx 1.26$. The Cantor set has dimension $\log_3 2 \approx 0.63$. Fractal dimension has found applications analyzing chaotic or noisy processes (see CHAOS).
