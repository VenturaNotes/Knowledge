## Source [^1]
- (symbol: $M$; unit: ampere per meter) A measure of the magnetic polarization that occurs when a material is placed in a magnetic flux. It is defined as the magnetic moment per unit volume and is the product of magnetic field strength, $H$, and susceptibility, $\chi_{\mathrm{m}}$:$$\begin{array}{l}M = H \chi_{\mathrm{m}} \\M = \frac{B}{\mu_0} - H \\\end{array}$$where $B$ is the magnetic flux density and $\mu_0$ is the permeability of free space.
