## Source [^1]
- A camera with two lenses set side-by-side that captures two images of the same scene and then merges them to produce a three-dimensional image. In most cases, the distance between the lenses is similar to that between human eyes, so the resulting three-dimensional images match human stereo vision. Some cameras have only a single lens that is moved sideways between exposures to obtain the two images.
