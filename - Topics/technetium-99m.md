## Source [^1]
- $n$. an isotope of the artificial radioactive element technetium. It emits gamma radiation only, with no beta particles, at a convenient energy for detection by a gamma camera and has a short half-life. For these reasons it is widely used in nuclear medicine as a tracer for the examination of many organs (see SCINTIGRAM). Symbol: Tc-99m.
