## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cytotoxic drug (an alkylating agent) used in the treatment of malignant disease, particularly sarcomas, testicular tumors, and lymphomas. Side-effects include nausea, vomiting, alopecia, and haemorrhagic cystitis; concomitant administration of mesna is recommended to prevent cystitis.
