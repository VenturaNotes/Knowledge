## Source [^1]
- $n$. a genus of Gram-positive nonmotile spherical bacteria occurring in grapelike clusters. Some species are saprophytes; others parasites. Many species produce exotoxins. The species S. aureus is commonly present on skin and mucous membranes; it causes boils and internal abscesses.
