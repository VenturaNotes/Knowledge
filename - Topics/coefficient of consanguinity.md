## Source [^1]
- $n$. The probability, symbolized by $F_{i j}$ or $f_{i j}$, that a pair of alleles belonging to different individuals are the same, given their degree of common ancestry. Also called the coefficient of kinship . Compare COEFFICIENT OF RELATIONSHIP.
