## Source [^1]
- Symbol da. A prefix used in the metric system to denote ten times. For example, 10 hertz $=1$ decahertz $(\mathrm{daHz})$.
## Source[^2]
- (symbol: da) A prefix to a unit, denoting a multiple of 10 of that unit.
