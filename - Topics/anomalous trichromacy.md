## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of color-blindness in which the proportions of three primary colors (1) that are mixed to match a comparison color deviate from the normal. Also called anomalous trichromasy, anomalous trichromatism, anomalous trichromatopsia, anomalous trichromopsia. See also Rayleigh equation. Compare anomalous dichromacy. anomalous trichromat $n$. A person with anomalous trichromacy.
