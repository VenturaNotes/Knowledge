## Source [^1]
- A bank or building-society account held in the names or two or more people, often husband and wife. On the death of one party the balance in the account goes to the survivor(s), except in the case of partnerships, executors' accounts, or trustees' accounts. Normally, any of the holders of a joint account is entitled to operate it alone.
