## Source [^1]
- A sale made on the basis that the buyer has inspected the goods and is buying them as a result of this inspection and not on any guarantee of quality or condition by the seller. The seller does not guarantee or imply that the goods are suitable for any particular purpose. Compare SALE BY DESCRIPTION; SALE BY SAMPLE.
