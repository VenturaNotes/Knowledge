## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) the state or quality of being human. In most ethical traditions, membership of the human species is seen as conferring a unique moral status, so that human life is considered inherently and particularly valuable and worthy of protection (see SANCTITY OF LIFE). Humanity may be defined in terms of a unique capacity to feel, reason, evoke emotional responses, or form relationships (see PERSONHOOD). 
- (2) compassion or benevolence.
