## Source [^1]
- A rate of charging that varies according to the time of the year. This is quite common in advertising, for example the Radio Times charges a higher page rate for its Christmas issue than for any other issue. Seasonal discounts are also common, especially in advertising, when sellers wish to encourage business in a slack period.
