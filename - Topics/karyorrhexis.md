## Source [^1]
- A stage of cell death (see NECROSIS) that involves fragmentation of a cell nucleus. The nucleus breaks down into small dark beads of damaged chromatin. This usually leads to further dissolution of the nucleus (karyolysis), whereupon the damaged chromatin gradually fades.
