## Source [^1]
- The number of pixels that make up a display, expressed as the product of its width and its height. There are a large number of standards for different display resolutions, most of which are determined by the Consumer Technology Association. See also 4K HD; STANDARD-DEFINITION; ULTRA EXTENDED GRAPHICS ARRAY; WIDESCREEN ULTRA EXTENDED GRAPHICS ARRAY.
