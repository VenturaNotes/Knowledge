## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. pubes) a bone forming the lower and anterior part of each side of the hip bone (see also PELVIS). The two pubes meet at the front of the pelvis at the pubic symphysis. See also PUBES.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. pubes) One of the three bones that make up each half of the pelvic girdle. It is the most anterior of the three pelvic bones. In mammals and many reptiles the pubes are united at a slightly movable joint, the pubic symphysis. See also ILIUM; ISCHIUM.
