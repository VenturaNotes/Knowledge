## Source [^1]
- $n$. a former term for an individual having both male and female sex organs or sex organs containing both ovarian and testicular cells. It is now considered offensive. See INTERSEX.
## Source[^2]
- (bisexual) 
- (1) An animal, such as the earthworm, that has both male and female reproductive organs. In sequential hermaphroditism the individual functions first as one sex, then as the other, as occurs in most sponges. 
- (2) A plant whose flowers contain both stamens and carpels. This is the usual arrangement in most plants. See also InCOMPATIBILITY. Compare DIOECIOUS; MONOECIOUS.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. 
- (1) Any plant or animal with the reproductive organs of both sexes; in the case of humans, usually having a female sex chromosome but being partly masculinized by abnormally high levels of androgens in the womb. Compare PSEUdohermaphrodite. adj. 
- (2) Of, relating to, or resembling a hermaphrodite. Compare EPICENE (1). hermaphroditism $n$. The congenital condition of being a hermaphrodite. \[Named after Hermaphroditos in Greek mythology, so called because he was the son of Hermes and Aphrodite, who merged with a female nymph to form one person]
