## Source [^1]
- A technique used to study written material (often advertising copy) to reduce it to meaningful units, using carefully applied rules. It provides an objective and systematic description of a communication's content.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A collection of techniques often used in qualitative research for the systematic and objective description and classification of the manifest or latent subject matter of written or spoken verbal communications, usually by counting the incidence or coincidence of utterances falling into several (usually predetermined) categories.
