## Source [^1]
- (EPS) an assessment of the electrical system of the heart by means of thin preshaped wires passed into the heart via the femoral vein. An electrocardiogram is recorded from various points within the heart, and the reaction of the heart to timed electrical stimuli is observed. The information obtained guides treatment of arrhythmia, particularly radiofrequency ablation.
