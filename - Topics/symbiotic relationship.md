## Source [^1]
- [[Acacia Tree]] and [[Acacia Ant]] [[Mutualism]] is a type of symbiotic relationship where both the acacia tree and the acacia ant benefit from each other
	- The acacia tree provides food and shelter for the ants while the ants protect the trees from herbivores ad help to spread its seeds
## Source[^2]
- There are four main symbiotic relationships
	- [[Mutualism]]
	- [[Commensalism]]
	- [[Parasitism]]
	- [[Competition]]
