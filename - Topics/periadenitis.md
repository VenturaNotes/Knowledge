## Source [^1]
- $n$. inflammation of tissues around a joint capsule, including tendons and bursae. Chronic periarthritis, which may be spontaneous or follow injury, is a common cause of pain and stiffness of the shoulder; it usually responds to local steroid injections or physiotherapy.
