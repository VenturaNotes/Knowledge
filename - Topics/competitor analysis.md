## Source [^1]
- The gathering of data about a competitor's products and prices in order to identify actual or future sources of competitive advantage. An understanding of a competitor's objectives, strategies, strengths, and weaknesses will help an organization to develop its own strategy. The analysis should reveal which competitors to attack and which to avoid. See BENCHMARKING; SWOT.
