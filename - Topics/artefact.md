## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) An article, such as a tool or a work of art, made by a person or people. 
- (2) Something that is observed in an empirical investigation and is not a natural occurrence but a by-product of the investigation itself. Also (especially in US) spelt artifact. artefactual or artifactual adj. \[From Latin arte by art, from ars skill or art + factum made, from facere to make]
