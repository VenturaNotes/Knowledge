## Source [^1]
- $n$. A memory disorder characterized by an inability to learn simple new skills in spite of an ability to perform complex skills learnt before the onset of the disorder. Also called dysmnesic syndrome. \[From Greek dys- bad or abnormal + mnasthai to remember + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
