## Source [^1]
- Transmission of information as sequential units. For example, if two computers connected by a single wire wish to communicate an 8-bit unit of information, the sending computer would transmit each of the eight bits in sequence over the wire, while the receiving computer would reassemble the sequential bits into the original 8 -bit units. Compare PARALLEL TRANSFER.
## Source[^2]
- Transfer of information along a single path in a computer or data processing system in which the characters move one after the other along the path.
