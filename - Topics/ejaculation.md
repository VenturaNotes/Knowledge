## Source [^1]
- $n$. the discharge of semen from the erect penis at the moment of sexual climax (orgasm) in the male. The constituents of semen are not released simultaneously, but in the following sequence: the secretion of Cowper's glands followed by that of the prostate gland and the spermatozoa and finally the secretion of the seminal vesicles. See also PREMATURE EJACULATION.
## Source[^2]
- The propulsion of semen out of the erect penis due to powerful rhythmic contractions of the urethra. An ejaculation coincides with the peak of sexual excitement (orgasm) and is accompanied by various physiological effects in the body, such as increased respiration rate and heart rate.
