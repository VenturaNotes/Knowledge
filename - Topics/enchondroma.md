## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. enchondromata) a benign cartilaginous tumor (see CHONDROMA) occurring within cortical bone, usually in the short tubular bones of the hands and feet. Such tumors are usually solitary and asymptomatic, but an enlarging lesion may fracture (the usual reason for presentation). When multiple tumors occur the condition is known as enchondromatosis. Compare ECCHONDROMA.
