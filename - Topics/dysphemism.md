## Source [^1]
- $n$. A usage of language in which an unpleasant or derogatory word or expression is substituted for a neutral one, as in jackals and hyenas of the press for journalists, or self-abuse for masturbation. Compare euphemism. \[From Greek dys- ill, bad, or abnormal + pheme speech, from phanai to speak]
