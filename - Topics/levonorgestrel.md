## Source [^1]
- $n$. a synthetic female sex hormone $\textendash$ a progestogen $\textendash$ used mainly in oral contraceptives, either in combination with an oestrogen or as a progestogen-only preparation. It is also used for emergency contraception (see POSTCOITAL CONTRACEPTION) and in the intrauterine system (see IUS).
