## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) facial expression, often a guide to a patient's state of health as well as emotions. The typical facies seen in a patient with enlarged adenoids is a vacant look, with the mouth drooping open. A Hippocratic facies is the sallow face, sagging and with listless staring eyes, that indicates approaching death. 
- (2) (in anatomy) a specified surface of a bone or other body part.
