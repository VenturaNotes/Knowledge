## Source [^1]
- A market for a security, commodity, currency, etc. in which there are currently a large number of transactions. In such a market the spread between bid and offer prices will be narrow and sizable transactions can take place without moving the price of the security, etc. A deep market is highly liquid. Compare THIN market.
