## Source[^2]
- adj. Of or relating to the eye. ophthalmologist $n$. A medical practitioner specializing in disorders of the eye. ophthalmology $n$. The branch of medicine concerned with disorders of the eye. ophthalmoscope $n$. An instrument for examining the interior of the eye, comprising a light, a mirror perforated by a hole through which the examiner looks, and a rotating disc for positioning lenses of varying strengths in front of the hole. \[From Greek ophthalmos an eyeball, from op(sethai) to see + thal(a)m(os) an inner room or chamber, from thalos a vault + -i k(os) of, relating to, or resembling]
