## Source [^1]
- The register kept by the Office of Fair Trading, as required by the Consumer Credit Acts 1974 and 2006, relating to the licensing or carrying on of consumer-credit businesses or consumer-hire businesses. The register contains particulars of undetermined applications, licences that are in force or have at any time been suspended or revoked, and decisions under the Act and any appeal from them. The public is entitled to inspect the register, which can now be viewed online.
