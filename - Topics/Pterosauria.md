## Source [^1]
- An extinct order of flying reptiles$\textemdash$the pterodactyls$\textemdash$that lived in the late Triassic, Jurassic, and Cretaceous periods (225-65 million years ago). Pterodactyls had beaked jaws and an elongated fourth finger that supported a membranous wing, with wingspans ranging from 50 cm to 13 m . They had long jointed tails, no feathers, and were capable of powered flapping flight.
