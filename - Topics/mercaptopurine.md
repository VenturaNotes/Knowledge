## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug that prevents the growth of cancer cells; it is used chiefly in the treatment of acute leukaemias and chronic myeloid leukaemia but also in severe Crohn's disease (see ANTIMETABOLITE). It commonly reduces the numbers of white blood cells; mouth ulcers and digestive upsets may also occur.
