## Source [^1]
- The value of orders received but not yet carried out by firms in certain sectors, mainly construction and engineering, where output is mainly produced to order, and not sold off the shelf. The size of an industry's order book may be expressed in cash terms, or as a month's output. Changes in order books are a leading economic indicator.
