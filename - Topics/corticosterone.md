## Source [^1]
- $n$. a steroid hormone (see CORTICOSTEROID) synthesized and released in small amounts by the adrenal cortex.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A corticosteroid hormone belonging to the glucocorticoid group, secreted by the adrenal cortex in response to serious injury or stress, shifting the body from carbohydrate to fat metabolism, regulating blood pressure, having anti-inflammatory effects, and sometimes suppressing immune responses.
