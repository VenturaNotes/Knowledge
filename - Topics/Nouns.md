## Source [^1]
- Nouns are words that identify or name people, places, or things. 
- Nouns can function as 
	- the subject of a clause or sentence
	- an object of a verb
	- an object of a preposition
- Examples of nouns
	- Cat
	- Book
	- Table
	- Girl
	- Plane
