## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug related to vitamin A (see RETINOID) that is administered orally in the treatment of severe acne that has failed to respond to other treatment and topically for mild to moderate acne. Possible side-effects include dry skin, nose bleeds, eyelid and lip inflammation, muscle, joint, and abdominal pains, fetal abnormalities (it should not be taken during pregnancy), diarrhoea, and some disturbances of vision.
