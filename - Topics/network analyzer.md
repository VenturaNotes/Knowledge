## Source [^1]
- An instrument used for measuring the incident, reflected, and transmitted power at a two-port network, particularly at radio and microwave frequencies. This will provide the scattering parameters of the network under test. Both scalar (magnitude only) and vector (magnitude and phase) measurements can be made.
