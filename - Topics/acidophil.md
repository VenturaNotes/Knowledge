## Source [^1]
- (acidophilic) adj. 
- (1) (in histology) describing tissues, cells, or parts of cells that stain with acid dyes (such as eosin). See also ADENOMA. 
- (2) (in bacteriology) describing bacteria that grow well in acid media.
