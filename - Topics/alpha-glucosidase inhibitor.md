## Source [^1]
- any member of a group of oral hypoglycaemic drugs, including acarbose (Glucobay), used for treating type 2 diabetes mellitus. They reduce the breakdown and absorption of carbohydrates in the intestine by blocking the action of an important enzyme ( $\alpha$-glucosidase) in this process. Side-effects include flatulence and diarrhoea.
