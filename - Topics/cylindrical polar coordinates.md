## Source [^1]
- A technique for giving three-dimensional coordinates $(r,\phi ,z)$ of a point. Radial distance $r$ is the distance of the point from the vertical $z$-axis, azimuth angle $\phi$ specifies a rotation from the $x$-axis, and height $z$ gives the distance to move parallel to the $z$-axis to reach the point. See also POLAR COORDINATES; SPHERICAL POLAR COORDINATES.
