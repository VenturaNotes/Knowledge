## Source [^1]
- The standard deviation of an estimator of a population parameter. The standard error of the sample mean, from a sample of size $n$, is $\sigma / \sqrt{n}$, where $\sigma^2$ is the population variance. 
## Source[^2]
- (of a statistic) The square root of the estimated variance of a statistic; a measure of the reliability of this statistic as an estimator of the population parameter from the sample.
