## Source [^1]
- (DSB-SC) A modified version of amplitude modulation that removes all or most of the carrier wave frequency, leaving just the double sidebands intact. As the carrier wave frequency has one-third of the total signal energy, this results in significant power savings. The disadvantage is that simple circuits such as diode detectors can no longer be used for demodulation.
