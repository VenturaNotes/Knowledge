## Source [^1]
- Any of a class of amines that possess a catechol $\left(\mathrm{C}_{6} \mathrm{H}_{4}(\mathrm{OH})_{2}\right)$ ring. Including dopamine, adrenaline, and noradrenaline, they function as neurotransmitters and/or hormones.
