## Source [^1]
- (CPW) A transmission line medium comprising three conductors on the surface of a dielectric substrate: two ground planes and a central signal line, separated by gaps. The characteristic impedance is determined by the ratio of the gap width to the thickness of the dielectric substrate, and the dielectric constant of the substrate.
