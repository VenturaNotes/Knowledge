## Source [^1]
- adj. Sharp or pointed; penetrating or piercing; important or critical; of or relating to sensation or perception that is finely discriminating; of or relating to a disorder or symptom that begins abruptly and subsides quickly. Compare CHRONIC. \[From Latin acutus sharpened, from acuere to sharpen]
## Source[^2]
- adj. 
- (1) describing a disease of rapid onset, severe symptoms, and brief duration. Compare CHRONIC. 
- (2) describing any intense symptom, such as severe pain.
