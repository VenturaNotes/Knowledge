## Source [^1]
- (physostigmine) An alkaloid, derived from the calabar bean plant, that inhibits cholinesterase by covalently binding with it (see inhibition). Eserine is used to treat the eye condition glaucoma, delayed gastric emptying, and the effects of certain poisons, notably atropine.
