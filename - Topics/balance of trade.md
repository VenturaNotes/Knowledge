## Source [^1]
- Balance of trade is the difference between the value of a country's exports and the value of a country's imports for a given period

## Source[^2]
- The excess of visible exports over visible imports. This is a major, but far from the only, component of the balance of payments on current account.
## Source[^3]
- The accounts setting out the results of a country's trading position. It is a component of the balance of payments, forming part of the current account. It includes visibles (i.e. imports and exports in physical merchandise) but not invisible earnings and expenditure (receipts and expenditure on such services as insurance, finance, freight, and tourism).
