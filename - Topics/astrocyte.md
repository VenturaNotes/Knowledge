## Source [^1]
- (astroglial cell) n. a type of cell with numerous sheet-like processes extending from its cell body, found throughout the central nervous system. It is one of the several different types of cell that make up the glia. The cells have been ascribed the function of providing nutrients for neurons and possibly of taking part in information storage processes.
