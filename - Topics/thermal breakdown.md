## Source [^1]
- (thermal runaway) Breakdown of a reverse-biased p-n junction caused by generation of excess free charge carriers due to the cumulative interaction between increasing junction temperature and increasing power dissipation. As the temperature is increased the effect is to reduce the voltage at which avalanche breakdown occurs.
