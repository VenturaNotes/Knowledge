## Source [^1]
- $n$. a powerful antihistamine drug used to treat allergic conditions and $\textendash$ because of its sedative action $\textendash$ insomnia; it is also used as a premedication and an antiemetic. Side-effects include drowsiness, dizziness, and confusion.
