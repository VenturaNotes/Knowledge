## Source [^1]
- A device or material that possesses a band-pass frequency-response characteristic, equivalent to an inductor-capacitor (LC) network. Some ceramic and crystalline materials possess such resonances with very high Q factor, and can be used in filters and oscillators to determine the frequency behavior.
