## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug used in the treatment of high blood pressure (hypertension) and also to relieve urinary retention due to benign enlargement of the prostate gland (see ALPHA BLOCKER). Common sideeffects include dizziness, headache, palpitations, and nausea.
