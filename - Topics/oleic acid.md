## Source [^1]
- An unsaturated fatty acid with one double bond, $\mathrm{CH}_{3}\left(\mathrm{CH}_{2}\right)_{7} \mathrm{CH}\text{:} \mathrm{CH}\left(\mathrm{CH}_{2}\right)_{7} \mathrm{COOH}$. Oleic acid is one of the most abundant constituent fatty acids of animal and plant fats, occurring in butterfat, lard, tallow, groundnut oil, soya-bean oil, etc. Its systematic chemical name is cis-octadec-9-enoic acid.
