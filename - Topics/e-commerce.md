- The practice of advertising and selling goods and services over the internet. This has rapidly become established as a standard business practice.

## Source[^2]
- (e-business) In general, commerce using electronic media. Usually it is taken to mean commerce using the Internet, for sale of goods and services. Internet trading has expanded enormously over the last 20 years, with the creation of companies that only trade online (often known as dotcom companies). Other commercial organizations have a combination of traditional trading with a significant amount of Internet activity (sometimes referred to as bricks-and-clicks or clicks-and-mortar). See also EMONEY.
