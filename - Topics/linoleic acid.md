## Source [^1]
- A liquid polyunsaturated fatty acid with two double bonds, $\mathrm{CH}_{3}\left(\mathrm{CH}_{2}\right)_{4} \mathrm{CH}: \mathrm{CHCH}_{2} \mathrm{CH}: \mathrm{CH}\left(\mathrm{CH}_{2}\right)_{7} \mathrm{COOH}$. Linoleic acid is abundant in many plant fats and oils, e.g. linseed oil, groundnut oil, and soya-bean oil. It is an essential fatty acid.
