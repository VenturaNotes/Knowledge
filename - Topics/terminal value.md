## Source [^1]
- (TV) The value of an investment at the end of an investment period taking into account a specified rate of interest over the period. The formula is the same as that for compound interest, i.e.$$T V=P(1+r)^{t},$$where $T V$ is the final amount at the end of period, $P$ is the principal amount invested, $r$ is the interest rate, and $t$ is the time in years for which the investment takes place.
