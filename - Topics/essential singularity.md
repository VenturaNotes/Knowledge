## Source [^1]
- An isolated singularity $a$ of a complex-valued function $f(z)$ such that the Laurent series of $f(z)$ at $a$ includes infinitely many negative powers. For example, the function $sin(1/z)$ has an essential singularity at $z=0$. Compare POLE, REMOVABLE, SINGULARITY.
