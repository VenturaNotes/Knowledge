## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) describing a tumor that does not invade and destroy the tissue in which it originates or spread to distant sites in the body, i.e. a tumor that is not cancerous. Benign tumors may nonetheless cause serious morbidity or mortality by compressing or obstructing vital structures. 
- (2) describing any disorder or condition that does not produce harmful effects. Compare MALIGNANT.
