## Source [^1]
- $n$. inflammation of the tissues around a ureter. This is usually associated with inflammation of the ureter itself (ureteritis) often behind an obstruction caused by a stone or stricture. Treatment is directed to relieving any obstruction of the ureter and controlling the infection with antibiotics.
