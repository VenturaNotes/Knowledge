## Source [^1]
- $n$. the process that causes the biological similarity between parents and their offspring. Genetics is the study of heredity.
## Source[^2]
- The transmission of characteristics from parents to offspring via the chromosomes. The study of heredity (genetics) was first undertaken by Gregor Mendel (see MENDEL's LAWS).
## Source[^3]
- $n$. The transmission from parents to their offspring of genetic factors that partly determine individual characteristics. hereditary adj. Of or relating to characteristics that can be transmitted genetically from parents to offspring. See also gene, heritability, Mendel's laws. \[From Latin hereditas inheritance, from heres an heir]
