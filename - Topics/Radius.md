## Source [^1]
- $n$. the outer and shorter bone of the forearm (compare ULNA). It partially revolves about the ulna, permitting pronation and supination of the hand. The head of the radius articulates with the humerus. The lower end articulates both with the scaphoid and lunate bones of the carpus (wrist) and with the ulna (via the ulnar notch on the side of the bone). $\textemdash$radial adj.
