## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of catatonia arising as a secondary or symptomatic effect of a general medical condition, characterized by catatonic signs such as motor immobility, excessive and purposeless motor activity, catalepsy or cerea flexibilitas, extreme negativism (1) or mutism, peculiarities of voluntary movement, echolalia, or echopraxia.
