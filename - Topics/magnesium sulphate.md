## Source [^1]
- a magnesium salt used in the form of mixtures or powders as an osmotic laxative for rapid evacuation of the bowel. It is also administered intravenously to treat magnesium deficiency and serious arrhythmias and to prevent the recurrence of convulsions in eclampsia.
