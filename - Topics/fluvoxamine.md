## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antidepressant drug that acts by prolonging the action of the neurotransmitter serotonin (5-hydroxytryptamine) in the brain (see SSRI). Side-effects may include sleepiness, agitation, tremor, vomiting, and diarrhoea.
