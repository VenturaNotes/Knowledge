## Source [^1]
- (CRL) the longest measurement of the fetus from end to end. Measurement of the CRL of the embryo in the first trimester has been shown to be the most accurate parameter for assessment of gestational age; the measurement is less accurate at the end of the first trimester because of fetal flexion.
