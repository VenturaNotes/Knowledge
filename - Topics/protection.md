## Source [^1]
- (1) The use of trade policy to raise profits and employment in industries liable to competition from imports. Protection may be via tariffs, import quotas, or voluntary export restraints and other non-tariff barriers to trade. See CONTINGENT PROTECTION; EFFECTIVE PROTECTION; NOMINAL PROTECTION.
