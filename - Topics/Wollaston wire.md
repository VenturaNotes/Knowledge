## Source [^1]
- Extremely fine platinum wire that is used for electroscope wires, microfuses, and hot-wire instruments. It is produced by coating platinum wire with a sheath of silver, drawing them together into a relatively fine uniform diameter wire, and then dissolving the silver with a suitable acid. Diameters down to about one micrometre may be produced by this method.
