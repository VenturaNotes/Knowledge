## Source [^1]
- Hereditary lack of pigmentation (see MELANIN) in an organism. Albino animals and human beings have no color in their skin, hair, or eyes (the irises appear pink from underlying blood vessels). The allele responsible is recessive to the allele for normal pigmentation.
