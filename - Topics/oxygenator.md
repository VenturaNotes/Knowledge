## Source [^1]
- $n$. a machine that oxygenates blood outside the body. It is used together with pumps to maintain the patient's circulation while he or she is undergoing open heart surgery (see HEART-LUNG MACHINE) or to improve the circulation of a patient with heart or lung disorders that lower the amount of blood oxygen.
