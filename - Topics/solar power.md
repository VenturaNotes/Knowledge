## Source [^1]
- Electrical energy obtained from sunlight. The two main techniques for doing this are the use of photovoltaic systems and sunlight concentration using lenses and mirrors. Photovoltaic systems are continually improving in efficiency and reducing in cost, so that solar power is starting to emerge as a cost-effective means of electricity generation.
