## Source [^1]
- $n$. one of the cells in the pulp of a tooth that form dentine. Odontoblasts line the pulp and have small processes that extend into the dentine. The process of dentine formation is known as dentinogenesis.
## Source[^2]
- A cell that is responsible for producing the dentine of vertebrate teeth. Odontoblasts are found around the lining of the pulp cavity and have processes that extend into the dentine.
