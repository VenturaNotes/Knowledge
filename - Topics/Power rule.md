## Synthesis

### Power Rule for Integration
- $\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+C$
	- To find integral of $x$
		- $x^1$ $\to$ $\frac{x^{1+1}}{1+1}$ $\to$ $\frac{x^2}{2}$
	- So integral of x is $\frac{x^2}{2} + C$
