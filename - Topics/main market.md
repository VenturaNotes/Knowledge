## Source [^1]
- The premier market for the trading of equities on the London Stock Exchange. The listing requirements are more stringent, and the liquidity of the market is greater, than on the Alternative Investment Market. A company wishing to enter this market must have audited trading figures covering at least five years and must place $25 \%$ of its shares in public hands. The main market currently deals in over 2500 securities.
