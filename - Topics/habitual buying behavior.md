## Source [^1]
- Consumer behavior in the buying of cheap frequently purchased items, characterized by low consumer involvement and few significant brand differences. Purchases are made out of habit, rather than a strong commitment to a brand. Generally, price and sales promotions are used to stimulate sales, as consumers tend to base their decisions on past behaviour and have no need for further information.
