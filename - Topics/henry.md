## Source [^1]
- $n$. the SI unit of inductance, equal to the inductance of a closed circuit with a magnetic flux of 1 weber per ampere of current. Symbol: H.
## Source[^2]
- (symbol: H) The SI unit of inductance and permeance. The self- or mutual inductance of a closed loop is one henry if a current of one ampere gives rise to a magnetic flux of one weber.
