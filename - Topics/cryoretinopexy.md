## Source [^1]
- $n$. the use of extreme cold to freeze areas of weak or torn retina in order to cause scarring and seal breaks. It is used in cryosurgery for retinal detachment and also in certain other ischaemic conditions of the retina (e.g. diabetic retinopathy).
