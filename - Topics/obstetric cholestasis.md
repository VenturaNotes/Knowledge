## Source [^1]
- a pregnancy-related condition characterized by intense pruritus (itch) $\textendash$ and consequent sleep deprivation $\textendash$ in the absence of a skin rash, with abnormal liver function tests and elevated bile acids, all of which remit following delivery. The clinical importance of obstetric cholestasis lies in the potential fetal risks, which may include prematurity and intrauterine death.
