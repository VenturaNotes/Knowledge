## Source [^1]
- $n$. a major division of an organ or part of an organ, especially one having a rounded form and often separated from other lobes by fissures or bands of connective tissue. For example, the brain, liver, and lung are divided into lobes. $\textemdash$lobar adj.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A structurally distinct and usually rounded part of any larger organ or body part. The four lobes of each cerebral hemisphere are the frontal lobe, the parietal lobe, the temporal lobe, and the occipital lobe (see illustration). \[From Latin lobus a lobe, from Greek lobos an earlobe]
