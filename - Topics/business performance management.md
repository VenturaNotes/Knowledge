## Source [^1]
- (BPM) A system of metrics for analyzing whether key performance indicators are being achieved by a company. Software systems for BPM typically use dashboards that show particular indicators and help executives to take decisions based on the information shown. A balanced scorecard approach is often adopted. BPM is also known as corporate performance management (CPM) or enterprise performance management (EPM).
