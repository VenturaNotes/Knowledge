## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of ataxia caused by a lesion in the anterior lobe of the cerebellum, usually as a consequence of alcohol dependence, characterized by staggering movement even when sober and inability to stand still with feet together. See also Romberg's sign, TANDEM Gait. Compare LOCOMOTOR ATAXIA.
