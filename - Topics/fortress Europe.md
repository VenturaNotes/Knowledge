## Source [^1]
- An expression of worries about European isolationism. The fear was that closer integration will lead not only to fewer obstacles to trade and mobility of capital and labour within Europe, but also to intensified restrictions on trade and factor mobility with the rest of the world. There is currently no evidence to suggest that restrictions are intensifying.
