## Source [^1]
- (1) See metameric SEGMENTation. 
- (2) See Cleavage. 
- (3) Describing a type of movement occurring in the stomach and small intestine in which regions of the gut wall undergo alternating ringlike contraction and relaxation. This produces a back-and-forth movement of the gut contents, breaking them up and mixing them with digestive juices to promote digestion and absorption of nutrients. Compare PERISTALSIS.
