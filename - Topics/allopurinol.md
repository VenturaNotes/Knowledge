## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug used for the prevention of acute attacks of gout and of kidney stones. It acts by reducing the level of uric acid in tissues and blood. Side-effects include nausea, vomiting, diarrhoea, headache, fever, stomach pains, and skin rashes. Occasionally, nerve damage and enlargement of the liver may occur.
