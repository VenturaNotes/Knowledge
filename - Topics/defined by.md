## Source [^1]
- A [[set]] can be defined by giving all its elements
	- A := {2, 5, 6}
		- The := means "defined by" here
		- The lefthand side is defined by the righthand side
		- It's a sign that reminds you that a new name/variable is introduced at this point
		- #question Why can't we just use the = sign in this case?
