## Source [^1]
- A means of assessing the requirements of a filter from the filter response specification. The steepness factor $A_{\mathrm{S}}$ for a low-pass filter response is given by the ratio $f_{\mathrm{S}} / f_{\mathrm{c}}$, where $f_{\mathrm{S}}$ is the frequency having the minimum required stop-band attenuation and $f_{\mathrm{c}}$ is the cut-off frequency of the pass-band. Once the steepness factor is calculated from the required filter response, it is compared with published normalized filter curves and a design is selected that meets or exceeds the requirements.
