## Source [^1]
- The size of a complex number as determined by its real and imaginary parts. The complex number $x + iy$ has a modulus given by $\sqrt[2]{x^2 + y^2}$ . If the complex number is plotted using $x$ and $y$ as Cartesian coordinates, the modulus is the distance of that point from the origin.
