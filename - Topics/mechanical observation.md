## Source [^1]
- Observation techniques involving mechanical observers either in conjunction with, or in place of, human observers. Examples include the motion-picture or video camera, the audimeter, the psychogalvanometer, the eye-camera, and the pupilometer. None of these would be used without the knowledge and permission of the respondent.
