## Source [^1]
- (symbol: $\tau$) 
- (1) The time interval during which the dielectric polarization of a point in a dielectric falls to $1/e$ of its original value due to the electric conductivity of the dielectric. 
- (2) The travel time of a charge carrier in a conductor or semiconductor before it is scattered and loses its momentum. 
- (3) The travel time of an electron's spin in a magnet before it is scattered and changes its orientation.
