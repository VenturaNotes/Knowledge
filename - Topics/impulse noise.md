## Source [^1]
- Noise of large amplitude and some statistical irregularity, affecting an analogue channel severely but (relatively) infrequently. In contrast, white noise affects it (relatively) unseverely but continuously. Impulse noise affecting an analogue channel carrying a binary signal usually causes burst errors.
## Source[^2]
- Noise of large amplitude and very short duration that results from a disturbance or series of disturbances.
