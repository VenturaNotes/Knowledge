## Source [^1]
- $n$. inflammation of the tissues around the kidney. This is usually due to spread of infection from the kidney itself (see PYELONEPHRITIS; PYONEPHROSIS). The patient has pain in the loins, fever, and fits of shivering. Prompt treatment of the underlying renal infection is required to prevent progression to an abscess.
