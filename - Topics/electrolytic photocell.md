## Source [^1]
- An electrolytic cell, constructed from certain materials such as selenium electrodes in selenium dioxide solution, that is sensitive to light when a small external d.c. voltage is applied across it. The cell has a linear sensitivity of about one milliampere per lumen of luminous flux.
