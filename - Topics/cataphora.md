## Source [^1]
- $n$. In speaking or writing, the use of a pronoun or other word to refer forward to something mentioned later in a sentence or linguistic expression. In the sentence When I was questioned, this is what I told the police: I heard a loud argument at about midnight, the word this exemplifies cataphora, because it refers forward to I heard a loud argument at about midnight. See also DEIXIS. Compare anAPHORA. cataphoric adj. \[From Greek kata down + pherein to carry]
