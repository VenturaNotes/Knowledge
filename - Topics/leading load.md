## Source [^1]
- (capacitive load) A reactive load usually containing resistance and capacitance that carries a leading current with respect to the voltage across the terminals. A pure capacitance introduces a lead of $90^{\circ}$ or one quarter wavelength. Compare LAGGING LOAD.
