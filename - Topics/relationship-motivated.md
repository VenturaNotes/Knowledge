## Source [^1]
- (person-oriented) Describing a leadership style that is concerned with maintaining positive relationships within the group, removing causes of friction, and lifting morale. A relationship-motivated leader will demonstrate trust of subordinates, respect for their ideas, and consideration for their feelings. Compare TASK-MOTIVATED. See contingency theories of leadership; Least Preferred Co-worker Scale.
