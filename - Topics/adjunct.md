## Source [^1]
- $n$. a subsidiary drug used in treating a disorder, which provides additional benefits to the main drug used in treatment. For example, the dopamine receptor agonist cabergoline is used as an adjunct to levodopa in the treatment of Parkinson's disease. $\textemdash$adjunctive adj.
