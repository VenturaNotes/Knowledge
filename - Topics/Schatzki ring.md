## Source [^1]
- a ringlike constriction in the lower part of the gullet (oesophagus). The cause is unclear. Patients may be asymptomatic or experience episodic difficulty in swallowing. Diagnosis is made at gastroscopy or barium swallow. Antisecretory agents and endoscopic balloon dilatation are reserved for symptomatic patients. \[R. Schatzki (20th century), German-born US physician]
