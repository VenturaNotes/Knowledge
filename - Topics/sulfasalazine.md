## Source [^1]
- (sulphasalazine) $n$. a drug that combines aminosalicylic acid and sulfapyridine (a drug of the sulphonamide group), used in the treatment of ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, and rheumatoid arthritis (see also DISEASE-MODIFYING ANTIRHEUMATIC DRUG). The most common sideeffects are nausea, loss of appetite, and raised temperature.
