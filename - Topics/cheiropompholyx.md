## Source [^1]
- (acute vesicular eczema of the hands) $n$. a type of blistering eczema affecting the palms and fingers. The thickness of the skin in these areas prevents the pinhead vesicles from breaking and eventually the skin peels after a period of intense itching. See POMPHOLYX.
