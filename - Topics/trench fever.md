## Source [^1]
- an infectious disease caused by the bacterium Bartonella quintana and transmitted by body lice (Pediculus humanus corporis). It was prevalent among soldiers during both World Wars and is now seen mainly in homeless people. Symptoms include variable fewer and persistent pain in the shins and other bones.
