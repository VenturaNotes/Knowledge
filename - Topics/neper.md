## Source [^1]
- (symbol: Np) A dimensionless unit used in telecommunications to express the ratio of two amplitudes. It is the natural logarithm of the ratio of two amplitudes, usually voltages or currents. Thus if two currents, $I_{1}$ and $I_{2}$, differ by $N$ nepers then$$N = \log_ {e} \left(\frac {I _ {2}}{I _ {1}}\right)$$One neper equals 8.686 decibels.
