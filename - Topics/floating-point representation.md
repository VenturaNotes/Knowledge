## Source [^1]
- A method of representing numbers by means of a predetermined number of significant digits - the mantissa - together with a decimal or binary multiplier - the exponent: the number $x$ can be written as $$x=y\times n^2$$where $y$ is the mantissa, $n$ is either 10 or 2, and $z$ is the exponent in integer form. Compare FIXED-POINT REPRESENTATION.
