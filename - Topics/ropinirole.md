## Source [^1]
- $n$. a dopamine receptor agonist (see DOPAMINE) used to treat Parkinson's disease (either alone or in conjunction with levodopa) and restless legs syndrome. Side-effects include nausea, drowsiness, and swelling (oedema) of the legs.
