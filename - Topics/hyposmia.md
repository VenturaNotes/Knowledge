## Source[^2]
- $n$. Abnormally diminished sensitivity to all or a limited number of odorants. See also anosmia, cacosmia, Dysosmia, parosmia, SPECIFIC anosmia. Compare hyperosmia. hyposmic adj. \[From Greek hypo under + osme a smell + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
