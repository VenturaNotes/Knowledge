## Source [^1]
- (electrostatic microphone) A type of microphone in which a diaphragm forms one plate of a capacitor. Variations of sound pressure cause movements of the diaphragm and these alter the capacitance. Corresponding changes in potential difference across the capacitor are thus produced.
