## Source [^1]
- (inquiry) An initial request from a potential purchaser for information regarding the quality, price, delivery date, etc., of a particular product. This may be the standard practice in some trades and commercial markets or it may be a consumer's response to an advertisement, sales promotion, or direct marketing approach.
