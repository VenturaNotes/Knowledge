## Source [^1]
- The symbol $\small\Lambda$. It is sometimes used to indicate exponentiation as in $10 \small\Lambda 2$ (for $10^{2}$ ). It is also used to indicate a control character, as in $\small\Lambda$ (holding down the control key and pressing ' $a$ ').
