## Source [^1]
- (selective serotonin and noradrenaline reuptake inhibitor) any one of a class of antidepressant drugs that increase the availability of serotonin and noradrenaline by blocking the reabsorption of these neurotransmitters by nerve endings in the brain. Side-effects include gastrointestinal disturbances, giddiness, nausea, and sweating. The group includes venlafaxine (also licensed for the treatment of generalized anxiety disorder) and duloxetine (also licensed for treating generalized anxiety disorder, diabetic neuropathy, and stress incontinence in women).
