## Source [^1]
- $n$. Features of an experimental situation that encourage certain types of behavior from the research participants or subjects and can contaminate the results, especially when this behavior arises from research participants' expectations or preconceptions or from their interpretations of the experimenter's expectations. See also EXPERIMENTER EFFECT, EXPERIMENTER EXPECTANCY EFFECT, REACTIVITY.
