## Source [^1]
- $n$. A term introduced in 1916 (in the German form Legasthenie) by the Hungarian psychiatrist Paul Ranschburg (1870-1945) to denote specific reading disorder. \[From Latin legere to read + Greek astheneia weakness, from $a$- without + sthenos strength + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
