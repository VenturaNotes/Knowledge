## Source [^1]
- three bones in the tarsus $\textendash$ the lateral (external), intermediate (middle), and medial (internal) cuneiform bones $\textendash$ that articulate respectively with the first, second, and third metatarsal bones in front. All three bones articulate with the navicular bone behind.
