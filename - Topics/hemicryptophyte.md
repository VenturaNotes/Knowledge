## Source [^1]
- A plant life form in Raunkiaer's system of classification (see PHYSIOGNOMY). Hemicryptophytes are typically herbaceous perennials, such as grasses, which produce perennating buds at the soil surface, where the buds are protected by leaf or stem bases.
