## Source [^1]
- $n$. a small hairy fly of the widely distributed genus Phlebotomus. Adult sandflies rarely exceed 3 mm in length and have long slender legs. The blood-sucking females of certain species transmit various diseases, including leishmaniasis, sandfly fever, and bartonellosis.
