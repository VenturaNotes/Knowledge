## Source [^1]
- $n$. A condition of fatigue and disorientation brought about by travelling across several time zones in a short period, especially in an eastward direction, resulting in a mismatch between exogenous temporal cues and endogenous circadian rhythms. See also melatonin. jet-lagged adj.
