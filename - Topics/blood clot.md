## Source [^1]
- a solid or semisolid mass formed as the result of blood coagulation, either within the blood vessels and heart after death or elsewhere in the body during life (a mass of coagulated blood formed in the cardiovascular system during life is called a thrombus: see THROMBOSIS). A blood clot consists of a meshwork of the protein fibrin in which various blood cells are trapped.
