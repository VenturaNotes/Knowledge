## Source [^1]
- The addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division of two floating-point operands to produce a floating-point result. There is an international standard (IEEE standard 754: 1985) that is designed to ensure the reproducibility of floating-point operations. See also FLOATING-POINT NOTATION, FLOPS.
