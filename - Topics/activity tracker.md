## Source [^1]
- A portable or wearable device that tracks a user's movement and heart activity, recording it and transferring it to a computing device for subsequent analysis. They often take the form of a wristwatch with GPS and pulse sensors, communicating to a user's smartphone, typically using Bluetooth.
