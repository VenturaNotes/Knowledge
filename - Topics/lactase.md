## Source [^1]
- $n$. an enzyme, secreted by the glands of the small intestine, that converts lactose (milk sugar) into glucose and galactose during digestion.
## Source[^2]
- ($\boldsymbol{\beta}$-galactosidase) The enzyme that breaks down the milk sugar, lactose, to glucose and galactose by catalyzing the hydrolysis of galactose residues.
