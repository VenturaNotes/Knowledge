## Synthesis
- The fundamental economic problem where resources are limited, but human wants are unlimited
	- Example: A government must decide between investing in healthcare or education
## Source [^1]
- It is a fact of life
- It means that human wants for goods, services, and resources exceed what is available
- The ultimate scarce resource is time
## Source[^2]
- The property of being in excess demand at a zero price. This means that in equilibrium the price of a scarce good or factor must be positive.
