## Source [^1]
- The second-layer (data link layer) protocol suite that is a subset of HDLC and is used in X25-based networks in setting up channels between DTE and DCE. An alternative protocol, LAP-B, developed after LAP, allows the DTE/DCE interface to operate in 'balanced mode'.
