## Source [^1]
- (of functions) Less than. The limit of a function at $a$ from below is the limit of $f(x)$ as $x \rightarrow a$ for values of $x<a$. It can be written as $f(a^-)$ or $\lim _{x \rightarrow a} f(x)$. Compare ABOVE.
