## Source [^1]
- The interaction of electromagnetic radiation with a negative ion to detach an electron from the ion and leave a neutral atom or molecule, i.e.$$\mathrm {M} ^ {-} + h \nu \rightarrow \mathrm {M} + \mathrm {e} ^ {-}$$where $h\nu$ represents a photon of frequency $\nu$ and $h$ is the Planck constant. The mechanism in this process is exactly the same as for photoionization of a neutral species. The ionization potential of the negative ion is equal to the electron affinity of the atom or molecule.
