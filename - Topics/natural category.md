## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) A category imposed by the sense organs, such as the class of visual sensations giving rise to the sensation of blue or the class of taste sensations giving rise to the sensation of saltiness, in contradistinction to an artificially chosen category. 
- (2) Another name for a basic-level category, but this is avoided in careful usage.
