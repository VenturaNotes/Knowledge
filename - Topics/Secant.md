## Synthesis

## Source[^1]
- Periodic with period 2$\pi$
## Source[^2]
- Even Function
## Source[^3]
- For Right Triangles
	- $sec\theta = \frac {\text{hyp}}{\text{adj}}$
## Source[^4]
-  For [[Unit Circle]]
	- $sec\theta = \frac {\text{r}}{\text{x}}$
## Source[^5]
- Derivative of $sec(x)$
	- $\text{sec}x\text{tan}x$
## Source[^6]
- Equivalent
	- $sec^2x = (secx)^2$
