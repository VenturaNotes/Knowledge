## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antimetabolite that interferes with pyrimidine synthesis and is used to suppress the symptoms of acute myeloblastic leukaemia and lymphomatous meningitis. It can damage the normal bone marrow, leading to various blood cell disorders. Other side-effects are nausea, vomiting, mouth ulcers, and diarrhoea.
