## Source[^2]
- Computer programs that are designed for a particular purpose or application. For example, accounts programs, games programs, and educational programs are all applications software. Applications that can be downloaded to mobile devices such as smartphones are known as mobile apps or apps. Compare SYSTEMS SOFTWARE. See BUSINESS SOFTWARE PACKAGE.
