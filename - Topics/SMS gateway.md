## Synthesis
- A service that allows computers to send and receive SMS text messages to and from a phone or other SMS-enabled device
## Source[^1]
- An SMS Gateway enables a computer to send and receive SMS text messages to and from an SMS capable device over the global telecommunications network (normally to a mobile phone)
- The SMS Gateway translates the message sent, and makes it compatible for delivery over the network to be able to reach the recipient
