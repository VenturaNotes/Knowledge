## Source [^1]
- $n$. A term sometimes used for a stimulus (1) that is incapable of evoking a response from an organism, such as light or sound that is too faint for the visual or auditory receptors to detect. It is an oxymoron and is avoided in careful usage. Compare ADEQUATE STIMULUS.
