## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug of the fluoropyrimidine class that is used in treatment of advanced or metastatic colorectal cancer, stomach cancer, and breast cancer. Side-effects may include blood disorders (see MYELOSUPPRESSION) and mouth ulcers.
