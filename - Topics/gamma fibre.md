## Source [^1]
- $n$. A medium-diameter partly myelinated type of efferent somatic nerve fibre, composed of gamma motor neurons and having intermediate conduction velocity. A typical example is a fibre supplying a muscle spindle. US gamma fiber. Also written $\gamma$ fibre. Compare alpha fibre, Ia fibre, Ib fibre, II fibre, C fibre. \[From gamma $(\gamma)$ the third letter of the Greek alphabet]
