## Source [^1]
- Selling by means of dealing directly with consumers rather than through retailers. Traditional methods include mail order (see MAIL-ORDER HOUSE), direct-mail selling, cold calling, telephone selling, and door-to-door calling. More recently telemarketing, direct radio selling, magazine and TV advertising, and online computer shopping have been developed. See also DIRECT-RESPONSE TV ADVERTISING; HOME SHOPPING CHANNELS.
