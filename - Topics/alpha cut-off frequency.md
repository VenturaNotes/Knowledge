## Source [^1]
- (symbol: $f_{\alpha}$) The frequency at which the common-base forward-current transfer ratio, $\alpha$, of a bipolar junction transistor has fallen to $1/\sqrt{2}$ (i.e. 0.707) of the low-frequency value.
