## Source [^1]
- The official address of a UK company, to which all correspondence can be sent. Statutory registers are kept at the registered office, the address of which must be disclosed on stationery, e-mails, company websites, and the company's annual return. Any change must be notified to the Registrar of Companies within 14 days.
