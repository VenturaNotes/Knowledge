## Source [^1]
- Preliminary marketing research to clarify the exact nature of a problem to be solved. Quite often sales managers recognize that there is a problem but do not understand the dimensions of the problem. In these circumstances preliminary research is conducted with small numbers of potential customers to define the nature of the problem.
