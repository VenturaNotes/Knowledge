## Source [^1]
- $n$. the process of brushing or scraping the surface of a slowly healing ulcer or wound to remove granulation tissue, which $\textendash$ though a stage in the healing process $\textendash$ sometimes overgrows or becomes infected and therefore delays healing. Grattage is used in the treatment of trachoma.
