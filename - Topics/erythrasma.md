## Source [^1]
- $n$. a chronic skin infection due to the bacterium Corynebacterium minutissimum, occurring in such areas as the armpits, groin, and toes, where skin surfaces are in contact. It fluoresces coral-pink under Wood's light and can be treated with topical or systemic antibiotics.
