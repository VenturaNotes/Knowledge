## Source [^1]
- (pl. phonotaxes) The movement of an organism in relation to a sound source. For example, females are often attracted by the courtship song of a potential mate (i.e. positive phonotaxis), or animals may flee from the sound of a predator (i.e. negative phonotaxis).
