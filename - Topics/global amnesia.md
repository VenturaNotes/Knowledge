## Source [^1]
- $n$. Severe or total anterograde amnesia resulting from damage to the diencephalon or medial temporal lobe, often accompanied by partial retrograde amnesia, with short-term memory and perceptual-motor skills generally remaining unimpaired. Compare TRANSIENT GLOBAL AMNESIA.
