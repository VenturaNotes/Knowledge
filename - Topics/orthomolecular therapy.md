## Source [^1]
- $n$. An approach to the treatment of mental disorders based on the doctrine that every disorder can be alleviated with a specific chemical substance. The approach was developed by the Nobel prizewinning US chemist Linus (Carl) Pauling (1901-94). Also called megavitamin therapy, orthomolecular psychiatry, vitamin therapy. \[From Greek orthos right + English molecular]
