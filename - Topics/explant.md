## Source [^1]
- (1) n. live tissue transferred from the body (or any organism) to a suitable artificial medium for culture. The tissue grows in the artificial medium and can be studied for diagnostic or experimental purposes. Tumor growths are sometimes examined in this way. 
- (2) n. silicone rubber material sutured to the outside of the eyeball over a retinal tear or hole (see PLOMBAGE). The resulting indent allows the retina to reattach. 
- (3) $v b$. to transfer live tissue for culture outside the body. $\textendash$explantation $n$.
