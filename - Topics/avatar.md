## Source [^1]
- A graphical object that is created and maintained by software to mimic a human or other being. Avatars are most often used in computer games, especially \*virtual worlds, to represent the player within the game
## Source[^2]
- An avatar is a computer-generated 3D figure that represents a human user in the online world. These often cartoonish avatars allow users to adopt an identity on chat and entertainment websites without exposing their personal information and photos.
