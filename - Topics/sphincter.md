## Source [^1]
- $n$. a specialized ring of muscle that surrounds an orifice. Contractions of the sphincter partly or completely close the orifice. Sphincters are found, for example, around the anus (anal sphincter) and at the openings between the oesophagus and stomach (lower oesophageal sphincter, LOS) and between the stomach and duodenum (pyloric sphincter).
