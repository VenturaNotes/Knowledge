## Source [^1]
- A device that enables electrical apparatus to be connected or disconnected from a source of supply or other equipment. It consists of two separable portions, the 'male' plug and the 'female' socket, with metal contacts that engage each other when connection is effected. A polarized plug is constructed so that engagement with the socket is only possible in one position.
