## Source [^1]
- $n$. an anticonvulsant drug used to treat partial epilepsy and neuropathic pain, including peripheral neuropathy and postherpetic neuralgia. Side-effects include gastrointestinal disturbances, dizziness, drowsiness, and shaky movements. Pregabalin (Lyrica) has similar uses and effects; it is also licensed to treat generalized anxiety disorder.
