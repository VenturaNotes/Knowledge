## Source [^1]
- An auditor eligible to carry out statutory audits in any member state of the European Union, in accordance with the Eighth Company Law Directive. This was brought into UK law by the Companies Act 1989, which gave the Recognized Supervisory Bodies power to approve such auditors. Registers of individuals and firms eligible to act as registered auditors are kept.
