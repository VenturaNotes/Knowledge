## Source [^1]
- (1) A derogatory term for a firm of brokers, dealers, agents, etc., of questionable standing and frail resources, that is unlikely to be a member of an established trade organization. See BOILER ROOM. 
- (2) A travel agent who provides a service for travellers who book late, require the lowest prices, and will accept a minimum standard of service.
