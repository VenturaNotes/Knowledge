## Source [^1]
- A manager with responsibility for running the resource inputs that constitute an operating system. Most managers have an operational function within their overall job description, e.g. a human resource manager (see HUMAN-RESOURCE MANAGEMENT) has to make use of inputs consisting of cash, skilled personnel, documentation, etc., to produce an output consisting of an appropriately skilled workforce.
