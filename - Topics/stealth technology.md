## Source [^1]
- A combination of technologies designed to make aircraft, ships, vehicles, and even people hard to observe. They range from the simplest forms of camouflage, through coatings to reduce thermal emissions and absorb sonar or radar, to careful design of surfaces to minimize the return signal sent back to an active radar or sonar system.
