## Source [^1]
- The use of a screen of high permeability magnetic material in order to protect electric circuits, devices, or other apparatus from the effects of magnetic fields. Magnetic screening is often used to surround sensitive alternating-current measuring instruments and thus shield them from stray external magnetic fields.
