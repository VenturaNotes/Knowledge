## Source [^1]
- The $n \times n$ matrix in which $a_{ij} = corr(X_i, X_j)$ so that the correlations between all pairs of variables are contained in it. All elements $a_{ii}$ in the leading diagonal will be 1 and the matrix is a symmetric matrix.
