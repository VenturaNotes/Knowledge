## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. mycelia) the tangled mass of fine branching threads that make up the feeding and growing part of a fungus.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. mycelia) A network of hyphae that forms the body of a fungus. It consists of feeding hyphae together with reproductive hyphae, which produce sporangia and gametangia.
