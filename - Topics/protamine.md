## Source [^1]
- Any of a group of proteins of relatively low molecular weight found in association with the chromosomal DNA of vertebrate sperm cells. They contain a single polypeptide chain comprising about 67% arginine. Protamines serve in packaging the highly condensed DNA of the germ-cell chromosomes by replacing the histone proteins that normally associate with the DNA. Protamine sulphate is used therapeutically to treat heparin overdosage.
