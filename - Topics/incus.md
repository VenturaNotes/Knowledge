## Source [^1]
- $n$. a small anvil-shaped bone in the middle ear that articulates with the malleus and the stapes. See OSSICLE.
## Source[^2]
- (anvil) (pl. incudes) The middle of the three ear ossicles of the mammalian middle ear.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. The middle of the three small bones in the middle ear, transmitting sound vibrations from the malleus to the stapes. Referred to non-technically as the anvil. Compare malleus, stapes. \[From Latin incus an anvil, from incudere to forge, alluding to its resemblance to a blacksmith's anvil]
