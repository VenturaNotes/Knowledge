## Source [^1]
- $n$. A mood episode with similar symptoms to a manic episode but not sufficiently severe to cause marked impairment in social or occupational functioning or to require hospitalization, though characterized by unrealistic optimism, overactivity, a decreased need for sleep, and often reckless spending of money. Also called hypomania or hypomanic disorder. \[From Greek hypo under + mania madness]
