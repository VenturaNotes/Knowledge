## Source [^1]
- The single most important equation in communications signal processing. It specifies the optimum way to combine signals linearly from a number of sources to produce the best estimate of the original signal. It can be written as$$w = P^{*} R^{-1},$$where $w$ is the vector of weighting coefficients, $P^{*}$ is the cross-correlation matrix, and $R$ is the autocorrelation matrix between the inputs.
