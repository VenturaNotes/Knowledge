- A bridge, modified from the Maxwell bridge, in which an inductance, $L$, is compared with a capacitance, $C$. Using a null-point detector instrument I (see diagram), resistances $R_4$ and $X$ are adjusted until, when the bridge is balanced,$$\begin{array}{l}

R_2 R_3 = R_1 R_4 \\

L = C \left[ R_2 R_3 + (R_3 + R_4) X \right]

\end{array}$$
