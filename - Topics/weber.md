## Source [^1]
- $n$. the SI unit of magnetic flux, equal to the flux linking a circuit of one turn that produces an e.m.f. of 1 volt when reduced uniformly to zero in 1 second. Symbol: Wb.
## Source[^2]
- (symbol: Wb) The SI unit of magnetic flux. One weber is the magnetic flux that, linking a circuit of one turn, produces an electromotive force of 1 volt when it is reduced to zero at a uniform rate in one second.
