## Source [^1]
- The use of formulae, usually expressed in terms of finite differences, for estimating derivatives of a function $f(x)$ from values of the function. For example $$f'(x) \approx \frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}$$and $$f''(x) \approx \frac{f(x+h) - 2f(x)+f(x-h)}{h^2}$$where $h$ is a small positive increment
