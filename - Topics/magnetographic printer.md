## Source [^1]
- An obsolete type of printer in which the required image was first written on a band or drum of magnetic recording material as a pattern of closely spaced magnetic poles. The image was then developed by brushing it with a pigment that was also ferromagnetic and thermoplastic. The image was transferred and bonded to paper by applying pressure and/or heat.
