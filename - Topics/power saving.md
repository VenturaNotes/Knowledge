## Source [^1]
- An operational mode in which a device that is idle or has a light workload takes measures to reduce its power consumption. There are two main approaches: to slow down the operating clock frequency, which reduces power requirements; and to remove the power voltage completely from subparts of the device not currently required. Examples are smartphones or laptops going into sleep mode, or a computer display being dimmed or switched off when there has been no user activity for a period of time.
