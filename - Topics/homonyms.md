## Source [^1]
- $n$. Words that are spelt the same way but have different meanings, such as bear (animal) and bear (carry), also called homographs, or that have the same pronunciation but different spelling or meaning, such as rest (relaxation) and wrest (take by force), also called homophones. See also polyseMY. Compare heteronyms, homographs. \[From Greek homos same + onoma a name]
