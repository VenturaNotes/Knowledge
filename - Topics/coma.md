## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) a state of unrousable unconsciousness. See also Glasgow Coma Scale. 
- (2) (in optics) an aberration, inherent in certain optical designs or due to imperfection in the lens or cornea, that results in off-axis point sources (e.g. stars) appearing to have a tail, like the coma of a comet. -comatic adj.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A state of deep unconsciousness and absence of responses to external and internal stimuli caused by severe head injury, brain tumor, loss of blood supply to the brain, acute infection, stroke, drug overdose, or poisoning. comatose adj. In a state of coma. \[From Greek koma heavy sleep]
