## Source [^1]
- a rare tumor of fetal remnants of the ovaries or testes. In women it is an aggressive malignant ovarian tumor that develops in adolescence and may secrete alpha-fetoprotein and human chorionic gonadotrophin, which can be used as tumor markers. It is treated by oophorectomy with adjuvant chemotherapy.
