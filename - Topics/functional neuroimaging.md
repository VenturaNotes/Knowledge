## Source [^1]
- The use of images or scans of the brain showing activity levels to determine a relationship between such activity and the mental tasks being performed during the imaging. Suitable images can be obtained from a number of techniques, including functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) and electroencephalography. See NEUROIMAGING.
