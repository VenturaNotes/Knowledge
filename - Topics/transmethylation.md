## Source [^1]
- $n$. the process whereby an amino acid donates its terminal methyl $\left(-\mathrm{CH}_{3}\right)$ group for the methylation of other compounds. Methionine is the principal methyl donor in the body and the donated methyl group may subsequently be involved in the synthesis of such compounds as choline or creatinine or in detoxification processes.
