## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) in a state of unconsciousness. 
- (2) describing those mental processes of which a person is not aware. 
- (3) (in psychoanalysis) denoting the part of the mind that includes memories, motives, and intentions that are not accessible to awareness and cannot be made conscious without overcoming resistances. In addition to the individual unconscious, Jung postulated the existence of a collective unconscious. Compare SUBCONSCIOUS.
