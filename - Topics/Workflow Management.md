## Source [^1]
- Workflow management can take one of two definitions. First, it can involve the way that applications and work products are managed. This management can be exclusively internal, or it can be external, and capture the way that outside vendors are related to a final work product. Second, it may refer to automated events or processes. In this instance, workflow management refers to what happens in order to automate certain events or tasks.
