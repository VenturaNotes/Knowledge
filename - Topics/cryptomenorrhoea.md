## Source [^1]
- $n$. severe cyclical abdominal pain in association with amenorrhoea, usually about a year after the expected onset of menstruation and usually due to an imperforate hymen. On parting the labia, a bulging blue membrane is seen above the hymen and a pelvic mass is felt on examination. Treatment is by incision of the membrane.
