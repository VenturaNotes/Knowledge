## Source [^1]
- Telepresence coupled with some ability to manipulate things in a remote location. The controls can be common user-interface devices, such as a keyboard or mouse, but can also be feedback devices such as haptic interfaces. Typical applications include controlling machines in hazardous environments, as in pipeline inspection. One focus of current research is the feasibility of performing surgery remotely. See also ROBOTICS.
