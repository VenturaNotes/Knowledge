## Synthesis
- 
## Source [^1]
- (1) A synthesizer that responds only to a MIDI input. It has no keyboard or other performance interface of its own. 
- (2) A system that upsamples a sampled signal. 
- (3) Short for volume expander. See VOLUME COMPRESSOR. 
- (4) Short for gate expander.
