## Source [^1]
- (1) an accumulation of fluid (pleural effusion) in the lung in patients with heart failure, which resembles a lung tumor on radiological examination. 
- (2) a swelling in the abdomen or elsewhere, caused by local muscular contraction or the accumulation of gases, that mimics a swelling caused by a tumor.
