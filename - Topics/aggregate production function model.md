## Source [^1]
- The Aggregate Production Function Model illustrates the relationship between total output (GDP) and the inputs used to produce that output, primarily labor and capital. 
- This model helps to understand how changes in these inputs, along with technological advancements, can influence economic growth and productivity levels within an economy.
