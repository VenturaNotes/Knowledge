## Source [^1]
- A painful condition of the wrist or lower arm that can be caused by prolonged use of badly designed or installed keyboards, though it is found in other unrelated activities involving repetitive muscular movements over a long period of time. RSI is not confined to the arms and can be experienced in other limbs.
