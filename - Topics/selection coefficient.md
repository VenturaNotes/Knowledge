## Source [^1]
- Symbol $s$ or $t$. A measure of the sum of the forces acting to oppose the reproductive success of a particular organism, phenotype, or genotype. It varies between 0 and 1 and is related to fitness $(W)$ by the equation: $W=1-s$. Hence, as the selection coefficient increases, fitness decreases.
