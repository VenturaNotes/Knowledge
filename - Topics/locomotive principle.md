## Source [^1]
- The idea that growth in an economy, or in the world economy, depends on growth in some leading sector or leading country. The leader, which may be an industry with favorable investment opportunities, or a country with a strong balance of payments, can by expanding serve as a locomotive to pull the less dynamic parts of the economy or of the world after it.
