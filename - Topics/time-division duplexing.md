## Source [^1]
- In a bidirectional radio communications system, the technique of transmitting in one direction at a different time from the other direction. Systems can operate by both time-division duplexing and frequency-division duplexing; an example is GSM (see GLOBAL SYSTEM FOR MOBILE COMMUNICATIONS).
