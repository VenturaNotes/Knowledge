## Source [^1]
- $n$. The branch of statistics devoted to investigating the influence of one or more independent variable acting on more than one dependent variable. The multi- in the name refers to the multiplicity of dependent variables. See also multivariate analysis of variance (MANOVA). Compare BIVARIATE STATISTICS, UNIVARIATE STATISTICS.
