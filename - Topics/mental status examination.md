## Source [^1]
- $n$. An assessment of a person's mental condition, typically covering appearance (visible signs of disorder, weight, dress, hygiene, grooming, sociability, motor activity); mood (facial expression, emotional responses); speech and thought (content and form, language); neuropsychological signs and symptoms (history of neurological signs and symptoms, sensation and localization, apraxia and dyspraxia, attention and concentration, time and place orientation, memory, general intellectual functioning, judgement and insight); and personality (defence mechanisms and recent changes in personality). See also Mental Status Examination Report. Compare Mini-Mental State Examination. MSE abbrev.
