## Source [^1]
- A method of communicating written messages over the international telephone network, using a Telex (teleprinter) machine. Although e-mail has replaced Telex in virtually all business applications, it is still used by shipping, news services, and the military.
## Source[^2]
- (1) A method of communicating written messages over the public telephone system, using teleprinters. It has been largely superseded by email. 
- (2) A message so communicated.
