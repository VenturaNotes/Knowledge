## Source [^1]
- (V-) Denoting a speech sound, such as the final consonants in ‘dish’, ‘off’, and ‘loss’, that does not involve vibrating vocal folds because it is noise-based, resulting from turbulent airflow through a narrow constriction in the vocal tract. Compare VOICED.
