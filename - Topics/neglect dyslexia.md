## Source [^1]
- $n$. A type of dyslexia in which either the initial parts of words are misread (left neglect dyslexia) or the terminal parts of words are misread (right neglect dyslexia), the errors not being simple deletions but typically guesses of real though incorrect words with approximately the right number of letters.
