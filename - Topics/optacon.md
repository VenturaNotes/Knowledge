## Source [^1]
- $n$. An electronic aid for blind people that transforms a pattern of light-intensity differences into a pattern of vibrations that can be felt by the fingertips, making possible such activities as reading printed text. Compare Braille, optohapt. \[Coined from Greek optikon neuter of optikos of or relating to vision, modified to avoid opticon, a word already in use for an enlargement of the optic lobe in an insect brain associated with its compound eye]
