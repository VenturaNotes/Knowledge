## Source [^1]
- A noxious smog produced by the reaction of nitrogen oxides (e.g. from car exhausts, coal burning) with volatile hydrocarbons in the presence of ultraviolet light from the sun. The reaction is very complex and one of the products is ground-level ozone. See also AIR POLLUTION.
