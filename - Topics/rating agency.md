## Source [^1]
- An organization that monitors the credit backing of institutions and of bond issues and other forms of public borrowings. It may also give a rating of the risks involved in holding specific stocks. The three best known are Standard \& Poor's, Moody's, and Fitch, all of which have been in existence for over 100 years. See also CREDIT RATING.
