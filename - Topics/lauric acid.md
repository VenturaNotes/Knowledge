## Source [^1]
- (dodecanoic acid) A white crystalline fatty acid, $\mathrm{CH}_{3}\left(\mathrm{CH}_{2}\right)_{10} \mathrm{COOH}$. Glycerides of the acid are present in natural fats and oils (e.g. coconut and palm-kernel oil).
