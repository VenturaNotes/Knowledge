## Source [^1]
- The state of a company that does not have sufficient capital or reserves for the size of its operations. For example, this may be due to the company growing too quickly. Although such a company may be making profits it may be unable to convert these profits sufficiently quickly into cash to pay its debts. Compare OVERCAPITALIZATION.
