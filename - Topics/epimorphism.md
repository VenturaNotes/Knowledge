## Source [^1]
- A morphism $f: X \to Y$ with the property that for all morphisms $g$ between $Y$ and $Z$, $g_1 \circ f = g_2 \circ f \implies g_1 = g_2.$ In category theory, epimorphisms are the analogue of surjections. Compare MONOMORPHISM
