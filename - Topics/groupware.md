## Source [^1]
- (collaborative software) Software that supports the cooperation of a group of people, usually working in a distributed environment.
## Source[^2]
- Groupware are programs that allow people to work together and meet a common goal, while located remotely from each other.
## Source[^3]
- Computer software that enables collaborative working on a joint project. Using groupware, members of a team can work together despite being in different locations (e.g. by using shared documents). Groupware was a precursor of web-based collaborative working systems.
