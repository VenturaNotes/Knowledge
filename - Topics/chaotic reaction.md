## Source [^1]
- A type of chemical reaction in which the concentrations of reactants show chaotic behavior. This may occur when the reaction involves a large number of complex interlinked steps. Under such conditions, it is possible for the reaction to display unpredictable changes with time. See also Bistability; Oscillating Reaction.
