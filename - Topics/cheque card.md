## Source [^1]
- A plastic card issued by a retail bank to its customers to guarantee cheques drawn on the customer's current account up to a specified limit. The card carries the account number, the name of the customer, and has to be signed by the customer. In the UK cheque cards have been replaced by multifunctional cards, which also function as cash cards and debit cards.
