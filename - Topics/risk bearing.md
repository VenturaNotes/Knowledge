## Source [^1]
- Being exposed to the consequences of uncertain future events. Risk bearing can arise from many economic activities. Being the owner of a small firm brings exposure to the risk of high or low profit levels. Investing in shares creates exposure to risky returns. The amount of risk an individual or a firm should bear is chosen as part of economic decision-making. See also PORTFOLIO THEORY.
