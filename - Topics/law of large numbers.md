## Source [^1]
- A number of statistical results related to the idea that as the number of trials of a random experiment increases, the percentage difference between the expected and actual numbers of successful outcomes goes to zero. The weak law of large numbers proves that the sample mean of a sequence of $n$ independent identically distributed random variables converges to their common mean as $n$ tends to infinity.
