## Source [^1]
- $n$. The name given in 1994 by the Nobel prizewinning English molecular biologist Francis H(arry) C(ompton) Crick (1916-2004) to the proposition that a person's mental experiences are entirely explained by the behavior of neurons, glial cells, and the atoms, ions, and molecules that constitute and influence them.
