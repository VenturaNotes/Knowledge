## Source [^1]
- $n$. a sudden spasm of the muscles. Occasional myoclonic jerks occur between seizures in patients with idiopathic epilepsy, and myoclonus is a major feature of some progressive neurological illnesses with extensive degeneration of the brain cells (including the spongiform encephalopathies). Myoclonic jerks on falling asleep (nocturnal myoclonus) occur in normal individuals. $\textendash$myoclonic adj.
