## Source [^1]
- A 1986 amendment to the Treaty of Rome, governing the conduct of the European Community. This made a large number of changes arising from the Cockfield Report (1985), Completing the Internal Market. These included permitting decisions by majority voting, an increase in the powers of the European Parliament, and recognition of the European Monetary System.
