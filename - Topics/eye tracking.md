## Source [^1]
- An image processing technique that determines where an eye is looking by analysis of digital images of that eye. The images are usually obtained from a head-mounted camera, or one mounted close to the display being viewed. Eye-tracking data can be used to determine where an individual’s attention was at any given moment.
