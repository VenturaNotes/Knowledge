## Source [^1]
- A wire of uniform resistance provided with a sliding contact that can make a connection at any desired point along the length. Slide wires are used to provide a variable resistance, as a potentiometer, or to provide a desired resistance ratio (see WHEATSTONE BRIDGE). An overall length of one metre is commonly chosen.
