## Source [^1]
- $n$. the condition resulting from excessive consumption of vitamins. This is not usually serious in the case of water-soluble vitamins, when any intake in excess of requirements is excreted in the urine. However, the fat-soluble vitamins A and D are toxic if taken in excessive amounts.
