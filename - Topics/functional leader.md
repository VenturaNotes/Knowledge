## Source [^1]
- In the social psychology of groups, any group member who performs the functions associated with the role of leader, whether he or she has a formal leadership role or not. These functions may be either goaloriented (e.g. suggesting solutions to problems, inciting action) or relationship-oriented (e.g. reducing conflict, raising morale).
