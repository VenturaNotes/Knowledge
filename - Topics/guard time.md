## Source [^1]
- (1) A period in a time-division multiplex access scheme between the transmissions of two users, left quiet to prevent interference between the two transmissions. 
- (2) A period in an orthogonal frequency division modulation transmission between two symbols, left quiet to prevent interference between the two symbols.
