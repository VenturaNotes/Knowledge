## Source [^1]
- $n$. a synthetic female sex hormone (see PROGESTOGEN) administered by mouth to treat menstrual disorders, including endometriosis. Its main use, however, is in contraceptives, either alone or in combination with an oestrogen; it is also used in hormone replacement therapy.
