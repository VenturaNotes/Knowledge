## Source [^1]
- (PIN, personal identification number) A number issued to a holder of a magnetic card, for example a credit card or bank card, that the card holder is required to keep secret. Together with the magnetic card the pin acts as an identifier and password to access computer-based services such as ATMs, EPOS, or EFTS.
