## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in anatomy) the point or type of contact between two bones. See JOINT.
## Source[^2]
- The attachment of two bones, usually by means of a joint. The thigh bone (femur), for instance, articulates with the pelvic girdle.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. The production of speech sounds by using the speech organs to modify the airstream, usually from the lungs. Consonants are classified chiefly by place of articulation and manner of articulation. \[From Latin articulus a little joint, hence articulare to provide with joints + -ation indicating a process or condition]
