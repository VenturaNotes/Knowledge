## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of a number of mushrooms containing substances that are taken as psychedelic or hallucinogenic drugs, notably the fly agaric mushroom Amanita muscaria and the liberty cap mushroom Psilocybe semilanceata. See also TEONANACTYL.
