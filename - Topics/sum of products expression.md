## Source [^1]
- A Boolean function expressed as a sum of product terms, i.e. as an OR of AND terms containing uncomplemented or complemented variables. An example is$$f=\left(x \wedge y^{\prime}\right) \vee\left(x^{\prime} \wedge z\right)$$The function is also realizable as the NAND of a group of NAND terms. See also STANDARD SUM OF PRODUCTS, PRODUCT OF SUMS EXPRESSION.
