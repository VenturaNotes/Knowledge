## Source [^1]
- $n$. The pure tone with the lowest frequency forming a component of a complex tone, or the analogous sine wave in some other complex oscillatory vibration. Also called a fundamental. See also missing fundamental illusion. Compare harmonic.
## Source[^2]
- (1) The frequency of a sinusoidal component of a periodic quantity that has the same period as the periodic quantity. 
- (2) The lowest frequency present in a complex vibration. See also HARMONIC.
