## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychophysics, a procedure in which judgements are made without any explicit standards of comparison, as when an observer is asked to rate a sound for loudness on a scale from 0 to 100. Also called the method of single stimuli. Compare method of comparative JUDGEMENT.
