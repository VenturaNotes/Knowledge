## Source [^1]
- $n$. the operation performed to restore free passage through a blocked Fallopian tube. The blocked portion of the tube is removed surgically and the continuity is then restored. It is performed in women who have been sterilized previously by tubal occlusion (see STERILIZATION) and in others whose Fallopian tubes have become blocked as a result of pelvic infection.
