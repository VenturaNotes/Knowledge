## Source [^1]
- $n$. The application of genetic techniques to determine the degree of relatedness of people, both living and dead. It is based on the analysis of both junk DNA and polymorphic genes. The first use of the term was by the US science writer Tom Siegfried (born 1950) in an article entitled 'Genetic Genealogy and the Search of Eve' in the Dallas Morning News on 20 February 1989. \[From Greek genesis birth + -itikos resembling or marked by + genes born + logos word, discourse, or study]
