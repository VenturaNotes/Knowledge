## Source [^1]
- $n$. A compulsion characterized by a repetitive pattern of sexual behavior that is recognized by the person concerned as either clearly excessive or not realistically capable of yielding sexual satisfaction. It typically involves brief sexual encounters with numerous partners and is found in both heterosexual and homosexual men and women. US compulsive sexual behavior. CSB abbrev.
