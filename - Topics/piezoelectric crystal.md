## Source [^1]
- A crystal that exhibits the piezoelectric effect. All ferroelectric crystals are piezoelectric as well as certain nonferroelectric crystals and some ceramics. The best-known examples of piezoelectric crystals include quartz crystal, Rochelle salt, and barium titanate. See also PIEZOELECTRIC OSCILLATOR.
