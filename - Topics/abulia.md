## Source [^1]
- $n$. A deficit of will or motivation, often leading to an inability to make decisions or plans. \[From Greek $a$ - without + boule will + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
## Source[^2]
- $n$. a lack of will and initiative resulting in the inability to act or make decisions independently. It occurs in neurological diseases involving the frontal lobes and/or basal ganglia, such as traumatic brain injury, stroke, and dementia. Abulia is also seen in psychiatric diseases, including schizophrenia. It is a disorder of motivation and may result from dysfunction of the dopamine-mediated reward system.
