## Source [^1]
- A symbol used to denote lags of a variable: if $L$ is the lag operator then $L y_{t} \equiv y_{t-1}, L^{2} y_{t} \equiv L\left(L y_{t}\right)=y_{t-2}$, etc. Standard rules of summation and multiplication can conveniently be applied to the expressions containing lag operators.
