## Source [^1]
- adj. describing or relating to a mass of malignant tissue that has infiltrated the epithelium and broken through the skin surface. Often in the shape of a fungus, it may be infected, smell strongly, and cause pain and is most likely to occur in the advanced stages of cancer, particularly of the breast, head, or neck.
