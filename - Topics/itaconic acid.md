## Source [^1]
- (methylene succinic acid) A product of the fermentation of carbohydrates by strains of the filamentous fungus Aspergillus. Itaconic acid is produced on an industrial scale and used commercially as a plasticizer and lubricant in the production of adhesives and fibres and as a source of 'biopolyesters'.
