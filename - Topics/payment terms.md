## Source [^1]
- The agreed way in which a buyer pays the seller for goods. The commonest are cash with order or cash on delivery; prompt cash (i.e. within 14 days of delivery); cash in 30,60 , or 90 days from date of invoice; letter of credit; cash against documents; documents against acceptance; or acceptance credit.
