## Source [^1]
- (1) The state of the atmosphere in which the relative humidity is over 100%. This occurs in pure air where no condensation nuclei are available. Supersaturation is usually prevented in the atmosphere by the abundance of condensation nuclei (e.g. dust, sea salt, and smoke particles). 
- (2) The state of any vapor whose pressure exceeds that at which condensation usually occurs (at the prevailing temperature).
