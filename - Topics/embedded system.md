## Source [^1]
- A dedicated computer designed and programmed specifically to assist in the operation of a larger system. Typically, small special-purpose computers with limited storage and speed are used, often with simple connectors for communicating with the rest of the system. They can be found in a wide range of devices, including domestic appliances, vehicles, and robots. See also PIC MICROCONTROLLER.
