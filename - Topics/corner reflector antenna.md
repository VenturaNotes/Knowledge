## Source [^1]
- A special form of reflector (see DIRECTIONAL ANTENNA) in which two reflecting plates are joined so as to form a corner. The feed element, usually a dipole, is placed within the region of the corner depending on the application. The included angle is often $90^{\circ}$, in which case the corner reflector will return the received signal in exactly the same direction as it received it; this makes it useful as, for example, a passive target in radio or communication applications.
