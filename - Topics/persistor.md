## Source [^1]
- A device, commonly in the form of a miniature bimetallic printed circuit, that depends for its operation on the sharp changes of resistance in a metal as it passes from a state of superconductivity to its normal resistive state. It can be used as a low-temperature storage element or very fast switch.
