## Source [^1]
- adj. cold-blooded: being unable to regulate the body temperature, which fluctuates according to that of the surroundings. Reptiles and amphibians are cold-blooded. Compare HOMOIOTHERMIC. $\textemdash$poikilothermy $n$.
