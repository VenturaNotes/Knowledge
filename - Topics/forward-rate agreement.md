## Source [^1]
- (FRA) 
- (1) A contract between two parties that determines the rate of interest that will apply to a future loan or a deposit, which may or may not materialize. 
- (2) A specified amount of a specified currency to be exchanged on an agreed future date at a specified rate of exchange.
