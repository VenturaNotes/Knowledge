## Source [^1]
- $n$. a mucoid tumor of the peritoneum, often seen in association with myxomas of the ovary. In pseudomyxoma peritonei there are recurrent deposits of mucin-producing cells in the abdomen, which can be difficult to clear by surgery and may prove fatal.
