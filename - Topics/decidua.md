## Source [^1]
- $n$. the modified mucous membrane that lines the wall of the uterus during pregnancy and is shed with the afterbirth at parturition (see ENDOMETRIUM). There are three regions: the decidua capsularis, a thin layer that covers the embryo; the decidua basalis, where the embryo is attached; and the decidua parietalis, which is not in contact with the embryo. $\textendash$decidual adj.
