## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug that reduces fever by lowering the body temperature. Several analgesic drugs have antipyretic activity, including aspirin and paracetamol.
## Source[^2]
- A drug that reduces fever by lowering body temperature. Certain analgesic drugs, notably paracetamol (acetaminophen), aspirin, and phenylbutazone, also have antipyretic properties.
