## Source [^1]
- a disorder associated with proliferation of B lymphocytes producing heavy chains $\textendash$ one of the two types of polypeptide chains (the other being light chains) that make up the structure of immunoglobulins. It results in the production of abnormal immunoglobulins with distorted heavy chains and no light chains.
