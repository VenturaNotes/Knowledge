## Source[^2]
- $n$. The study of apparently paranormal psychological phenomena or psi phenomena, especially extra-sensory perception (telepathy, clairvoyance, and precognition) and psychokinesis. Also called psychical research. See also file drawer problem, Ouija board, psi, psi-missing. parapsychological adj. parapsychologist $n$. One who practises or studies parapsychology. \[From Greek para beside or beyond + English psychology]
