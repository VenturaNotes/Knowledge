## Source [^1]
- (1) A voltage that exceeds the normal voltage applied between two conductors or between a conductor and earth. 
- (2) (at a given electrode in an electrolytic cell) The amount by which the applied e.m.f. necessary to release hydrogen on that electrode from a particular electrolyte exceeds the e.m.f. required to liberate hydrogen from the same electrolyte on a standard platinum electrode.
