## Source [^1]
- $n$. a bacterial or plant cell without its cell wall.
## Source[^2]
- (energid) The living unit of a cell, consisting of the nucleus and cytoplasm bounded by the plasma membrane. Protoplasts of bacterial and plant cells can be prepared by removing the cell wall; they are used to study the processes involved in cell metabolism and reproduction.
