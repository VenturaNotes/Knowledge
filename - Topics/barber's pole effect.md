## Source [^1]
- $n$. The phenomenon whereby a rotating pole painted with spiral stripes of alternating colors, if viewed through a horizontal slit, is seen as marks moving horizontally, and if viewed through a vertical slit as marks moving vertically, although the movement of the stripes themselves is the same in both cases.
