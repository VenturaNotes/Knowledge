## Source [^1]
- $n$. any cancer of connective tissue. These tumors may occur in any part of the body, as they arise in the tissues that make up an organ rather than being restricted to a particular organ. They can arise in fibrous tissue, muscle, fat, bone, cartilage, synovium, blood and lymphatic vessels, and various other tissues. See also CHONDROSARCOMA; FIBROSARCOMA; LEIOMYOSARCOMA; LIPOSARCOMA; LYMPHANGIOSARCOMA; OSTEOSARCOMA; RHABDOMYOSARCOMA. $\textemdash$sarcomatous adj.
