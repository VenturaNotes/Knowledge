## Source [^1]
- $n$. Either of two small rounded protrusions from the back of the hypothalamus, forming the terminal pillars of the fornix, implicated in emotion, sexual arousal, and memory. Also spelt mamillary body. \[From Latin mammilla a nipple, diminutive of mamma a breast]
