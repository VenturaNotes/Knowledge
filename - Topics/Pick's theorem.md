## Source [^1]
- The area of a polygon with vertices on a planar integer lattice, as drawn below, equals $i + \frac{b}{2} - 1$, where $i$ denotes the number of lattice points interior to the polygon and $b$ denotes the number of lattice points on the polygon’s boundary. In the diagram, $i = 2$ and $b = 7$, so that the area is $4\frac{1}{2}$.
