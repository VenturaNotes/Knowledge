## Source [^1]
- (1) The capacitance of an electrical system, such as a transmission line, considered to be distributed along its length. See DISTRIBUTED CIRCUIT. 
- (2) The capacitance between individual turns on a coil or between adjacent conductors. The distributed capacitance in a coil lowers the inductance of the coil and may be represented by a single capacitor across the terminals.
