## Source [^1]
- Funds operated by the European Union (EU) to improve economic conditions in the poorest regions of member countries. Objective 1 funding, for example, is directed to regions whose per capita gross domestic product is under $3 / 4$ of the EU average. The intention of the funds is to reduce regional inequality in the EU.
