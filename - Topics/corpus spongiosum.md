## Source[^2]
- $n$. The spongy blood-carrying tissue of the penis or clitoris that, together with the corpora cavernosa, forms the erectile tissue capable of responding to sexual excitement. See also cyclic GMP, nitric oxide. \[From Latin corpus body + spongiosum spongy]
