## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) describing nerve fibres that release acetylcholine as a neurotransmitter. 
- (2) describing receptors at which acetylcholine acts to pass on messages from cholinergic nerve fibres. 
- (3) describing drugs that mimic the actions of acetylcholine (see PARASYMPATHOMIMETIC). Compare ADRENERGIC.
