## Source [^1]
- $n$. circulating mature red blood cells and that part of the bloodforming system of the body that is directed towards the production of red blood cells. The erythron is not a single organ but is dispersed throughout the blood-forming tissue of the bone marrow. See also ERYTHROPOIESIS.
