## Source [^1]
- n. A shape consisting of a circular disc with a missing segment, used as an icon in an electronic computer game in which the player's task is to guide the icon through a maze while devouring other icons and eluding attacks; also, because of its resemblance in shape, the name given to the symbol used at each vertex of a Kanizsa triangle to create illusory contours. Also spelt PacMan, Pacman, or pacman. \[Trademark]
