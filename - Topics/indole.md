## Source [^1]

## Source[^2]
- $n$. A crystalline substance $\mathrm{C}_{8} \mathrm{H}_{7} \mathrm{N}$ forming the basis of the indigo molecule, occurring naturally in coal tar, some plants such as jasmine, and the urine of civets and other mammals, also an important constituent of indoleamine. Also spelt indol. \[From indigo a tropical plant formerly cultivated for its dark blue dye, also a source of indole + Latin oleum oil]
