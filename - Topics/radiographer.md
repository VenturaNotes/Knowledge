## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) (diagnostic radiographer) a person who is trained in the technique of taking X-ray images of parts of the body. In modern practice a radiographer may also perform other imaging techniques, including magnetic resonance imaging, ultrasound, and nuclear medicine. 
- (2) (therapeutic radiographer) a person who is trained in the technique of treatment by radiotherapy.
