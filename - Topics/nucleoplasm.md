## Source [^1]
- (karyoplasm) $n$. the protoplasm making up the nucleus of a cell.
## Source[^2]
- (karyoplasm) The material contained within the nucleus of a cell. The nucleoplasm is bound by the nuclear envelope, which separates it from the cytoplasm.

- $n$. The protoplasm in the nucleus (1) of a cell. \[From Latin nucleus a kernel + plasma a form]
