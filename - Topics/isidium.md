## Source [^1]
- (pl. isidia) An outgrowth from the thallus of a lichen, $0.01\textendash0.03 \mathrm{~mm}$ in diameter, that includes both algal and fungal cells. Isidia can detach from the main body of the lichen and serve as structures of vegetative propagation. Compare SOREDIUM.
