## Source [^1]
- (Pyemotes) n. a genus of widely distributed tiny predaceous mites. P. ventricosus occasionally attacks humans and causes an allergic dermatitis called grain itch. This complaint most usually affects those people coming into contact with stored cereal products, such as hay and grain.
