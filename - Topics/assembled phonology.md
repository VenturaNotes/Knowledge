## Source [^1]
- $n$. The sound of a word or verbal expression as it is built up from knowledge of the usual sounds of letters and sequences of letters. This method fails with irregular words such as yacht in English, but in regular languages such as German or Spanish the correct pronunciation can always be worked out in this way. Compare ADDRESSED PHONOLOGY.
