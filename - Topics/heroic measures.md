## Source [^1]
- a mainly US term for therapies instituted to save a patient's life when all other options have failed. These may have serious or even fatal side-effects, but the risk is considered worth taking since the patient will surely otherwise die. Many people specify 'no heroic measures' in their advance directive ('living will').
