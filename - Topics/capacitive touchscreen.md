## Source [^1]
- A touchscreen with capacitive sensors that use the effect of body capacitance to detect the close proximity of a finger. Found on high-end smartphones, they generally do not work well through gloves; a user wearing work gloves may need to use a stylus designed to link body capacitance to the screen. See also RESISTIVE TOUCHSCREEN.
