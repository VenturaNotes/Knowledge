## Source [^1]
- $n$. A visual line, edge, or border outlining a part of the visual field, usually determined by abrupt lightness, color, or texture gradients but sometimes induced as a subjective or illusory contour without any corresponding retinal image gradient. See also Ehrenstein's Brightness Illusion, illusORY CONTOUR, KANIZSA TRIANGLE, ZERO-CROSSING.
