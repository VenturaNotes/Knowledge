## Synthesis
- A concentrator is a device that combines multiple channels onto a single [[transmission medium]]
## Source [^1]
- In digital communication systems, a circuit or system that, through a process of coding and/or multiplexing, enables more information to be transmitted through a communication channel. The use of such coding and/or multiplexing is known as concentration.
