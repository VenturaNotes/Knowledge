## Source [^1]
- $n$. the terminal part of the large intestine, about 12 cm long, which runs from the sigmoid colon to the anal canal. Faeces are stored in the rectum before defecation. $\textemdash$rectal adj.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. rectums or recta) The muscular portion of the alimentary canal between the colon and the anus. Its main function is the storage of faeces and their elimination via the anus.
