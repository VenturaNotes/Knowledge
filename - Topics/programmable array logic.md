## Source [^1]
- A form of PLA in whose sum of products the products (AND operations) are programmable, but the sums (OR operations) are either fixed, or programmable only to a limited extent. In the latter case, any product terms that can take part in a programmable sum are called shareable $\mathbf{P}$ terms.
