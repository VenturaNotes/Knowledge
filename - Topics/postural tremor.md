## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of tremor not easily classifiable as either a passive tremor or an intention tremor, appearing when an attempt is made to maintain a posture, resulting sometimes from lesions in the basal ganglia as in parkinsonism, with a typical frequency of 3-6 hertz, or as a sign of cerebellar syndrome, with a typical frequency of 2.5-4 hertz. See also POSTUROGRAPHY.
