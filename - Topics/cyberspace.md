## Source [^1]
- An informal word first thought to have been used by the novelist William Gibson to refer to the total data on all the computers on all the networks in the world. The word has passed into common use as a way of referring to any large collection of network-accessible computer-based data
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A notional electronic environment created by computer networks, such as the internet, in which people communicate and interact. \[From cyber(netics) + space]
