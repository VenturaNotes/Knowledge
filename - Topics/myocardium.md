## Source [^1]
- $n$. the middle of the three layers forming the wall of the heart (see also ENDOCARDIUM; EPICARDIUM). It is composed of cardiac muscle and forms the greater part of the heart wall, being thicker in the ventricles than in the atria. $\textemdash$myocardial adj.
