## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) In experimental design, any variable apart from the independent variable that is controlled by the experimenter by being randomized, held constant, statistically controlled, or suppressed in some other way (also called a controlled variable). 
- (2) Any variable whose values are used to separate cases into subgroups in a cross-tabulation.
