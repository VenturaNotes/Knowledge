## Source [^1]
- n. The synchronization of the phases of two or more waveforms, so that their peaks and troughs occur simultaneously, as when auditory receptors in the organ of Corti synchronize their receptor potentials with the vibration of the basilar membrane for sounds below about 4,000 hertz.
