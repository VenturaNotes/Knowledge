## Source [^1]
- $n$. any disease of the peripheral nerves, usually causing weakness and numbness. In a mononeuropathy a single nerve is affected and the extent of the symptoms depends upon the distribution of that nerve. In a polyneuropathy (see PERIPHERAL NEUROPATHY) many or all of the nerves are involved and the symptoms are most profound at the extremities of the limbs. See also DIABETIC NEUROPATHY. $\textendash$neuropathic adj.
