## Source [^1]
- $n$. A drug that is closely related to amphetamine and that has a long-lasting action, used as a central nervous system stimulant and appetite suppressant. Common street names for it include crank, crystal, crystal meth, meth, and speed (more properly reserved for amphetamine). See also amphetamine-related disorders, ice, yaba. \[From meth(yl) the alkyl radical $\mathrm{CH}_{3}$ derived from methane by removal of one hydrogen atom, from Greek methy wine + hyle wood + English amphetamine]
