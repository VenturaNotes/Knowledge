## Source [^1]
- $n$. the five bones of the foot that connect the tarsus (ankle) to the phalanges (toes).
## Source[^2]
- (pl. metatarsi) The foot (or corresponding part of the hindlimb) in terrestrial vertebrates, consisting of a number of rod-shaped bones (metatarsals) that articulate with the bones of the ankle (see tarsus) and those of the toes (see phalanges). The number of metatarsals varies between species: in the basic pentadactyl limb there are five, but this number is reduced in some species.
