## Source [^1]
- aka jolt
- The derivative of acceleration with respect to time, second derivative of velocity, and third derivative of displacement.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. the sudden contraction of a muscle in response to a nerve impulse. Examples are the ankle jerk and the knee jerk (see PATELLAR REFLEX). Eliciting these and other jerks is a means of testing the nerve pathways, via the spinal cord, which are involved in reflexes.
