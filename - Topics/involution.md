## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) the shrinking of the uterus to its normal size after childbirth. 
- (2) atrophy of the corpus luteum, which occurs after pregnancy or if implantation of the embryo does not occur. 
- (3) atrophy of an organ in old age.
## Source[^2]
- (1) A decrease in the size of an organ or the body. It may be associated with functional decline, as occurs in the aging process, or follow enlargement, as when the uterus returns to its normal size after pregnancy. 
- (2) The turning or rolling inwards of cells that occurs during the development of some vertebrate embryos.
