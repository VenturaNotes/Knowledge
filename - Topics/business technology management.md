## Source [^1]
- (1) A strategic management process that aims to maximize value by achieving a complete integration of a company's IT practices with its structures, processes, decision-making, and evolving strategic goals. 
- (2) A comprehensive service offered to smaller companies that do not have their own IT department; it typically involves database and network management, security policy, and general advice and support.
