## Source [^1]
- $n$. The general feeling of bodily existence arising from the sum of bodily sensations as distinct from the particular sensations themselves; the vital sense. Also called coenesthesia. US cenesthesis, cenesthesia. \[From Greek koinos common + aisthesis feeling]
