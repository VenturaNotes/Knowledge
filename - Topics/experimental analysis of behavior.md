## Source [^1]
- $n$. The application of the theory of operant conditioning to behavior, especially in animals, focusing almost exclusively on the effects of reinforcement on rate of response. The term can also be applied to the study of behaviour through experiments generally, but in practice it has tended to become confined to the meaning defined above. US experimental analysis of behavior.
