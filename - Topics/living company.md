## Source [^1]
- A company that is managed for long-term success rather than for short-term profit. Such companies tend to be sensitive to changes in the business environment but wary of fashionable ideas and cautious with finance; they usually have a cohesive organizational culture and a commitment to developing the talents of their employees over the mid-to-long term.
