## Source [^1]
- Any of various pigment-containing plastids in plant cells. Red, orange, and yellow chromoplasts contain carotenoid pigments and are responsible for the coloration of fruits and flowers. See also PLASTOGLOBULUS. Compare CHLOROPLAST; LEUCOPLAST.
