## Source [^1]
- Any device that utilizes one or more electron beams as an essential part of its operation. Several electrodes may be present to form, control, and direct the electron beam. The deflected beam is made to strike a fluorescent screen in measuring instruments such as the cathode-ray tube. See also ELECTRON MICROSCOPE; KLYSTRON.
