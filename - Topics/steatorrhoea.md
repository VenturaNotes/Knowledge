## Source [^1]
- $n$. the passage of excess fat in the faeces (more than $5 \mathrm{~g} /$day) due to reduced absorption of fat by the intestine (see MALABSORPTION). The faeces are pale, malodorous, may look greasy, and are difficult to flush away.
