## Source [^1]
- (1) (in botany) The condition in a flower in which the male and female reproductive organs mature at the same time, thereby allowing self-fertilization. Compare DICHOGAMY. 
- (2) Breeding between individuals that are similar in some way or share certain traits; inbreeding.
