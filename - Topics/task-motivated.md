## Source [^1]
- (task-oriented) Describing a leadership style that is concerned with setting goals, structuring tasks, and measuring performance. A task-motivated leader will focus on such activities as planning, scheduling, defining roles and responsibilities, and providing performance-related feedback. Compare RELATIONSHIP-MOTIVATED. See CONTINGENCY theories of leadership; Least Preferred Co-worker Scale.
