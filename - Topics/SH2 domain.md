## Source [^1]
- (Src homology 2 domain) A functional unit of a protein, typically comprising 60-100 amino acids, that forms a binding site for another protein. It is named after the prototype of such domains in members of the Src tyrosine kinase family and is found in many intracellular signalling proteins.
