## Source [^1]
- In chartist analysis, a pattern of fluctuation in the price of a security, in which a rise is followed by a fall, then a second rise to the same level. This pattern is thought to indicate that the security is meeting resistance to a move to a higher level. Compare DOUBLE BOTTOM. See also ASCENDING TOPS; RESISTANCE LEVEL.
