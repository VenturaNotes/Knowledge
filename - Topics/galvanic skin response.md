## Source [^1]
- $n$. A fall in the resistance of the skin to the passage of a weak electric current, usually measured in the palm of the hand in units of ohms, indicative of emotion or physiological arousal, also called a psychogalvanic response (PGR) or an electrodermal response (EDR). See also polygraph. Compare skin CONDUCTANCE RESPONSE. GSR abbrev. \[From galvanic of or relating to current electricity, named after the Italian physiologist Luigi Galvani (1737-98) who first showed that muscles contract when electricity is passed through them]
