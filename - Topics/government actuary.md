## Source [^1]
- A government officer who works with a small team of actuaries to produce detailed population projections on the basis of observed trends and to assess their implications for national planning and public expenditure. He or she provides a consulting service to government departments and the public sector, giving advice on social security schemes, superannuation arrangements, and on government supervision of insurance companies.
