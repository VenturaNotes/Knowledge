## Source [^1]
- (in dentistry) the combined width of the junctional epithelium and connective tissue attachment around the root surface of a tooth: in other words, the distance between the deepest point of the gingival sulcus and the alveolus. Dental restorations must respect this natural dimension if harmful consequences are to be avoided.
