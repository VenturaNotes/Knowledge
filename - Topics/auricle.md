## Source[^2]
- $n$. 
- (1) Another name for the pinna or external part of the ear. 
- (2) The atrium or upper chamber of the heart, or a small sac in the atrium of the heart. 
- (3) Any ear-shaped biological structure. auricular adj. \[From Latin auricula the external ear, from auris an ear]
