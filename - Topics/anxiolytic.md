## Source [^1]
- adj. describing a group of drugs used for the short-term treatment of anxiety of various causes. Formerly known as minor tranquillizers, they include the benzodiazepines and buspirone. Common side-effects of these drugs are drowsiness and dizziness, and prolonged use may result in dependence.
