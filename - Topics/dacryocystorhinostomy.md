## Source [^1]
- (DCR) $n$. an operation to relieve blockage of the nasolacrimal duct (which drains tears into the nose), in which a communication is made between the lacrimal sac and the nose by removing the intervening bone. It is done via a skin incision (external DCR) or through the nose (endonasal DCR). See DACRYOCYSTITIS; LACRIMAL APPARATUS.
