## Source [^1]
- n. A term introduced in 1915 by the Swiss linguist Ferdinand de Saussure (1857-1913) to denote language as manifested in concrete utterances of particular speakers. Parole is seen as one of the two major aspects of langage, the other being langue, and it is analogous to the concept of performance (2). \[Old French parole speech]
