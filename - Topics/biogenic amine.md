## Source [^1]
- Any amine that is produced by living organisms, especially the physiologically active amines that serve as neurotransmitters in animals. These include adrenaline (epinephrine), noradrenaline (norepinephrine), dopamine, serotonin (5hydroxytryptamine), and histamine.
