## Source [^1]
- $n$. the destruction of tissues or cells brought about by the actions of their own enzymes. See LYSOSOME.
## Source[^2]
- The process of self-destruction of a cell, cell organelle, or tissue. It occurs by the action of enzymes within or released by lysosomes. See also LYSIS.
