## Source [^1]
- A type of cell in which the electrodes are made from the same metal and are immersed in two different concentrations of one salt of the metal. The e.m.f. produced by the metal dissolving in the weaker solution and being deposited by the stronger is dependent upon the substances used and their concentrations.
