## Source [^1]
- $n$. Absence of kinaesthesis or muscle sense, usually resulting from damage to the central nervous system. Also called akinaesthesis . US akinesthesia, akinesthesis. \[From Greek $a$ - without + kineein to move + aisthesis sensation, from aisthanesthai to feel + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
