## Source [^1]
- A storage service provider (SSP) is a company that offers cloud-based data centers for storing and managing electronic information. SSPs provide a wide range of services, including backup and disaster recovery, data security, storage management, and archiving. Businesses use them to store large amounts of data in secure locations.
