## Source [^1]
- (maxterm) A sum (OR) of $n$ Boolean variables, uncomplemented or complemented but not repeated, in a function of $n$ variables. With $n$ variables, $2^{n}$ different standard sum terms are possible. The complement of any standard sum term is a standard product term, or minterm.
