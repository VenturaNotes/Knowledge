## Source [^1]
- $n$. a technique for X-ray examination of the salivary glands. A series of X-ray images is taken after introducing contrast medium through a cannula into the ducts of the parotid or submandibular glands in the mouth. It enables the presence of degenerative disease or stones blocking the ducts to be detected.
