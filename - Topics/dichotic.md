## Source [^1]
- adj. Of or relating to the simultaneous presentation of a different auditory message to each ear via earphones, a task used to study attention and laterality of functions, based on the fact that the nerve fibers that carry auditory signals from each ear are almost entirely crossed to the auditory cortex of the opposite cerebral hemisphere. Often confused with binaural. See also shadowing. Compare dichHAPTIC TEChnique, Diotic. \[From Greek dicha in two, from dis twice + ous, otos an ear]
