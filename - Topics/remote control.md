## Source [^1]
- A control unit that is operated some distance from the system being controlled, without any cable or control rods connecting them. The communication between controller and system uses electromagnetic waves, usually in the radio or infrared spectrum. Most consumer electronic products now come with remote controls, and many car keys can operate door locks remotely. See also REMOTELY OPERATED VEHICLE.
