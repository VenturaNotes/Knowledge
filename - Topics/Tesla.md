## Source [^1]
- $n$. the SI unit of magnetic flux density, equal to a density of 1 weber per square metre. This unit is important in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). Most MRI scanners operate between 0.1 and 2.5T , but some modern research scanners may go up to 7 T. Symbol: T.
