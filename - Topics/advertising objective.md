## Source [^1]
- (communication goal) A specific communications (advertising) task to be achieved, with a specific target audience, during a specific period. In the context of marketing, the objectives will be to gain attention, to be understood, to be believed, and to be remembered (i.e. to inform, persuade, compare, and remind).
