## Source [^1]
- $n$. In DSM-5, a communication disorder affecting the expression or understanding of language, requiring the following four criteria for diagnosis: persistent difficulty in using language, including reduced vocabulary, limited ability to structure sentences, and impairments in the ability to hold a conversation; language ability substantially below age norms, affecting social, academic, or occupational functioning; onset in early development; and the language difficulties not being attributable to auditory or visual impairments, motor dysfunction, neurological impairment, or general delay in development. See also aphasia, autism SPECTRUM DISORDER, DYSARTHRIA, SELECTIVE MUTISM.
