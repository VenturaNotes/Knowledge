## Source [^1]
- Any of a group of 20-carbon polyunsaturated fatty acids, particularly arachidonic acid, and their derivatives. The eicosanoids include various biologically important molecules, notably the prostaglandins, prostacyclins, thromboxanes, and leukotrienes.
