## Source [^1]
- $n$. a bony outgrowth within a ligament, usually within the ligaments of the intervertebral joints, seen in the sero-negative spondyloarthropathies; for example, ankylosing spondylitis, reactive arthritis, and psoriatic arthritis. Fusion of syndesmophytes across the joints between vertebrae contributes to rigidity of the spine, seen in advanced cases of these diseases.
