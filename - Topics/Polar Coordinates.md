## Source [^1]
- polar coordinates A technique for giving two-dimensional coordinates $(r,\phi)$ of a point. Radial distance $r$ is the distance along the horizontal $x$-axis, and polar angle $\phi$ describes an anti-clockwise rotation. See also CYLINDRICAL POLAR COORDINATES; SPHERICAL POLAR COORDINATES.
