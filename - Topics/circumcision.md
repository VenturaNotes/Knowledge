## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) surgical removal of the foreskin of the penis. This operation is usually performed for religious and ethnic reasons but is sometimes required for medical conditions, mainly pathological phimosis due to balanitis xerotica obliterans and paraphimosis. 
- (2) (female circumcision) see FEMALE GENITAL MUTILATION.
