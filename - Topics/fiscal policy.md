- The use of government spending and taxation to influence macroeconomic conditions. Fiscal policy was actively pursued to sustain full employment in the post-war years; however, monetarists and others have argued that this set off the inflation of the 1970s. Fiscal policy has remained generally 'tight' in most Western countries since the 1980s. Compare MONETARY POLICY.
