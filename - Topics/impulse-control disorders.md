## Source [^1]
- $n$. A class of mental disorders in DSM-IV characterized by a failure to resist impulses, drives, or temptations to behave in ways that are damaging to oneself or others. In DSM-5, they appear under disruptive, impulse-control, and conduct disorders. See ANTISOCIAL PERSONALITY DISORDER, CONDUCT DISORDER, INTERMITTENT EXPLOSIVE DISORDER, KLEPTOMANIA, OPPOSITIONAL DEFIANT DISORDER, PYROMANIA. See also INTERNET ADDICTION SYNDROME, INTERNET GAMING DISORDER, ROAD RAGE, ROID RAGE, SUBSTANCE-RELATED AND ADDICTIVE DISORDERS.
