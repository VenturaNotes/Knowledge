## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antidepressant drug that reversibly inhibits the enzyme monoamine oxidase A. The adverse effects of irreversible MAO inhibitors, which include severe hypertensive reactions when cheese or other tyramine-containing foods are eaten by patients taking them, are less likely with reversible inhibitors. Moclobemide is used to treat major depression and social anxiety disorder.
