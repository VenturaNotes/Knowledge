## Source [^1]
- $n$. An aspect of ergonomics or human factors psychology concerned with the optimal design of human-machine interfaces, including the design and placement of instrument panels and controls, taking into account population stereotypes and findings from research into perception and other relevant areas of psychology. See also POPULATION STEREOTYPE.
