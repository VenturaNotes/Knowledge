## Source [^1]
- $n$. A neurotoxic amino acid that is found in the Japanese red alga or seaweed Digenia simplex and that acts as a powerful central nervous system stimulant by potentiating the neurotransmitter glutamate, causing destruction to postsynaptic cells with glutamate receptors. \[From Japanese kainin-so red alga]
