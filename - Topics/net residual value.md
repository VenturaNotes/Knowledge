## Source [^1]
- (disposal value) The expected proceeds from the sale of an asset, net of the costs of sale, at the end of its estimated useful life. It is used for computing the straight-line method and diminishing-balance method of depreciation, and also for inclusion in the final year's cash inflow in a discounted cash flow appraisal.
