## Source [^1]
- A smart machine is a device or piece of software capable of learning based on experiences and producing unanticipated results. Smart machines are embedded with machine-to-machine and/or cognitive computing technologies, such as deep learning, intelligent automation, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. Smart machines can reason, solve problems, make decisions, and take action.
