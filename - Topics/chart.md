## Source [^1]
- (1) A diagrammatic or graphical representation of data, as in pie chart
- (2) (coordinate map) Around each point of a manifold, a homeomorphism from a neighborhood of the point onto its image in Cartesian space $\mathbb{R}^{n}$. The collection of charts makes an atlas, and transition maps relate coordinates in overlapping neighborhoods.
