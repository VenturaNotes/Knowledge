## Source [^1]
- $n$. The effect of a signal pre-empting a subsequent error, such as the efferent output of the extra-ocular muscles during a saccade, suppressing the sensation of movement that would otherwise be created by the motion of images across the retinas during the saccade. See also visual SUPPRESSION. Compare FEEDBACK.
