## Source [^1]
- (UHD) A display with a resolution of at least $3840 \times 2160$ pixels, and a 16:9 or wider aspect ratio, as defined by the Consumer Technology Association. It is similar to the 4K resolution standard. Also defined is a variant that has four times the number of pixels ($7680 \times 4320$).
