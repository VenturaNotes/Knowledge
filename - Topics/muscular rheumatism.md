## Source [^1]
- any aching pain in the muscles and joints. Commonly the symptoms are due to fibrositis; wear and tear of the joints (osteoarthritis); or to inflammation of the muscles associated with abnormal immune reactions (polymyalgia rheumatica). Generalized muscle pain with specific tender points and fatigue is called fibromyalgia.
