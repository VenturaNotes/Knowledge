## Source [^1]
- Distortion of an audio signal when its amplitude is too large and so levels off at a maximum value, effectively chopping off the high-volume peaks. Clipped signals produce more power than their unclipped counterparts, and hence can cause damage, particularly to loudspeakers. See CLIPPING.
