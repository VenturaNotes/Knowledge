## Source [^1]
- An entity that is recognized by the law as a legal person, i.e. one having legal rights and duties distinct from the individuals who comprise it. For example, a company is a person in the sense that it can sue and be sued, hold property, etc., in its own name. It is not, however, an individual or natural person. See CORPORATION.
