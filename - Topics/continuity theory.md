## Source [^1]
- $n$. A theory of learning espoused by the US psychologist Clark L. Hull (1884-1952), among others, according to which an organism learns a small amount about all stimuli (1) that it encounters, and learning is therefore gradual and continuous. Compare DISCONTINUITY THEORY.
