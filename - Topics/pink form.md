## Source [^1]
- (preferential form) An application form in a flotation that is printed on pink paper and usually distributed to employees of the company to give them preference in the allocation of shares. Up to $10 \%$ of a share issue can be set aside under London Stock Exchange rules for applications from employees or from shareholders in a parent company that is floating a subsidiary.
