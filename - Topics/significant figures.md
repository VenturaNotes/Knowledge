## Source[^1]
- Methods for Mixed Operations
	- Keep track of sig figs but don't round until final operation (<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">more likely correct</mark>)
			- (14.84 $\times$ 0.55) - 8.02 = (8.1<u>62</u>) - 8.02 = 0.1<u>42 </u>= 0.1
## Source[^2]
- Mixed Operations
- Round to sig figs on each operation(<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">less likely correct</mark>)
			- $\frac{(4.23 + 7.6)}{0.50} = \frac {11.8}{0.50} = 23.6 = 24$
				- Rounded the 11.8 first because of sig figs and then divided
