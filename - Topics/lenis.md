## Source [^1]
- adj. Of or relating to a consonant speech sound articulated with relatively weak muscular tension of the organs of speech and breath pressure, such as the initial phonemes in bee, die, go, vow, though, and zoo. See also CLIPPING. Compare FORTIS. \[From Latin lenis gentle]
