## Source [^1]
- $n$. a temporary loss of reflex activity in the brainstem or spinal cord following destruction of the cerebral cortex. As time passes this state of suppressed reflex activity is replaced by one of unduly exaggerated reflexes and spasticity of the limbs.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A temporary disruption of brain activity caused by a localized injury, resulting in a generalized breaking up of a pattern of functioning, not caused directly by the lesion itself but arising from indirect or secondary effects in other areas of the central nervous system. \[From Greek dia through + schizein to split]
