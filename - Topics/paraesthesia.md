## Source [^1]
- $n$. a spontaneously occurring tingling sensation, sometimes described as pins and needles. It may be due to partial and temporary damage to a peripheral nerve, such as that caused by external pressure on the affected part, but can also result from damage to sensory fibres in the spinal cord or from peripheral vascular disease. Compare DYSAESTHESIA.
