## Source [^1]
- Any plant that produces seeds. Most seed plants belong to the phyla Anthophyta (flowering plants) or Coniferophyta (conifers); others include the Cycadophyta, the Ginkgophyta (gingkos), and the Gnetophyta (see GYMNOSPERM).
