## Source [^1]
- $n$. A paraphilic disorder in DSM-5 characterized by intense, recurrent sexual arousal from sexual activity with a child who has not yet reached puberty, or associated fantasies or urges over a period of at least six months. To meet the diagnostic criteria, the individual must either have acted out this behaviour, or the fantasies or urges must have caused marked distress or interpersonal problems. US pedophilic disorder. See also PAEDOPHILIA.
