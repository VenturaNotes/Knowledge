## Source [^1]
- $n$. a bronchodilator drug: a short-acting beta agonist (see SYMPATHOMIMETIC) used in the treatment of asthma and other respiratory disorders characterized by reversible airways obstruction and to inhibit premature labour. Common side-effects include nervousness and dizziness.
