## Source [^1]
- $n$. an abnormal cell present in the blood and the bloodforming organs in one type of leukaemia (lymphoblastic leukaemia). It has a large nucleus with very scanty cytoplasm and is thought to be the precursor of the lymphocyte. $\textemdash$lymphoblastic adj.
