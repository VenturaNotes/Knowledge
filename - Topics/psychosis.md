## Source [^1]
- $n$. one of a group of mental illnesses that feature loss of contact with reality. The psychoses include schizophrenia, major disorders of affect (see BIPOLAR AFFECTIVE DISORDER), major paranoid states, drug intoxication, and organic mental disorders. They can be chronic or transient. Psychotic disorders manifest some of the following: delusions, hallucinations, severe thought disturbances, abnormal alteration of mood, poverty of thought, ego disturbances, and inappropriate affect. Many cases of psychotic illness respond well to antipsychotic drugs. $\textemdash$psychotic adj.
