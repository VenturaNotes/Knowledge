## Source [^1]
- $n$. A defective ability to name colors in spite of an intact ability to match and distinguish them, usually interpreted as a form of nominal aphasia (see aphasia). US color dysnomia. Compare agnosia, COLOR-BLINDNESS. \[From Greek dys- bad or abnormal + onoma a name + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
