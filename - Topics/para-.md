## Source [^1]
- combining form denoting 
- (1) beside or close to. Example: paranasal (near the nasal cavity). 
- (2) resembling. Example: paradysentery (a mild form of dysentery). 
- (3) abnormal. Example: paralalia (abnormal speech).
