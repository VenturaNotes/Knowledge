## Source [^1]
- An approach in empirical econometrics where model evaluation and selection is performed by a computerized algorithm according to pre-programmed decision rules. The algorithm largely mimics the general-to-specific approach to econometric modelling in which the statistically insignificant variables in each round of estimation are eliminated until only significant variables remain.
