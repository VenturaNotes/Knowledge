## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug related to the tricyclic antidepressants, that is used to relieve moderate or severe depression associated with anxiety. Side-effects are milder than with tricyclic antidepressants, the commonest being drowsiness.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A tetracyclic antidepressant drug, used to treat depression, especially when it is accompanied by a sleep-wake disorder, and other disorders of which depression is a feature.
