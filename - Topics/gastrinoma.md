## Source [^1]
- $n$. a rare tumor that secretes the hormone gastrin, which stimulates excessive gastric acid production. Such tumors most frequently occur in the pancreas; about half of them are malignant. Patients present with intractable gastroduodenal ulceration, abdominal pain, and diarrhoea (the Zollinger-Ellison syndrome). Gastrinomas may be a feature of MENS type 1.
