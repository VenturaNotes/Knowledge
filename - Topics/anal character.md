## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a personality pattern determined by fixation (2) at the anal stage and characterized by the adult personality traits of orderliness, frugality, and obstinacy, sometimes called the three Ps: pedantry, parsimony, and petulance. Also called the anal personality. See ANAL TRIAD. See also OBSESSIVE-COMPULSIVE PERSONALITY DISORDER.
