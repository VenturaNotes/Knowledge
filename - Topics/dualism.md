## Source [^1]
- $n$. The state of being twofold or double, especially a philosophical doctrine regarding the mind-body problem according to which reality comprises two realms of existence, usually identified as mind and matter, or two types of entities, mental and physical. See also Cartesian dualism, CATEGORY MISTAKE, EPIPHENOMENALISM, INTERACTIONISM (1), PSYCHOPHYSICAL PARALLELISM. Compare DOUBLE-ASPECT THEORY, IDENTITY THEORY, MONISM. \[From Latin dualis twofold, from duo two]
