## Source [^1]
- $n$. the science or practice of correcting deformities caused by disease of or damage to the bones and joints of the skeleton. This specialized branch of surgery may involve operation, manipulation, traction, orthoses, or prostheses. $\textemdash$orthopaedic adj.
