## Source [^1]
- Augmented analytics is the use of machine learning (ML) and natural language processing (NLP) to enhance data analytics, data sharing and business intelligence.
## Source[^2]
- Augmented analytics uses technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning, and natural language processing to enhance data analysis efforts. These technologies can be used across data analytics workflows, from preparing and cleansing data to generating and exploring new insights. Augmented analytics benefits both experienced analysts and non-technical citizen data scientists by allowing them to spend less time on tedious manual tasks and more time on valuable analysis.
