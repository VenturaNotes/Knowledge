## Source[^2]
- adj. Of, relating to, or involving the simultaneous use of two ears or the presentation of the same audible stimulus to both ears. Often confused with dichotic. Compare monaural. \[From Latin bis twice, bini two by two + auris an ear + -alis of or relating to]
