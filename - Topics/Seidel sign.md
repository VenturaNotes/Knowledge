## Source [^1]
- (1)  a sickle-shaped scotoma appearing as an upward or downward extension of the blind spot. 
- (2) a test to confirm leakage of the aqueous humor. Fluorescein sodium dye is instilled and viewed with cobalt blue light; a bright green flow of liquid is seen at the site of the leakage. \[E. Seidel (1882-1948), German ophthalmologist]
