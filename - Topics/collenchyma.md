## Source [^1]
- A plant tissue (see GROUND TISSUES) that consists of living cells with additional cellulose thickening in their walls, providing support and protection for young stems and leaves. It is most commonly found in the stem cortex. Compare PARENCHYMA; SCLERENCHYMA.
