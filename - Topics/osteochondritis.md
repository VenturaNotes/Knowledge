## Source [^1]
- (osteochondrosis) $n$. any one of a group of conditions affecting areas of growth at the epiphyses and apophyses of bones before skeletal maturity. Symptoms of localized pain and swelling are most common during periods of rapid growth in early adolescence. There are three types of osteochondritis: crushing (see Köhler's disease; Kienböck's disease; Legg-Calvé-Perthes disease; Scheuermann's DISEASE); splitting (see OSTEOCHONDRITIS DISSECANS); and pulling (see Osgood-Schlatter disease; Sever's disease).
