## Source [^1]
- (1) The amount by which the full capacity of a barrel or similar container exceeds the volume of the contents, as a result of evaporation or leakage. 
- (2) As defined by HM Revenue and Customs, the actual contents of a barrel or similar container at the time of importation into the UK. The difference between this volume and the full capacity is called the vacuity.
