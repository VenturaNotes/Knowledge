## Source [^1]
- Techniques used to forecast future trends, e.g. the demand for a product, when there is little meaningful data to use as the basis of statistical techniques, or when it is considered necessary to triangulate the results of statistically based projections. Typical techniques include making use of salesforce estimates, juries of expert opinion (see Delphi technique), and surveys of user expectations. Compare QUANTITATIVE FORECASTING TECHNIQUES.
