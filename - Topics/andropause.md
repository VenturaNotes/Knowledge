## Source [^1]
- $n$. the decline in production of testosterone that occurs with age in men. Unlike the female menopause, there is no circumscribed time at which testosterone production irreversibly ceases. Instead it tends to undergo a steady reduction from the third decade onwards.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A male counterpart of the female menopause, caused chiefly by a decline in the level of testosterone in the blood, typically about 1 per cent per annum from age 40 onwards. Symptoms include decreased libido, asthenia, impotence, and depression. See also irritable male syndrome. \[From Greek andros a man + pausis a halt]
