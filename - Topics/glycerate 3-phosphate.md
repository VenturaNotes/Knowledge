## Source [^1]
- A phosphorylated three-carbon monosaccharide that is an intermediate in the Calvin-Bassham-Benson cycle of photosynthesis and also in glycolysis. It was formerly known as 3-phosphoglycerate or phosphoglyceric acid (PGA).
