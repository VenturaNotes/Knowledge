## Source [^1]
- A type of blog in which the length of messages is restricted, typically to hundreds of characters only. Some microblogging services also allow pictures and videos to be posted. The 'immediacy' of microblogging can make it an appealing medium to advertisers or social campaigners. A very popular microblogging service is Twitter.
