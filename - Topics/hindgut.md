## Source [^1]
- $n$. the back part of the embryonic gut, which gives rise to part of the large intestine, the rectum, bladder, and urinary ducts. See also CLOACA.
## Source[^2]
- (1) The posterior part of the alimentary canal of vertebrates, comprising the posterior section of the colon. 
- (2) The posterior section of the alimentary canal of arthropods. See also FOREGUT; MIDGUT.
