## Source [^1]
- (symbol: Pt) A metal, atomic number 78, that is extremely stable and noncorroding and is used as an electrical contact or conductor at high temperatures or when chemical attack is likely. It is also used as a diffusion barrier in multilayer metal contacts, preventing interactions between the constituent metals.
