## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in neurophysiology) the triggering of a conducted impulse in the membrane of a muscle cell or nerve fibre. During excitation a polarized membrane becomes momentarily depolarized and an action potential is set up.
## Source[^2]
- (1) The act or process of stimulating a cell, especially a nerve cell. 
- (2) The response of a cell that has been stimulated.
