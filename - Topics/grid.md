## Source [^1]
- (1) An electrode that has an open structure, such as a mesh or a plate with a hole in it, thus allowing an electron beam to pass through it. See THERMIONIC VALVE. 
- (2) The nationwide high-voltage transmission line system that interconnects many electricity power stations. It transmits voltages of up to 400 kilovolts. Voltages as high as $735~\mathrm{kV}$ are used in some countries.
