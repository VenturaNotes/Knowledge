## Source [^1]
- a rare disorder characterized by chronic urticaria, fever, bone or joint pain, and enlarged lymph nodes in the neck. There are increased levels of an antibody, IgM (see IMMUNOGLOBULIN), produced by plasma cells. Treatment with anakinra is very effective. \[L. Schnitzler (20th century), French dermatologist]
