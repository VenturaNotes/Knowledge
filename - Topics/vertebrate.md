## Source [^1]
- Any one of a large group of animals comprising all chordates (see Chordata) that possess a backbone made of vertebrae (see vertebral column). Vertebrates include the fishes, amphibians, reptiles (including birds), and mammals. Characteristically in vertebrates, the vertebral column replaces the notochord during development as the principal supporting structure.
