## Source [^1]
- (rational) Mathematically, a number that is fractional and is defined as the ratio of two whole numbers: the numerator (an integer) and the denominator (a strictly positive integer). In $a / b, a$ is the numerator and $b$ the denominator. See also RATIONAL TYPE, REAL NUMBERS.
