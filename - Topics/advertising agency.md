## Source [^1]
- A business organization specializing in planning and handling advertising on behalf of clients. A full-service agency provides a range of services to clients, including booking advertising space, designing and producing advertisements, devising media schedules, commissioning research, providing sales promotion advice, and acting as a marketing consultant. The departments within an agency include research, planning, creative design, media bookings, production, and accounts. Most advertising agents work on the basis of a commission on the total sums spent by the client.
