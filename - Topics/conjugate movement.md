## Source [^1]
- $n$. A coordinated movement, as of the two eyes turning together in the same direction or the pupils of both eyes constricting at the same time (a conjugate eye movement). Also called a consensual movement. See also Deiters' nucleus, pupillary light reflex. Compare disjunctive eye movement. \[From Latin coniugare to join together, from con- together + iugare to marry or join, from iugum a yoke]
