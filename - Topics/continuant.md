## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any speech sound such as $/ l /, / \mathrm{f} /, / \mathrm{r} /$, or $/ \mathrm{s} /$ whose articulation involves incomplete blockage of the airstream through the vocal tract. Compare Plosive.
