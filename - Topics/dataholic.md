## Source [^1]
- $n$. An informal term for a person addicted to information, especially a person who spends an excessive amount of time gathering information from the internet and suffers from withdrawal symptoms when deprived of access to information. See Internet Addiction SYndrome, INTERNET GAMING DISORDER. \[Formed on the model of alcoholic, from data + (alco)holic]
