## Source [^1]
- An effect observed when electromagnetic waves are transmitted, as during broadcast transmissions, due to the topography of the region between the transmitter and receiver. A loss of signal strength at the receiver is usually observed compared to the expected signal strength when transmission is over a uniformly flat region.
