## Synthesis
- An experiment that takes place in real-world settings
## Source [^1]
- A research method that uses some controlled elements of traditional lab experiments, but takes place in natural, real-world settings
## Source[^2]
- An experiment that is conducted in the 'real world' rather than in a laboratory. The organization of a field experiment mirrors that of a laboratory experiment except for the fact that subjects are making decisions within their natural environment. See also NATURAL EXPERIMENT.
