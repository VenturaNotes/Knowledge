## Source [^1]
- n. a deficiency of oxygen in the tissues. See also ANOXIA; HYPOXAEMIA.
## Source[^2]
- A deficiency of oxygen in body tissues, which can result from living in an oxygendeficient environment, inadequate inspiration, or deficiency of red blood cells or haemoglobin (required for oxygen transport).
