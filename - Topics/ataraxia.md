## Source [^1]
- $n$. Tranquillity or freedom from anxiety. ataractic adj. Capable of calming or tranquillizing. An ataractic drug is a tranquillizer. \[From Greek ataraktos undisturbed, from $a$- without + tarassein to trouble + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
