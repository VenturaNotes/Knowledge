## Source [^1]
- $n$. a decrease in the proportion of red blood cells relative to the plasma, brought about by an increase in the total volume of plasma. This may occur in a variety of conditions, including pregnancy and enlargement of the spleen (see HYPERSPLENISM). Compare HAEMOCONCENTRATION.
