## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any drug having a dibenzodiazepine nucleus in its chemical structure, including carbamazepine, various tricyclic antidepressant drugs, and a class of atypical antipsychotic drugs such as clozapine that block both serotonin and dopamine receptors and cause fewer neuroleptic-induced side-effects than are associated with conventional neuroleptic drugs. See also dopamine antagonist. \[From Greek dis twice + English benzodiazepine]
