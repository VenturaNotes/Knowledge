## Source [^1]
- $n$. A psychological state characterized by loss of the sense of individuality and a submerging of personal identity and accountability in a group. In some circumstances it can lead to a relaxation of inhibitions and the release of antisocial behaviour, and it has been used to explain certain forms of mob behaviour. See also bystander effect, diffusion of RESPONSIBILITY. Compare INDIVIDUATION.
