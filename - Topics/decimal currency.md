## Source [^1]
- A currency system in which the standard unit is subdivided into 100 parts. Following the example of the USA in 1792, most countries have introduced a decimal system. However, it was not until 15 February 1971 that decimalization was introduced in the UK, following the recommendations of the Halesbury Committee of 1961.
