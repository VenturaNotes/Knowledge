## Source [^1]
- $n$. a steroid synthesized in the adrenal glands, ovaries, and testes. Pregnenolone is an important intermediate product in steroid hormone synthesis and can $\textendash$ depending on the pathways followed $\textendash$ be converted to corticosteroids (glucocorticoids or mineralocorticoids), androgens, or oestrogens.
