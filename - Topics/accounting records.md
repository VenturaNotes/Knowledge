## Source [^1]
- The records kept by a company to comply with the Companies Act, which requires companies to keep accounting records sufficient to show and explain their transactions and to prepare accounts that give a true and fair view of their activities. Accounting records take the form of manual or computerized ledgers, journals, and the supporting documentation.
