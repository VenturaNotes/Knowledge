## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) An organism composed of two or more genetically distinct tissue types or created by combining genes from two or more different individuals or species. Also called a mosaic . Also spelt chimaera. 
- (2) In Greek mythology, a fire-breathing monster with a lion's head, a goat's body, and a serpent's tail. chimeric or chimaeric adj.
