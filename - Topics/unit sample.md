## Source [^1]
- A sampled waveform, usually denoted $\delta[n]$, that consists of an isolated sample with an amplitude of unity and all other samples with amplitudes of zero:$$\begin{align}

\delta[n]=0, \quad n\neq 0 \\\delta[n]=1, \quad n=0\end{align}$$
