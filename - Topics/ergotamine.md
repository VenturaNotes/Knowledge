## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug that causes constriction of blood vessels and is used to relieve migraine. It is administered in combination with cyclizine and caffeine. Common side-effects are nausea and vomiting, and ergotism may develop as a result of high doses; because of this it has largely been superseded by $5\mathrm{HT}_{1}$ agonists.
