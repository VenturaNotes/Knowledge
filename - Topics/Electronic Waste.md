## Source [^1]
- Electronic waste (e-waste) refers to electrical and electronic equipment that has been discarded, is old, or has reached the end of its life.
## Source[^2]
- Unwanted and discarded electronic products. These contain many toxic substances and so care is needed for their disposal. In the EU the Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment Directive (WEEE) governs their treatment.
