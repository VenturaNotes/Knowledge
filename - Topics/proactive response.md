## Source [^1]
- A management strategy in which a company seeks to pre-empt criticisms of its social or environmental practices by devising policies to deal with such problems before they arise. This anticipatory strategy is the most engaged of responsive strategies (compare ACCOMMODATING RESPONSE; DEFENSIVE RESPONSE; OBSTRUCTIVE RESPONSE).
