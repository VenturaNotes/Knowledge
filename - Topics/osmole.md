## Source [^1]
- $n$. a unit of osmotic pressure equal to the molecular weight of a solute in grams divided by the number of ions or other particles into which it dissociates in solution.
## Source[^2]
- Symbol osmol. The amount of substance that gives rise to or contains one mole of osmotically active particles when dissolved in water.
