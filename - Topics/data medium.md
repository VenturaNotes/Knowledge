## Source [^1]
- A material having defined properties, including a physical variable that can be used to represent data. The defined properties ensure that the [[medium]] is compatible with devices that can record or read data on the medium. Examples of data media are magnetic disks, optical disks, and magnetic tape, and also paper used for printer output.
- Plural of "medium" is "media"
