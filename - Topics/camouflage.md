## Source [^1]
- A high degree of similarity between an animal and its visual environment, which enables it to be disguised or concealed. By blending into the background the animal can elude predators or remain invisible to potential prey. See also CRYPTIC COLORATION; MASQUERADE; MIMICRY. Compare WARNING COLORATION.
