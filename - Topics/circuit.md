## Source [^1]
- (1) The combination of a number of electrical devices and conductors that, when interconnected to form a conducting path, fulfill some desired function. See also INTEGRATED CIRCUIT, LOGIC CIRCUIT, PRINTED CIRCUIT. 
- (2) A physical (electrical) connection used for communication. See also CIRCUIT SWITCHING, VIRTUAL CIRCUIT. 
- (3) (of a graph) Another name for cycle.
