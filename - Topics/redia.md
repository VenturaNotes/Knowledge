## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. rediae) the third-stage larva of a parasitic fluke. Rediae develop within the body of a freshwater snail and undergo a process of asexual reproduction, giving rise to many fourth-stage larvae called cercariae. See also MIRACIDIUM; SPOROCYST.
