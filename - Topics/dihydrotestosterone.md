## Source [^1]
- (DHT) $n$. a product formed from the action of the enzyme $5 \alpha$-reductase on testosterone. Mostly derived from the peripheral conversion of testosterone, some DHT is also secreted directly by the testes. DHT is an estimated 2.5 times more potent than testosterone but is present in much smaller amounts in the circulation.
