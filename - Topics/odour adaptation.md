## Source [^1]
- $n$. A temporary reduction in sensitivity to an odor following prolonged exposure to it, the phenomenon that causes workers in noisome factories to be oblivious of the stench, except for a short while after they first arrive for work, and that makes people in general insensitive to their own body odors. US odor adaptation.
