## Source [^1]
- (arachnoid mater) $n$. the middle of the three membranes covering the brain and spinal cord (see MENINGES), which has a fine, almost cobweb-like, texture. Between it and the pia mater within lies the subarachnoid space, containing cerebrospinal fluid and large blood vessels; the membrane itself has no blood supply.
