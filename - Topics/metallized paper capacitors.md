## Source [^1]
- Capacitors that are constructed using a paper impregnated with a salt for the dielectric, which is coated in a metallic film to provide the parallel plate electrodes. The film is then usually tightly wound to minimize the volume. Such capacitors are often used for suppression of high-voltage burst interference, providing a self-healing breakdown phenomenon to prevent the transmission of the high-voltage interference.
