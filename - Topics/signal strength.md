## Source [^1]
- A measure of how much power a radio antenna is receiving from a given radio transmitter, commonly expressed as the number of decibels above 1 milliwatt (dBm). Broadcast transmitters have typical power levels in the 80 dBm range, while mobile phones have an output level ranging of 20-33 dBm. Signal strength is often indicated on mobile devices using a four- or five-bar display.
