## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antimuscarinic drug used to reduce muscle tremor and rigidity in parkinsonism. Common side-effects include dry mouth, blurred vision, and giddiness.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Procyclidine hydrochloride, an anticholinergic drug that is used in the treatment of parkinsonism. See also ANTIPARKINSONIAN.
