## Source [^1]
- $n$. a reduction in the number of platelets in the blood. This results in bleeding into the skin (see PURPURA), spontaneous bruising, and prolonged bleeding after injury. Thrombocytopenia may result from failure of platelet production or excessive destruction of platelets. $\textemdash$thrombocytopenic adj.
