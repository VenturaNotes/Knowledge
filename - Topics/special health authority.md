## Source [^1]
- a type of NHS trust that provides services across the NHS in England, rather than in a single defined geographical area. There are four special health authorities, which exist as arms-length bodies of the Department of Health and Social Care, independent of government: NHS Blood and Transplant, NHS Business Services Authority, NHS Resolution and NHS Counter Fraud Authority.
