## Source [^1]
- (basal taxon) (in cladistics) A group of organisms that diverges early in the evolutionary history of a clade (see CLadistics). In a cladogram or phylogenetic tree (see PHYLOGRAM) the basal group branches near the root of the tree, which represents the common ancestor of the clade.
