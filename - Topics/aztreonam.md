## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antibiotic administered by injection or inhalation used to treat infections of the lungs, bones, skin, and soft tissues caused by Gram-negative organisms (see Gram's StAIN), including lung infections in patients with cystic fibrosis. Possible side-effects include skin rashes, diarrhoea, and vomiting.
