## Source [^1]
- $n$. an $\mathrm{H}_{2}$-receptor antagonist (see ANTIHISTAMINE) that is used in the treatment of peptic ulcers and gastro-oesophageal reflux disease. Sideeffects include headache, diarrhoea, rash, and drowsiness.
