## Source [^1]
- $n$. A mental disorder in DSM-5 characterized by the sudden appearance of delusions, hallucinations, disorganized speech, disorganized thinking, or grossly disorganized behavior such as catatonia, the episode lasting at least one day but less than four weeks. Compare SCHIZOPHRENIA.
