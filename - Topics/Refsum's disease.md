## Source [^1]
- an inherited disorder of lipid metabolism resulting in abnormal accumulation of phytanic acid (a fatty acid) in body tissues. This results in a peripheral neuropathy affecting the sensory and motor nerves, diminishing vision due to retinitis pigmentosa, and unsteadiness (ataxia) caused by damage to the cerebellum. \[S. Refsum (20th century), Norwegian physician]
