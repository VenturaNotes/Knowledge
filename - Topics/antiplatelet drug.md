## Source [^1]
- any one of a class of drugs that reduce platelet aggregation (see PLATELET ACTIVATION) and therefore the formation of clot (see THROMBOSIS). Examples are abciximab, aspirin, clopidogrel, prasugrel, and ticagrelor.
