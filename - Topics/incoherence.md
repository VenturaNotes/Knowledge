## Source [^1]
- $n$. Loose, rambling speech that lacks meaningful connections between words or phrases within sentences or clauses and is incomprehensible to others. Extreme instances are sometimes called word salad. See also disorgAnized SPEECH, DISORGANIZED THINKING.
