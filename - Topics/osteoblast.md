## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cell, originating in the mesoderm of the embryo, that is responsible for the formation of bone. See also ossification.
## Source[^2]
- Any of the cells, found in bone, that secrete collagen and other substances that form the matrix of bone (see OSTEOID). Osteoblasts are derived from osteoprogenitor cells in the bone marrow; they eventually become surrounded by bone within small cavities (lacunae) as osteocytes. See also ossification.
