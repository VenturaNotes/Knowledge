## Source [^1]
- a drug effective against viruses that cause disease. Antiviral drugs include DNA polymerase inhibitors (e.g. aciclovir, foscarnet, ganciclovir), ribavirin, and oseltamivir; they are used for treating herpes, cytomegalovirus and respiratory syncytial virus infections, and influenza. Antiviral drugs are also used for treating HIV infection and AIDS (see ANTIRETROVIRAL DRUG).
