## Source [^1]
- $n$. the surgical removal of a stone from the kidney by an incision into the kidney substance. It is normally performed in combination with an incision into the renal pelvis (see PYELOLITHOTOMY). See also PERCUTANEOUS NEPHROLITHOTOMY.
