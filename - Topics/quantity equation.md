## Source [^1]
- The equation $M V=P T$ relating the price level and the quantity of money. Here $M$ is the quantity of money, $V$ is the velocity of circulation, $P$ is the price level, and $T$ is the volume of transactions. The quantity equation is the basis for the quantity theory of money.
