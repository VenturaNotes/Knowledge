## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of cluster sampling involving drawing a cluster sample in two or more successive stages. For example, to survey hospital patients throughout a country, a survey researcher might select a sample of hospitals randomly, then within each hospital select a single ward randomly, then select individual patients from each ward randomly.
