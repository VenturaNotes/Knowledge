## Source [^1]
- A chronic condition developing in adulthood due to overproduction of (or oversensitivity to) growth hormone, usually caused by a tumour in the pituitary gland. This leads to a gradual enlargement of the bones, causing characteristic coarsening of the facial features and large hands and feet.
