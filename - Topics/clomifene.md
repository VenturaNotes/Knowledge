## Source [^1]
- (clomiphene) $n$. a synthetic nonsteroidal compound (see ANTIOESTROGEN) that induces ovulation and subsequent menstruation in women who fail to ovulate (for example, due to polycystic ovary syndrome). Common side-effects are hot flushes and abdominal discomfort.
