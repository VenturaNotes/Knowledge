## Source [^1]
- An occupational pension scheme in which payments are determined by the employee's final salary. See DEFINED-BENEFIT PENSION SCHEME.
## Source[^2]
- A form of defined benefit pension scheme for which the annual pension is a proportion of pensionable earnings at or near retirement. The proportion is typically determined by the number of years of contribution to the scheme. See also CAREER AVERAGE SCHEME; DEFINED CONTRIBUTION.
