## Source [^1]
- Capital refers to the financial resources and physical assets that are used in the production of goods and services. 
- It plays a crucial role in economic growth, as it includes investments in machinery, buildings, and tools that enhance productivity. 
- The effective use of capital is essential in addressing scarcity, as it allows for the efficient allocation of resources to meet the needs and wants of society.
