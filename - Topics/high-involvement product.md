## Source [^1]
- A product that involves the consumer in taking time and trouble before deciding on a purchase. This will include looking in several catalogues, shops, etc., to compare prices and the products themselves. High-involvement products include such major purchases as cars and houses, as well as computers, home entertainment systems, and many other domestic products. Compare LOW-INVOLVEMENT PRODUCT.
