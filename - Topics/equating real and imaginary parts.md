- complex numbers $a+bi$ and $c+di$ are equal if and only if $a=c$ and $b=d$. Using this fact is called equating real and imaginary parts. For example, if $(a+bi)^2 = 5 + 12i,$ then $a^2-b^2 = 5$ and $2ab = 12$
