## Source [^1]
- $n$. One of the six primary odors in Henning's prism, one of the four in the Crocker-Henderson system, and one of the seven in the stereochemical theory, resembling the odor of lavender or rose petals. US fragrant odor. Also called flowery odor or floral odor.
