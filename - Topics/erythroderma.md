## Source [^1]
- (exfoliative dermatitis) $n$. abnormal reddening, flaking, and thickening of the skin affecting a wide area of the body. Commoner after the age of 50, erythroderma affects men three times as often as women; it may result from pre-existing skin disease, such as eczema or psoriasis, or be caused by drugs or lymphoma. This clinical picture is a form of acute skin failure.
