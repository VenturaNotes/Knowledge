## Source [^1]
- (statement of comprehensive income) A financial statement showing the extent to which shareholders' equity has increased or decreased from all the various gains and losses recognized in the period. It includes profits and losses for the period, together with all other movements on reserves reflecting recognized gains and losses attributable to shareholders. Such a statement has been a required item in the annual accounts of UK companies since 1993.
