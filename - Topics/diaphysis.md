## Source [^1]
- $n$. the body, or shaft, of a long bone, which ossifies from a primary center. It comprises a thick cylinder of compact bone surrounding a large medullary cavity. Compare EPIPHYSIS; PHYSIS. -diaphyseal adj.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. diaphyses) The shaft of a mammalian limb bone, which in immature animals is separated from the ends of the bone (see EPIPHYSIS) by cartilage.
