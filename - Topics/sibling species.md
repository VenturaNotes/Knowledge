## Source [^1]
- (cryptic species) Two or more groups of organisms that cannot be readily distinguished by their appearance or other traditional taxonomic criteria but whose members cannot interbreed successfully. For example, many North American species of cricket, occupying the same geographical range, can be distinguished only by their song.
