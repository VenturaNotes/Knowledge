## Source [^1]
- $n$. In genetics, any generation following a parental generation that has been designated arbitrarily as the first in a hereditary line. By convention, $\mathrm{F}_{1}$ designates the first filial generation, $\mathrm{F}_{2}$ the second filial generation, and so on. \[From Latin filialis pertaining to a son or daughter, from filius a son]
