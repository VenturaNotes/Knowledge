## Source [^1]
- (Sierpinski gasket) A fractal, named after the Polish mathematician Waclaw Sierpinski. It is formed as follows: given an equilateral triangle, remove the central quarter of the triangle, thus forming three smaller equilateral triangles; repeat this for each of these three triangles, and then repeat indefinitely. Its Hausdorff dimension equals $log_23.$
