## Source [^1]
- $n$. a region in the brain that controls the amount of food intake. Appetite suppressants probably decrease hunger by changing the chemical characteristics of this centre.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A mechanism in the hypothalamus that is sensitive to glucose levels in the blood and that regulates eating. It is located in the lateral hypothalamic feeding centre, where stimulation of it increases eating and destruction inhibits eating, and in the ventromedial hypothalamus, where stimulation inhibits eating and destruction increases eating. \[From appetite + Greek statikos causing to stand, from histanai to stand + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
