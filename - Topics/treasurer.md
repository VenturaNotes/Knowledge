## Source [^1]
- (1) A person who is responsible for looking after the money and other assets of an organization. This may include overseeing the provision of the organization's finances as well as some stewardship over the way in which the money is spent. 
- (2) In more recent times, the manager responsible for an organization's relationship with financial markets.
