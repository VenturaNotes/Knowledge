## Source [^1]
- Reduction of the space needed to define an entity using one of the many techniques available. The [[compression factor]] is the ratio of the storage size of an uncompressed representation to that of the compressed representation. See also DATA COMPRESSION, IMAGE COMPRESSION, SPEECH COMPRESSION.
## Source[^2]
