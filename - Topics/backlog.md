## Source [^1]
- An accumulation of unfulfilled orders held by a firm. This may result from an inability to cope with the demand for a product during a particular period or it may be a deliberate policy to even out an irregular demand, or to avoid having to hold excessive stocks or to increase short-term production capacity by paying overtime rates.
