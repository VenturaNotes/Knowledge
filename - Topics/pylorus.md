## Source [^1]
- $n$. the lower end of the stomach, which leads to the duodenum. It terminates at a ring of muscle $\textendash$ the pyloric sphincter $\textendash$ which contracts to close the opening by which the stomach communicates with the duodenum. $\textemdash$pyloric adj.
