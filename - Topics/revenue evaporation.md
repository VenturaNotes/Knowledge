## Source [^1]
- A sudden fall in income from the sale of a product or service, particularly when this is owing to fundamental change in the nature of a market, e.g. as a result of technological innovation. Recent examples of this have occurred in the telecommunications and media industries, where revenue from voice calls and print has declined owing to the advent of Internet-based alternatives.
