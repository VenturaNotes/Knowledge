## Source [^1]
- An increase, reduction, or any other change in the share capital of a company. If permitted by the articles of association, a limited company can increase its capital as appropriate. It can also rearrange its existing capital and cancel unissued shares. The requirement for a company to specify its maximum authorized share capital was removed under the Companies Act 2006. See also REDUCTION OF CAPITAL.
