## Source [^1]
- reflex contraction of the stapedius muscle in the middle ear, attached to the stapes bone, in response to loud sounds. This protects the cochlea from noise damage by limiting the amount of sound that is transmitted via the stapes. The reflex can be measured using a tympanometer.
