## Source [^1]
- The branch of data science applying mathematics to large data sets with the aims of devising strategies for competitive advantage and value creation, particularly in relation to commerce.

## Source[^2]
- Analytics is an umbrella term for various business intelligence (BI) and application-related initiatives within an organization. The term could refer to the process of analyzing data from specific internet domains (website analytics). Or it could be related to the process of analyzing a specific content area, such as sales or marketing, to generate better BI. Analytics can also refer to the use of statistical and mathematical data to cluster or segment data sets or predict future scenarios that might impact an organization.
