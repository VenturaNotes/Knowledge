## Source [^1]
- adj. describing a condition that is recognized at birth or that is believed to have been present since birth. Congenital malformations include all disorders present at birth whether they are inherited or caused by an environmental factor.
## Source[^2]
- Present at birth. Congenital disorders of the body may be due to genetic factors, e.g. Down's syndrome, or caused by injury or environmental factors, e.g. drugs (such as thalidomide), chemicals (such as dioxin), and infections (such as those caused by Listeria and cytomegalovirus).
