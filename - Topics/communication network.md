## Source [^1]
- n. A group of individuals and the communication channels between some or all of the pairs, indicating the routes along which information can flow or messages can be transmitted, the structure of the network often being determined by the organizational structure of the group. See also CENTRALIZED COMMUNICATION NETWORK, DECENTRALIZED COMMUNICATION NETWORK.
