## Source [^1]
- n. (pl. labra) a lip or liplike structure; occurring, for example, around the margins of the articulating socket (acetabulum) of the hip bone.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. labra) The upper lip in the mouthparts of an insect. It is formed from a plate of cuticle hinged to the head above the mouth and is used in feeding.
