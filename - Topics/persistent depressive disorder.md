## Source [^1]
- $n$. A depressive disorder introduced in DSM-5 for a condition characterized by feelings of depression on most days, lasting for at least two years, or at least one year for children and adolescents, accompanied by poor appetite or overeating, insomnia or hypersomnia, fatigue or lack of energy, low self-esteem, poor concentration, or feelings of hopelessness, the symptoms falling short of major depressive disorder. Compare BIPOLAR DISORDERS, CYCLOTHYMIC DISORDER, DYSTHYMIA.
