## Source [^1]
- $n$. the SI unit of absorbed dose of ionizing radiation, being the absorbed dose when the energy per unit mass imparted to matter by ionizing radiation is 1 joule per kilogram. It has replaced the rad. Symbol: Gy.
## Source[^2]
- Symbol Gy. The derived SI unit of absorbed dose of ionizing radiation (see RADIATION UNITS). It is named after the British radiobiologist L. H. Gray (1905-65).
