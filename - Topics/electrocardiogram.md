## Source [^1]
- (ECG) $n$. a tracing of the electrical activity of the heart recorded by electrocardiography (see illustration). It aids in the diagnosis of heart disease, which may produce characteristic changes in the ECG. See QRS Complex; Q-T interval; S-T SEGMENT.
