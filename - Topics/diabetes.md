## Source[^2]
- $n$. Any of several diseases characterized by the excretion of abnormally large quantities of urine. diabetes insipidus $n$. A form of diabetes caused by a disorder of the pituitary gland and malfunction of the kidneys. diabetes mellitus $n$. A form of diabetes caused by a loss of insulin secretion from the islets of Langerhans, leading to a failure of carbohydrate metabolism and excess sugar in the urine.
