## Source [^1]
- adj. describing or relating to sensory nerve fibres responsible for the fine degrees of sensation, as of temperature and touch. Compare PROTOPATHIC.
## Source[^2]
- adj. Of or relating to certain sensory nerve fibres in the skin that are capable of fine discrimination between small degrees of variation. \[From Greek epikritikos decisive, from epi on + krinein to separate or judge + -itikos resembling or marked by]
