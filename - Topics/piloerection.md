## Source [^1]
- $n$. Raising of hair into an upright position as a result of contraction of smooth muscles attached to the base of hair follicles, usually in response to cold or as part of a startle reflex, in humans often accompanied by goose pimples. Also called a pilomotor response. \[From Greek pilos hair + English erection]
