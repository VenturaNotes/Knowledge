## Source [^1]
- Behavior judged to be good, just, right, and honourable, based on principles or guides from a specific ethical theory. However, ethical theories may vary from person to person, country to country, or company to company. Ethical realism accepts that although morality does not apply internationally, the ethical values of a trading partner should be respected.
