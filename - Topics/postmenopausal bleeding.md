## Source [^1]
- (PMB) bleeding from the female genital tract occurring more than 12 months after the last menstrual period. Atrophic vaginitis is a common cause. Endometrial cancer occurs in up to 10% of cases, and PMB may also be a marker of ovarian, cervical, or more rarely vaginal or vulval cancer.
