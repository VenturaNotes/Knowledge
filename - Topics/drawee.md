## Source [^1]
- (1) The person on whom a bill of exchange is drawn (i.e. to whom it is addressed). The drawee will accept it (see ACCEPTANCE) and pay it on maturity. 
- (2) The bank on whom a cheque is drawn, i.e. the bank holding the account of the individual or company that wrote it. 
- (3) The bank named in a bank draft. Compare DRAWER.
