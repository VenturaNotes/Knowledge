## Source [^1]
- Any tree constructed from a full tree of depth $k$ by deleting some of the leaf nodes and the arcs leading to them. In a complete binary tree, the deleted nodes are often constrained to be the rightmost terminal (leaf) nodes
- The term is also sometimes used as a synonym for full tree
