## Source [^1]
- $n$. A person with a particular trait or disorder, serving as the starting point for a study in which the person's blood relatives are investigated in order to obtain information about the genetic transmission of the characteristic in question. Also called an index case. \[From Latin probandus to be proved, from probare to prove]
