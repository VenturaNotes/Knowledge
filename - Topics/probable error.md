## Source [^1]
- $n$. A measure of the degree of dispersion, variability, or scatter in a group of scores, equal to half the interquartile range. It can be calculated by multiplying the standard deviation by 0.6745 . Compare INTERQUARTILE RANGE, RANGE, SEMI-INTERQUARTILE RANGE, VARIANCE. PE abbrev.
