## Source [^1]
- (symbol: n) A prefix to a unit, indicating a submultiple of one billionth, $10^{-9}$, of that unit, as in nanosecond.
## Source[^2]
- combining form denoting
- (1) extremely small size.
- (2) one thousand-millionth part $\left(10^{-9}\right)$.
