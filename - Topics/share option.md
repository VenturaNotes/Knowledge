## Source [^1]
- (1) A benefit sometimes offered to employees, especially new employees, in which they are given an option to buy shares in the company for which they work at a favorable fixed price or at a stated discount to the market price. The difference between the value of the share acquired (or its sale proceeds) and the amount paid to exercise the option is usually subject to income tax. See Enterprise Management INCENTIVES; SAVINGS RELATED SHARE OPTION SCHEME.
