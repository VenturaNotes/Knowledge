## Source [^1]
- (category discounter) An exceptionally aggressive off-price retailer that offers branded merchandise in clearly defined product categories at heavily discounted prices. Their predatory pricing strategy and ability to decimate much of the competition in their sector explains the name. See also BIG-BOX STORE.
