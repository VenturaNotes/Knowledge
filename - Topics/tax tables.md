## Source [^1]
- Tables issued by HM Revenue and Customs to employers to assist them in calculating the tax due from their employees under the pay-as-you-earn system (see PAYE). The tables are provided for either weekly or monthly payments. In practice, employers now calculate salaries, wages, and tax deductions by computer, using a program into which the tax tables are incorporated.
