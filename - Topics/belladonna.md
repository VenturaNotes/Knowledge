## Source [^1]
- $n$. the deadly nightshade plant (Atropa belladonna) or the mixture of alkaloids derived from it, which include atropine and hyoscine.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Either of two alkaloid drugs, atropine or hyoscyamine, extracted from the leaves and roots of the deadly nightshade plant Atropa belladonna. \[From Italian bella donna fair lady, because one of its pharmacological effects is to enlarge the pupils of the eyes, thereby enhancing a woman's sexual attractiveness]
