## Source [^1]
- (1) The law applied by the civil courts in the UK, as opposed to ecclesiastical, criminal, or military law. It is, thus, the law that regulates dealings between private citizens that are not subject to interference by the state. Its chief divisions include the law of contract, torts, and trusts. 
- (2) The law generally in force on the Continent, which has its basis in Roman law.
