## Source [^1]
- A resource for which the stock decreases whenever the resource is being used and does not increase over the timescale relevant for economic decision-making. Examples include deposits of coal, oil, or minerals. The adjustment speed of depletable resources is so slow that they can be modelled as made available once and only once by nature.
