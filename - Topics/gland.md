## Source [^1]
- $n$. an organ or group of cells that is specialized for synthesizing and secreting certain fluids, either for use in the body or for excretion. There are two main groups of glands: the exocrine glands, which discharge their secretions by means of ducts, and the endocrine glands, which secrete their products $\textendash$ hormones $\textendash$ directly into the bloodstream. See also SECRETION.
