## Source [^1]
- (bladder enhancement) a surgical method of increasing the capacity of the bladder to provide a safe, functional, and low-pressure storage reservoir for urine. This is usually achieved by ileocystoplasty or ileocaecocystoplasty (see CYSTOPLASTY).
