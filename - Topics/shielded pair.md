## Source [^1]
- (screened pair) A transmission line that consists of two wires enclosed in a metal sheath. If the two wires are twisted around each other within the conducting sheath they form a shielded twisted pair (STP). The shield provides additional isolation from external sources of electromagnetic interference. See also PAIR.
