## Source [^1]
- $n$. the SI unit of luminous intensity, equal to the intensity in a given direction of a source that emits monochromatic radiation of frequency $540 \times 10^{12} \mathrm{~Hz}$ and has a radiant intensity in that direction of $1 / 683$ watt per steradian. Symbol: cd.
