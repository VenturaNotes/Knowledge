## Source [^1]
- $n$. A depressive disorder introduced in DSM-5 to address concerns about excessive diagnosis of bipolar disorder in children. It can be diagnosed in children between 6 and 18 years of age who have exhibited, for at least 12 months, chronic, severe, persistent irritability and recurrent temper tantrums, verbal rages, or physical aggression directed to people or property at least three times per week on average, and whose mood between outbursts is irritable or angry. Compare BIPOLAR DISORDER, INTERMITTENT EXPLOSIVE DISORDER, OPPOSITIONAL DEFIANT DISORDER.
