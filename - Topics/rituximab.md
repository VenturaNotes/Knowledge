## Source [^1]
- n. a monoclonal antibody that acts against the protein CD20, located mainly on the surface of B lymphocytes; it is used in the treatment of many lymphomas and leukaemias, including non-Hodgkin's lymphoma and chronic lymphocytic leukaemia. It is also licensed for the treatment of severe rheumatoid arthritis and vasculitis. Side-effects include hypersensitivity reactions and immunosuppression.
