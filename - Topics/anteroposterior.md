## Source [^1]
- (AP) adj. from the front to the back. In radiography, AP denotes a radiograph in the coronal plane taken when the X-ray beam is directed from the front to the back of the patient with the X-ray film or detector at the back to capture the image. Compare DECUBITUS; POSTEROANTERIOR.
