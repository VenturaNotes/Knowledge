## Synthesis
- Total amount of a good or service that consumers demand over a given interval of time
## Source [^1]
- A term used in economics to describe the total amount of a good or service that consumers demand over a given interval of time 
## Source[^2]
- The quantity of a good demanded at a given price. The quantity demanded traces out the demand curve as the given price is changed.
