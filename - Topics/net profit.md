## Source [^1]
- (net margin; net profit margin) The gross profit less all the other costs of an organization in addition to those included in the cost of sales. It is shown before and after taxation in the profit and loss account.
## Source[^2]
- The profits of an organization after all expenses have been taken into account. This can be measured either before or after deduction of taxes payable.
