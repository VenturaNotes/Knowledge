## Source [^1]
- $n$. union of the fragments of a fracture in an unsatisfactory position. It occurs if fracture reduction is inadequate or the splintage is inadequate to maintain the bone fragments in the correct position until healing occurs. Malunion may require surgical correction with osteotomy.
