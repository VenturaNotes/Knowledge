## Source [^1]
- (lipoidosis) n. (pl. lipidoses) any disorder of lipid metabolism within the cells of the body. The brain lipidoses (see GAUCHER's DISEASE; Hunter's syndrome; Hurler's syndrome; Tay-Sachs disease) are inborn defects causing the accumulation of lipids within the brain.
