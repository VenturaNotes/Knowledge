## Source [^1]
- $n$. a synthetic corticosteroid used topically to reduce inflammation in such skin conditions as eczema; it may also be used in the short-term treatment of psoriasis. Side-effects include burning, itching, and local eruptions. Administered as an implant into the vitreous humour, it is used to treat oedema associated with diabetic retinopathy.
