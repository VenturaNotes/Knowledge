## Source [^1]
- (1) An agent for buyers and sellers who also trades on his or her own account. 
- (2) A member of the London Stock Exchange who, since the Big Bang, has functioned both as a stockbroker and a jobber, but only in a single capacity in any particular transaction. See MARKET MAKER.
