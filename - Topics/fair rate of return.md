## Source [^1]
- The level of profit that federal and/or state regulators judge is adequate return for a utility. The fair rate of return is set by public utility commissions at a level that allows the utility to provide an acceptable service to its customers, pay a dividend to shareholders, meet the interest payments on its bonds, and undertake maintenance and investment.
