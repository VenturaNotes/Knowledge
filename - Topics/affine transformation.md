## Source [^1]
- A transformation which preserves collinearity and therefore the straightness and parallel nature of lines and the ratios of distances. The affine transformations of $\mathbb{R}^n$ are of the form $v \mapsto Av + b$ where $A$ is invertible and $b \in \mathbb{R}^n$.
