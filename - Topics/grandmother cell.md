## Source [^1]
- $n$. Based on the discovery of feature detectors, a hypothetical cell (improbably exaggerated, but based on reality) in the visual cortex that might respond maximally to a highly specific stimulus such as the viewer's own grandmother. The term was introduced by the US neuroscientist Jerry (Jerome Ysroael) Lettvin (1920-2011) in a lecture to students at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 1969 in which he suggested that about 18,000 neurons could underlie a specific conscious experience.
