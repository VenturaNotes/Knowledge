## Source [^1]
- (1) T/F: Gifts to charities are deductible because they serve the private interests of donors
	- False - Gifts to charities are deductible because they serve the public interest. The fact that a donor's interests are also achieved through a give is not relevant for tax purposes
- (2) T/F: Gifts to friends, family, and the needy are deductible if the gift was motivated by charitable intent
	- False - Gifts are deductible only if given to IRS-approved organizations
