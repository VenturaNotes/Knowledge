## Source [^1]
- (1) A colloquial name for a bucket shop that specializes in selling securities over the telephone. So-called boiler-room scams involve the selling of worthless shares to gullible investors, usually by cold calling. 
- (2) That part of a business or organization in which the real day-today work takes place, unseen by the public (and possibly by higher management).
