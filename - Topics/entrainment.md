## Source [^1]
- The synchronization of an inherent biological rhythm in an organism with an external cycle of events, such as the daily day-night cycle, the ebbing and flowing of tides, or the seasons. See BIOLOGICAL CLOCK.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. The adjustment of a biological clock to synchronize with an external Zeitgeber such as the diurnal cycle of light and dark. entrain $v b$. \[From French entrainement dragging along, from entrainer to drag along, from en- in + trainer to drag]
