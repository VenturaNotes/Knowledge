## Source [^1]
- (TOC) An approach to the planning of production that focuses attention on the capacity constraints or bottlenecks that occur in the process, rather than attempting to maximize loading across the whole system.
	- Examples of TOC solutions include:
		- providing additional training for employees to improve working speeds;
		- replacing an existing bottleneck machine with a faster one;
		- changing the design of a product to reduce manufacturing time.
- See also OPTIMIZED PRODUCTION TECHNOLOGY.
