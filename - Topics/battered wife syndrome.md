## Source [^1]
- $n$. A non-technical term used loosely to denote the physical and psychological consequences of physical abuse, usually of a woman by her husband or partner, sometimes involving post-traumatic stress disorder or prolonged duress stress disorder. See also spouse abuse. Compare BATTERED BABY SYNDROME. BWS abbrev.
