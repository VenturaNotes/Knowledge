## Source [^1]
- n. An instrument for measuring luminous intensity, older types usually involving visual comparisons of the illumination from a specified light source with that from a standard comparison source, and more modern types using photoelectric cells that generate electrical signals in response to light. photometry $n$. The measurement of luminous intensity, luminous flux, and related phenomena, or the branch of physics concerned with these phenomena. \[From Greek phos, photos light + metron a measure]
