## Source [^1]
- (pharyngeal reflex) a normal reflex action caused by contraction of pharynx muscles when the soft palate or posterior pharynx is touched. The reflex is used to test the integrity of the glossopharyngeal and vagus nerves.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A reflex contraction of muscles in the pharynx and retraction of the tongue in response to stroking of the velar or pharyngeal surfaces. Its absence is a sign of a lesion in the vagus nerve or the glossopharyngeal nerve. Also called the pharyngeal reflex. \[From gag to retch]
