## Source [^1]
- A single set of accounting records containing both the financial accounts and the cost accounts of an organization in an integrated form (see FINANCIAL ACCOUNTING; MANAGEMENT ACCOUNTING). This avoids the necessity of reconciling separate financial and cost books and at the same time ensures that both records are based on the same data.
