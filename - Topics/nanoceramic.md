## Source [^1]
- A nanostructure made from ceramic materials. Owing to their tiny size, nanoceramics can have a number of unusual electrical and magnetic properties, such as ferromagnetism, piezoelectricity, and magnetoresistance. Possible applications include energy storage and supply, and transfer.
