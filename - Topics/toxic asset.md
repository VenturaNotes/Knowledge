## Source [^1]
- An asset that has no market value since there is no longer any demand for it on the secondary market. This exposes the holder to losses. The term was widely applied to mortgage-backed securities and collateralized debt obligations during the 2008-9 financial crisis. See also TOXIC DEBT.
