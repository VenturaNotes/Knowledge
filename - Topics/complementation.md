## Source [^1]
- $n$. In genetics, the combination of a pair of homologous chromosomes, with a recessive mutant gene in one and a normal dominant allele at the same locus in the other, resulting in a normal phenotype, the deficiency being corrected by the normal allele of the other chromosome.
