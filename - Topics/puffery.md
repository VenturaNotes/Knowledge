## Source [^1]
- The practice of exaggerating a product's good points in advertising or other promotional media. Flamboyant claims of this kind that are not likely to be taken literally by consumers are considered part of the normal rhetoric of advertising: as such, they are not prohibited by the Fair Trading Act.
