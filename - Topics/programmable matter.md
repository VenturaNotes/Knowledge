## Source [^1]
- Matter that is capable of altering its physical properties by changing the configuration of its basic parts under either external or internal program control. Currently, this is only achievable with small systems of small modular robots. The future vision is of bulk materials with computing nanostructures that can alter properties such as transparency, reflectivity, or thermal conductivity on command. See also CLAYTRONICS; MEMS; METAMATERIALS.
