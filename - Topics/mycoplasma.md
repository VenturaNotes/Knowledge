## Source [^1]
- $n$. one of a group of minute nonmotile bacteria that lack a rigid cell wall and hence display a variety of forms. The group includes some species that cause severe respiratory disease in cattle, sheep, and goats; one of these, Mycoplasma pneumoniae, causes atypical pneumonia in humans.
