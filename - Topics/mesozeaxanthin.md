## Source [^1]
- $n$. a yellow pigment in the macula at the back of the eye, where the vision is sharpest. The macula also contains the dietary carotenoids lutein, from which mesozeaxanthin is derived, and zeaxanthin, and all three pigments are thought to protect the eye from the aging process.
