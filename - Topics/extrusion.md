## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in dentistry) 
- (1) the forced eruption of a tooth either by means of an orthodontic appliance or surgically; for example, to realign a tooth that has failed to erupt or has been accidentally forced into the jaw. 
- (2) the partial displacement of a tooth from its socket as a result of traumatic injury.
