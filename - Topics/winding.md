## Source [^1]
- A complete group of insulated conductors in an electrical machine, transformer, or other equipment that is designed either to produce a magnetic field or to be acted upon by a magnetic field. It may consist of a number of separate suitably shaped conductors, electrically connected, or a single conductor shaped to form a number of loops or turns.
