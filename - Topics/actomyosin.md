## Source [^1]
- The complex formed from the interaction of the proteins actin and myosin during the process of muscle contraction. See also Sliding FilAment THEORY.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A protein synthesized from actin and myosin in skeletal muscles that, when stimulated, shrinks and causes muscle contraction, the energy being supplied by ATP.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. a protein complex formed in muscle between actin and myosin during the process of contraction. See STRIATED MUSCLE.
