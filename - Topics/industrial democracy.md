## Source [^1]
- Any system in which workers participate in the management of an organization or in sharing its profits. Participative decision making can improve industrial relations and add to workers' job satisfaction and motivation. Various schemes have been tried, including worker directors on the boards of some nationalized industries, workers' councils, and profit-sharing schemes.
