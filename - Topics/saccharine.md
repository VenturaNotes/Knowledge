## Source [^1]
- $n$. a sweetening agent (E954). Saccharine is 300 times as sweet as sugar and has no energy content. It is very useful as a sweetener in diabetic and low-calorie foods. Saccharine is stable at high temperatures and therefore can be used in cooking.
