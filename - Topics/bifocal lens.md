## Source [^1]
- a lens with two principal focal lengths: usually the upper part of the lens gives a sharp image of distant objects and the lower part gives a sharp image of near objects. Examples are bifocal spectacles, bifocal contact lenses, and bifocal intraocular lenses. See also TRIFOCAL LENSES; MULTIFOCAL LENSES.
