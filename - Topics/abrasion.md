## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) a graze: a minor wound in which the surface of the skin or a mucous membrane is worn away by rubbing or scraping. 
- (2) the non-carious loss of hard tooth substances caused by external mechanical factors (i.e. not by chewing or tooth-to-tooth contact). It is commonly associated with an aggressive toothbrushing technique and is enhanced by erosion. 
- (3) any rubbing or scraping action that produces surface wear.
