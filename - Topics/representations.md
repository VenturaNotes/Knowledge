## Source [^1]
- (1) Information given by a person wishing to arrange insurance about the nature of the risk that is to be covered. If it contains a material fact it must be true to the best knowledge and belief of the person wishing to be insured. See UTMOST GOOD FAITH. 
- (2) Information given by one party in furtherance of a deal.
