## Source [^1]
- $n$. The omission of a speech sound that would normally be present, especially in allegro speech, as when the second /t/ sound in the phrase next time is omitted, or when terrific is pronounced as a two-syllable rather than a three-syllable word. Compare haplology. \[From Latin elidere to knock + -ion indicating an action, process, or state]
