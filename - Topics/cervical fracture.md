## Source [^1]
- a fracture of a vertebra in the neck (see CERVICAL VERTEBRAE). Cervical fractures range from minor, requiring no treatment, to those associated with paralysis and instant death. Treatment can be support with a collar, skull traction, an orthosis attached to the skull, or surgery, depending on the severity of the fracture.
