## Source [^1]
- $n$. the distinctive pattern of minute ridges in the outer horny layer of the skin. Every individual has a unique pattern of loops ($70 \%$), whorls (25%), or arches (5%) (see illustration). Fingerprint patterns can show the presence of inherited disorders. See also DERMATOGLYPHICS.
