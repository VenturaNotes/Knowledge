## Source [^1]
- A website that seeks to act as an attractive user-friendly access point to the Internet. Portals typically provide services (email, instant messaging, games, online shopping, etc.), content (news, listings of events, etc.), facilities for online shopping, and a search engine to search the rest of the Web.
## Source[^2]
- A portal is a high-traffic website with a wide range of content, services and vendor links that is client-friendly. It provides an easy route to information and additional links that clients need 24/7. Portals allow clients to easily access sensitive information while keeping security high through client-specific logins.
