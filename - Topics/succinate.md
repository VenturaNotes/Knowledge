## Source [^1]
- A salt of succinic (butanedioic) acid, $\mathrm{HOOC}\left(\mathrm{CH}_{2}\right)_{2} \mathrm{COOH}$, a four-carbon fatty acid. Succinate occurs in living organisms as an intermediate in metabolism, especially in the Krebs cycle.
