## Source [^1]
- $n$. a device for recording the force of a muscular contraction. A small hand-held dynamometer may be used to record the strength of a patient's grip. A special optical dynamometer measures the action of the muscles controlling the shape of the lens of the eye.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Any of a number of different devices for measuring muscular strength or effort. dynamometry $n$. The measurement of power or force. \[From Greek dynamis power + metron measure]
