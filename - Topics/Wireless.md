## Source [^1]
- Usually refers to radio connection between devices. See also Bluetooth, IRDA, WiFi, wireless LAN.
## Source[^2]
- (1) A means of electrical communications between systems or parts of a system by means of radio, infrared, or acoustic waves, without using connecting wiring. Examples include personal wireless communications by mobile phone (see CELLULAR COMMUNICATIONS) and wireless local area networks (WLANs) in which infrared or microwaves are used as a carrier between the network outlet and end-user device. 
- (2) A radio receiver.
