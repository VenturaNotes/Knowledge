## Source [^1]
- (bill of materials) A document drawn up by a quantity surveyor showing in detail the materials and parts required to build a structure (e.g. factory, house, office block), together with the price of each component and the labor costs. The bill of quantities is one of the tender documents that goes out to contractors who wish to quote for carrying out the work.
