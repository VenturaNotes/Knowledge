## Source [^1]
- combining form denoting 
- (1) extremity; tip. Example: acrohypothermy (abnormal coldness of the extremities (hands and feet). 
- (2) height; promontory. Example: acrophobia (morbid dread of heights). 
- (3) extreme; intense. Example: acromania (an extreme degree of mania).
