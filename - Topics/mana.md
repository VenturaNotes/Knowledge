## Source [^1]
- $n$. A Melanesian and Polynesian word (also found in Maori), imported into analytical psychology by Carl Gustav Jung (1875-1961), denoting a supernatural life force, normally originating from the head or the spirit world, that can be concentrated in other people or objects and inherited and transmitted between people, and that confers high social status and ritual power.
