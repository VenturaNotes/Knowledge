## Source [^1]
- (1) An option by a seller to sell double the quantity of securities for which he or she has sold an option, if it is so desired. In some markets this is called a put-of-more option. 
- (2) An option by a buyer to buy double the quantity of securities for which he or she has bought an option, if desired. In some markets this is called a call-of-more option.
