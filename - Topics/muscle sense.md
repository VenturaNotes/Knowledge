## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of proprioception and kinaesthesis mediated by sensory receptors in the muscles and stimulated by contraction. The term is also used non-technically to denote proprioception or kinaesthesis in general, but this is avoided in careful usage. See also Golgi TENDON ORGAN, MUSCLE SPINDLE.
