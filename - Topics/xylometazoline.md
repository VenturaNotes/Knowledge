## Source [^1]
- $n$. a sympathomimetic drug that constricts blood vessels (see VASOCONSTRICTOR). It is applied topically as a nasal decongestant for the relief of the common cold and sinusitis. Toxic effects are rare, but the congestion may worsen transiently when treatment stops.
