## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of social influence in which a person yields to group pressure in the absence of any explicit order or request from another person to comply, as in the Asch experiment. See also anticonformity, law of SOCIAL IMPACT. Compare COMPLIANCE, OBEDIENCE.
