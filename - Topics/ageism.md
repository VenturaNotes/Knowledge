## Source [^1]
- $n$. The belief in the intrinsic superiority of people within a certain age range, often accompanied by prejudice, stereotyping, and discrimination on the basis of age, usually against old people. Also spelt agism , especially in the US. Compare ableism, ethnocentrisM, fattism, HETEROSEXISM, RACISM, SEXISM, SPECIESISM. 
- ageist or agist $n$. 
	- (1) One who practises or advocates ageism. adj. 
	- (2) Of or relating to ageism. \[Coined in 1969 by the US gerontologist Robert N(eil) Butler (1927-2010) from age on the model of racism]
