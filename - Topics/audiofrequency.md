## Source [^1]
- (AF) Any frequency to which a normal human ear responds. The audible range in practice extends from about 20 to $20~000~\mathrm{Hz}$. Intelligible speech can be obtained in a communication system if a frequency range from about 300 to $3400~\mathrm{Hz}$ is reproduced. Any electronic device, such as an amplifier, choke, or transformer, that operates in this range is known as an audiofrequency device or audio device; similarly any effect, such as distortion, that involves audiofrequencies is termed an audiofrequency effect or audio effect.
