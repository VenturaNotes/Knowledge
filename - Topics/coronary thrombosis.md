## Source [^1]
- the formation of a blood clot (thrombus) in the coronary artery, which obstructs the flow of blood to the heart. This is usually due to underlying atheroma of the arterial wall, and results in the death (infarction) of part of the heart muscle. For symptoms and treatment, see MYOCARDIAL INFARCTION.
