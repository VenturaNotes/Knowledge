## Source [^1]
- $n$. The three-stage biological response of an organism to stress (1) according to the Austrian-born Canadian physician and endocrinologist Hans Selye (1907-82), comprising an alarm reaction, a resistance stage, and an exhaustion stage. Also called the adaptation syndrome. GAS abbrev.
