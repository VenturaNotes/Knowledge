## Source [^1]
- $n$. The basic unit or quantum of light or other electromagnetic radiation, considered as a particle with zero rest mass and energy equal to $h f$, where $h$ is Planck's constant ( $6.6262 \times 10^{-34}$ Joule seconds) and $f$ is the frequency (1) of the radiation in hertz. \[From Greek phos, photos light + -on by analogy with electron]
