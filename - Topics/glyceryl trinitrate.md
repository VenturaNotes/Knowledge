## Source [^1]
- (nitroglycerin) a drug that dilates blood vessels and is used mainly to prevent and treat angina (see vasODILATOR). Side effects may include flushing, headache, and fainting. Glyceryl trinitrate is also applied topically in the treatment of anal fissures.
