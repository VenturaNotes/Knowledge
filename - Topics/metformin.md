## Source [^1]
- $n$. a biguanide drug that is used to treat type 2 diabetes. It may cause loss of appetite and minor digestive upsets; it should not be used in patients with kidney disease, in whom it may cause lactic acidosis. See also ORAL HYPOGLYCAEMIC DRUG.
