## Source [^1]
- a device, resembling a small hearing aid, that produces sounds of many frequencies at equal intensities and is used in the treatment of tinnitus. Also known as a broad-band sound generator, earlevel sound generator, noiser, or wide-band sound generator, it was formerly known as a tinnitus masker.
