## Source [^1]
- The practice of short-selling a stock without borrowing the shares or ensuring that the shares can be borrowed. It is illegal in the US and prohibited by exchanges in several other countries. When the seller does not obtain the shares within the required time frame, the result is known as a 'fail to deliver'.
