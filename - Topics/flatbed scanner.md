## Source [^1]
- A scanner in which the sheet to be scanned is placed flat onto the bed of the device. Such scanners may handle a single sheet at a time or may be provided with a document feeder.
## Source[^2]
- An image scanner in which the object to be scanned is placed on a flat sheet of glass. The image is usually covered on top, while the imaging device scans the object from beneath the glass.
