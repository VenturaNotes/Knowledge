## Source [^1]
- (VMS) A distribution channel structure in which producers, wholesalers, and retailers act as a unified system. One channel member owns the others, has contracts with them, or has so much power that they all have to cooperate. See administered VMS; CONTRACTUAL VMS; CORPORATE VMS.
