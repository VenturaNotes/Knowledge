## Source [^1]
- A blue copper-containing protein that is found in chloroplasts and acts as an electron carrier molecule in the light-dependent reactions of photosynthesis (see PHOTOPHOSPHORYLATION). Plastocyanin consists of amino acid groups in association with a copper molecule which gives this compound a blue color.
