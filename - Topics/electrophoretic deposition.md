## Source [^1]
- A technique for coating a material making it an electrode in a bath containing a colloidal suspension of charged particles. Under suitable conditions, the particles are attracted to, and deposited on, the electrode. Electrophoretic deposition is used extensively in industry; for example, in applying paint to metal components.
