## Source [^1]
- A hole created in the valence band of a semiconductor when an electron is thermally excited into the conduction band. Relaxation of a conduction electron into an unoccupied energy level in the valence band results in band-to-band recombination (see RECOMBINATION PROCESSES). See also SEMICONDUCTOR.
