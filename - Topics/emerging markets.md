## Source [^1]
- Economies progressing towards the advanced stage of economic development. These are mainly newly industrialized countries, such as Taiwan or Brazil, or recently liberalized economies, such as Hungary or Poland. A common feature of the emerging market economies is a higher degree of economic, financial, or political uncertainty, in comparison to the developed countries.
