## Source [^1]
- (MCS) a disorder of consciousness distinct from persistent vegetative state (PVS) and locked-in syndrome (see VEGETATIVE STATE). Unlike PVS, patients with MCS have partial preservation of conscious awareness although the level of awareness frequently fluctuates over time.
