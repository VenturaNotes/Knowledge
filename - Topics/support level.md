## Source [^1]
- In technical analysis, a floor level of prices for a security or commodity at which the price tends to stop falling and begin to rebound. A fall below this level is regarded as marking a significant shift to lower prices. Compare RESISTANCE LEVEL. See also DOUBLE BOTTOM.
