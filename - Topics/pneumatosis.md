## Source [^1]
- $n$. the occurrence of gas cysts in abnormal sites in the body. Pneumatosis cystoides intestinalis is the occurrence of multiple gascontaining cysts in the intestinal wall. Its cause is unknown; it can be treated by hyperbaric oxygenation.
