## Source [^1]
- (1) To make a device inactive. 
- (2) To suppress the action of an interrupt.
## Source[^2]
- (1) To place the output(s) of a device in the disconnected or high-impedance state. 
- (2) An input to a tristate logic device or integrated-circuit chip that disables the device.
