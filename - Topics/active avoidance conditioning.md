## Source [^1]
- $n$. Learning to respond in a way that results in the avoidance of a punishing or aversive stimulus, as when a rat is trained to jump over a barrier whenever a light comes on in order to avoid electric shock that follows shortly after. Also called active avoidance learning . Compare ESCAPE CONDITIONING, PASSIVE AVOIDANCE CONDITIONING. See also AVOIDANCE CONDITIONING.
