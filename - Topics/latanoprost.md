## Source [^1]
- $n$. a prostaglandin drug (an analogue of $\mathrm{PGF}_{2}$ ) that both increases the drainage of aqueous humor from the eye and reduces the production of aqueous humor. It is used as a treatment for open-angle glaucoma.
