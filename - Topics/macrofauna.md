## Source [^1]
- The larger animals, collectively, which can be observed without the aid of a microscope (compare MICROFAUNA). The macrofauna sometimes includes small soil-dwelling invertebrates, such as annelids and nematodes, but these may be separated into an intermediate category, the mesofauna. Compare MEIOFAUNA.
