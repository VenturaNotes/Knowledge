## Source [^1]
- $n$. the formation of new blood vessels, which occurs during wound healing and is promoted by growth factors (see VASCULAR ENDOTHELIAL GROWTH FACTOR). This process is also seen in many malignant tumours and has become a target for anticancer therapy (see ANGIOGENESIS INHIBITOR).
