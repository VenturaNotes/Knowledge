## Source [^1]
- Trademark An alloy of approximately 62% nickel, 15% chromium, and 23% iron. It has a very high resistivity and can operate at extremely high temperatures; it is therefore suitable for use in making thin-film resistors, wire-wound resistors, and heating elements.
