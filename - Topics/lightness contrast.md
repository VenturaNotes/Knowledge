## Source [^1]
- $n$. The apparent darkening of an object or image when viewed against, alongside, or immediately after a lighter one, or an apparent lightening of an object or image juxtaposed with a darker one. Also called brightness contrast. See also contrast (2), simultaneous contrast, SUCCESSIVE CONTRAST, VISUAL INDUCTION.
