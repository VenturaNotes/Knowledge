## Source [^1]
- Originally denoting an advertisement displayed above the fold in printed media; in computer terms it indicates that a banner advertisement or other content on a web page is displayed without the need to scroll. This is likely to give higher clickthrough, but note that the location of the 'fold' within the web browser is dependent on the screen resolution of a user's own computer.
