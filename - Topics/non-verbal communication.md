## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any form of communication apart from language (1), including paralanguage (non-verbal aspects of speech), facial expression, communicative gaze and eye contact, kinesics (gestures and other communicative bodily movements), and proxemics (communicative use of personal space). See also Clever Hans, equilibrium HYPOTHESIS, EXPRESSIVE BEHAVIOR, SEMIOTICS, SOCIAL SKILLS TRAINING. NVC abbrev.
