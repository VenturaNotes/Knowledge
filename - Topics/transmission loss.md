## Source [^1]
- The reduction in power between any two points 1 and 2 in a telecommunication system given by the ratio $P_{2} / P_{1}$, where $P_{1}$ and $P_{2}$ are the powers at points 1 and 2 respectively and 2 is further away from the source of power than 1. For a given transmission path at a designated frequency the transmission loss is given by the ratio of the available power at the input of a receiver to the power transmitted from the output of the transmitter.
- In any system where gain is introduced to the signal, the transmission gain is given by the ratio of the powers $P_{2} / P_{1}$. This represents the increase in power between the points 1 and 2 where 2 is again further from the source of power than 1.
