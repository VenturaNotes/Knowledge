## Source [^1]
- Unified Communications as a Service (UCaaS) is a cloud delivery model that comprises six communication functions:
	- Enterprise telephony 
	- Meetings via audio, video, and web conferencing
	- Unified messaging 
	- Instant messaging
	- Mobility 
	- Communications-enabled business processes
- A service provider operates and maintains the above functions on behalf of an organization, freeing up labor and resources. The provider delivers services for a monthly subscription fee and manages communication infrastructure such as racks, data centers, and audio-visual software.
