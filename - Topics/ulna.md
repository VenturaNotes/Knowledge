## Source [^1]
- $n$. the inner and longer bone of the forearm (see illustration). It articulates with the humerus and radius above and with the radius and indirectly with the wrist bones below. At its upper end is the olecranon process and coronoid process; at the lower end is a cone-shaped styloid process. $\textemdash$ulnar adj.
