## Source [^1]
- A complete assemblage of plant, equipment, and the necessary buildings at a place where electrical energy is received and where it may be either converted from alternating current to direct current, stepped up or stepped down by means of transformers, or used for control purposes. The substation usually receives power from one or more power stations.
