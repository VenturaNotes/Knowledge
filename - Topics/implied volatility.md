## Source [^1]
- The volatility of the underlying security that is implied by the market price of an option contract. The market price for the option is observed and an option pricing formula (for example, the formula derived from solving the Black-Scholes equation) is used to determine the volatility of the underlying security that is consistent with the price within the model used.
