## Source [^1]
- Removal of those parts of a scene that are obscured by objects nearer to the viewer. There is a whole range of methods for doing this operation. The Z-buffer is often used to hold depth information concerning objects. See HIDDEN-LINE REMOVAL, HIDDEN-SURFACE REMOVAL.
