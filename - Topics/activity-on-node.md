## Source [^1]
- (AON) One of two alternative conventions for the visual representation of network diagrams (see NETWORK MODELLING). In AON the activities, which make up the network, are located on nodes, with the lines in between simply indicating the logical sequence. Compare ACTIVITY-ON-ARROW.
