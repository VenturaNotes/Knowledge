## Source [^1]
- (uvulopharyngopalatoplasty, UPPP, uvulovelopharyngoplasty, UVPP) n. a surgical operation to remove the uvula, part of the soft palate, and the tonsils in the treatment of snoring and obstructive sleep apnoea.
