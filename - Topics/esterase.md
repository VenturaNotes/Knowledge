## Source [^1]
- $n$. an enzyme that catalyses the hydrolysis of esters into their constituent acids and alcohols. For example, fatty-acid esters are broken down to form fatty acids plus alcohol.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Any enzyme that hydrolyses an ester into an alcohol and an acid. \[From ester + -ase denoting an enzyme, from diastasis separation]
