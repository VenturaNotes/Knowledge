## Source [^1]
- $n$. Merging of slightly different images from the two eyes, arising from binocular disparity, into a single stereoscopic perception. Also called simply fusion or binocular interaction . See also corresponding retinal points, horopter, Panum's fusion area, Panum's limiting case. Compare BINOCULAR RIVALRY.
