## Source [^1]
- (osmic acid) a colorless or faintly yellowish compound used to stain fats or as a fixative in the preparation of tissues for microscopical study. Osmium tetroxide evaporates readily, the vapour having a toxic action on the eyes, skin, and respiratory tract.
## Source[^2]
- (osmium(IV) oxide) A yellow solid, $\mathrm{OsO}_{4}$, made by heating osmium in air. It is used as a fixative in electron microscopy.
