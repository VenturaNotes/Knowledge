## Source [^1]
- an assessment tool for newborn babies, consisting of a chart in which a combination of vital signs, neurological involvement, observation of blue skin and lips, and level of behavioral response indicate the appropriate level of concern. It is colour-coordinated from red to green to enable standardized escalation of concerns about the sick baby.
