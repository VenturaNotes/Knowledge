## Source [^1]
- a fixed distressing belief that one's body or part of one's body is deformed, or an irrational fear that it might become so. Previously known as dysmorphophobia, it is usually considered to be a phobic rather than a delusional disorder. Treatment consists of cognitive behavioural therapy and occasionally SSRIs.
