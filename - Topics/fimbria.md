## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. fimbriae) a fringe or fringelike process, such as any of the finger-like projections that surround the opening of the ovarian end of the Fallopian tube. $\textemdash$fimbrial adj.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. fimbriae) 
- (1) (in anatomy) Any fringelike structure, such as the circle of fingerlike extensions surrounding the entrance to a fallopian tube. 
- (2) (in microbiology) Another name for a short pilus (sense 1).
