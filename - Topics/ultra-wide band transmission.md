## Source [^1]
- (UWB) Any technique in which the signal consists of very short pulses, so that the spectrum of the signal is a significant proportion of the carrier frequency. UWB systems are being proposed for the next generation of wireless local area networks. They have the ability to transmit very high data bit rates without interfering with existing narrow-band services, by spreading the transmit power over a very wide frequency band.
