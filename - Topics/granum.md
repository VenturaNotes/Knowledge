## Source [^1]
- (pl. grana) A stack of platelike bodies (thylakoids), many of which are found in plant chloroplasts (each chloroplast contains about 50 grana). Grana bear the light-receptive pigment chlorophyll and contain the enzymes responsible for the light-dependent reactions of photosynthesis.
