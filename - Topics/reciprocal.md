## Source [^1]
- The multiplicative inverse of a quantity may, when the operation of multiplication is commutative, be called its reciprocal. Thus, the reciprocal of 2 is $\frac 12$, the reciprocal of $3x+4$ is $1/(3x+4)$, and the reciprocal of $1/(3x+4)$ is $3x+4$.
