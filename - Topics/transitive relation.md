## Source [^1]
- A relation $R$ defined on a set $S$ and having the property that, for all $x, y$, and $z$ in $S$, $$\begin{align}&\text{whenever }xRy \text{ and }yRz \\ &\text{then }xRz\end{align}$$The relations 'is less than' defined on integers, and 'is subset of' defined on sets are transitive.
