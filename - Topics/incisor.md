## Source [^1]
- $n$. any of the four front teeth in each jaw, two on each side of the midline. They are normally spade-shaped and narrow-edged for cutting. See also DENTITION.
## Source[^2]
- A sharp flattened chisel-shaped tooth in mammals that is adapted for biting food and$\textemdash$in rodents$\textemdash$for gnawing. In humans there are normally two pairs of incisors (central and lateral) in each jaw. See PERMANENT TEETH.
