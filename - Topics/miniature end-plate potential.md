## Source [^1]
- $n$. A small variation in the electrical potential across the membrane of a resting muscle cell, below the threshold required to trigger a contraction of the muscle fiber, caused by the release of a small quantity of acetylcholine by a motor neuron at the motor end plate. Compare end-plate potential. MEPP abbrev.
