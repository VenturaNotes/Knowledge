## Source [^1]
- $n$. an area of hard thickened skin on or between the toes or elsewhere on the foot: a type of callosity produced by ill-fitting shoes. The horny skin layers form an inverted pyramid that presses down into the deeper skin layers, causing pain. A corn may be treated by applying salicylic acid or by podiatry.
