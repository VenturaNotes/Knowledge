## Source [^1]
- $n$. any one of several enzymes in the intestine that cause the breakdown of a peptide, removing an amino acid.
## Source[^2]
- Any enzyme that cleaves amino acids from the N-terminus of peptides or polypeptides. For example, membrane-bound aminopeptidases in the small intestine break down peptides and dipeptides into amino acids.
