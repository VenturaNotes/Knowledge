## Source [^1]
- A figure of merit that indicates how closely a measured impedance matches a standard impedance, both in magnitude and phase angle. For a perfect match the return loss would be infinite, indicating that all the incident power had been absorbed in the impedance with none of the energy being reflected; if the match was not perfect, some of the energy would be reflected.
