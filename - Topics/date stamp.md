## Source [^1]
- A date stamped on the packaging of prepacked perishable food to indicate either the date by which it must be sold by a retailer (sell-by date) or the date by which it must be consumed by the consumer (consume-by date). This is a legal requirement for prepacked perishable food sold in the UK.
