## Source [^1]
- (lipstick indicator) The tendency for sales of lipstick and similar items to rise sharply during or immediately before a recession. In times of low consumer confidence, it is said, women will increase their spending on small indulgences, such as lipstick, as a substitute for major purchases, such as designer dresses or handbags.
