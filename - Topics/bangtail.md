## Source [^1]
- A type of envelope usually having an attached perforated 'tail', which can be used as a response note or order form. Known as a two-in-one type of letter, it is normally used as part of one-piece mailer or self-mailer in direct marketing. This arrangement reduces the costs of direct-mail selling.
