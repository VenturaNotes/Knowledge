## Source [^1]
- A test applied as a condition of receipt of some benefit, for example a pension. This may be applied to an individual, a family, or a household, and may take account of income, assets, or both.
## Source[^2]
- Any assessment of the income and capital of a person or family to determine their eligibility for benefits provided by the state or a charity.
