## Source [^1]
- $n$. a streptococcal infection of the skin, especially the face, characterized by redness and swelling; it usually has a sharply defined margin, which may differentiate erysipelas from the otherwise similar cellulitis. The patient is ill, with a high temperature. Phenoxymethylpenicillin (penicillin V) is the treatment of choice.
