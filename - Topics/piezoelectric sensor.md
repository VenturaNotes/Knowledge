## Source [^1]
- A sensor that uses the piezoelectric effect to measure force or strain. Such sensors undergo very little deflection themselves and are linear over a wide range of forces. Piezoelectric sensors are robust, reliable, and very widely used. See also STRAIN GAUGE.
