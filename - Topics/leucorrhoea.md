## Source [^1]
- $n$. a whitish or yellowish discharge of mucus from the vaginal opening. It may occur normally, the quantity usually increasing before and after menstruation. An abnormally large discharge may indicate infection of the lower reproductive tract, e.g. vaginitis, Chlamydia, or bacterial vaginosis.
