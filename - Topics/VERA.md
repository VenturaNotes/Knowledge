## Source [^1]
- a framework designed to improve communication between healthcare professionals and patients with dementia. It consists of four stages $\textendash$ validation, emotion, reassurance, and activity $\textendash$ which enable staff to acknowledge their patients' perception of a situation, even where this is at variance with reality.
