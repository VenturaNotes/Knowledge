## Source[^2]
- $n$. Compulsive overeating; another name for bulimia (1), but usually reserved for cases in which it is caused by a lesion in the medial forebrain bundle, the hypothalamus, or some other part of the brain involved in appetite. Compare hyperorexia. \[From Greek hyper over + phagein to consume + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
