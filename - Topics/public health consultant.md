## Source [^1]
- (in Britain) a medical consultant with postgraduate training in public health. Formerly known as community physicians, such consultants undertake public health functions, either as Directors of Public Health in local authorities or as consultants in public health in local authorities, Public Health England, or elsewhere. See also PUBLIC HEALTH SPECIALIST.
