## Source [^1]
- $n$. Exceptional episodic memory. People with this trait spend much of their time thinking about past events in their own lives and have an extraordinary capacity to recall them. The condition was first identified in a case of a 41-year old Jewish woman and reported in an article in the journal Neurocase in 2006, where it was originally called hyperthymestic syndrome. See also eidetic image. Compare mNemonist. \[From Greek hyper over + thymesia remembering, from thymos spirit or mind + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
