## Source [^1]
- adj. relating to or situated at or close to the front of the body or to the anterior part of an organ.
## Source[^2]
- Describing the surface of a plant or animal that is nearest or next to the ground or other support, i.e. the lower surface. In bipedal animals, such as humans, it is the forwarddirected (anterior) surface. Compare DORSAL.
