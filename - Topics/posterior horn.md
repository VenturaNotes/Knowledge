## Source [^1]
- $n$. Either of the two dorsal ends of the H-shaped column of grey matter running throughout the length of the spinal cord and receiving inputs from most of the afferent nerves. It includes the substantia gelatinosa. See also funiculus. Compare ANTERIOR HORN.
