## Source [^1]
- The parts of speech are the categories to which different words are assigned, based on their meaning, structure, and function in a sentence
- The seven main parts of speech:
	- Nouns
	- Pronouns
	- Verbs
	- Adjectives
	- Adverbs
	- Prepositions
	- Conjunctions
- Other categories of words
	- Particles
	- Determiners
	- Gerunds
