## Source [^1]
- $n$. a benzodiazepine used to relieve moderate or severe anxiety and tension, including that associated with insomnia. It is also used for premedication and for treating status epilepticus. It may cause drowsiness, dizziness, blurred vision, and nausea.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A benzodiazepine drug used as an anxiolytic and sedative. Also called Ativan (trademark).
