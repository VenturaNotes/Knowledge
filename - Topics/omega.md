## Source [^1]
- The last Greek letter, 'long' o. Its lower case symbol $\omega$, is often used to represent angular velocity, angular frequency, and the smallest infinite ordinal number, i.e. that of the natural numbers. Its upper case symbol $\Omega$ often denotes sample space or solid angle
