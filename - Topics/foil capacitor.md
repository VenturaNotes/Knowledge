## Source [^1]
- A capacitor in which the electrodes are metal foil. The term is most commonly applied to paper capacitors but some polystyrene or polyester film capacitors use foil electrodes and one form of tantalum electrolytic capacitor uses a tantalum foil as one of the electrodes.
