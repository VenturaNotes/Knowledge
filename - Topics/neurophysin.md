## Source [^1]
- Either of two cysteine-rich carrier proteins that bind to oxytocin (neurophysin I) and antidiuretic hormone (neurophysin II) in the neurohypophysis of the pituitary and are secreted with them. They have no hormonal activity and are cleaved from their respective hormones following secretion.
