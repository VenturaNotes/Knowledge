## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug that causes constriction of blood vessels and widening of the bronchial passages (see SYMPATHOMIMETIC). It is used mainly as a nasal decongestant but also to reverse hypotension following epidural or spinal anaesthesia. It may cause nausea, insomnia, headache, and dizziness.
