## Source [^1]
- In UK public companies, a committee of non-executive directors who decide the pay of executive directors. Many such bodies were set up as a result of the Greenbury Report of 1995. Under the Corporate Governance Code they are now recommended for all public listed companies. See also AUDIT COMMITTEE.
