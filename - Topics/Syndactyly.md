## Source [^1]
- The medical term for two or more fingers or toes that are fused together or "webbed" is syndactyly (sin-dak-tuh-lee)
- Affects boys more often than girls
- Affects both hands about 50% of the time
- Can sometimes be seen prior to birth by [[ultrasound]]
## Source[^2]
- A congenital condition where two or more digits (fingers or toes) are fused together
## Source[^3]
- $n$. congenital webbing of the fingers. Adjacent fingers are joined along part or all of their length. They may be joined only by skin, or the bones of the fingers may be joined. Treatment is surgical separation of the fingers, and skin grafts may be required.
