## Source [^1]
- The medical term for two or more fingers or toes that are fused together or "webbed" is syndactyly (sin-dak-tuh-lee)
- Affects boys more often than girls
- Affects both hands about 50% of the time
- Can sometimes be seen prior to birth by [[ultrasound]]
