## Source [^1]
- $n$. A bitter-tasting carbamate that acts as a central nervous system depressant and is used as a sedative and muscle relaxant, the best known of the class of propanediols. See also Sedative-, hypnotic-, or anxiolytic-related disorders. Also called Miltown (trademark). MPB abbrev. \[From me(thyl) the alkyl radical $\mathrm{CH}_{3}$ derived from methane by removal of one hydrogen atom, from Greek methy wine + hyle wood + pro(pyl) + (dicar)bamate]
