## Source [^1]
- $n$. A receiver operating characteristic applied to experimental data from recognition experiments. Target items correctly recognized are counted as hits, and non-target items incorrectly identified as target items are counted as false alarms (2). Also written memory-operating characteristic. See also signal detection theory. MOC abbrev.
