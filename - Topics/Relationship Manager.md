## Source [^1]
- A relationship manager (RM) is someone in an organization who liaises with clients, suppliers, partners, and investors. An RM might work on behalf of an entire organization or a department such as information services (IS). RM roles and responsibilities include negotiating contracts, maintaining business-critical communications, and networking with industry professionals and influencers. An RM helps businesses improve external relationships and reputation with outreach, engagement, and community-based activities. Typical RM skillsets include:
	- Collaboration
	- Critical thinking
	- Organization
	- Experience in a sales or marketing role
	- Leadership
	- Problem-solving
	- Time management
