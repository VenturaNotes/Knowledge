## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a term introduced by the South African statesman and philosopher Jan Christiaan Smuts (1870-1950) in his book Holism and Evolution (1926, p. 262) and adopted by the US psychologist Henry Alexander Murray (1893-1988) to denote a theory of personality and social behavior in which a person's needs and personality are considered as an integrated whole. See also Edwards Personal Preference Schedule, NEED (3), NEED-PRESS, PRESS, TAT.
