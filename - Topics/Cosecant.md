## Details
- Periodic with period 2$\pi$ [^1]
- Odd Function [^2]
- For Right Triangles [^3]
	- $csc\theta = \frac {\text{hyp}}{\text{opp}}$
-  For [[Unit Circle]] [^4]
	- $csc\theta = \frac {\text{r}}{\text{y}}$
- Derivative of $csc(x)$  [^5]
	- $-csc(x)cot(x)$
