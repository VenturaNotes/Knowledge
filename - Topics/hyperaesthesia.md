## Source [^1]
- $n$. excessive sensibility, especially of the skin.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Increased sensitivity of any of the sense organs, especially touch. US hyperesthesia. Also called superaesthesia or superesthesia. See also Heimdallr sensitivity, thigmohyperaesthesia, transliminality. hyperaesthesic, hyperesthesic, or hyperesthetic adj. \[From Greek hyper over + aisthesis sensation, from aisthanesthai to feel + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
