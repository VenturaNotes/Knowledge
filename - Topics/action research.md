## Source [^1]
- $n$. A term coined in the 1940s by the German-US psychologist Kurt Lewin (1890-1947) for a form of applied research in which experimental techniques are combined with programmes of social action to bring about desired social changes. See also PARTICIPATORY ACTION RESEARCH. Compare BASIC RESEARCH, DESCRIPTIVE RESEARCH, EVALUATION RESEARCH, EXPLORATORY RESEARCH.
