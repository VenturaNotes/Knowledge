## Source [^1]
- $n$. a nonsteroidal anti-androgen commonly used to treat locally advanced and metastatic prostate cancer. It binds to and blocks the androgen receptor but does not reduce the levels of testosterone in the blood, thus preserving libido and general energy levels. Side-effects include breast enlargement, tenderness, and pain.
