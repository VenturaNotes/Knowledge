## Source [^1]
- (fixed expense) An item of expenditure that remains unchanged, in total, irrespective of changes in the levels of production or sales. Examples are business rates, rent, and some salaries. Compare SEMIVARIABLE COST; VARIABLE COST.
## Source[^2]
- The part of total costs which does not depend on the level of current production. Fixed costs do not affect the profit-maximizing level of output in the short run, though in the longer run a firm which cannot cover its fixed costs will become insolvent and exit.
