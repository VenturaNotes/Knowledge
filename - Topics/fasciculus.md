## Source [^1]
- (fascicle) $n$. a bundle, e.g. of nerve or muscle fibers.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A small bundle or bunch, especially a bundle of nerve fibers such as the arcuate fasciculus, fasciculus cuneatus, fasciculus gracilis, inferior longitudinal fasciculus, superior longitudinal fasciculus, subthalamic fasciculus, or uncinate fasciculus. Also called a fascicle. \[From Latin fasciculus a small bundle, diminutive of fascis a bundle]
