## Source [^1]
- The tangible assets of an organization less its current liabilities. In analyzing the affairs of an organization the net tangible assets indicate its financial strength in terms of being solvent, without having to resort to such nebulous (and less easy to value) assets as goodwill.
## Source[^2]
- The tangible assets of an organization less its current liabilities. This is different from total assets less total liabilities. On the assets side it excludes intangibles such as goodwill, and on the liabilities side it excludes debts not due for payment within the next financial year.
