## Source [^1]
- The basis upon which the earnings per share of a company is calculated, taking into account both constant and variable elements in the company's tax charge. According to International Accounting Standard 33, Earnings per Share, a listed company must show the earnings per share on the net basis on the face of the profit and loss account.
