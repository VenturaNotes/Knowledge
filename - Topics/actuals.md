## Source [^1]
- (1) (physicals) Commodities that can be purchased and used, as opposed to goods traded on a futures contract. 
- (2) In futures contracts or forward dealing, the commodity underlying a contract. 
- (3) Expenses or receipts that have actually occurred, as opposed to targets, budgets, or other projections.
