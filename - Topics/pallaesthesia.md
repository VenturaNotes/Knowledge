## Source [^1]
- $n$. Sensation of vibration transmitted through the skin and bones. US pallesthesia. Also called palmaesthesia or palmesthesia. See also Meissner's corpuscle, Pacinian corpuscle, vibration receptor, vibratory SENSE. Compare pallanaesthesia. \[From Greek pallein to shake + aisthesis feeling + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
