## Source [^1]
- (1) The total weight of a package including its contents and the packing. The net weight is the weight of the contents only, i.e. after deducting the weight of the packing (often called the tare). 
- (2) The total weight of a vehicle (road or rail) and the goods it is carrying, as shown by a weighbridge. The net weight is obtained by deducting the tare of the vehicle.
