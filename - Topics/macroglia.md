## Source [^1]
- $n$. one of the two basic classes of glia (the non-nervous cells of the central nervous system), divided into astrocytes and oligodendrocytes. Compare MICROGLIA.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A generic name for three types of neuroglia found in the central nervous system, namely astrocytes, ependymal cells, and oligodendrocytes. See also glia. Compare microglia. \[From Greek makros large + glia glue]
