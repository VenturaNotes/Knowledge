## Source [^1]
- A former US manufacturer of high-performance workstations and servers, running operating systems based on UNIX. Founded in 1982, Sun grew spectacularly and became one of the major suppliers of non-PC computers. It was an important member of the Open Group and was largely responsible for the development of Java.
