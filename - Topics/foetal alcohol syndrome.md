## Source [^1]
- $n$. A highly variable pattern of congenital defects, including small stature, intellectual disability, pixie-like facial features, and malformations of the skull and brain, that tend to occur in infants of mothers who drink large quantities of alcohol during pregnancy. Also spelt fetal alcohol syndrome. FAS abbrev.
