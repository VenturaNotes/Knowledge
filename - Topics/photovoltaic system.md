## Source [^1]
- A system for generating electricity from sunlight using photovoltaic cells. The d.c. voltage produced by the cells is converted to a.c. by the use of an inverter. Typically, domestic installations have the solar cells on the roof, and the system connects to the electricity grid so it can feed excess electricity back into it.
