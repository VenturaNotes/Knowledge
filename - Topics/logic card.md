## Source [^1]
- A printed circuit board that is of a standard size and carries a number of digital logic devices in a circuit arrangement capable of fulfilling a specific function. The board will, in general, also carry a standard connector by means of which power and ground connections are provided and control and data signals may be transferred to and from a standard bus.
