## Source [^1]
- An obsolete storage medium consisting of a magnetic disk pack or disk cartridge that could be removed from its host system for library storage, and be replaced by another EDS of the same type. The store could be fitted to another host system having the same type of drive and means of recording and reading data. Compare FIXED DISK DRIVE.
