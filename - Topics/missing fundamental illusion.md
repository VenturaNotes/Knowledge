## Source [^1]
- $n$. An auditory illusion of pitch perception that arises if the set of overtones or harmonics associated with a certain fundamental frequency are presented to a listener without the fundamental frequency: the listener perceives the sound as having the pitch of the fundamental although the fundamental frequency is not present in the sound. See also COMBINATION TONE, OTOGENIC TONE.
