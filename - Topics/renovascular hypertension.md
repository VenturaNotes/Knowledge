## Source [^1]
- disease affecting the arterial supply to the kidneys, leading to ischaemia and resultant stimulation of the reninangiotensin-aldosterone axis. In the major vessels, the most common cause is atheromatous plaque disease. Other causes are fibromuscular dysplasia and Takayasu's disease.
