## Source [^1]
- A senior management committee of a bank or financial institution that has overall responsibility for setting and overseeing risk-control policies in relation to the balance sheet (see ASSET-LIABILITY MANAGEMENT; GAP ANALYSIS). In some cases it will also be responsible for general management policy.
