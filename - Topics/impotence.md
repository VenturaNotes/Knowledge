## Source [^1]
- $n$. inability of a man to have sexual intercourse (coitus). See ERECTILE DYSFUNCTION.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. 
- (1) Weakness or powerlessness. 
- (2) More specifically in relation to males, incapacity to perform sexual intercourse owing to an inability to achieve or to maintain an erection, believed to affect between 13 and 21 per cent of men aged 20-80 on at least some occasions. Also called erectile dysfunction or erectile disorder (ED), erectile impotence, or male erectile disorder, Also, less commonly, an inability to have an orgasm or to ejaculate. Also called ejaculatory impotence, male orgasmic disorder, or orgasmic impotence. Both forms of impotence may be classified either as primary impotence, if successful intercourse has never been possible, and secondary impotence, if at least one successful intercourse has been possible. See also cyclic GMP, de-reflection, erectile disorder, premature (early) ejaculation, Viagra. impotent adj. \[From Latin in- not + potentia power]
