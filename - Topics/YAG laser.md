## Source [^1]
- a type of laser whose active medium is a crystal of yttrium, aluminium, and garnet. It is used for cutting tissue, for example in lens capsulotomy or iridotomy. Specialized types (e.g. the holmium:YAG laser) are used for various specific purposes.
