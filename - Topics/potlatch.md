## Source [^1]
- $n$. A competitive winter festival involving extravagant feasting, ostentatious distribution of gifts, and ceremonial destruction of valuable possessions, performed by the Kwakiutl and other American Indians of the north-west coastal area as a display of wealth and a method of maintaining or enhancing the prestige of chiefs and clans. \[Chinook, from Nootka patlatsch a gift]
