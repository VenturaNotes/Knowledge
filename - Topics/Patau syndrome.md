## Source [^1]
- a chromosome disorder in which there are three no. 13 chromosomes (instead of the usual two), causing abnormal brain development, severe learning disabilities, and defects in the heart, kidney, and scalp. Affected individuals rarely survive. \[K. Patau (20th century), US geneticist]
