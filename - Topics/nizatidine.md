## Source [^1]
- $n$. an $\mathrm{H}_{2}$-receptor antagonist (see ANTIHISTAMINE) used to treat gastric and duodenal ulcers and prevent their recurrence and to treat gastro-oesophageal reflux disease. Side-effects include diarrhoea, dizziness, sweating, and headache.
