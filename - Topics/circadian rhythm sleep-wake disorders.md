## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of several sleep-wake disorders in DSM-5, characterized by persistent or recurrent disruption of sleep, resulting in hypersomnia or insomnia, caused by a mismatch between the exogenous environmental sleep-wake schedule and the person's endogenous circadian rhythm. The disorder may arise from a persistent pattern of late sleeping, jet lag, or shift working. Also called desynchronized sleep rhythm . Compare DELAYED SLEEP-PHASE SYNDROME.
