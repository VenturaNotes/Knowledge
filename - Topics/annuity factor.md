## Source [^1]
- The factor that converts a lump sum into a payment per period that is payable for a specified period of time. The inverse of the annuity rate, which determines the present value of a set of payments of unit value per period. Common usage often mentions annuity rate when, in fact, referring to the annuity factor. See also ANNUITY.
