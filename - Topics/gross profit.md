## Source [^1]
- (gross margin; gross profit margin) The difference between the sales revenue of a business and the cost of sales. It does not include the costs of finance, administration, or distribution. Compare NET PROFIT.
## Source[^2]
- The profit of a company before deducting depreciation allowances or taxation but after deducting debt interest.
