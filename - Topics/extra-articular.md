## Source [^1]
- adj. not involving a joint. The term is commonly used to specify a fracture pattern or the position of a bone tumor. It is also used to describe nonarticular or systemic manifestations of severe rheumatoid arthritis; for example, inflammation of the eyes, lungs, and heart, skin nodules and vasculitis, and nerve damage (neuropathy). See also INTRAARTICULAR; PERIARTICULAR.
