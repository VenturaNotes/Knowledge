## Source [^1]
- (1) n. the chromosome set of an individual or species described in terms of both the number and structure of the chromosomes. 
- (2) n. the representation of the chromosome set in a diagram. 
- (3) $v b$. to determine the karyotype of a cell, as by microscopic examination.
