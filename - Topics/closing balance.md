## Source [^1]
- The debit or credit balance on a ledger at the end of an accounting period, which will be carried forward to the next accounting period. A debit closing balance (such as an accrual) will be carried forward to the credit side of a ledger and a credit closing balance (such as a prepayment) will be carried forward to the debit side of a ledger.
