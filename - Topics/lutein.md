## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) a yellow carotenoid pigment and potent antioxidant found in many vegetables. Like the related pigment zeaxanthin (see also MESOZEAXANTHIN), it occurs in high concentrations in the human eye. For this reason these pigments are thought to be crucial to healthy vision. 
- (2) the yellow pigment of the corpus luteum.
