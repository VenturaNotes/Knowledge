## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) surgical removal of the bony walls of an antrum. See ANTROSTOMY. 
- (2) (distal gastrectomy) a surgical operation in which the gastric antrum is removed. Indications for antrectomy include peptic ulcer disease resistant to medical treatment, tumors, perforation, and gastric outlet obstruction.
