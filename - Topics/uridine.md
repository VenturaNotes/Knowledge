## Source [^1]
- $n$. a compound containing uracil and the sugar ribose. See also NUCLEOSIDE.
## Source[^2]
- A nucleoside consisting of one uracil molecule linked to a D-ribose sugar molecule. The derived nucleotide uridine diphosphate (UDP) is important in carbohydrate metabolism.
