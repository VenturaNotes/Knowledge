## Source [^1]
- (1) (mother-to-child transmission) transmission of an infection, such as HIV, hepatitis B, or hepatitis C, from mother to fetus via the placental circulation. 
- (2) (in population genetics) inheritance of an allele or condition from either the father or mother.
