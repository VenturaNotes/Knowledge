## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of amnesia resulting from loss of neurons in the diencephalon, especially the midline thalamus and the mammillary bodies of the hypothalamus. This pattern of pathology is often associated with Korsakoff's psychosis or syndrome as a consequence of alcohol abuse and thiamine deficiency.
