## Source [^1]
- $n$. a technique for recording heart sounds and murmurs simultaneously with the ECG, which is used as a reference tracing. The sound is picked up by a microphone placed over the heart. The tracing is a phonocardiogram. It provides a permanent record of heart sounds and murmurs and is useful in their analysis.
