## Source [^1]
- $n$. A releasing hormone that is secreted by the hypothalamus in response to a biological clock at the beginning of a woman's menstrual cycle and that in turn stimulates the pituitary gland to secrete luteinizing hormone. Also called gonadotrophin-releasing hormone LH-RH abbrev.
