## Source [^1]
- $n$. Anxiety induced in a person performing some task while being observed by others and feeling anxious about being judged or appraised by them. One of its effects is to increase the person's level of arousal, which may improve or impair performance, depending on the nature of the task. See also AUDIENCE EFFECT, COACTION EFFECT, DRIVE THEORY OF SOCIAL FACILITATION.
