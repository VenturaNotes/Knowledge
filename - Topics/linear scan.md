## Source [^1]
- (1) A sweep of the electron beam in a cathode-ray tube in which the beam scans the screen with constant velocity, usually by application of a sawtooth waveform to the deflection plates or coils. See also TIMEBASE. 
- (2) A scan using a radar beam that moves with constant angular velocity.
