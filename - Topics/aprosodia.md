## Source [^1]
- $n$. A flat, unmodulated speaking voice, lacking the normal range of prosody, often indicative of affective flattening or a lesion in the frontal lobe of the right cerebral hemisphere. Compare DYSPROSODY. \[From Greek $a$- without + prosoidia a song set to music, from pros towards + oide a song + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
