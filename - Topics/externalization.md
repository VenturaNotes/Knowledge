## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a defence mechanism whereby a person unconsciously attributes inner impulses to the external world, as when a child converts unconscious angry or aggressive impulses into a fear of monsters or demons in the dark. A common form of externalization is projection (1). See also DEFENSIVE TECHNIQUE. externalize $v b$.
