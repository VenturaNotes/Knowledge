## Source [^1]
- Sources of energy that do not use up the earth’s finite mineral resources. Nonrenewable energy sources are fossil fuels and fission fuels. Various renewable energy sources are being used or investigated, including geothermal energy, hydroelectric power, nuclear fusion, solar energy, tides, wind power, and wave power.
