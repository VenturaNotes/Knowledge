## Source [^1]
- $n$. An obsolescent term for a level of intellectual disability associated with an IQ approximately between 50 and 69 (in adults, mental age from 9 to 12 years), equivalent to the educational category of educable mentally retarded. Sometimes called feeblemindedness or mild mental subnormality.
