## Source [^1]
- The process by which a living cell engulfs a minute droplet of liquid. It involves a mechanism similar to phagocytosis. See ENDOCYTOSIS.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. the intake of small droplets of fluid by a cell by cytoplasmic engulfment. It occurs in many white blood cells and in certain kidney and liver cells. Compare PHAGOCYTOSIS.
