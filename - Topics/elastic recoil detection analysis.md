## Source [^1]
- (ERDA) A technique used for surface analysis of a material up to depths of one micrometer. The technique is similar to Rutherford back scattering (RBS), but uses a beam of energetic heavy charged particles rather than the alpha particles used in RBS. The ERDA technique is used for detecting and measuring the concentration of light elements in the material, including hydrogen.
