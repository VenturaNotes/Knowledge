## Source [^1]
- Any of a range of Apple Macintosh personal computers first introduced in 1998. The monitor, CPU, and CD drive are all contained in a single unit and stylish design is a characteristic feature. Recent versions have all the hardware integrated into the display monitor itself, which is flat, thin, and widescreen.
