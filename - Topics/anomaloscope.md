## Source [^1]
- $n$. an instrument for testing color discrimination. By adjusting the controls the subject has to produce a mixture of red and green light to match a yellow light. The matching is done on a brightly illuminated disc viewed down a telescope.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A device for mixing light from the red and green parts of the visible spectrum and comparing the result with a standard yellow to measure defects in color vision according to the Rayleigh equation. \[So called because it is used to detect color anomalies]
