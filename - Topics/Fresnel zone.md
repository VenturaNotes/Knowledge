## Source [^1]
- The locus of points that a radio signal can pass through, so that the total path length from the transmitter to the receiver is less than one half-wavelength greater than the direct path. It is often assumed that for a radio path to be considered unblocked, a fixed proportion of the Fresnel zone must be free from obstructions.
