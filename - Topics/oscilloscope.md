## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cathode-ray tube designed to display electronically a wave form corresponding to the electrical data fed into it. Oscilloscopes are used to provide a continuous record of many different measurements, such as the activity of the heart and brain. See ELECTROCARDIOGRAPHY; ELECTROENCEPHALOGRAPHY.
