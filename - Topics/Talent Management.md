## Source [^1]
- Talent management is the process of attracting, selecting, and keeping high-quality employees within an organization. It involves various HR processes such as recruitment, onboarding, succession, workforce planning, employee engagement, performance management, and learning and development. Talent management is a business strategy that helps build a more efficient workforce, improves productivity, and enhances employee experiences.
