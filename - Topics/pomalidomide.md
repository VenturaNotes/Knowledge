## Source [^1]
- $n$. a derivative of thalidomide with similar properties. It is taken in the treatment of relapsed or refractory myeloma. The main side-effects include lethargy, low blood counts, and thromboembolism; this drug should not be used in women who are pregnant or capable of becoming pregnant.
