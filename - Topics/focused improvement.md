## Source [^1]
- An approach to improving a system's performance that concentrates on identifying the current constraints on performance and taking steps to eliminate them one at a time. Advocates of focused improvement argue that it yields immediate measurable benefits more readily than more general approaches such as continuous improvement and total quality management.
