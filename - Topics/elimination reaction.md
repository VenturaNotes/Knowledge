## Source [^1]
- A reaction that can be regarded as the opposite of an addition reaction, resulting in the formation of two molecules from one molecule. Elimination reactions are divided into two types, known as E1 and E2 reactions. An E1 reaction is a unimolecular reaction that occurs in two steps. An E2 reaction is a bimolecular reaction that occurs in one step.
