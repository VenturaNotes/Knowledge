## Source [^1]
- $n$. a molecule consisting of two or more amino acids linked by bonds between the amino group $(-\mathrm{NH})$ and the carboxyl group $(-\mathrm{CO})$. This bond is known as a peptide bond. See also POLYPEPTIDE.
