## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) a soft mass of chewed food. 
- (2) a large dose of a drug administered by rapid injection, as opposed to infusion.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. boluses) The ball of chewed food bound together with saliva that is formed in the mouth by the action of the tongue. The bolus is shaped to a size that allows it to pass into the oesophagus after being swallowed (see DEGLUTITION).
