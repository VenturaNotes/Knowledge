## Source [^1]
- A bus used for the connection of digital system components such as processors, memories, and disk controllers to form a computer entity. Its use may be restricted to the connection of devices within the immediate locality of the processor and it may be implemented as a backplane. See also LOCAL AREA NETWORK, PCI, VME bus.
