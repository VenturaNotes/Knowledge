## Source [^1]
- (symbol: $B$; unit: siemens) The imaginary part of the admittance, $Y$, which is given by$$Y = G + jB$$where $G$ is the conductance. For a circuit containing both resistance, $R$, and reactance, $X$, the susceptance is given by$$B = -\frac{X}{\left(R^2 + X^2\right)}$$
