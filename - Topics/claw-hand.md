## Source [^1]
- $n$. flexion and contraction of the fingers with extension at the joints between the fingers and the hand, giving a claw-like appearance. Any kind of damage to the nerves or muscles may lead to claw-hand; causes include injuries, syringomyelia, and leprosy. See also DuPUYTREN's CONTRACTURE.
