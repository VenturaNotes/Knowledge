## Source [^1]
- (FDG) $n$. a variant of normal glucose in the body that is not metabolized and therefore accumulates in areas of high metabolism, such as tumors and areas of infection. It can be labelled with radioactive fluorine-18 and is in common use in PET scanning (see POSITRON EMISSION TOMOGRAPHY).
