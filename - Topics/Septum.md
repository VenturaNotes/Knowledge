## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. septa) a partition or dividing wall within an anatomical structure. For example, the atrioventricular septum divides the atria of the heart from the ventricles. $\textemdash$septal adj. $\textemdash$septate adj.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. septa) Any dividing wall in a plant or animal. Examples are the septa that separate the chambers of the heart.
