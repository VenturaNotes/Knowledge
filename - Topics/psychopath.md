## Source [^1]
- $n$. a person who behaves in an antisocial way and shows little or no guilt for antisocial acts and little capacity for forming emotional relationships with others. Psychopaths tend to respond poorly to treatment and do not learn from punishment, but many mature as they age. See also ANTISOCIAL PERSONALITY DISORDER. —psychopathic adj. —psychopathy $n$.
