## Source [^1]
- A reaction involved in the catabolism of amino acids that assists their excretion from the body. An example of an oxidative deamination is the conversion of glutamate to $\alpha$-ketoglutarate, a reaction catalysed by the enzyme glutamate dehydrogenase. See DEAMINATION.
