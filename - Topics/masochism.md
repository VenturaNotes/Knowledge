- $n$. Another name for sexual masochism. The term is also used loosely to denote extreme self-damaging behavior not accompanied by sexual gratification. masochistic adj. \[Named after the Austrian historian and novelist Leopold von Sacher-Masoch (1836-95) who wrote about it, notably in his novel Venus im Pelz (1870), translated into English as Venus in Furs (1902), in which the heroine Wanda subjects the narrator to sexually stimulating suffering]
