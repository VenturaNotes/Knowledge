## Source [^1]
- Bronze that contains at least $0.18\%$ of added phosphorus. The addition of the phosphorus enhances the tensile strength, ductility, and shock resistance of the alloy. Phosphor-bronze in strip form has been widely used for galvanometer suspensions and other similar applications, including bearing surfaces.
