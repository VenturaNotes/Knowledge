## Source [^1]
- The four-dimensional space-time of special relativity. It is $\mathbb{R}^4$ with the Minkowski ‘metric’$$g(\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}) = u_0 v_0 - u_1 v_1 - u_2 v_2 - u_3 v_3.$$
- The linear isometries (see LINEAR MAP) of Minkowski space are the Lorentz transformations.
