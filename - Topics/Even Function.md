### Details
- Function f is even if f(x) = f(-x)
	- Even functions are symmetric about the y-axis
## Source[^2]
- A function $f$ where $f(-x) = f(x)$ for all $x$. By contrast, an odd function satisfies $f(-x) = -f(x)$ for all $x$. Every function can be written as the sum of an even and odd function. The concept of even and odd functions plays an important role in the theory of Fourier series, in particular the analysis of harmonics.
