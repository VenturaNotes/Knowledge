## Source [^1]
- $n$. A binding site in a region of DNA to which a gene regulator can become attached, thereby affecting the expression of a structural gene by increasing or repressing the synthesis of its protein product. Also called an operator. See GENE ACTIVATOR, GENE REPRESSOR. See also OPERON.
