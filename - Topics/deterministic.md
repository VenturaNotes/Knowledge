## Source [^1]
- Denoting an algorithm, machine, method, process, procedure, program, etc., the resulting behavior of which is uniquely determined by the inputs and initial state. See also NONDETERMINISM, PSEUDORANDOM, STATISTICAL METHODS.
## Source[^2]
- adj. Of, relating to, or consisting of a non-random process or variable whose past completely determines its future forever, so that it can be equally well predicted from its remote past as from its recent past. Compare STOCHASTIC.
