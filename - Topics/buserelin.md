## Source [^1]
- $n$. a gonadorelin analogue used for the treatment of endometriosis, to help in the management of advanced prostate cancer, and in the treatment of infertility by in vitro fertilization. Possible side-effects include hot flushes, headache, emotional upset, and loss of libido.
