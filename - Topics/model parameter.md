## Source [^1]
- In modeling, a value that characterizes a specific instance of the class of systems being modeled. These values can be considered as inputs to the model; they are kept fixed if the behavior of the corresponding instance is being examined, and are varied if the relationship between the behavior of the system and the parameter is of interest.
