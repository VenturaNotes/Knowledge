## Source [^1]
- The study of methods of producing and analyzing spectra using spectroscopes and other instruments. Spectroscopy is employed in a wide range of biological research areas, such as biochemistry and toxicology, for the identification of metabolites and other compounds of biological significance. See MASS SPECTROMETRY.
