## Source [^1]
- $n$. The surgical grafting of brain tissue from an aborted foetus into the brain of an adult to repair damage caused by disease or injury, as in the replacement of dopamine-secreting brain tissue in the substantia nigra or striatum of patients with Parkinson's disease. Also spelt fetal brain transplantation.
