## Source [^1]
- an enzyme that catalyses the hydrolysis of triglycerides in chylomicrons and very low-density lipoproteins to free fatty acids, which are absorbed from the capillaries into local tissues. Deficiency of this enzyme results in severe hypertriglyceridaemia.
