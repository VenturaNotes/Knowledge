## Source [^1]
- $n$. inflammation of the foreskin. This usually occurs in association with inflammation of the glans penis (balanitis; see BALANOPOSTHITIS). Pain, redness, and swelling of the foreskin occurs due to bacterial infection. Treatment is by antibiotic administration, and subsequent circumcision prevents further attacks.
