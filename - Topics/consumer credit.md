## Source [^1]
- Credit granted to consumers by suppliers of goods and services. This may be granted by the suppliers themselves accepting payment by installments or on deferred terms, or by the use of credit cards and other systems by which the supplier is paid at once by a credit institution, which then collects payments in installments from the customer.
