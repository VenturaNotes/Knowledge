## Source [^1]
- (eccrine) adj. describing a type of secretion in which the glandular cells remain intact during the process of secretion.
## Source[^2]
- adj. Of or relating to glands such as sweat glands that produce secretions without undergoing degeneration. Compare APOCRINE, HOLOCRINE. \[From Greek meros part or share + krinein to separate]
