## Source [^1]
- SCIV is an inventory management term that refers to tracking and viewing inventory levels across all nodes in a supply chain. This includes the manufacturing, distribution, and retail stages. The benefits of SCIV include improved efficiency through better planning and reduced costs due to less waste.
