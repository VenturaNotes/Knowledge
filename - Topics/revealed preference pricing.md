## Source [^1]
- A group of methods of obtaining a monetary value for non-marketable goods and services. The methods include hedonic pricing, wage premia, and the use of travel costs as a lower bound on willingness to pay. They can be used to value environmental goods, such as clean air or biodiversity, and to put a value upon loss of life.
