## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) describing a tumor that invades and destroys the tissue in which it originates and has the potential to spread to other sites in the body via the bloodstream and lymphatic system. See CANCER; METASTASIS. 
- (2) describing any disorder that becomes life-threatening if untreated (e.g. malignant hypertension). Compare BENIGN.
