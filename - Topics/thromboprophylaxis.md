## Source [^1]
- $n$. measures taken to reduce the risk of venous thrombosis and therefore avoid thromboembolism during hospitalization. Techniques include tight support stockings to minimize venous stasis and subcutaneous injections of a low-molecular-weight heparin to thin the blood.
