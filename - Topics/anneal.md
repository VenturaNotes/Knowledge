## Source [^1]
- To improve a material's properties by heating it to a high temperature and then cooling it slowly. Typically, this results in reducing the brittleness of the material. In semiconductor manufacture annealing is used for a variety of purposes, ranging from controlling doping levels to repairing damage from earlier manufacturing steps.
