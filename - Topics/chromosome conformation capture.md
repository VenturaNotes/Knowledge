## Source [^1]
- (3C) A technique used to investigate the three-dimensional configuration and interactions of chromosomes in cells. It provides information about how various nucleotide sequences, such as promoters and enhancers, physically come together to regulate gene activity, as well as other aspects of chromatin organization.
