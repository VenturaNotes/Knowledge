## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antidepressant drug that acts by prolonging the action of the neurotransmitter serotonin (5-hydroxytryptamine) in the brain (see SSRI). It is taken for the treatment of depression, obsessive-compulsive disorder, and panic disorder; side-effects may include dizziness, agitation, tremor, nausea, diarrhoea, and drowsiness.
