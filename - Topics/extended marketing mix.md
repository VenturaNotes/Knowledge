## Source [^1]
- An addition to the traditional marketing mix of product, price, place, and promotion (the four Ps ) to include three further Ps, when marketing services, i.e.:
	- physical evidence-the tangible elements of a service that enable consumers to evaluate it;
	- people-an evaluation of the personnel providing the service (e.g. knowledge, competence, politeness);
	- process-an evaluation of the overall experience provided by the service to ensure that all the elements of which it consists operate effectively together.
