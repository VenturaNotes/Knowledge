## Synthesis
- A non-zero vector is any vector that has at least one component that is not zero
	- #question Do you call the numbers inside a vector elements or components?
### Examples
1. $\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}$
2. $\begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 5 \end{pmatrix}$
3. $\begin{pmatrix} -2 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}$
4. $\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}$
