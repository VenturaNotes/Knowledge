## Source [^1]
- $n$. A lipotropin (a fat-mobilizing protein hormone) that is released by the anterior pituitary and that contains beta-endorphin as the final sequence of 31 amino acids of its polypeptide chain. Also written beta-lipotrophin, $\beta$-lipotropin, or $\beta$-lipotrophin.
