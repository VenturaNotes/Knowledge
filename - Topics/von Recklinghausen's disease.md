## Source [^1]
- (1) a syndrome due to excessive secretion of parathyroid hormone (hyperparathyroidism), characterized by loss of mineral from bones, which become weakened and fracture easily, and formation of kidney stones. Medical name: osteitis fibrosa. 
- (2) see NEUROFIBROMATOSIS. \[F. D. von Recklinghausen (1833-1910), German pathologist]
