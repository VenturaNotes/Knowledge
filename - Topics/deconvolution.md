## Source [^1]
- The process of reversing the convolution of two signals. Deconvolution is typically performed for a recorded signal that is a mixture (convolution) of real data and noise, in order to separate the two. Unlike convolution, there is no simple formula, and some knowledge of the nature of the noise component is required for a successful outcome.
