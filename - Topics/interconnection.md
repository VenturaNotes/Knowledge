## Source [^1]
- (1) Any method of providing an electrical path between any of the materials (metals, semiconductors, etc.) that combine to form a circuit. 
- (2) Connections between and external to any functional item that forms a circuit or system of circuits. Functional items include component parts, devices, subassemblies, and assemblies. Compare INTRACONNECTION.
