## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) relating to the nonreproductive parts of the body. A somatic mutation cannot be inherited. 
- (2) relating to the body wall (i.e. excluding the viscera), e.g. somatic mesoderm. Compare SPLANCHNIC. 
- (3) relating to the body rather than the mind.
