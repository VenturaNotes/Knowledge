## Source [^1]
- (1) Abbreviation for società a responsabilità. It appears after the name of an Italian company, being equivalent to the British abbreviation Ltd (i.e. denoting a private limited liability company). 
- (2) Abbreviation for société à responsabilité limitée. It appears after the name of a French private limited company. Compare SA.
