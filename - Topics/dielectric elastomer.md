## Source [^1]
- A smart material with high elasticity that produces large physical deformation and force when electric voltage is applied to it. Materials of this kind currently being investigated include graphite powder, carbon nanostructures, metal films, ionic gels, silicone, and rubber. See also PIEZOELECTRICITY.
