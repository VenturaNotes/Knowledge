## Source [^1]
- (whitlow) $n$. an inflamed swelling of the nail folds. Acute paronychia is usually caused by infection with Staphylococcus aureus. Chronic paronychia occurs mainly in those who habitually engage in wet work; it is associated with secondary infection with Candida albicans. It is vital to keep the hands dry in order to control chronic paronychia.
