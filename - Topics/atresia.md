## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) congenital absence or abnormal narrowing of a body opening. See BILIARY ATRESIA; DUODENAL ATRESIA; TRICUSPID ATRESIA. 
- (2) the degenerative process that affects the majority of ovarian follicles. Usually only one Graafian follicle will ovulate in each menstrual cycle. $\textemdash$ atretic adj.
