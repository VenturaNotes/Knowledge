## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cytotoxic drug derived from an extract of the mandrake plant that interferes with DNA replication (see TOPOISOMERASE INHIBITOR). It is used mainly in the treatment of bronchial carcinoma, lymphomas, and testicular tumors. Side-effects include alopecia, nausea, and marrow suppression.
