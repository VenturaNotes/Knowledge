## Source [^1]
- $n$. The rate at which a person performs simple perceptual or cognitive tasks such as apprehension, scanning, retrieval, and response to stimuli. Measures of processing speed correlate weakly with psychometric measures of intelligence. See also CHOICE REACTION TIME, INSPECTION TIME, NERVE CONDUCTION VELOCITY.
