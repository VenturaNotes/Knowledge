## Source [^1]
- $n$. A technique for studying memory by measuring the amount of time or the number of trials required to learn a certain amount of information or a certain skill and then determining how much less time or how many fewer trials are required to relearn the material or skill to the same standard after a period of forgetting, the reduction providing an index of the extent of the original learning.
