## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of the fundamental tastes that are believed to underlie all tastes, usually the four vertices of Henning's tetrahedron: sweet (like sugar or aspartame), sour (like vinegar or lemon juice), salty (like sea water or table salt), and bitter (like quinine or orange peel). See also umami.
