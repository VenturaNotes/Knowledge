## Source [^1]
- A way of transferring the entire graphical or textual contents of a display screen to a printer. Each pixel of the display appears as a dot of suitable density on the printer. Colour screens can be dumped to colour printers. A screenshot is a screen dump in which the display screen pixels are recorded in a data file.
