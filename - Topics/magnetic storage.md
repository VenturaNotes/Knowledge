## Source [^1]
- A data storage technique in which data bits are represented by differing degrees of magnetization of a layer of suitable material. This material may take different forms, including magnetic tape, magnetic disk, and magnetic stripe card, with corresponding differences in how the read-write head moves over the magnetic surface. See also MAGNETIC RECORDING.
