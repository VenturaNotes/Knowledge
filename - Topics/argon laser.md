## Source [^1]
- a type of laser that utilizes argon gas to produce a beam of intense light, used especially in eye surgery to treat disease of the retina (e.g. diabetic retinopathy) or glaucoma (as in argon laser trabeculoplasty). See also PHOTOCOAGULATION.
