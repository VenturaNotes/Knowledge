## Source [^1]
- (Hz) The SI unit of frequency. A periodic phenomenon has a frequency of one hertz if each cycle of the phenomenon repeats itself in a period of one second. A megahertz $(\mathrm{MHz})$ is one million $\left(10^{6}\right)$ hertz; a gigahertz $(\mathrm{GHz})$ is 1000 million $\left(10^{9}\right)$ hertz.
