## Source [^1]
- $n$. In phenomenology, suspension of preconceptions, interpretations, and explanations as a precondition for the experience and description of uninterpreted sense experiences and ideas. Also called bracketing. \[From Greek epoche stoppage or cessation, from epechein to hold back or pause, from epi on + echein to hold or have]
