## Source [^1]
- A tuning device used in a television or radio receiver. It contains a set of resonant circuits each tuned to the frequency of one of the separate broadcast channels. One or more manually operated switches, termed band switches, allow the particular circuit corresponding to the desired channel to be selected by the user.
