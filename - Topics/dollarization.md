## Source [^1]
- The adoption by a country of the US dollar in place of its own currency, usually as a means of controlling inflation and interest-rate volatility. Partial dollarization is said to occur when a country gives the US dollar equal status to its own currency or pegs its currency one-to-one with the dollar.
## Source[^2]
- The use by a country of a foreign currency (often the dollar) in parallel with, or as an alternative to, the domestic currency.
