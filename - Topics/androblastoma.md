## Source [^1]
- (arrhenoblastoma) n. a rare tumor of the ovary, composed of Sertoli cells, Leydig cells, or both. It can produce male or female hormones and may give rise to masculinization; in children it may cause precocious puberty. Up to $30 \%$ of these tumors are malignant, but probably as many as $85 \%$ of all cases are cured by surgery alone.
