## Source [^1]
- (MTN) A note issued in a eurocurrency with a maturity of about three to six years. Although MTNs were traditionally unsecured, recent years have seen a growth in asset-backed medium-term notes (ABMTN), in which the MTN is secured against assets held by a structured investment vehicle.
