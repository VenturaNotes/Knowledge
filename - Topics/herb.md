## Source [^1]
- (1) A herbaceous plant, i.e. a seed-bearing plant that does not form hard woody tissue. Compare Forb. 
- (2) A plant with medicinal or culinary uses. Culinary herbs are usually plants whose leaves are used for flavoring food; examples are mint and parsley.
