## Source [^1]
- any one of a class of drugs that block the action of the hormone angiotensin II, which constricts blood vessels; they are therefore useful in treating hypertension. These drugs include candesartan, irbesartan, losartan, telmisartan, and valsartan. Sideeffects are usually mild.
