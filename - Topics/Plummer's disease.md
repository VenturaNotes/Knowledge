## Source [^1]
- a hyperfunctioning, usually benign, adenoma of the thyroid gland, which can be palpated and appears as a 'hot nodule' on radioactive thyroid scanning. Treatment is to control the nodule with antithyroid drugs and then remove it surgically or destroy it permanently with radioactive iodine. \[H. S. Plummer (1874-1937), US physician]
