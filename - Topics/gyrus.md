## Source [^1]
- n. (pl. gyri) a raised convolution of the cerebral cortex, between two sulci (clefts).
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A ridge, especially one of the convolutions on the surface of the brain that are separated by sulci. See angular gyrus, Cingulate Gyrus, dentate Gyrus, Heschl's Gyrus, inferior temporal Gyrus, parahippoCAMPAL GYRUS, POSTCENTRAL GYRUS, PRECENTRAL GYRUS, SUPERIOR TEMPORAL GYRUS. gyri pl. \[From Latin gyrus a circle or turning, from Greek gyros a circle or ring]
