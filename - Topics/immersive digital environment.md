## Source [^1]
- A virtual reality system that works with most of the senses to give a very strong feeling of being present within a digitally generated world. The generated world can be based on reality or entirely imaginary. Technologies used to achieve full immersion include head-mounted displays, haptic interfaces, and virtual surround sound.
