## Source [^1]
- (megalocephaly) $n$. abnormal largeness of the head in relation to the rest of the body. Compare MICROCEPHALY.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Congenital enlargement of the head without increased intracranial pressure (compare HYDROCEPHALUS), resulting in some degree of intellectual disability. macrocephalic adj. \[From Greek makros large + kephale head]
