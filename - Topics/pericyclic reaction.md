## Source [^1]
- A type of concerted chemical reaction that proceeds through a cyclic conjugated transition state. Pericyclic reactions include cheletropic reactions, some cycloadditions, sigmatropic reactions, and electrocyclic reactions. They can be understood using frontier orbital theory or the Woodward-Hoffmann rules.
