## Source [^1]
- the region of the embryo that proliferates rapidly, producing mesoderm cells that spread outwards between the layers of ectoderm and endoderm.
## Source[^2]
- The longitudinal groove that develops in the gastrula during the development of bird and mammal embryos. The cells in the primitive streak proliferate rapidly to form mesoderm cells, which migrate to the interior of the embryo.
