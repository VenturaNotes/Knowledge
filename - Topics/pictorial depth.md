## Source [^1]
- $n$. Depth perception arising from cues presented in two-dimensional pictures or images, only some of the monocular cues being functional under such conditions, namely aerial perspective, chiaroscuro, elevation in the visual field, interposition, linear perspective, relative size, sfumato, and texture gradient.
