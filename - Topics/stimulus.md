## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. stimuli) any agent that provokes a response, or particular form of activity, in a cell, tissue, or other structure, which is said to be sensitive to that stimulus.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. stimuli) Any change in the external or internal environment of an organism that provokes a physiological or behavioral response in the organism. Specific receptors are sensitive to particular types of stimuli, such as sounds, smells, pain, injury, visual stimuli, or temperature.
