## Source [^1]
- $n$. A cluster of neurons at the back of each side of the hypothalamus, synthesizing vasopressin and oxytocin and transporting them through their axons to the posterior pituitary. \[From Greek para beside + Latin ventriculus a little belly, diminutive of venter, ventris a belly, alluding to its location near the walls of the third ventricle]
