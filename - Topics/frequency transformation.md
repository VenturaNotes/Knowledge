## Source [^1]
- The conversion of a normalized low-pass filter to a high-pass, band-pass, or band-stop filter type using the following substitutions for s in the normalized low-pass filter response transfer function (where s is the complex operator: see S-DOMAIN CIRCUIT ANALYSIS):
	- low-pass to high-pass: $s \to \omega_c / s$
	- low-pass to band-pass: $s \to (s^2 + \omega_c^2) / Bs$
	- low-pass to band-stop: $s \to Bs / (s^2 + \omega_c^2)$
- where $\omega_{c}$ is the cut-off frequency and $B$ is the bandwidth.
