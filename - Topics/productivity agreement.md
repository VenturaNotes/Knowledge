## Source [^1]
- An agreement between an employer and a union in which an increase in wages is given for a measured increase in productivity. To arrive at such an agreement, productivity bargaining is needed to reach a compromise between the increase in wages demanded by the unions and the increase in productivity demanded by the employers.
