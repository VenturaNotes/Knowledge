## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychometrics, a form of criterion validity in which the predictor scores are obtained in advance of the criterion scores, as when a test of scholastic aptitude is validated against scores on tests of school performance obtained months or years later. Compare CONCURRENT VALIDITY.
