## Source[^2]
- $n$. An oestrogenic hormone secreted by the ovaries and found in the urine of pregnant females, having similar action and uses to oestradiol. US estrone. \[From Latin oestrus a gadfly or frenzy + -one indicating a ketone, from Greek -one a feminine name suffix]
