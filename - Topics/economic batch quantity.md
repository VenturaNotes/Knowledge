## Source [^1]
- A refinement of the economic order quantity to take into account circumstances in which the goods are produced in batches. The formula is:$$Q=[2 c d r / h(r-d)] 1 / 2$$where $Q$ is the quantity to be purchased or manufactured, $c$ is the cost of processing an order for delivery, $d$ is the demand in the period for that stock item, $h$ is the cost of holding a unit of stock, and $r$ is the rate of production.
