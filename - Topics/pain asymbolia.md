## Source [^1]
- $n$. A condition caused by a lesion in the insula resulting in stimuli that normally cause pain being perceived but not experienced as unpleasant and not causing suffering. Its existence shows that the unpleasant feeling of pain is not merely a perception of tissue damage. \[From English pain + Greek $a$- without + symbolon a token, from syn together + ballein to throw]
