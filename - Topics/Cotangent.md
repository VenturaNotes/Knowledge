## Characteristics
- Periodic with period $\pi$ [^1]
- Odd Function [^2]
- For Right Triangles [^3]
	- $cot\theta = \frac {\text{adj}}{\text{opp}}$
-  For [[Unit Circle]] [^4]
	- $cot\theta = \frac {\text{x}}{\text{y}}$
- Derivative of cot(x) [^5]
	- $-csc^2x$
