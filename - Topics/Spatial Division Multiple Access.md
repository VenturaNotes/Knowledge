## Source [^1]
- Spatial division multiple access, or SDMA, is a channel access method in the world of telecommunications or computer networks. It refers to an advanced multiple antenna technique that makes use of parallel spatial pipes to transmit signals. This satellite technology is used for cellular devices that move throughout various locations. It allows frequencies to be transmitted quickly and efficiently while the devices are on the move.
