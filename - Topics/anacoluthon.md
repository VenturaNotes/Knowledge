## Source [^1]
- $n$. A sentence showing a lack of grammatical sequence, or a grammatical construction in which one part of a sentence does not fit grammatically with another, such as the following utterance: The main reason for my feeling of guilt$\textemdash$ and I'm not the sort of person given to wallowing in guilt, although I have had periods in my life when I felt depressed, probably because both my parents tended to be depressives. anacolutha pl. anacoluthic adj. \[From Greek an- not + akolouthos following]
