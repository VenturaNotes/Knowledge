## Source [^1]
- adj. Of or relating to a distribution of payoffs such that any redistribution that benefited one individual would be detrimental to one or more others. 
- Pareto optimality $n$. 
- Paretian adj. Of, relating to, or having the property of Pareto optimality. \[Named after the French-born Italian economist and sociologist Vilfredo Pareto (1848-1923) who introduced the concept in his mathematical treatment of income distribution]
