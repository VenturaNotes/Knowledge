## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of synaesthesia (1) in which stimuli not normally associated with color, such as musical tones or numbers, elicit sensations of color. When the stimuli are auditory it is also called chromatic audition, color audition, colored audition, color hearing, or colored hearing . US chromesthesia. \[From Greek chroma color + aisthesis feeling + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
