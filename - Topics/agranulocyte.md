## Source [^1]
- Any white blood cell (see LEUCOCYTE) with a nongranular cytoplasm and a large spherical nucleus; lymphocytes and monocytes are examples. Agranulocytes are produced either in the lymphatic system or in the bone marrow and account for $30 \%$ of all leucocytes. Compare GRANULOCYTE.
