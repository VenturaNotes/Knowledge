## Source [^1]
- $n$. A technique for monitoring human activity through an actigraph or activity monitor that measures gross motor activity by continually recording gross movements of a bodily area such as the hip or wrist. It is useful for determining a person's level of potentially calorieburning activity and for studying dyssomnias, eating disorders, and chronic fatigue syndrome. Software applications are available for using phones as actigraphs. \[From Latin actus an act + Greek graphein to write]
