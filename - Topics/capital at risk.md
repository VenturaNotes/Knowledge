## Source [^1]
- An assessment of the amount of an organization's capital that needs to be set aside to cover worst-case losses in excess of the average; it is used in banking to calculate both capital adequacy requirements and certain performance measures, such as risk-adjusted return on capital (RAROC). It is usually based on the value-at-risk methodology. See also Basle Accords.
