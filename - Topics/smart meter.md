## Source [^1]
- A meter that records electricity consumption data, which it then transmits to the supplier for billing and monitoring. Apart from eliminating the need for human meter readers, this makes it possible for the utility to interact with the smart meter to reduce unpredictability in electricity usage. For instance, the smart meter could negotiate a preferential night electricity rate with the utility, and then connect to smart appliances in the home, telling them to defer operations until the cheap rate applies.
