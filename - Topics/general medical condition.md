## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any disorder not considered to be a mental disorder, specifically any disorder not included in the section devoted to mental and behavioral disorders in ICD-10 and used in DSM-IV as a term of convenience, without any implication of a fundamental or sharp distinction between mental disorders and general medical conditions.
