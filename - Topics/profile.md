## Source [^1]
- $n$. An outline, especially of a person's face in side view. In psychometrics, a graphic representation of a person's scores on several tests or subtests using a common scale. See also CRIMINAL PROFILING. profiler $n$. One who creates profiles. \[From Italian profilo a profile, from Latin pro forth + filare to spin or draw a line, from filum a thread]
