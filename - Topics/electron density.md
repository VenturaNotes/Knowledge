## Source [^1]
- (1) The number of electrons per unit mass of a material. 
- (2) The number of electrons per unit volume of a material. See CARRIER CONCENTRATION. 
- (3) (equivalent electron density) The product of the ion density in an ionized gas and the ratio of the electron mass to the mass of a gas ion.
