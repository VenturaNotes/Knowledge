## Source [^1]
- adj. Pertaining to any musical scale that contains seven notes or tones, separated by five intervals of a tone (2) and two intervals of a semitone, the most familiar examples being the major and minor scales of Western music. Compare chromatic (2). \[From Latin diatonicus diatonic, from Greek diatonikos diatonic, from dia between + tonos a tone + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
