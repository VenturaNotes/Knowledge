## Source [^1]
- (LDCs) Also referred to as developing countries, defined as such according to a variety of indicators including income, literacy, and life expectancy. According to the United Nations classification, these are the countries that comprise all regions of Africa, Asia (excluding Japan), Latin America, and the Caribbean, plus Melanesia, Micronesia, and Polynesia. Sometimes this is extended to include the Transcaucasian and Central Asian republics of the former Soviet Union.
