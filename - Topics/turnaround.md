## Source [^1]
- (recovery) A strategy designed to reverse the decline in profitability of a firm or subsidiary, enabling it to achieve a viable and sustainable future. Firms in crisis require drastic action to effect a turnaround, which may involve managerial changes, redundancies (see DOWNSIZING), or closure to avoid receivership. See SHARPBENDER.
