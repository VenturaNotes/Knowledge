## Source [^1]
- $n$. A property of nonsense syllables and other materials that are themselves meaningless of being, to varying degrees, suggestive of meaningful words or ideas. For example, according to empirical studies, the nonsense syllables BAL, FUT, and MOD have very high association value, whereas CIJ, VAF, and XEJ have very low association value.
