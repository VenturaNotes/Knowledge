## Source [^1]
- $n$. (Of or relating to) anything, especially a psychotropic drug, that induces or tends to induce euphoria. See anabolic Steroid, ANTIDEPRESSANT, ANXIOLYTIC, APHRODISIAC, BETEL, CANNABIS, CATNIP, EMPATHOGEN, HALLUCINOGEN, KAVA, MEPHEDRONE, MESCALINE, NARCOTIC, OPIUM, PHANTASTICANT, PSYCHEDELIC, STIMULANT DRUG. \[From Greek euphoria good ability to endure, from eu good + pherein to bear]
