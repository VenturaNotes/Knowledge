## Source [^1]
- $n$. the presence in the blood of the nucleated precursors of the red blood cells (erythroblasts). This may occur when there is an increase in the rate of red cell production, as in haemorrhagic or haemolytic anaemia, or in infiltrations of the bone marrow by tumors, etc.
