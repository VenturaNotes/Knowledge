## Source [^1]
- A plant life form in Raunkiaer's system of classification (see phySiOGNOMY). Therophytes are annual plants that complete their life cycle in a short period when conditions are favorable and survive harsh conditions as seeds. They are typically found in deserts and other arid regions.
