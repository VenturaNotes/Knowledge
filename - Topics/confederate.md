## Source [^1]
- $n$. A person taking part in a conspiracy. In research methodology, an accomplice of the experimenter in research involving deception, pretending to be an experimental participant or subject, along with the real participants, but actually playing out an assigned role. Also called a stooge . Compare NAIVE PARTICIPANT.
