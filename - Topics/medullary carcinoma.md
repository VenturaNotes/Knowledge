## Source [^1]
- a tumor whose consistency was thought to resemble that of bone marrow. Medullary carcinoma of the thyroid has associations with tumors of other organs (multiple endocrine neoplasia; see MENS) and is often familial: it arises from the $\mathrm{C}$ cells of the thyroid and produces calcitonin, which can often be used as a tumor marker.
