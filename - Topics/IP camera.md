## Source [^1]
- A digital video camera that is connected to the Internet. The term is used almost exclusively for cameras used for security and surveillance. They have the advantage over closed-circuit television systems in that it is possible to send audio back to the camera, so a remote operator can speak to any individual they can observe. The term webcam is more general and is used to refer to IP cameras used for other purposes.
