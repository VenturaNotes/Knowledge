## Source [^1]
- Stomata is plural
- It's a tiny pore or opening found on the surface of plant leaves, primarily on the underside, which is surrounded by specialized cells called [[guard cell|guard cells]] and allows for the exchange of gases like carbon dioxide and oxygen between the plant and its environment
- Enables gas exchange necessary for [[photosynthesis]] (crucial for plant survival)
