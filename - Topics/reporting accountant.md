## Source [^1]
- (1) An accountant or firm of accountants who report on the financial information provided in a prospectus. They may or may not be the company's own auditors. It is usual for reporting accountants to have had previous experience of new issues and the preparation of prospectuses. 
- (2) See AUDIT EXEMPTION.
