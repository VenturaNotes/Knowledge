## Source [^1]
- $n$. any abnormality of the macula of the eye. For example, bull's-eye maculopathy describes the appearance of the macula in some toxic conditions (e.g. chloroquine toxicity) and in some hereditary disorders of the macula. See also EPIRETINAL MEMBRANE.
