## Source [^1]
- (ROC) Given a Laplace transform of the form$$F(s) = \int_{0}^{+\infty} e^{-st} f(t) dt$$this is the range of values for $s$ for which the integral on the right-hand side converges. Typically, this range is determined by the real part of the complex number $s$.
