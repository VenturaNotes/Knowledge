## Source [^1]
- (1) In robotics, a technique used to plan robot motion. It is based on a similar technique in animation, where certain ‘key frames’ are created by hand to identify key points in the movement; intermediate frames are then interpolated automatically to give the full motion. 
- (2) In video image compression, occasional full frames are interspersed with partial difference frames. See DATA COMPRESSION.
