## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) The specialized language associated with a particular profession or field of activity. See also psychobabble, REGISTER (2). 
- (2) In developmental psychology, an unintelligible but well-articulated utterance of a child learning to speak. 
- (3) Meaningless gibberish. See also JARGON APHASIA. \[French jargon, origin obscure]
