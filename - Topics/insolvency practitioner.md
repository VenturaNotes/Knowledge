## Source [^1]
- A person authorized to undertake insolvency administration as a liquidator, provisional liquidator, administrator, administrative receiver, or nominee or supervisor under a voluntary arrangement. Insolvency practitioners must be members of an approved professional body, such as the Insolvency Practitioners Association or the Institute of Chartered Accountants.
