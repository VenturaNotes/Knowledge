## Source [^1]
- $n$. A paraphilic disorder in DSM-5 characterized by intense, recurrent sexual arousal from exposure of genitals to unsuspecting others, or associated fantasies or urges over a period of at least six months. To meet the diagnostic criteria, the individual must either have acted out this behavior with a non-consenting person, or the fantasies or urges must have caused clinically significant distress or impairment in social, occupational, or other areas of life. See also exhibitionism.
