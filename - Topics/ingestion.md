## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) the process by which food is taken into the alimentary canal. It involves chewing and swallowing. 
- (2) the process by which a phagocytic cell takes in solid material, such as bacteria.
## Source[^2]
- (feeding) A method of heterotrophic nutrition in which bulk food is taken into an organism and subsequently digested (see DIGESTION). Ingestion is the principal mechanism of animal nutrition. See also MACROPHAGOUS; MICROPHAGOUS.
