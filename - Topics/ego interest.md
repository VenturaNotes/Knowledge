## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, cathexis of the ego by ego instincts, in contrast to narcissism, which is cathexis of the ego by sexual instincts. Sigmund Freud (1856-1939) drew this distinction in 1915/17 in 'A Metapsychological Supplement to the Theory of Dreams' (Standard Edition, XIV, pp. 217-36, at p. 223).
