## Source [^1]
- A gene that is taken from one organism and inserted into the germ line of another organism, either of the same or a different species, so that it is replicated as part of the genome and present in all the recipient's cells. The resulting organism is described as transgenic. See GENETICALLY MODIFIED ORGANISMS.
