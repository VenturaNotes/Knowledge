## Source [^1]
- The creation of complex debt instruments by securitization or the addition of derivatives to existing instruments. Structured finance typically involves the pooling of assets, the tranching of liabilities (see TRANCHE), and the creation of special purpose vehicles to limit risk. The prevalence of structured finance products based on subprime mortgages is generally seen as a key factor precipitating the financial crisis of 2007-08.
