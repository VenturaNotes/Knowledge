## Source [^1]
- Any process in which free electrons encounter and fill free holes in a semiconductor, thereby releasing energy. Although there are quite a wide range of ways in which this can occur, these can be classified into two main groups. Processes in the first group release the energy in the form of photons. Those in the second involve the transfer of energy to another free carrier.
