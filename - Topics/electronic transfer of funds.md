## Source [^1]
- (ETF) The transfer of money from one bank account to another by means of computers and communications links. Banks routinely transfer funds between accounts using computers; ETF is also used in home banking services. See also ELECTRONIC FUNDS TRANSFER AT POINT OF SALE.
