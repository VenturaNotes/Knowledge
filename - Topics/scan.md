## Source[^2]
- (1) n. examination of the body or a part of the body using ultrasonography, computerized tomography (CT), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), or scintigraphy (see SCINTIGRAM). 
- (2) n. the image obtained from such an examination. 
- (3) $v b$. to examine the body using any of these techniques.
