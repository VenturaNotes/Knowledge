## Source [^1]
- (1) The current applied to the starter electrode of a glow-discharge tube (see GAS-DISCHARGE TUBE) in order to initiate a glow discharge across the main gap. 
- (2) The current at one electrode of a gas-filled tube that causes gas breakdown to occur at a different electrode.
