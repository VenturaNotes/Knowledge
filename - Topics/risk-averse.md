## Source [^1]
- Describing a risk preference in which a decision maker's attraction to an investment or other economic choice rises with higher average returns and falls with greater standard deviations of returns. Compare RISK-NEUTRAL; RISK-SEEKING. See also PORTFOLIO THEORY; UTILITY THEORY.
