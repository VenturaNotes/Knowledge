## Source [^1]
- (string galvanometer) A galvanometer that has a tightly strung conducting thread between the poles of a strong electromagnet. A current passed through the thread causes it to be deflected at right angles to the magnetic field. The deflection is observed with a high-power microscope. The instrument is highly sensitive and can detect currents of $10^{-11}$ ampere.
