## Source [^1]
- A sudden change in the energy state of an atom between two of its energy levels. A transition may be caused by absorption of energy, as in the photoelectric effect, or may occur when an atom in an excited state reverts to a state of lower energy. In the latter case it is accompanied by the emission of a photon of energy. Both these effects are utilized in the laser.
