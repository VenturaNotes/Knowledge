## Source [^1]
- In the International Monetary Fund classification these are countries characterized by high per capita income, diversified exports, and close integration into the global financial system. The 39 advanced economies include, among others, the G7 countries and all countries in the euro area. In 2015, according to the IMF, this group of economies contributed 42.4 per cent of world GDP, whereas their share in world population was only 14.6 per cent.
