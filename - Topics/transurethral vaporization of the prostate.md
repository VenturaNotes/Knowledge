## Source [^1]
- (TUVP) a technique that vaporizes (rather than resects) prostate tissue; it is associated with less bleeding during the procedure. TUVP is used to treat lower urinary tract symptoms thought to be due to benign prostatic hyperplasia (see PROSTATE GLAND) or urinary retention.
