## Source [^1]
- $n$. an analgesic drug that also reduces inflammation and fever (see NSAID). It is used to treat rheumatoid arthritis, dysmenorrhoea, and gout. Side-effects may include digestive upsets and rashes: gastric bleeding can be prevented by administering naproxen in combination with misoprostol.
