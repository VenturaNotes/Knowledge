## Source [^1]
- Electrons that impinge on a surface and cause secondary emission of electrons from the surface. The term is also sometimes used to describe electrons released from atoms by one of the processes of electron emission other than secondary emission. Such electrons however are more commonly described in terms of the relevant process of emission, as with thermionic electrons.
