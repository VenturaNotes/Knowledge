## Source [^1]
- $n$. an amino acid produced in the liver as a by-product during the conversion of ammonia to urea.
## Source[^2]
- (Orn) An amino acid, $\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{~N}\left(\mathrm{CH}_{2}\right)_{3} \mathrm{CH}\left(\mathrm{NH}_{2}\right) \mathrm{COOH}$, that is not a constituent of proteins but is important in living organisms as an intermediate in the reactions of the urea cycle and in arginine synthesis.
