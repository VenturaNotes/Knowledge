## Source [^1]
- adj. Of or relating to a phenomenon or something that is directly sensed or perceived, often (as in phenomenal regression) in contradistinction to the objective or physical reality giving rise to the sensation or perception. \[From Greek phainomenon a thing shown, from phainein to show + Latin -alis of or relating to]
