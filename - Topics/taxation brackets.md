## Source [^1]
- Figures between which taxable income or wealth is taxed at a specified rate. For example, in the UK taxable income up to $£ 30,000$ attracts tax at the basic rate of $20 \%$, whereas between $£ 32,000$ and $£ 150,000$ the higher rate of $40 \%$ applies (2016-2017). See also BRACKET INDEXATION.
