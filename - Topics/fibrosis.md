## Source [^1]
- $n$. thickening and scarring of connective tissue, most often a consequence of inflammation or injury. Pulmonary interstitial fibrosis is thickening and stiffening of the lining of the air sacs (alveoli) of the lungs, causing progressive breathlessness. See also CYSTIC FIBROSIS; RETROPERITONEAL FIBROSIS. $\textendash$fibrotic adj.
