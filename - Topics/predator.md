## Source [^1]
- An animal that obtains its food by predation. All predators are carnivores, although not all carnivores are predators.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Any species of animal that hunts and kills members of another species for food. Also called a carnivore. predation $n$. The hunting and killing for food by one animal species (the predator) of another (the prey). predatory or predacious adj. Habitually hunting and killing animals of other species for food. \[From Latin praedator a plunderer or hunter, from praeda booty]
