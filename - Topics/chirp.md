## Source [^1]
- A signal whose frequency changes uniformly with time, between determined low and high frequencies. Chirps are commonly used in sonar or radar systems to help improve both precision and range, two performance aspects that are usually involved in a trade-off. They are also used to implement spread spectrum transmission systems.
