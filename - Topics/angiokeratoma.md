## Source [^1]
- $n$. a localized collection of thin-walled blood vessels covered by a cap of warty material. It is most often seen as angiokeratoma of Fordyce, purple papules on the scrotum or vulva of the elderly, which should not be treated unless they bleed easily. Angiokeratomas can also occur on the hands and feet of children. The condition is not malignant and its cause is unknown. Angiokeratomas may be removed surgically. See also FABRY DISEASE.
