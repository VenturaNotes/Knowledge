## Source [^1]
- If heat flows through a strip of conductor or semiconductor that is placed in a magnetic field, where the heat flow and magnetic field are at right angles, a temperature gradient is set up in the strip, orthogonal to both heat flow and magnetic field directions. This is the thermal analogue of the Hall effect.
