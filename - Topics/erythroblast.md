## Source [^1]
- $n$. any of a series of nucleated cells (see NORMOBLAST; PROERYTHROBLAST) that pass through a succession of stages of maturation to form red blood cells (erythrocytes). Erythroblasts are normally present in the blood-forming tissue of the bone marrow, but they may appear in the circulation in a variety of diseases (see ERYTHROBLASTOSIS). See also ERYTHROPOIESIS.
