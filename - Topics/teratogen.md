## Source [^1]
- $n$. any substance, agent, or process that induces the formation of developmental abnormalities in a fetus. Known teratogens include such drugs as thalidomide and alcohol; such infections as German measles and cytomegalovirus; and also irradiation with X-rays and other ionizing radiation. ## Source[^2]
- Any environmental factor that acts on a fetus to cause congenital abnormality. Examples include ionizing radiation (e.g. X-rays), nutritional deficiencies, drugs (e.g. thalidomide), toxic chemicals, and virus infections (e.g. rubella).
