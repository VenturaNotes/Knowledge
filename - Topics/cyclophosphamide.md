## Source [^1]
- $n$. an alkylating agent used to treat a variety of cancers, including chronic lymphocytic leukaemia, lymphomas, and solid tumors, often in combination with other cytotoxic drugs. It also has immunosuppressant properties and is used in treating conditions, notably rheumatoid arthritis, requiring a reduced immune response. Common side-effects are nausea, vomiting, and $\textendash$ particularly at high doses $\textendash$ hair loss. See also MESNA.
