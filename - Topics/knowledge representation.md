## Source[^2]
- $n$. The encoding and storage of knowledge in computational models of cognition. It is a major branch of artificial intelligence and is also studied in cognitive psychology, logic, computer science, and linguistics. The representation of informal and intuitive human knowledge is one of its major unsolved problems.
