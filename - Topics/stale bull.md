## Source [^1]
- A dealer or speculator who has a long position in something, usually a commodity, which is showing a paper profit but which the dealer cannot realize as there are no buyers at the higher levels. Being fully committed financially and therefore unable to increase the bull position, the dealer may be unable to trade.
