## Source [^1]
- (1) A security offering a fixed interest and maturing on a fixed date. 
- (2) The final repayment of a loan, which consists of the whole of the sum borrowed. In a bullet loan, interim repayments are interest-only repayments, the principal sum being repaid in the final bullet. Compare AMORTIZING LOAN.
