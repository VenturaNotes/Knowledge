## Source [^1]
- To eliminate terms from an expression, usually through one of the four basic arithmetical operations, to produce a simplified form. For example, $x^{2}+3 x-2=x^{2}-2 x+8$ can be reduced $3 x-2=-2 x+8$ by cancelling $x^{2}$ from both sides, which is effectively subtracting from both sides.
