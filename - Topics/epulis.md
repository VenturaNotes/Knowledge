## Source [^1]
- $n$. a swelling on the gum or alveolar mucosa. There are three types: fibromatous (originating from the fibrous tissues in the mouth), ossifying (originating from fibrous and bony tissues), and acanthomatous (emerging from the periodontal ligament). A parulis is an epulis at the opening of a sinus tract.
