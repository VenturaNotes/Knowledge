## Source [^1]
- $n$. The swollen tip of an axon that forms a synapse (1) with part of another nerve fiber, muscle, or gland. Also called a synaptic terminal, terminal bouton, end foot, or end bulb. \[From French bouton a button, alluding to its appearance]
