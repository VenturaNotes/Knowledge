## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) a mental illness or a symptom of a mental illness characterized by delusions, sometimes organized into a system. The person believes him- or herself to be a target of persecution or the victim of a conspiracy. Antipsychotic medication is often beneficial. 
- (2) a state of mind in which the individual has a strong belief that he or she is persecuted by others and therefore displays behavior marked by suspiciousness. Severe paranoia can lead to the diagnosis of personality disorder. $\textemdash$paranoid adj.
