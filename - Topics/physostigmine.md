## Source [^1]
- $n$. An alkaloid extracted from the Calabar bean that functions as a reversible anticholinesterase drug, inhibiting the breakdown of acetylcholinesterase. Also called eserine. Formula $\mathrm{C}_{15} \mathrm{H}_{21} \mathrm{~N}_{3} \mathrm{O}_{2}$. \[From Latin Physostigma venenosum the Calabar bean plant, from Greek physa a bladder + stigma a mark]
