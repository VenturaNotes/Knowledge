## Source [^1]
- (1) A device for converting alternating current to direct current or vice versa. 
- (2) A device that changes the frequency of a signal; a frequency changer. 
- (3) A device, such as an impedance converter, that has different electrical properties at its input and output and may be used to couple dissimilar circuits. 
- (4) A device, such as a compiler, that changes an information code. 
- (5) A transducer that converts energy of one type, such as sound waves or electromagnetic radiation, into electrical energy.
