## Source [^1]
- (LTE) A registered trademark of the European Telecommunications Standards Institute for a standard for wireless communication for data and telephony that goes beyond current 3G standards. Commonly referred to as 4G LTE, it uses a different part of the frequency spectrum from that currently used for 2G and 3G telephony.
