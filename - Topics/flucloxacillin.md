## Source [^1]
- $n$. a semisynthetic penicillin that is effective against bacteria producing penicillinase and is used to treat infections caused by penicillinase-producing staphylococci. Possible side-effects are allergic reactions to penicillin, gastrointestinal disturbances, and (rarely) liver damage.
