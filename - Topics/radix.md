## Source [^1]
- (base) The number of distinct digits in a fixed-radix number system. These digits represent the integers in the range zero to one less than the radix. The radix of a number can be indicated by means of a subscript, as in $24_{8}$ or $101_{2}$. See also BINARY SYSTEM, HEXADECIMAL NOTATION, OCTAL NOTATION.
