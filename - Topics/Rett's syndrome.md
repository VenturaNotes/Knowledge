## Source [^1]
- a disorder affecting young girls, in which stereotyped movements (see STEREOTYPY) and social withdrawal appear during early childhood. Intellectual development is often impaired and special educational help is needed. The condition is inherited as an X-linked (see SEX-LINKED) dominant characteristic. \[A. Rett (1924-97), Austrian paediatrician]
