## Source [^1]
- $n$. A misshapen and ugly somatotype believed by the German psychiatrist Ernst Kretschmer (1888-1964) and his followers to be prone to schizophrenia. See also Kretschmer Constitutional type. Compare asthenic body type, athletic body type, pyknic body type. \[From Greek dys- bad or abnormal + plastikos mouldable, from plassein to form + -itikos resembling or marked by]
