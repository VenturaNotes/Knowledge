## Source [^1]
- $n$. The smallest dosage of a drug that produces signs or symptoms of toxicity in an organism. Also called minimal toxic dosage. See also therapeutic index, therapeutic ratio, toxic dosage. Compare MEDIAN EFFECTIVE DOSAGE, MEDIAN LETHAL DOSAGE, MEDIAN TOXIC DOSAGE, MINIMUM EFFECTIVE DOSAGE, MINIMUM LETHAL DOSAGE. MTD abbrev.
