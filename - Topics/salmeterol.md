## Source [^1]
- $n$. a sympathomimetic drug: a long-acting beta agonist used, with inhaled corticosteroids, as a bronchodilator to control chronic asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. Possible side-effects include palpitations, a paradoxical worsening of the condition, and a lowering of the blood potassium level.
