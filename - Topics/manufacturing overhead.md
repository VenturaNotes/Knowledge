## Source [^1]
- (factory overhead; production overhead) The costs of production that cannot be traced directly to the product or cost unit. Apart from the direct costs, all other costs incurred in the manufacturing process are the manufacturing overhead; examples include depreciation of machinery, factory rent and business rates, cleaning materials, and maintenance expenses.
