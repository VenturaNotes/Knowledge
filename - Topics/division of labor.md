## Source [^1]
- When we divide and subdivide the tasks involved with producing a good or service, workers and businesses can produce a greater quantity of output
	- This means a group of workers specializing in certain tasks can produce much more than the same number of workers who try to produce the entire good or service by themselves
	- [[Adam Smith]]'s reasoning:
		- (1) [[Specialization]] in a particular small job allows workers to focus on the parts of the production process where they have an advantage
