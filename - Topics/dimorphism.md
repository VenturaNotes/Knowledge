## Source [^1]
- The existence of two distinctly different types of individual within a species. An obvious example is sexual dimorphism in certain animals, in which the two sexes differ in coloring, size, etc. Dimorphism also occurs in some lower plants, such as mosses and ferns, that show an alternation of generations.
