## Synthesis
- A mathematical equation that defines a sequence of values in terms of previous terms in the sequence.
	- Provides a way to compute each term of the sequence using one or more of the earlier terms, along with a starting condition (called the [[initial condition]])
## Source[^1]
- A recursive definition without the initial conditions
