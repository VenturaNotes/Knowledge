## Source [^1]
- $n$. the application of the principles of nutrition to the selection of food and the feeding of individuals and groups. It is practised by dietitians, who use the most up-to-date public health and scientific research on food, health, and disease, which they translate into practical guidance to enable people to make appropriate lifestyle and food choices.
