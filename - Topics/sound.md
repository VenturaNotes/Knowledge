## Source [^1]
- (in surgery) 
- (1) n. a long rodlike instrument, often with a curved end, used to explore body cavities (such as the bladder) or to dilate stricture(s) in the urethra or other canals. 
- (2) $v b$. to explore a cavity using a sound.
