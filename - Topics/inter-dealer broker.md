## Source [^1]
- (1) A broker who acts as an intermediary between buyers and sellers in the interbank market or bond market; he or she typically uses voice broking to maintain constant contact with potential customers and to establish deals and prices. 
- (2) A specialized brokerage firm that acts in the same capacity. The leading such firm is the London-based ICAP.
