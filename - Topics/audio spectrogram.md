## Source [^1]
- A graph that plots how the spectrum of an audio signal varies over time. The horizontal $x$-axis represents time, while the vertical $y$-axis denotes frequency. A dot marks the presence of a corresponding frequency at the corresponding time, with the brightness, colour, or size of the dot giving an indication of the intensity of that frequency.
