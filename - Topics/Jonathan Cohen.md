## Synthesis
- A neuroscientist and psychiatrist, also a professor at Princeton University, and director of the Princeton Neuroscience Institute. His research focuses on the cognitive neuroscience of executive function, cognitive control, and decision-making, often utilizing computational modeling, functional neuroimaging (fMRI), and behavioral studies. He is a key figure in the development of computational models that link neural activity to cognitive processes.
