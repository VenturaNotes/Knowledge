## Source [^1]
- A curve or surface that is tangential to every curve or surface in a family. For example, if a family of circles has radius $a$ and center at a distance $b > a$ from a fixed point $C$, the envelope will be an annulus with the two circles having radii $b+a$ and $b-a$.
