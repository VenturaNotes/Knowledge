## Source [^1]
- A set of entities that are partially ordered; the word is frequently misused. See PARTIAL ORDERING.
## Source[^2]
- A type of social organization in which individuals are ranked according to their status or dominance relative to other group members. This affects their behavior in various ways, e.g. by determining their access to food or to mates. Many vertebrate animals and some invertebrates live in hierarchical social groups.
