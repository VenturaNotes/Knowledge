## Source [^1]
- a strong corrosive mineral acid, $\mathrm{HNO}_{3}$, the concentrated form of which is capable of producing severe burns of the skin. Swallowing the acid leads to intense burning pain and ulceration of the mouth and throat. Treatment is by immediate administration of alkaline solutions, followed by milk or olive oil.
