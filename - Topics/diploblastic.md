## Source [^1]
- Describing an animal whose early embryo has only two cell layers, an outer ectoderm and inner endoderm. These give rise to the tissues and organs of the mature animal. Diploblastic animals include the cnidarians, ctenophorans, and placozoans. Compare TRIPLOBLASTIC.
