## Source [^1]
- (Galois field) A (mathematical) field with a finite number of elements. The number of elements must be of the form $p^{k}$ where $p$ is some prime number and $k$ is a positive integer. Results concerning finite fields are of particular relevance in the areas of error detection and error correction.
