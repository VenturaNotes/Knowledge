## Source [^1]
- $n$. In the theory of cognitive development of the Swiss psychologist Jean Piaget (1896-1980), the developmental stage beginning at about 12 years when children become proficient in manipulating their internal representations of abstract concepts as well as concrete objects. See also mental OPERATION. Compare CONCRETE OPERATIONS, PRE-OPERATIONAL STAGE, SENSORIMOTOR STAGE.
