## Source [^1]
- $n$. Excessive and sometimes debilitating attention to trivial matters, such as the sound of a dripping tap. Compare aProsexia, hypoprosexia. \[From Greek hyper over + prosexis heedfulness, from prosechein to heed + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
