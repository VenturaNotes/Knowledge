## Source [^1]
- (HIP) The cognitive processes involved in thinking, remembering, interpreting, and decision making. The importance of HIP to business is that an understanding of the way in which people use information in the decision-making process should make it possible to determine the most appropriate information to be provided and the most suitable form.
