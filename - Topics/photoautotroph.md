## Source [^1]
- An autotrophic organism, such as a green plant or a photosynthetic bacterium, that synthesizes its organic materials from inorganic components using energy derived from the sun (solar energy) in the process of photosynthesis. See AUTOTROPHIC NUTRITION; PHOTOTROPH.
