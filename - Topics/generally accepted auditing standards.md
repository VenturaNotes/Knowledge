## Source [^1]
- (GAAS) In the USA, the broad rules and guidelines set down by the Auditing Standards Board of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA). In carrying out audit work for a client, a certified public accountant is also obliged to apply generally accepted accounting principles.
