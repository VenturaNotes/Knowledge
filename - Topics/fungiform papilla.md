## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of a large number of flat-topped, mushroomshaped, reddish-colored papillae on the upper surface of the tongue, most of which are studded with taste buds around their bases. See also SUPERTASTER. Compare CIRCUMVALLATE PAPILLA, FILOFORM PAPILLA, FOLIATE PAPILLA. fungiform papillae $p l$. \[From Latin fungus a mushroom + forma a shape]
