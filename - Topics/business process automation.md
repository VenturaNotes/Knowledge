## Source [^1]
- BPA is the automation of advanced business processes and functions. Companies can automate manual tasks, improve productivity, engagement, customer service, and other responsibilities. BPA allows managers to trigger workflows automatically, often without scripting. This method, therefore, can reduce manual labor and cut costs across an organization.
