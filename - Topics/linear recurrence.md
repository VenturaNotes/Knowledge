## Source [^1]
- A relationship that defines the next term in a sequence in the form of sums and differences of multiples of earlier terms in the sequence. For example,$$\begin{aligned}& a_{r+1}=2 a_{r}+1 \\& b_{r+1}+2 b_{r}-b_{r-1}=0\end{aligned}$$See also RECURRENCE.
