## Source [^1]
- $n$. The most distant point from the eye at which an object is focused on the retina without accommodation (1) of the crystalline lens, theoretically located at infinity for a normal eye but in practice usually set as 6 metres or 20 feet. See also range of accommodation. Compare NEAR POINT.
