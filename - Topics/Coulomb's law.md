## Source [^1]
- The mutual force, $F$, between two electrostatic point charges, $q_1$ and $q_2$, that results from the interaction of the electrostatic fields surrounding them is given by$$F = \frac{q_1 q_2}{4 \pi \varepsilon r^2}$$where $r$ is the distance between the charges and $\varepsilon$ the permittivity of the medium. Coulomb's law thus relates electrical and mechanical phenomena. See also AMPERE'S LAW.
