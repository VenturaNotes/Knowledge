## Source [^1]
- (AVMS; administered strategic alliance) A vertical marketing system in which a powerful channel captain (quite often a major retailing chain) coordinates marketing activities at all levels in the channel, including planning and management, even though it does not directly own the other channel members. Compare CONTRACTUAL VMS; CORPORATE VMS.
