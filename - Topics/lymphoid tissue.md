## Source [^1]
- a tissue responsible for the production of lymphocytes and antibodies. It occurs as discrete organs, in the form of the lymph nodes, tonsils, thymus, and spleen, and also as diffuse groups of cells not separated from surrounding tissue. See also IMMUNE SYSTEM.
## Source[^2]
- The type of tissue found in the lymph nodes, tonsils, spleen, and thymus. It is responsible for producing lymphocytes and therefore contributes to the body's defence against infection. See also BRONCHIAL-ASSOCIATED LYMPHOID TISSUE; GUT-ASSOCIATED LYMPHOID TISSUE; MUCOSAL-ASSOCIATED LYMPHOID TISSUE
