## Source [^1]
- A linear amplifier in which output current flows over the whole of the input current cycle, i.e. the angle of flow equals $2\pi$. These amplifiers have low distortion but low efficiency. Distortion can occur with large-signal operation due to the device transfer characteristics becoming nonlinear (see diagram).
