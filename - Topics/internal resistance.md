## Source [^1]
- (symbol: $r$; unit: ohm) A small resistance possessed by a cell, accumulator, or dynamo. It is given by$$r = \frac {(E - V)}{I}$$where $E$ is the e.m.f. generated by the device, $V$ is the potential difference across the terminals, and $I$ is the current. See also CELL.
