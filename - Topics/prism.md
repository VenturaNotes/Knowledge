## Source [^1]
- $n$. A transparent solid, usually with triangular or wedge-shaped cross-section and rectangular sides, through which white light is diffracted into a spectrum. It is also used to reflect rays of light, as in inverting spectacles. Compare diffraction grating. \[From Greek prisma something sawn off, from prizein to saw]
