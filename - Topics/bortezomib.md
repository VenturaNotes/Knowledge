## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cytotoxic drug that works by inhibiting proteasomes and causing death of malignant cells. It is used alone in the treatment of relapsed myeloma and in combination with other agents for the first-line treatment of myeloma. Side-effects include peripheral neuropathy, thrombocytopenia, and nausea.
