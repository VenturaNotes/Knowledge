## Source [^1]
- $n$. A synthetic opiate drug, being a narcotic analgesic that is less addictive than morphine and is sometimes taken as a street drug. Also called Talwin or Fortral (trademark). Formula: $\mathrm{C}_{19} \mathrm{H}_{27} \mathrm{NO}$. Compare PHENAZOCINE. \[From Greek pente five + azocine a chemical compound]
