## Source [^1]
- $n$. the study of the effects of radiation on living cells and organisms. Studies of the behavior of cancer cells exposed to radiation have important applications in radiotherapy, revealing why some tumors fail to respond to the treatment; this has led to the development of new radiotherapy techniques that make tumors more susceptible to treatment by radiation.
