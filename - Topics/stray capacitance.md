## Source [^1]
- Any capacitance in an electronic circuit or device that is due to interconnections, electrodes, or the proximity of elements in the circuit and is additional to the intentional capacitance of the circuit or device. Stray capacitance is usually unwanted but can sometimes be utilized as part of the tuning of a tuned circuit.
