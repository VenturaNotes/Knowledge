## Source [^1]
- $n$. A type of motor neuron disease beginning in middle age and progressing rapidly, causing death within five years, characterized by weakness and atrophy of the muscles initially of the hands, forearms, and legs, but spreading to most of the body and face, with dysarthria and dysphagia. Also called Lou Gehrig's disease. ALS abbrev. \[From Greek $a$ - without + mys muscle + trophe nourishment]
