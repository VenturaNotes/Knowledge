## Source [^1]
- A term that was used to describe the four largest UK 'high street' banks, namely Barclays, Lloyds, HSBC (formerly the Midland), and NatWest. The term is now more commonly applied to the four largest accounting firms: PricewaterhouseCoopers, Deloitte, Ernst and Young, and KPMG.
