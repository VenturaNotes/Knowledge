## Source [^1]
- A shunt capacitor that is used to provide a comparatively low impedance path for alternating current. The magnitude of the capacitance determines the frequency that it passes. Such a capacitor is used to prevent a.c. signals reaching a particular point in a circuit, or to separate out a desired a.c. component.
