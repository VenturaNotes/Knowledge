## Source [^1]
- A strategy that makes use of a company's salesforce and trade promotion activities to create consumer demand for a product. The producer promotes the product to wholesalers, the wholesalers promote it to retailers, and the retailers promote it to consumers. Thus, the product will be pushed through the distribution channel. Compare PULL STRATEGY.
