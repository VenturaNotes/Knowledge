## Source [^1]
- (RMR) The dry mass of the roots of a plant divided by the total dry mass of the entire plant, typically measured in grams per gram or kilograms per kilogram. This reflects the proportion of resources that a plant allocates to its root system, which varies with such factors as species, growing conditions, and season.
