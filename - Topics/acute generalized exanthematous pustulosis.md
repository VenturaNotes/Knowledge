## Source [^1]
- (toxic pustuloderma) a reaction to a medication, resulting in the appearance of fine sterile pustules on inflamed skin; the pustules may easily be overlooked. Common causes include penicillins, and pustular psoriasis must be excluded from the diagnosis.
