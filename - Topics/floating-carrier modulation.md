## Source [^1]
- (controlled-carrier modulation) A type of amplitude modulation in which the amplitude of the carrier wave does not remain constant but is automatically varied in a manner dependent upon the amplitude of the modulating wave, which is averaged over a short time period. The modulation factor therefore remains substantially constant.
