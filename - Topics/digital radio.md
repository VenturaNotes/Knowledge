## Source [^1]
- A radio transmission system that will replace amplitude modulation (AM) and frequency modulation (FM) broadcasts. Digital radio is made up of a single block of frequencies called a multiplex. The multiplex is able to carry stereo- and mono-radio channels as well as services, such as text and data. It is possible to carry more services on one frequency, allowing the spectrum to be used more efficiently. This spectrum is called Band III in the UK.
