## Source [^1]
- $n$. one of the cells of the glia, responsible for producing the myelin sheaths of the neurons of the central nervous system and therefore equivalent to the Schwann cells of the peripheral nerves.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A type of neuroglial cell with few dendrites and few branches, responsible for wrapping myelin sheaths around axons in the central nervous system. Also called oligodendroglia. Compare Schwann CELL. \[From Greek oligos few or little + dendron a tree + kytos a vessel]
