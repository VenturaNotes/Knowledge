## Source [^1]
- soft fluffy spots in the retina resulting from accumulations of axoplasm in the nerve-fibre layer of the retina. These may indicate diseases causing hypoxia (oxygen deficiency) in the nervefibre layer (e.g. diabetes, hypertension, connective-tissue disease, or AIDS).
