## Source [^1]
- (second-order system) A circuit whose behavior is described by a second-order differential equation. Such circuits will contain two energy-storage elements (such as capacitors or inductors) that cannot be reduced to a single equivalent element. Compare FIRST-ORDER CIRCUIT.
