## Source [^1]
- $n$. A term used by educational psychologists in the UK since the 1980s to refer to intellectual disability; thus moderate learning difficulties corresponds approximately to moderate intellectual disability, severe learning difficulties to severe intellectual disability, and profound learning difficulties to profound mental retardation. Compare LEARNING DISABILITY, SPECIFIC LEARNING DISORDER.
