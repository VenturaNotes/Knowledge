## Source [^1]
- (EV) A statistical measure of central value often used in decision making. It is the sum of the possible outcomes in quantitative terms, such as units of output or sales, weights, or volumes, multiplied by the respective probabilities of these outcomes arising. Compare EXPECTED MONETARY VALUE.
