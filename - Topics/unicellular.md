## Source [^1]
- adj. describing organisms or tissues that consist of a single cell. Unicellular organisms include the protozoans, most bacteria, and some fungi.
## Source[^2]
- Describing tissues, organs, or organisms consisting of a single cell. For example, the reproductive organs of some algae and fungi are unicellular. Unicellular organisms include archaea, bacteria, protists, and certain fungi and algae. Compare ACELLULAR; MULTICELLULAR.
