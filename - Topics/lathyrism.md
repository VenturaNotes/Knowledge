## Source [^1]
- $n$. a disease, characterized by muscular weakness and paralysis, found among people whose staple diet consists mostly of large quantities of Lathyrus sativus, a kind of chick pea, and/or vetches and pulses related to it. Except in mild cases complete recovery does not occur, despite administration of an adequate diet and physiotherapy.
