## Source [^1]
- Talent acquisition refers to the method by which businesses will recruit new talent for their operations. This may be a formal or informal process. However, larger businesses typically have entire departments dedicated to the area of talent acquisition. It is a vitally important part of a business. and often operates under a larger human resources umbrella.
