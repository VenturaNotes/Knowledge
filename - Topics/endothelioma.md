## Source [^1]
- $n$. any tumor arising from or resembling endothelium. It may arise from the linings of blood or lymph vessels (haemangioendothelioma and lymphangioendothelioma respectively); from the linings of the pleural cavity or the peritoneal cavity (see MESOTHELIOMA); or from the meninges (see MENINGIOMA).
