## Source [^1]
- $n$. a $5 \mathrm{HT}_{1}$ agonist that is effective in the treatment of acute migraine and cluster headaches. Possible side-effects include sensations of tingling and tightness, dizziness, drowsiness, fatigue, chest pain, and a rise in blood pressure.
