## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in anatomy) a veil-like covering. The medullary velum is either of two thin layers of tissue that form part of the roof of the fourth ventricle of the brain.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. vela) 
- (1) A membranous or veil-like structure, such as certain sheets of white matter in the brain. 
- (2) See annulus; velamen.
