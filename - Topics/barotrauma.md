## Synthesis
- Physical injury to tissues caused by changes in air or water pressure, affecting areas like the ears, sinuses, and lungs, and can occur during activities such as flying or scuba diving.
## Source [^1]
- $n$. damage to the ear, Eustachian tube, or paranasal sinuses caused by changes in ambient pressure, as experienced during air flight or deep-sea diving.
