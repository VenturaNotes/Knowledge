## Source [^1]
- $n$. a protein complex that enhances the biosynthesis and secretion of follicle-stimulating hormone and helps to regulate the menstrual cycle. Inhibin is closely related and exerts an opposite effect: it down-regulates FSH synthesis and inhibits FSH secretion.
