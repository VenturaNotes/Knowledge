## Source [^1]
- The value of imports when they enter a country. C.i.f. value is the value of goods when they reach the port of entry to their country of destination: it includes their purchase price in the country of origin, and the freight and insurance costs of shipping them to a foreign port. C.i.f. does not include import duty or costs of transport within the country of destination.
