## Source [^1]
- $n$. failure of urine production. Urgent assessment is required to determine the cause, which can be failure of the renal or prerenal stages of urine production or obstruction of urine outflow from the kidneys. Anuria is associated with increasing uraemia and may require haemodialysis.
