## Source [^1]
- $n$. the long thin outer bone of the lower leg. The head of the fibula articulates with the tibia just below the knee; the lower end projects laterally as the lateral malleolus, which articulates with one side of the talus. $\textendash$fibular adj.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. fibulas or fibulae) The smaller and outer of the two bones between the knee and the ankle in terrestrial vertebrates. Compare TIBIA.
