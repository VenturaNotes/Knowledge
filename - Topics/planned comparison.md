## Source [^1]
- $n$. A statistical test of a difference between two means, usually carried out after analysis of variance but planned before the data were collected, and therefore validly using a $t$ test. Also called an $a$ priori test. See also MULTIPLE COMPARISONS. Compare A POSTERIORI TEST.
