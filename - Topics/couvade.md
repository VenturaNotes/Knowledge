## Source [^1]
- $n$. A custom in many tribal societies according to which the father of an unborn child behaves as if he were also bearing the child, takes to bed, experiences a sympathetic pregnancy, and is treated as if he were pregnant. It is sometimes considered an example of Munchausen's syndrome. \[From French couver to hatch, from Latin cubare to lie down]
