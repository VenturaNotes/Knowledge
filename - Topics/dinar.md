## Source [^1]
- (1) (DZD) The standard monetary unit of Algeria, divided into 100 centimes. 
- (2) The standard monetary unit of Bahrain (BHD), Iraq (IQD), Jordan (JOD), and Kuwait (KWD), divided into 1,000 fils. 
- (3) (TND) The standard monetary unit of Tunisia, divided into 1,000 millimes. 
- (4) (RSD) The standard monetary unit of Serbia, divided into 100 paras. 
- (5) (LYD) The standard monetary unit of Libya, divided into 1,000 dirhams.
