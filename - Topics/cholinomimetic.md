## Source [^1]
- adj. n. (Of or relating to) any substance, such as carbachol or methacholine, that mimics the action of acetylcholine in the nervous system. See also muscarine, nicotine. \[From (acetyl)choline + Greek mimesis imitation, from mimeisthai to imitate]
