## Source [^1]
- $n$. Surgical severing of a cerebral commissure linking the two hemispheres of the brain, usually the corpus callosum, performed on humans as a treatment for chronic, generalized epilepsy. Also called commissurectomy . See split-brain preparation. See also disCONNECTION SYndROME. \[From Latin commissura a connection + Greek tome a cut]
