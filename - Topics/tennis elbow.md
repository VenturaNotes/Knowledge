## Source [^1]
- a painful condition causing degeneration of the origin of the common extensor tendon on the lateral epicondyle of the humerus, due to overuse of the forearm muscles. Treatment is by rest, massage, and local corticosteroid injection. If the symptoms do not settle, surgery may be required. See also TENDINITIS. Compare GOLFER'S ELBOW.
