## Source [^1]
- $n$. A surgical preparation in which the central nervous system is severed between the bottom of the medulla oblongata and the top of the spinal cord. In cats operated on in this way, a normal sleep-wake cycle has been observed in their EEGs. Compare CERVEAU ISolé. \[From French encéphale the brain + isolé isolated]
