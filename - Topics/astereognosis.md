## Source [^1]
- $n$. Inability to identify or recognize objects by touch, often resulting from a lesion in the somatosensory association area (see SOMATOSENSORY CORTEX). The unimpaired ability is called stereognosis. \[From Greek $a$- without + stereos solid + gnosis knowledge]
