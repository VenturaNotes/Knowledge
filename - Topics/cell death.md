## Synthesis
- It's when a cell ceases to function and is eventually eliminated from the body
- It is a natural and essential part of many biological processes, including:
	- Development
		- Cell death occurs during embryonic development to shape organs and tissues
	- Aging
		- As cells accumulate damage over time, they may undergo [[programmed cell death]]
	- Immune Functions
	- Tissue repair
## Source [^1]
- The event of a biological cell ceasing to carry out its functions
