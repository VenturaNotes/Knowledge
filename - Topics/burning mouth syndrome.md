## Source [^1]
- (BMS) a disorder characterized by a burning sensation in the mouth for which there is no obvious medical or dental cause. Other symptoms may include thirst, sore throat, and an unpleasant taste. BMS occurs most commonly in older females and may be related to menopause, stress, or vitamin deficiencies.
