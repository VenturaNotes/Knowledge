## Source [^1]
- $n$. A figure of speech in which a word or phrase is stated to mean something that it resembles but does not literally denote, as in Life is just a bowl of cherries (song title) or Life is just a bowl of cheerios (graffito). See also condensation. Compare simile. \[From Greek meta beside or beyond + pherein to carry]
