## Source [^1]
- $n$. an excessive involvement with oneself and one's self-importance, often to cover insecurity. In Freudian terms it is a state in which the ego has taken itself as a love object. Some degree of narcissism is present in most individuals, but when it is shown to an extreme degree it may become a personality disorder. $\textemdash$narcissistic adj.
