## Source [^1]
- a graph, customized to a pregnant woman's height, weight, and other factors, that plots fundal height and estimated fetal weight on ultrasound against weeks of gestation. The graph, which shows centile lines (see CENTILE CHART), improves prediction of a baby who is small for gestational age.
