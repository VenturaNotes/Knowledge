## Source [^1]
- A varactor diode in which the doping of the semiconductor is controlled to produce a variation of capacitance, $C$, with voltage, $V$ , that results in a linear variation of frequency with voltage when the varactor is used in a tuned circuit: $C \propto V^2$ . In comparison, in uniformly doped diodes, $C \propto \sqrt{V}$ .
