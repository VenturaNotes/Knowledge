## Source [^1]
- A chemical reaction in which two organic groups, $R_1$ and $R_2$, are joined together using a metal catalyst to form a compound $R_1\textendash R_2$, which is linked by a carbon-carbon bond. Examples include the Heck reaction and the Suzuki reaction.
