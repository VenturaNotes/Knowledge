## Source [^1]
- $n$. an increase in the diameter of blood vessels, especially arteries. This results from local vasodilator hormones (e.g. prostaglandins) or activation of the vasomotor center in the brain, which bring about relaxation of the arterial walls and a consequent lowering of blood pressure.
