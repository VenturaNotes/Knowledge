## Synthesis
- An algorithm used to perform encryption and decryption
- It transforms [[plaintext]] (readable data) into [[ciphertext]] (encoded data) to secure it from unauthorized access
## Source[^1]
- (1) An algorithm employed for encryption, or, in its inverse form, for decryption. See CRYPTOGRAPHY. 
- (2) An encrypted message.
