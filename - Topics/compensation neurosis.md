## Source [^1]
- $n$. A collection of symptoms presented by a person who has the prospect of receiving financial compensation for an industrial injury, failed surgical operation, car accident, or the like, susceptible to being interpreted as an attempt to profit from the incident. Compare FACTITIOUS DISORDER, MALINGERING.
