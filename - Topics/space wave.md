## Source [^1]
- A radiowave that travels between a transmitting and a receiving antenna situated above the ground and that includes the direct wave and the ground-reflected wave. It is the component of the ground wave that does not travel along the surface of the earth. If the two antennas are placed at a sufficient height above the ground the surface wave is negligible and only the space wave needs to be considered.
