## Source [^1]
- (fund raising under the guise of research) The unethical practice of deliberate deception by fund raisers masquerading as market researchers. Respondents are often invited to take part in a survey as a lead-in to a presentation, or as an attempt to obtain information that could be used for donor leads or mailing lists. Compare SUGGING.
