## Source [^1]
- $n$. In the back-propagation algorithm, adjustment of the weights of connections between units as a function of the discrepancies between actual output and target output as determined by a teacher unit. Also called the Widrow-Hoff rule. See also connectionism (1), Hebbian rule.
