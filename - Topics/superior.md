## Source [^1]
- adj. (in anatomy) situated uppermost in the body in relation to another structure or surface.
## Source[^2]
- Describing a structure that is positioned above or higher than another structure in the body. For example, in flowering plants the ovary is described as superior when located above the other organs of the flower (see HYPOGYNY). Compare INFERIOR.
