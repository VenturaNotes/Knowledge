## Source [^1]
- A 3D imaging technique in which light patterns, typically lines or grids, are projected onto a surface so that a three-dimensional model of the surface can be constructed from an analysis of the pattern distortions. This is an increasingly popular technique, as it does not require physical contact with the surface to be analyzed.
