## Source [^1]
- $n$. A condition sometimes observed in people with temporal lobe epilepsy, characterized by intense productiveness, often in writing or artistic work, between convulsions. Also called Dostoevsky syndrome. \[From Latin inter between + ictus struck, from icere to strike + -alis of or relating to]
