## Source [^1]
- a membrane in the cochlea of the ear that separates two of the three channels (scalae) that run the length of the spiral cochlea. The organ of Corti is situated on the basilar membrane, inside the scala media.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. The membrane of the organ of Corti in the inner ear, containing hair cells that convert sound waves into nerve impulses. See also PLACE THEORY, TRAVELLING WAVE.
