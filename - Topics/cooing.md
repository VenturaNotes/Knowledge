## Source [^1]
- $n$. The earliest identifiable stage in the development of infant vocalization, usually beginning at about three months and characterized by indeterminate vowel-like sounds, including phonemes not found in the language that the infant will eventually acquire. Cooing is eventually supplanted by babbling.
