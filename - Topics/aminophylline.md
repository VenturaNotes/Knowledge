## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug that relaxes smooth muscle and stimulates respiration. It is widely used to dilate the air passages in the treatment of severe asthma and some cases of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. Side effects may include nausea, vomiting, dizziness, and fast heart rate. See also THEOPHYLLINE.
