## Source [^1]
- $n$. an unsaturated hydrocarbon (a terpene), synthesized in the body, from which cholesterol is derived.
## Source[^2]
- An intermediate compound formed in the synthesis of cholesterol; it is a hydrocarbon containing 30 carbon atoms. The immediate oxidation of squalene to squalene 2,3-epoxide is the last common step in the synthesis of sterols in animals, plants, and fungi.
