## Source [^1]
- (EOP) Calibrated values that describe the position of a camera in the real world. These include the location of its focal point and the three angles that define its orientation. The EOPs of a camera are required when photos taken with it are used to derive precise measurements, for example as a result of aerial photographic surveys. The parameters feed into the calculations that match camera pixels to image coordinates. See also INTERIOR ORIENTATION PARAMETERS.
