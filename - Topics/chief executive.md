## Source [^1]
- The officer of a company or other organization responsible for seeing that decisions on principles by the board of directors are actually implemented in day-to-day operations. The chief executive is frequently also chairman or president of a company; opinion is divided as to whether these offices are better separated or combined. The US term for this is chief executive officer (CEO).
