## Source [^1]
- $n$. the study of the chemistry, biochemistry, and other aspects of carbohydrates and carbohydrate complexes, especially glycoproteins. Elucidation of the structure and role of the sugar molecules of glycoproteins has important medical implications and has led to the development of new drugs, such as tissue-type plasminogen activators, drugs that affect the immune system, and antiviral drugs.
