## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cystic swelling in the scrotum containing sperm. The cyst arises from the epididymis (the duct conveying sperm from the testis) and can be felt as a lump above the testis. Needle aspiration of the cyst reveals a milky opalescent fluid that contains sperm. Treatment is by surgical removal.
