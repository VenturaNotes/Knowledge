## Source [^1]
- A bank or building-society account designed for the investment of personal savings. The rates tend to be higher than old-fashioned deposit accounts and interest-bearing current accounts. Some accounts offer instant access to funds, while others require that notice be given, typically 30, 60, or 90 days.
