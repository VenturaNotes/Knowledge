## Source [^1]
- A concept according to which economic processes do not progress towards a unique predetermined equilibrium but instead reach one of many equilibria, depending on the process of getting there along a particular path. It implies irreversibility of the effect of transitory random shocks, or accidental events, upon the long-run outcome. See also HYSTERESIS.
