## Source [^1]
- (intermediate-term) Denoting a time period of medium duration, the extent of which will differ according to context. For example, in accounting 'medium-term' describes liabilities of between one and ten years, in money markets it means maturities of greater than a year, in bond markets a period of five to ten years, and in eurobond markets one of two to seven years.
