## Source [^1]
- A commodity, also called primary product or primary good, is a good sold for production or consumption just as it was found in nature 
- Commodities include
	- crude oil
	- coal
	- copper or iron ore
	- rough diamonds
	- agricultural products
		- wheat
		- coffee beans
		- cotton
