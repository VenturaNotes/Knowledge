## Source [^1]
- In audio communication systems, a means of increasing the performance of a communications channel by compressing the audio signal before modulating it onto a carrier for transmission and expanding it after demodulation in the receiver. The technique is commonly used in land-mobile communication systems. See also DATA COMPRESSION.
