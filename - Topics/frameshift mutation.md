## Source [^1]
- $n$. A mutation resulting from the insertion or deletion of a base pair, or a number of base pairs that is not a multiple of three, causing subsequent base pairs to be grouped wrongly into triplets at the stage of translation into amino acids. See also FRAMESHIFT SUPPRESSION. \[So called because the genetic code is in effect read through a moving window or frame displaying three bases at a time]
