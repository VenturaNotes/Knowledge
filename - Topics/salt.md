## Source [^1]
- A compound formed by reaction of an acid with a base, in which the hydrogen of the acid has been replaced by metal or other positive ions. Typically, salts are crystalline ionic compounds, such as $\mathrm{Na}^{+} \mathrm{Cl}^{-}$(sodium chloride).
