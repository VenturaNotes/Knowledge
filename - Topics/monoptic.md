## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) Having one eye. 
- (2) Of or relating to the (usually successive) presentation of different stimuli to the same eye. Compare DICHoptiC, MONOCULAR. \[From Greek monos alone or single + optikos of or relating to vision, from optos visible, from ops an eye]
