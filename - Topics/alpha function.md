## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a term introduced by the Indian-born British psychoanalyst Wilfred R(uprecht) Bion (1897-1979) to denote the process whereby the raw material of sensory experience, namely beta elements, are transformed into elements suitable for mental 'digestion' in the form of alpha elements.
