## Source [^1]
- $n$. the windpipe: the part of the air passage between the larynx and the main bronchi, i.e. from just below the Adam's apple, passing behind the notch of the sternum (breastbone) to behind the angle of the sternum. The upper part of the trachea lies just below the skin, except where the thyroid gland is wrapped around it. $\textemdash$tracheal adj.
