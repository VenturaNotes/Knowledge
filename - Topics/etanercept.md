## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cytokine inhibitor that is used in the treatment of severe rheumatoid and psoriatic arthritis, severe ankylosing spondylitis, and severe psoriasis that have not responded to other treatments. Side-effects include nausea and vomiting, headache, and fever.
