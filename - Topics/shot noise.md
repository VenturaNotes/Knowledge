## Source [^1]
- (Schottky noise) Noise that occurs in any electrical device when electrons must overcome a potential barrier by means of their kinetic energy. As each electron crossing the barrier is a random event, the resulting current has a random nature. Shot noise occurs in junction devices, such as a p-n junction diode or bipolar junction transistor.
