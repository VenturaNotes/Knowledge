## Source [^1]
- A shorter form of annual accounts that may be filed by a company qualifying as a small or medium-sized company under the UK Companies Acts. The use of abbreviated accounts can cut costs and save time. It can also minimize the information made available to others, especially business rivals. See also SUMMARY FINANCIAL STATEMENT.
