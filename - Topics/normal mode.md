## Source [^1]
- The mode of an oscillating system in which every part is in sinusoidal motion at the same frequency and maintains the same phase relationship with every other part throughout. The frequencies at which this can occur for a given system are known as its resonant frequencies. The general motion of such a system can be expressed as a superposition of its normal modes over different frequencies.
