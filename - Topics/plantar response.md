## Source [^1]
- $n$. A curling inwards of the toes when the sole of the foot is stroked with a hard object such as a key, replacing the infantile Babinski reflex from about 2 years of age in neurologically normal infants. Also called the plantar reflex. \[From Latin plantaris of the sole of the foot, from planta the sole of the foot]
