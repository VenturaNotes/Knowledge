## Source [^1]
- $n$. the activation of normal fibroblasts that occurs if they cluster and make cell-to-cell contact. This induces the production of chemokines, growth factors, enzymes, etc., which can influence cancer cells and inflammation. It leads to programmed cell death (see APOPTOSIS).
