## Source [^1]
- swelling of the vocal folds of the larynx due to a build-up of fluid in Reinke's space, between the internal fibromuscular layer of the vocal fold and its overlying mucosa. It is caused by smoking, vocal overuse, gastro-oesophageal reflux, or thyroid disease. \[F. B. Reinke (1862-1919), German anatomist]
