## Source [^1]
- A form of digital modulation in which the frequency of the carrier for a modulated signal is rapidly switched between a number of predetermined possible frequencies (rather than a fixed allocation of frequencies). A number of transmitters may then share the same range of frequencies. See also DIGITAL COMMUNICATIONS.
