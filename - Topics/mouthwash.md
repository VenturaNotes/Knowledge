## Source [^1]
- $n$. an aqueous solution used for rinsing of the mouth and teeth. Mouthwashes are used to prevent dental caries and gingivitis (see also CHLORHEXIDINE), to strengthen teeth against decay (see FLUORIDE), and to treat mild throat infections.
