## Source [^1]
- A differential equation where the independent variable does not explicitly occur. For example $\frac{\mathrm{d} v}{\mathrm{~d} t}=-k v$ is autonomous, but $\frac{\mathrm{d} v}{\mathrm{~d} t}=3 t^{2}+4 t-3$ is not.
