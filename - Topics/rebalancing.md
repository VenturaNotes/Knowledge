## Source [^1]
- (1) In finance, the adjustment of a hedge in order to improve its effectiveness as circumstances change. 
- (2) The adjustment of an investment portfolio over time to better reflect its underlying strategy. For example, a market-tracking hedge will require changes over time in order to reflect the differing importance of particular securities.
