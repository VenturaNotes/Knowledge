## Source [^1]
- $n$. an alkaloid, derived from opium, that relaxes smooth muscle. In combination with morphine and codeine, it is administered for relief of postoperative pain and severe chronic pain, but is now rarely used. It may cause abnormal heart rate. Papaverine may also be given by injection into the corpora cavernosa of the penis to treat erectile dysfunction.
