## Source [^1]
- an anomaly or anatomical variation in which there is a slight indentation of the endometrium at the top of the uterus. Unlike a septate uterus, in which the indentation extends into a septum that divides the interior of the uterus, and a bicornuate uterus, it does not normally affect reproductive function. See also UTERUS DIDELPHYS.
