## Source [^1]
- The term STEM refers to the academic disciplines of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. STEM science programs often focus on biology, physics, and chemistry. STEM technology programs focus on mobile technologies, computer programming, data analytics, and game development. STEM engineering programs focus on civil engineering, robotics, electrical engineering, and electronics. STEM math programs focus on geometry, algebra, calculus, and other branches of mathematics.
