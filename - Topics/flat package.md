## Source [^1]
- A low profile package for an integrated circuit that has small pins or flat connectors around the outside edge. Flat packages are mounted with their connectors on the surface of a printed circuit board, rather than inserted into holes. See also DUAL-FLAT NO-LEADS; QUAD-FLAT NO-LEADS.
