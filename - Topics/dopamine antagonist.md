## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any drug such as olanzapine or one of the phenothiazines, butyrophenones, or dibenzodiazepines that inhibits the action of dopamine by blocking the dopamine receptors. See also ANTAGONIST (3), NEUROLEPTIC (1). Compare DOPAMINOMIMETIC.
