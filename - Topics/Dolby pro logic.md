## Source [^1]
- An enhanced decoder for Dolby surround sound with additional hardware elements in the decoding chip. Directional cues in a film sound track are emphasized to give better steering accuracy to effects. Additionally, a dedicated center channel is extracted to locate the dialogue more accurately, requiring a centre channel speaker.
