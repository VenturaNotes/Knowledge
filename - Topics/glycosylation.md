## Source [^1]
- The process in which a carbohydrate is joined to another molecule, such as a protein to form a glycoprotein or to a lipid to form a glycolipid. Glycosylation occurs in the rough endoplasmic reticulum and the Golgi apparatus of cells.
## Source[^2]
- The process in which a carbohydrate is joined to another molecule, such as a protein to form a glycoprotein or to a lipid to form a glycolipid (see Glyceride). Glycosylation occurs in the rough endoplasmic reticulum and the Golgi apparatus of cells.
