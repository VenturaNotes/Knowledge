## Source [^1]
- The gain obtained from an amplifier (often an operational amplifier) by creating a feedback loop around the amplifier by the use of an external network. By contrast, the gain of the amplifier without this feedback network is termed the open-loop gain. See also GAIN-BANDWIDTH PRODUCT.
