## Source [^1]
- $n$. One of the two types of afferent neurons conveying signals to the cerebellum, projecting from the contralateral inferior olivary nucleus and forming multiple synapses with single Purkinje cells of the cerebellar cortex. US climbing fiber. Compare mossy fiber.
