## Source [^1]
- A device that produces a digital image from a physical image or object when it is moved across the target region with a single line of photodetectors kept close to the target. In a flatbed scanner the image or object of interest is placed on a glass plate and scanned from underneath. In hand-held scanners the photodetector strip is fashioned as a wand that the user can move across the target scene or object. See also 3D SCANNER.
