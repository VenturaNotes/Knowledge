## Source [^1]
- Low-frequency (up to about 10 hertz) audible periodic variations in the pitch of the sound output in a sound-reproduction system. Wow is an unwanted form of frequency modulation and is usually caused by a nonuniform rate of reproduction of the original sound, such as by nonuniform tape speed or nonuniform rotation of a gramophone turntable. See also FLUTTER.
