## Source [^1]
- $n$. a red blood cell precursor (see ERYTHROBLAST) in which iron-containing granules can be demonstrated by suitable staining techniques. Sideroblasts may be seen in normal individuals and are absent in iron deficiency. A certain type of anaemia (sideroblastic anaemia) is characterized by the presence of abnormal ringed sideroblasts. $\textemdash$sideroblastic adj.
