## Source [^1]
- $n$. a retinoid drug applied as a solution for the treatment of acne and sun-damaged skin; side-effects include redness and burning of the skin and increased sensitivity to sunlight. It is also taken by mouth to treat acute promyelocytic leukaemia; side-effects may include fever, breathlessness, flushing, headache, and fetal abnormalities (it should not be taken during pregnancy).
