## Source [^1]
- (NGO) An independent voluntary association of people working together for a common purpose that does not include achieving government office, earning profit, or engaging in illegal activities. Oxfam and Médecins Sans Frontières are examples of NGOs. See also NON-PROFIT ORGANIZATION.
