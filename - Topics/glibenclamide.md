## Source [^1]
- $n$. a long-acting oral hypoglycaemic drug that reduces the level of sugar in the blood and is used to treat type 2 diabetes (see SULPHONYLUREA). It is not recommended for the elderly. Side-effects include mild digestive upsets and skin reactions.
