## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) Of, relating to, or having color. A chromatic color is a color having chroma, in contradistinction to an achromatic color without chroma. 
- (2) Pertaining to musical tones that are sharpened or flattened without changing the key of the passage, or the musical scale containing all twelve semitones of the octave, also called the chromatic scale. Compare diatonic.
- \[From Greek chromatikos of or relating to color, from chroma, -atos colour + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
