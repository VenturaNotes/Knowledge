## Source [^1]
- An error that results from the tendency of people to answer a marketing research question falsely, either through deliberate misrepresentation or unconscious falsification.
## Source[^2]
- Occurs when participants in a survey respond differently from the way they actually feel. This may be because the questions have been asked in a loaded manner or because the information sought is sensitive and the participant is not prepared to be honest. See also anonymity of survey data.
