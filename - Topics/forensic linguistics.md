## Source [^1]
- $n$. The application of linguistic techniques in the investigation of crimes to show, for example, that an incriminating recording of an utterance is unlikely to have been spoken by the defendant in a criminal trial, or that different passages in an alleged confession were probably written by different people. See also hAPAX LEGOMENON, STYLOSTATISTICS.
