## Source [^1]
- A statement of a company's central principles, which are meant to inform all its activities and goals. Such a statement is usually directed both internally, to the company's managers and other employees, and externally, to its customers, suppliers, and investors. Value statements may include commitments to ethical practices or social engagement. See also MISSION STATEMENT.
