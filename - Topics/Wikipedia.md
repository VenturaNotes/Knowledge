## Source [^1]
- An Internet encyclopedia that is compiled and maintained by its users using a wiki web application. Founded in 2001, it currently (2014) has over four and a half million articles in English and over 33 million in all of its 287 language editions. Although very popular and successful, its standards of reliability, consistency, and security against malicious alterations have been criticized.
