## Source [^1]
- $n$. the lens-shaped bone that forms the kneecap. It is situated in front of the knee in the tendon of the quadriceps muscle of the thigh. See also SESAMOID BONE.
## Source[^2]
- (kneecap) (pl. patellae) A small rounded movable bone that is situated in a tendon in front of the knee joint in most mammals (including humans). The function of the patella is to protect the knee.
