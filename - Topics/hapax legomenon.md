## Source [^1]
- $n$. A word that occurs only once in a specific text, or in the works of a particular author, or in a language (in which case it is a nonce word). Such words have special significance in stylostatistics and forensic linguistics. hapax dislegomenon (preferably simply dis legomenon) n. A twice-occurring word. hapax legomena and dis legomena pl. \[Greek: something said once, from hapax once + legomenon something spoken, from legein to speak]
