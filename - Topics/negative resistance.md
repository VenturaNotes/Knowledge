## Source [^1]
- (strictly negative differential resistance) A property of certain devices whereby a portion of the current-voltage characteristic has a negative slope, i.e. the current decreases with increasing applied voltage. Devices that exhibit negative resistance include the thyristor, tunnel diode, Gunn diode, magnetron, and the output port of any oscillator.
