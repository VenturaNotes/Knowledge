## Source [^1]
- ($p l$. vasa deferentia) either of a pair of ducts that conduct spermatozoa from the epididymis to the urethra on ejaculation. It has a thick muscular wall the contraction of which assists in ejaculation.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. vasa deferentia) One of a pair of ducts carrying sperm from the testis (or epididymis) to the outside, in mammals through the urethra.
