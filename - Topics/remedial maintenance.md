## Source [^1]
- Corrective maintenance is another name for remedial maintenance
- Maintenance that is performed after a fault, in hardware or software, has been found, in order to correct that fault. Compare PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE.
- See also SOFTWARE MAINTENANCE
