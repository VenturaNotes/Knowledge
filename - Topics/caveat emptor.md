## Source [^1]
- Literally translated from Latin: let the buyer beware. This summarizes the principle that the buyer has to bear the risk of quality of their purchase. Considered as a legal principle, this is inconsistent with much modern legislation and many regulations designed to promote public health and safety by protecting buyers, who cannot reasonably be expected to be experts on all the products they purchase.
