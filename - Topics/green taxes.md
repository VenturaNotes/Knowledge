## Source [^1]
- Taxes and other fiscal policies, including tax reliefs, that are intended to encourage firms to reduce the adverse effects of their operations on the environment. In the UK these include the climate change levy, the carbon reduction commitment energy efficiency scheme, the emissions trading scheme, the capital allowances on energy-efficient items, the landfill tax, and the aggregates levy.
