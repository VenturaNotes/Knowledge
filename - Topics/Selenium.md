## Source [^1]
- $n$. a trace element that is an essential component of the enzyme glutathione peroxidase, which catalyses the oxidation of glutathione by hydrogen peroxide. It thus has important antioxidant properties. The adult RNI (see Dietary Reference Values) is $60 ~\mu \mathrm{g} /$day for women and 70 $\mu \mathrm{g} /$day for men. Dietary deficiency of selenium results in cardiomyopathy; vegetarians and vegans can be at risk of deficiency. Symbol: Se.
