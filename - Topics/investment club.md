## Source [^1]
- A group of investors who, by pooling their resources, are able to make more frequent and larger investments on a stock exchange, often being able to reduce brokerage and to spread the risk of serious loss. The popularity of investment clubs has waxed and waned with the developments of investment vehicles and the value of stocks.
