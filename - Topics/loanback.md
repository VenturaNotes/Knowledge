## Source [^1]
- An arrangement in which an individual can borrow from the accumulated funds of his pension scheme. Usually a commercial rate of interest has to be credited to the fund for the use of the capital. Some life assurance companies offer loan facilities on this basis of up to fifteen times the annual pension premium.
