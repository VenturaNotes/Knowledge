## Source [^1]
- (1) The production of one or more hybrid organisms by the mating of genetically different parents. 
- (2) The production of hybrid cells. See CELL FUSION (somatic cell hybridization). 
- (3) See DNA HYBRIDIZATION.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A technique of genetic engineering for determining the degree of relatedness of two DNA or RNA samples by annealing (2) them to form hybrid molecules of heteroduplex DNA, the stability of which indicates the degree of relatedness. See also DNA hybridization, RECOMBINANT DNA.
