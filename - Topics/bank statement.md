## Source [^1]
- A regular record, issued by a bank or building society, showing the credit and debit entries in a customer's cheque account, together with the current balance. The frequency of issue will vary with the customer's needs and the volume of transactions going through the account. Cash dispensers enable customers to ask for a statement whenever one is needed.
