## Source [^1]
- A property of a control system such that an output, which is close to a desired equilibrium point, will move ever closer to that equilibrium over time, assuming that the inputs remain fixed. It is referred to as asymptotic as it may take an infinite time for the output to actually reach the equilibrium. See also STABILITY.
