## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antidepressant drug that acts by prolonging the action of the neurotransmitter serotonin (5-hydroxytryptamine) in the brain (see SSRI). Side-effects may include dizziness, agitation, tremor, nausea, and diarrhoea.
