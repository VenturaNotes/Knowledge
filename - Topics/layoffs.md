## Source [^1]
- Permanent terminations or temporary suspensions by a firm of the employment of all or part of its workforce. Workers may be permanently laid off if the firm is ceasing the operations which gave rise to the jobs, or temporarily laid off if demand for the product of the firm is low but is thought likely to recover.
