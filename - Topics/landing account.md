## Source [^1]
- An account prepared by a public warehousing company to the owner of goods that have recently been landed from a ship and taken into the warehouse. The account, which is usually accompanied by a weight note, states the date on which the warehouse rent begins and also the quantity of goods and their condition (from the external appearance of the packages).
