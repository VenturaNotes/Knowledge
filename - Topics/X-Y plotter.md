## Source [^1]
- An instrument that produces a graph showing the relationship between two varying signals. One signal causes the pen to move in the direction of the X-axis and the other independently causes it to move in the direction of the Y-axis. The corresponding instantaneous values of the two signals are plotted on the graph.
