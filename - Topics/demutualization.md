## Source [^1]
- The conversion of financial or other institutions owned by their members into companies owned by shareholders. Most UK building societies and insurance companies have gone through this process.
## Source[^2]
- The act by which a mutual, such as a building society, changes its status to that of a public limited company. In the 1980s and 1990s this was seen in the retail financial services industry worldwide.
