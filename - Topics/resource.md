## Source [^1]
- An economic or productive factor required to accomplish an activity
- Resources can include various inputs such as [[land]], [[labor]], [[capital]], and [[entrepreneurship]], which are essential for the production of goods and services
	- These resources are utilized in the production process to generate output and contribute to economic growth and development
	- Without resources, it would be impossible to carry out any economic activity effectively
