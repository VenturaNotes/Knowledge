## Source [^1]
- (1) adj. relating to the bones of the ankle and foot (tarsus). 
- (2) adj. relating to the eyelid, especially to its supporting tissue (tarsus). 
- (3) n. any of the bones forming the tarsus.
## Source[^2]
- (tarsal bone) One of the bones that form the ankle (see TARSUS) in terrestrial vertebrates.
