## Source [^1]
- (common-emitter forward-current transfer ratio; forward-current gain) (symbol: β) The short-circuit current-amplification factor in a bipolar transistor with common-emitter connection:$$\beta = \left(\frac {\partial I _ {C}}{\partial I _ {B}}\right) V _ {C E} \text { constant}$$where $I_{\mathrm{C}}$ is the collector current and $I_{\mathrm{B}}$ is the base current; the collector voltage, $V_{\mathrm{CE}}$, is constant. $\beta$ is always greater than unity and practical values up to 500 are used.
