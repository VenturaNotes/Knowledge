## Source [^1]
- This is the buildup of excess fluid between the layers of the [[pleura]] outside your lungs
- Healthcare providers split pleural effusion into two types, depending on the kind of fluid around your lungs. Excess fluid may be
	- Protein-poor and watery (transudative)
	- Protein-rich (exudative)
