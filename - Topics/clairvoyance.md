## Source [^1]
- n. Extra-sensory visual perception of objects or events, a conjectural paranormal phenomenon. When the objects or events are far away it is also called remote viewing. Compare Clairaudience, PRECOGNITION, TELAESTHESIA, TELEGNOSIS, TELEPATHY. clairvoyant adj. \[From French clair clear, from Latin clarus clear + French voir to see, from Latin videre to see]
