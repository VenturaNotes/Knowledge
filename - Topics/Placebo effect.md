## Source [^1]
- $n$. A positive or therapeutic effect resulting solely from the administration of a placebo (1). Across a wide range of mental and physical symptoms and disorders, between 30 and 40 per cent of patients show improvement following the administration of a placebo. See also selF-FULFILLING PROPHECY. Compare NOCEBO EFFECT.
