## Source [^1]
- The ability of a document that entitles its owner to some benefit to change hands, so that legal ownership of the benefit passes by delivery or endorsement of the document. For a document to be negotiable it must also entitle the holder to bring an action in law if necessary. See NEGOTIABLE INSTRUMENT.
