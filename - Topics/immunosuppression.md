## Source [^1]
- $n$. suppression of the immune response, usually by disease (e.g. AIDS) or by drugs (e.g. steroids, azathioprine, ciclosporin).
## Source[^2]
- The suppression of an immune response. Immunosuppression is necessary following organ transplants in order to prevent the host rejecting the grafted organ (see GRAFT); it is artificially induced by radiation or chemical agents that inhibit cell division of lymphocytes. Immunosuppression occurs naturally in certain diseases, notably AIDS.
