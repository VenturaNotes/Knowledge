## Source [^1]
- (symbol: $\mu$) In an active electronic device used to deliver a constant current to a load, the ratio of the incremental change in output voltage required to maintain the constancy of the current to a corresponding incremental change in the input voltage. $\mu$ is equal to $-A_{\mathrm{v}}$, where $A_{\mathrm{v}}$ is the voltage gain of the device.
