## Source [^1]
- $n$. an osmotic diuretic administered mainly to reduce intracranial pressure in brain injuries and also in the emergency treatment of glaucoma. Side-effects include fever and chills. Mannitol powder is inhaled to treat cystic fibrosis.
## Source[^2]
- A polyhydric alcohol, $\mathrm{CH}_{2} \mathrm{OH}(\mathrm{CHOH})_{4} \mathrm{CH}_{2} \mathrm{OH}$, derived from mannose or fructose. It is the main soluble sugar in fungi and an important carbohydrate reserve in brown algae. Mannitol is used as a sweetener in certain foodstuffs and as a diuretic in medicine to relieve fluid retention, e.g. in acute kidney failure, or to treat swelling inside the eye or around the brain.
