## Source [^1]
- $n$. air in the mediastinum visible on chest X-ray. It can be a complication of surgical emphysema due to pneumothorax, but the air can originate from the upper airways or the upper gastrointestinal tract. A rare cause is gas-forming organisms. See HAMMAN's SIGN.
