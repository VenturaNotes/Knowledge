## Source [^1]
- The subgroup of the symmetric group, $S_n$, containing all the even permutations of $n$ objects. It is denoted $A_n$ and has order $n!/2$ for $n > 1.$ For $n > 4$, it is the only proper, non-trivial, normal subgroup of $S_n$ and is a simple group
