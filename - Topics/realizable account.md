## Source [^1]
- An account drawn up on the dissolution of a partnership. The account is debited with the assets of the partnership and any expenses on realization; it is credited with the proceeds of any sales made. The difference between the total debits and credits is either a profit or loss on realization and must be shared between the partners in the profit-sharing ratio.
