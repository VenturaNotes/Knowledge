## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug used to produce a medical abortion; it acts by blocking the action of progesterone, which is essential for maintaining pregnancy. It is taken by mouth, followed after 36-48 hours by gemeprost or misoprostol intravaginally. Side-effects include faintness, headache, and vaginal bleeding.
