## Source [^1]
- (sodium alendronate) a bisphosphonate drug that is administered for the prevention and treatment of osteoporosis and fragility fractures in men and postmenopausal women or induced by corticosteroid therapy in women. Immediate side-effects may include abdominal pain, indigestion, and oesophageal irritation or pain. Long-term use (5-10 years) may increase the risk of poor dental healing and atypical fractures of the thigh bone (femur).
