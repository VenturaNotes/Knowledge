## Source [^1]
- A piece of equipment, such as a computer system box, whose dimensions are roughly in the same proportions and size as the cardboard boxes used to transport pizzas, i.e. the depth and width approximately equal and very much greater than the height. Such an enclosure can be conveniently placed beneath a monitor on a desk.
