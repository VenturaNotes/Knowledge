## Source [^1]
- Amounts received from an office or employment including all salaries, fees, wages, perquisites, and other profits as well as certain expenses and benefits paid or provided by the employer, which are deemed to be emoluments. They are subject to income tax. See also DIRECTORS' REMUNERATION.
