## Source [^1]
- $n$. The generic name of a quick-acting stimulant drug, prescribed for the treatment of narcolepsy, more generally used to promote daytime wakefulness and sometimes as a performance enhancer. It was first introduced in France in 1986 and later marketed in the UK and the US under the proprietary name Provigil. Formula $\mathrm{C}_{15} \mathrm{H}_{15} \mathrm{NO}_{2} \mathrm{S}$.
