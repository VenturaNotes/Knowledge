## Source [^1]
- The effect produced on the heart by the parasympathetic nerve fibers (which are carried in the vagus nerve) controlling the heart rate, The parasympathetic nerve fibers slow the heart rate, e.g. from approximately 70 beats per minute to 60 beats per minute. Hence they counteract the effects of the sympathetic nerve fibers. See parasympathetic nervous SYSTEM.
