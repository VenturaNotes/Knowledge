## Source [^1]
- $n$. a potent opioid analgesic (see OPIATE) used for the relief of severe pain (for example in cancer patients already receiving other opioids) and for pain relief during surgery.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A narcotic analgesic drug that has a pharmacological action similar to that of morphine and is used as an adjunct to general anaesthesia, as a sedative or neuroleptic (1) drug, and sometimes as a street drug. Its most common street name is China white. See also droperidol. \[A corruption of phenethyl]
