## Source [^1]
- A supergroup of eukaryote protists named after the initial letters of its constituent clades: stramenopiles, alveolates, and rhizarians. It is tentatively proposed on the basis of possible homologies revealed by whole-genome DNA sequencing of representatives of the three clades.
