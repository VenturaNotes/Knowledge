## Source [^1]
- (post office protocol) A protocol that defines the communication between an email client and a utility that can accept email on behalf of a user, holding it until such time as the user wishes to recover the messages. Version 3 ([[POP3]]) is the standard protocol for this on the Internet. See also IMAP.
