## Source [^1]
- $n$. In the theory of cognitive development of the Swiss psychologist Jean Piaget (1896-1980), the period between the sensorimotor stage and the stage of concrete operations, from about 2 to 7 years of age. See also CENTRATION, CONSERVATION, EGOCENTRISM, PRECONCEPT, PREDICATE THINKING, TRANSDUCTIVE REASONING.
