## Source [^1]
- $n$. A crest-shaped structure on the inner surface of the bulge or ampulla of each semicircular canal, containing hair cells that project into the gelatinous membrane of the canals, functioning as sensory receptors for the labyrinthine sense and responding only to accelerations of the head, which cause them to bend. Also called simply a crista . cristae acousticae pl. \[From Latin crista a ridge or crest + Greek akoustikos of or relating to sound, from akouein to hear + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
