## Source [^1]
- The process of determining as nearly as possible the value of a population parameter by using an estimator. The value of an estimator found from a particular sample is called an estimate.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. The branch of statistics devoted to estimating parameters on the basis of data from samples and analyzing the properties of estimators such as unbiasedness and efficiency.
