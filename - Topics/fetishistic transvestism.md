## Source [^1]
- $n$. A paraphilia characterized by recurrent sexually arousing fantasies, sexual urges, or behavior involving the wearing of clothes of the opposite sex and appearing as a member of the opposite sex. Also called transvestic fetishism. Compare transsexualism, TRANSVESTISM. \[From Latin trans across + vestire to dress]
