## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in dentistry) surgical removal of the apex of the root of a tooth, also referred to as retrograde root canal treatment, root resection, or root-end resection. The root end cavity is prepared and filled with a biocompatible material. The procedure is carried out when root canal treatment has failed and cannot be redone.
