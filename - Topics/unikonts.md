## Source [^1]
- A supergroup of eukaryote organisms that contains animals, fungi, amoebas, and some non-amoeboid protists. The relationships of these diverse groups have been proposed largely on the basis of molecular studies of their genomes. They comprise two large groups: the amoebozoans and the opisthokonts.
