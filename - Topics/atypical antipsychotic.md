## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of a class of drugs that are used in the treatment of schizophrenia and other psychotic disorders and that have fewer neuroleptic-induced side-effects than conventional neuroleptic (1) drugs. See CloZAPINE, OLANZAPINE, RISPERIDONE, SERTINDOLE.
