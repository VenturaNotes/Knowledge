## Source [^1]
- The in-phase signal path in a receiver system, when the incoming signal is converted to an in-phase component and a quadrature-phase (90°) component. This occurs, for example, in the detection of noncoherent digital FSK (frequency shift keyed) signals. The quadrature-phase signal path is called the Q channel.
