## Source [^1]
- $n$. the interaction of drugs with cells. It includes such factors as the binding of drugs to cells, their uptake, and intracellular metabolism.
## Source[^2]
- The study of the action of drugs on the body. Compare PHARMACOKINETICS.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. The interaction of drugs with the neuroreceptors that mediate drug action. Compare pharmacokinetics. \[From Greek pharmakon a drug + dynamikos of or relating to strength or power, from dynamis strength or power + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
