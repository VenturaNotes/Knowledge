## Source [^1]
- The two self-dual laws$$\begin{aligned}& x \vee(x \wedge y)=x \\& x \wedge(x \vee y)=x\end{aligned}$$(see DUALITY) that are satisfied by all elements $x, y$ in a Boolean algebra possessing the two operations $\lor$ and $\land$.
