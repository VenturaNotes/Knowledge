## Source [^1]
- $n$. A theory of the origins of language, according to which words originated as imitations of natural sounds in the environment. US onomatopeic theory. Also (dismissively) called the bow-wow theory or ding-dong theory. \[From onomatopoeia the formation of words that are imitative of the sounds of the concepts designated]
