## Source [^1]
- $n$. a jelly-like substance formed when tendons, ligaments, etc. containing collagen (a protein) are boiled in water. Gelatin has been used in medicine as a source of protein in the treatment of malnutrition, in pharmacy for the manufacture of capsules and suppositories, and in bacteriology for preparing culture media.
