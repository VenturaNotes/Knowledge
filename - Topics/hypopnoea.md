## Source [^1]
- $n$. an episode during sleep in which there is a reduction in the nasal airflow to less than $50 \%$ of normal, but more than $30 \%$ (see APNOEA), for more than 10 seconds. See also ObSTRUCTIVE SLEEP APNOEA.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Abnormally shallow breathing or decreased breathing rate. See obstructive sleep apnoea hypopnoea. US hypopnea. Compare apnoea. \[From Greek hypo under + pnein to breathe]
