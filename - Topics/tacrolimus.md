## Source [^1]
- $n$. a powerful immunosuppressant administered to prevent rejection of transplanted organs. Side-effects include gastrointestinal disturbances, tremor, headache, and liver and kidney impairment; it may have adverse effects on the heart. It is also used for the treatment for atopic eczema.
