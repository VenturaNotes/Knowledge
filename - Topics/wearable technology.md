## Source [^1]
- Sensors and computing devices small and flexible enough to be worn, typically to monitor various aspects of their user's lifestyle. Examples include smartwatches or the use of smart materials. Wearable technology usually communicates with the wearer's smartphone, using wireless technology such as Bluetooth.
