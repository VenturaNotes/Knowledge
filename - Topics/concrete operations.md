## Source [^1]
- $n$. According to the theory of cognitive development of the Swiss psychologist Jean Piaget (1896-1980), the stage of cognitive development between the ages of approximately 7 and 12 years during which a child becomes proficient at manipulating internal representations of physical objects and acquires mastery of the various forms of conservation. See also mental operation. Compare FORMAL OPERATIONS, PRE-OPERATIONAL STAGE, SENSORIMOTOR STAGE.
