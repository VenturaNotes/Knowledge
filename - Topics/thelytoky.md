## Source [^1]
- The phenomenon occurring in the reproduction of certain animals in which fertilized eggs give rise to males and unfertilized eggs to females. It is found among aphids and certain other insects, and in some mites. The males are diploid, whereas females are haploid and transmit only the maternal genome$\textemdash$their production represents thelytokous parthenogenesis. Compare ARRHENOTOKY.
