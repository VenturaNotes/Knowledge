## Source [^1]
- $n$. The second phase of the anal stage according to a suggestion made in 1924 by the German psychoanalyst Karl Abraham (1877-1925) in his book Versuch einer Entwicklungsgeschichte der Libido (A Study of the Developmental History of the Libido). In the first anal-expulsive phase, anal erotism is linked to evacuation of the bowels and the sadistic instinct is satisfied by destruction of faeces, whereas in this second anal-retentive phase, anal erotism is associated with retention of faeces, and the sadistic instinct is satisfied by possessive control. Also called the anal-retentive stage.
