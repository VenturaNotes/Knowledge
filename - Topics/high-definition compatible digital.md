## Source [^1]
- (HDCD) A patented encode/decode process to provide enhanced audio sound reproduction on compact discs (CDs). This is achieved by storing the audio data as 20-bit word information, compared with 16-bit for standard CDs. It employs a sophisticated system to encode the additional 4 bits onto the CD, while retaining complete compatibility with the existing CD format standard.
