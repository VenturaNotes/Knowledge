## Source [^1]
- $n$. An eating disorder in DSM-5 characterized by an apparent lack of interest in food or eating, leading to significant weight loss, nutritional deficiency, dependence on nutritional supplements, and marked impairment of psychosocial functioning. The diagnosis is made only if the symptoms are not explained by anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa, or any other mental disorder or medical condition, and if there is no evidence of any disturbance in the experience of body shape or weight.
