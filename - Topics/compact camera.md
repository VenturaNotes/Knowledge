## Source [^1]
- A small camera that is simple to use ('point and click'), with most exposure and shutter-speed settings determined automatically by the camera itself. Since the emergence of the camera phone there has been a trend for compact cameras to become more high-end, with improved lenses, but still retaining simplicity of use. See also COMPACT SYSTEM CAMERA; DSLR CAMERA.
