## Source [^1]
- aggregator model An Internet business model in which a website is used to aggregate and display information about products or services from a variety of suppliers. The aggregator can earn revenue through advertising and from referral fees for transactions made through the site. This model is widely used in price comparison sites, as for insurance products etc. Success depends chiefly on being able to attract a large volume of traffic to the site.
