## Source [^1]
- The average of the instantaneous power in an a.c. circuit over one complete cycle. It is given by the formula $P_{avg} = V I \cos(\phi)$ , where $V$ and $I$ are the r.m.s. values of voltage and current, and $\phi$ is the circuit power factor.
