## Source [^1]
- $n$. In Gestalt psychology, the mutual influence of parts of the visual field, a typical example being the influence of two adjacent patches of color on each other in simultaneous color contrast, other areas of the visual field not affecting the phenomenon. \[From Old French apertenir to appertain, from Latin appertinere to appertain, from ad to + pertinere to belong]
