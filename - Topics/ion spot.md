## Source [^1]
- (1) A darker area appearing on the screen of a cathode-ray tube and resulting from decreased luminescence of the screen (not necessarily of the phosphor) after bombardment by heavy negative ions in the beam. Use of an ion trap can minimize this effect. 
- (2) A spurious signal appearing in the output of camera tubes and image converters due to alteration of the charge pattern on the target or cathode by ion bombardment.
