## Source[^2]
- $n$. any substance that may be specifically bound by an antibody molecule. In order to generate antibodies specific for small molecules, the latter are attached to a larger molecule before immunization. The small molecule is called a hapten; the larger molecule is called a carrier. $\textemdash$antigenic adj.
