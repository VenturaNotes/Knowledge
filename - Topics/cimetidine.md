## Source [^1]
- $n$. an $\mathrm{H}_{2}$-receptor antagonist (see ANTIHISTAMINE) that reduces gastric acidity and is used to treat gastro-oesophageal reflux and peptic ulcers. Side-effects include rashes, dizziness, diarrhoea, and muscular pains.
