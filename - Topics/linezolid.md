## Source [^1]
- $n$. a member of a class of antibiotics $\textendash$ the oxazolidinones $\textendash$ that are active against Gram-positive bacteria, including MRSA. Linezolid is used to treat pneumonia and soft-tissue infections caused by these organisms when they have not responded to other antibacterials. The most common side-effects are headache, a metallic taste in the mouth, diarrhoea, and nausea.
