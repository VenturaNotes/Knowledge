## Source [^1]
- Augmented intelligence is the use of technology to enhance a human's ability to execute tasks, perform analysis and make decisions.

## Source[^2]
- Augmented intelligence is a design pattern that helps humans and artificial intelligence (AI) work together. That allows for better experiences, learning, and decision-making. Unlike regular AI, augmented intelligence can help rather than replace people. Humans and machines collaborate on projects to improve productivity and performance in various sectors including customer service, marketing, and manufacturing.
