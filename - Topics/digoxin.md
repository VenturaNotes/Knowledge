## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cardiac glycoside $\textendash$ a drug extracted from digitalis $\textendash$ that increases the force of heart muscle contraction and decreases heart rate. It is used mainly to control atrial fibrillation. Possible side-effects include nausea, vomiting, loss of appetite, diarrhoea, abdominal pain, and abnormal heart activity.
