## Source [^1]
- A subset $T$ of a semigroup $S$, where $T$ is closed under the dyadic operation ${ }^{\circ}$ defined on $S$. Let $x$ be an arbitrary element of $S$. Then the set consisting of$$x, x^{\circ} x, x^{\circ} x^{\circ} x, \ldots$$i.e. all powers of $x$, is a subsemigroup of $S$.
