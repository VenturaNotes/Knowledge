## Source [^1]
- Informal The conductor connecting the shield on a piece of cable to the earth on an enclosure or connector. The conducting shield typically becomes imperfect in these regions and is often a curved piece of wire, hence 'pigtail'. The consequence of using these rather than a continuous shield may be severe degradation of the EMC performance of the equipment.
