## Source [^1]
- (blood plasma) $n$. the straw-colored fluid in which the blood cells are suspended. It consists of a solution of various inorganic salts of sodium, potassium, calcium, etc., with a high concentration of protein (approximately $70 \mathrm{~g} / \mathrm{l}$ ) and a variety of trace substances.
