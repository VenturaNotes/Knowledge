## Source [^1]
- $n$. A triangular representation of the relationship between the hue and saturation (1) dimensions of colors, having hues arranged in their natural spectrum order around its perimeter, with similar hues close together and complementary colors opposite one another, the center representing white light and saturation increasing towards the perimeter, a part of which is occupied by the non-spectral color purple linking spectral blue with spectral red. Achromatic (1) color is not represented, and therefore neither is the lightness dimension of color. US color triangle. Compare COLOR CIRCLE, COLOR SOLID.
