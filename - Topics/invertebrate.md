## Source [^1]
- (1) n. an animal without a backbone. The following are invertebrate groups of medical importance: insects, ticks, nematodes, flukes, and tapeworms. 
- (2) adj. not possessing a backbone.
## Source[^2]
- Any animal that lacks a vertebral column (backbone). Invertebrates include all nonchordate animals as well as the more primitive chordates (see CHORDATA).
