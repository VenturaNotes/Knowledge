## Source [^1]
- A prefix indicating a multiple of $10^{15}$. In computing it has a value of $2^{50}$ (1,125,899,906,842,624). See also BINARY PREFIXES.
## Source[^2]
- Symbol P. A prefix used in the metric system to denote one thousand million million times. For example, $10^{15}$ metres $=1$ petametre $(\mathrm{Pm})$.
