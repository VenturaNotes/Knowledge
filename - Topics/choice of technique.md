## Source [^1]
- The choice of method of production where more than one method is possible. A technique is a set of inputs capable of producing a given output or set of outputs. If more than one set of inputs can produce the outputs, a choice of techniques must be made. A profitmaximizing firm prefers the technique for which the total cost of inputs is the lowest.
