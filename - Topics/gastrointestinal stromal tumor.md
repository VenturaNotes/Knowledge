## Source [^1]
- (GIST) a rare type of sarcoma arising from the gastrointestinal tract (usually the stomach), characterized by the presence of the receptor c-kit/CD117 on the surface of the tumor cells, which stimulates division of these cells. It displays a wide range of malignant behavior. Specific treatment is available with tyrosine kinase inhibitors.
