## Source [^1]
- $n$. the start of the menstrual periods and other physical and mental changes associated with puberty. The menarche occurs when the reproductive organs become functionally active and may take place at any time between 10 and 18 years of age. Compare GONADARCHE.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A female's first menstruation when she reaches puberty. \[From Greek men month + arche beginning]
