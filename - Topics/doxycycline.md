## Source [^1]
- $n$. a tetracycline antibiotic used to treat infections caused by Chlamydia, rickettsiae, mycoplasmas, and Brucella as well as Lyme disease. It may also be used in the prevention and treatment of malaria and the treatment of mouth ulcers and periodontitis. Side-effects are those of the other tetracyclines.
