## Source [^1]
- (1) An unusually large stock of a raw material held by an organization in anticipation of a shortage, transport strike, planned production increase, etc. 
- (2) A large stock of strategic materials, food, etc., built up by a government in anticipation of a war, a break in trading relations with a supplier nation, etc.
