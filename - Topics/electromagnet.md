## Source [^1]
- A device that is magnetized only when an electric current flows in it. It consists of a helical winding around which a magnetic field is produced when current flows through the winding. A ferromagnetic core is almost invariably used: the design of the core is a major factor in determining the magnetic flux density. The winding around which the field is produced is the field coil; the current used to produce the field is the field current.
