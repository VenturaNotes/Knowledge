## Source [^1]
- Investigation of the degree to which the behavior of a system is affected by a change in the value of some (explicit or implicit) parameter or variable, or by a combination of changes. For example, a simple analysis might determine how the performance of a system is impacted by changing the number and sizes of the storage buffers that are allocated to that system.
## Source[^2]
- The varying of parameters in a simulation to discover which parameters have the greatest influence on the features of interest.
