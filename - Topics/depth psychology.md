## Source [^1]
- $n$. A term introduced by the Swiss psychiatrist Eugen Bleuler (1857-1939) to denote psychological approaches that take account of unconscious (2) forces, especially psychoanalysis in its various forms and varieties. See also DEPTH INTERVIEW, DYNAMIC PSYCHOLOGY, METAPSYCHOLOGY.
