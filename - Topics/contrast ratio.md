## Source [^1]
- $n$. An index of the difference between the luminance of an image $\left(L_{i}\right)$ and the luminance of its background $\left(L_{b}\right)$ usually expressed as a percentage of the background luminance: $100 \times\left(L_{i}-L_{b}\right) / L_{b}$. Compare CONTRAST (4).
