## Source [^1]
- (paraboloid reflector; dish) A radiofrequency or microwave-frequency reflector (see DIRECTIONAL ANTENNA) that has a hollow concave paraboloid shape so that all waves passing through the focus will be reflected parallel to the axis of rotation.
