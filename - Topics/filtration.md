## Source [^1]
- The process of separating solid particles using a filter. In vacuum filtration, the liquid is drawn through the filter by a vacuum pump. Ultrafiltration is filtration under pressure; for example, ultrafiltration of the blood occurs in the nephrons of the vertebrate kidney.
