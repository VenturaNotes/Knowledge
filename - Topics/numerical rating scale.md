## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any rating scale in which the response categories are identified by numbers, such as a scale from -3 to +3 or from 0 to 10 . The main alternatives are rating scales with unlabelled response categories, indicated by a row of boxes, dashes, or other symbols; verbal rating scales, and graphic rating scales.
