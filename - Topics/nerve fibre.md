## Source [^1]
- the long fine process that extends from the cell body of a neuron and carries nerve impulses. Bundles of nerve fibres running together form a nerve. Each fibre has a sheath, which in medullated nerve fibres is a relatively thick layer containing the fatty insulating material myelin.
