## Source [^1]
- Project portfolio and program management (PPM) refers to project management capabilities. The technology behind this software can enable businesses to manage, analyze, and prioritize their product development programs. The technology uses programming to help businesses make decisions based on the parameters entered by the business in question. It will then display its analysis in an easy-to-understand format. This results in better and more data-informed decision-making.
