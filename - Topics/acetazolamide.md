## Source [^1]
- $n$. a carbonic anhydrase inhibitor used mainly in the treatment of glaucoma to reduce the pressure inside the eyeball and also as a preventative for epileptic seizures and altitude sickness. Side-effects include drowsiness and numbness and tingling of the hands and feet.
