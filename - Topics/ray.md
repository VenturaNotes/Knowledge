## Source [^1]
- (1) (in optics) A narrow beam of radiation. 
- (2) (in botany) See medullary ray. 
- (3) (in zoology) 
	- (a) A bony or horny skeletal element in the fin of a bony fish (class Osteichthyes). 
	- (b) Any of an order (Rajiformes) of cartilaginous fishes with flattened bodies.
