## Source [^1]
- A semiconductor in which impurities or imperfections have been introduced by doping in order to modify the charge-carrier concentration. An extrinsic semiconductor can be n-type or p-type according to whether the dominant charge carriers are electrons or holes. See SEMICONDUCTOR. Compare INTRINSIC SEMICONDUCTOR.
