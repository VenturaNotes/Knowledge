## Source [^1]
- An approach to analogue or digital signal processing in which the control, steering, or switching of currents is employed to carry out the electronic function or process. This is in contrast to more traditional circuits where the control of voltages is used to process the signal or information; for example, logic levels are traditionally expressed in terms of voltages. These are voltage-mode circuits.
