## Source [^1]
- The set of orthonormal vectors associated with coordinates in $n$-dimensional Cartesian space. In 3-dimensional space, the vectors $\mathbf{i}, \mathbf{j}, \mathbf{k}$ in the directions $O x, O y, O z$.
