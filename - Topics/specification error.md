## Source [^1]
- An error in estimation or inference caused by a false assumption in an econometric model. Specification error often results in the estimators of the model parameters being biased and inconsistent. Omitted variable bias and incorrect functional form are examples or specification error. See also RAMSEY REGRESSION EQUATION SPECIFICATION ERROR TEST.
