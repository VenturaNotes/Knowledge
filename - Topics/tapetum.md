## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) a layer of specialized reflecting cells in the choroid behind the retina of the eye. 
- (2) a band of nerve fibers that form the roof and wall of the lower posterior part of the corpus callosum.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. tapeta) 
- (1) A membranous layer, such as the thin sheet of nerve fibers in the wall of the lateral ventricles in the brain. 
- (2) A reflecting layer, containing crystals of guanine, in the choroid of the eye of many nocturnal vertebrates. It reflects light back onto the retina, thus improving vision and causing the eyes to shine in the dark. 
- (3) A layer of nutritive or nurse cells in the anther of a flowering plant, or in the sporangium of a fern, that contributes to the development and maturation of the pollen grains or spores.
