## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of a group of anticholinesterase compounds used as insecticides and pesticides that cause irreversible suppression of cholinesterase. Also called an organophosphorus compound. See also NERVE GAS, ORGANOPHOSPHATE POISONING. OP abbrev. \[From organo-indicating a compound containing an organic group + phosphate]
