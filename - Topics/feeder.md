## Source [^1]
- (1) The part of a radio system that conveys the radiofrequency energy from the transmitter to the antenna or from the antenna to the receiver. 
- (2) An electric line that conveys electrical energy from a generating station to a point of a distributing network without being tapped at any intermediate points. See TRANSMISSION LINE.
