## Source [^1]
- (1) An office, usually with a shop front and located in a shopping parade, that offers financial advice to investors, often on a walk-in basis. 
- (2) Specialist investment bankers, who cover a particular sector of the market, e.g. management buy-outs, acquisitions, etc.
