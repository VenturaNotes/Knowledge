## Source [^1]
- (ORT) the administration of an isotonic solution of various sodium salts, potassium chloride, glucose, and water to treat acute diarrhoea, particularly in children. In developing countries, it is the mainstay of treatment for cholera. Once the diarrhoea has settled, normal feeding is gradually resumed.
