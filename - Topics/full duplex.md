## Synthesis
- Where both parties can communicate simultaneously (e.g., telephone calls)
## Source[^1]
- Involving or denoting a connection between two endpoints, either physical or logical, over which data may travel in both directions simultaneously. See also HALF DUPLEX, SIMPLEX, RETURN CHANNEL.
