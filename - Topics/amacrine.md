## Source [^1]
- adj. Lacking a conspicuous axon, referring especially to neurons in the middle layer of the retina that link bipolar cells and retinal ganglion cells and that make only lateral connections, their cell bodies lying in the inner nuclear layer and connecting with other cells in the inner plexiform layer. There are many different kinds, the functions of which are not well understood. Compare horizontal cell. \[From Greek ama as soon as + krinein to separate]
