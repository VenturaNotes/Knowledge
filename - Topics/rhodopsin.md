## Source [^1]
- (visual purple) $n$. a pigment in the retina of the eye, within the rods, consisting of retinal $\textendash$ an aldehyde of retinol (vitamin A) $\textendash$ and a protein. The presence of rhodopsin is essential for vision in dim light. It is bleached in the presence of light and this stimulates nervous activity in the rods.
