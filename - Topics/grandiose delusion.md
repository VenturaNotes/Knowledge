## Source [^1]
- $n$. A delusion of inflated importance, power, wealth, knowledge, or identity, or of having a great but unrecognized talent or intelligence, having made a significant discovery, or of being or having a special relationship with a prominent person. See also GRANDIOSE DELUSIONAL DISORDER, MEGALOMANIA. Compare GRANDIOSE IDEAS OR ACTIONS.
