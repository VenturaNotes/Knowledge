## Source [^1]
- (tympanic cavity) the part of the ear that consists of an air-filled space within the petrous part of the temporal bone. It is lined with mucous membrane and is connected to the pharynx by the Eustachian tube and to the outer ear by the eardrum (tympanic membrane). Within the middle ear are three bones $\textendash$ the auditory ossicles $\textendash$ which transmit sound vibrations from the outer ear to the inner ear (see LABYRINTH).
