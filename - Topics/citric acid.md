## Source [^1]
- an organic acid found naturally in citrus fruits. Citric acid is formed in the first stage of the Krebs cycle, the important energy-producing cycle in the body.
## Source[^2]
- A white crystalline hydroxy carboxylic acid, $\mathrm{HOOCCH}_{2} \mathrm{C}(\mathrm{OH})$ $(\mathrm{COOH}) \mathrm{CH}_{2} \mathrm{COOH}$. It is present in citrus fruits and is an intermediate in the Krebs cycle in plant and animal cells.
