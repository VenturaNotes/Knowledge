## Source [^1]
- An electronic pan-European trading platform established by a consortium of nine leading investment banks in 2008. Turquoise was originally limited to equities trading but subsequently expanded into futures and options. The London Stock Exchange acquired a controlling interest in 2009 and in 2011 Turquoise was merged with the LSE's derivatives platform EDX London.
