## Source [^1]
- The power level of the signal being radiated from an antenna. Total radiated power is a simple measurement of all the antenna radiation, whereas effective radiated power is a measure of how much power is sent along the direction of the antenna's main lobe. See also ANTENNA EFFICIENCY.
