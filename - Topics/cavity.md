## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) (in anatomy) a hollow enclosed area; for example, the abdominal cavity or the buccal cavity (mouth). 
- (2) (in dentistry) a. the hole in a tooth caused by caries or abrasion. b. the hole shaped in a tooth by a dentist to retain a filling.
