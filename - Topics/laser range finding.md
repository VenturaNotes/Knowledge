## Source [^1]
- Measuring distance to a remote object by sending a pulse of laser light towards it and noting how long it takes for a return echo of that light to arrive. Using the time of flight concept in conjunction with the known speed of light allows the calculation of the distance. For very high accuracy at short distances, triangulation techniques using two or more lasers may be used. See also LIDAR.
