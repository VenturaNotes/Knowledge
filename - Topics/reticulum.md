## Source [^1]
- $n$. a network of tubules or blood vessels. See ENDOPLASMIC RETICULUM; SARCOPLASMIC RETICULUM.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. reticula) The first of four chambers that form the stomach of ruminants. Like its companion chamber, the rumen, it acts essentially as a fermentation vat, housing a dense population of bacteria and protists that digest the plant material ingested by the animal. See RUMINANTIA.
