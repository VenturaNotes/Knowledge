## Source [^1]
- A branch of electronics concerned with components and devices whose dimensions are significantly less than one micrometer, and with associated electrical phenomena. Many of these devices and components rely on quantum mechanical phenomena by virtue of their small size, and as a result demonstrate electrical behavior that cannot be observed in larger structures. See QUANTUM DOT; QUANTUM WIRE. See also NANODEVICE; NANOTECHNOLOGY.
