## Source [^1]
- $n$. an enzyme that continues the digestion of proteins by breaking down peptones into smaller peptide chains (see PEPTIDASE). It is secreted by the pancreas in an inactive form, trypsinogen, which is converted in the duodenum to trypsin by the action of the enzyme enteropeptidase.
