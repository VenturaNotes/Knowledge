## Source [^1]
- (carrageen) A naturally occurring polysaccharide isolated from red algae (Rhodophyta). The polymer is composed of D-galactose units, many of which are sulphated. K-carrageenan is a gelling agent and stabilizing agent used in foods, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.
