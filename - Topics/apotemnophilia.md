## Source [^1]
- n. A paraphilia characterized by recurrent sexually arousing fantasies, sexual urges, or behavior associated with having one of one's own limbs (usually a leg) amputated. It should be carefully distinguished from acrotomorphilia. Also (preferably) called xenomelia. \[From Greek apo away + temnein to cut + philos loving, from phileein to love + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
