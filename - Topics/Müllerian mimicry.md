## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of mimicry in which two or more organisms that are harmful or unpalatable to a predator resemble one another in shape or coloration and thereby jointly deter predation. Compare Batesian mimicry. \[Named after the German zoologist Johann Friedrich Theodor (Fritz) MÃ¼ller (1821-97) who first described it]
