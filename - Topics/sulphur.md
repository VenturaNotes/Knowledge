## Source [^1]
- $n$. a nonmetallic element that is active against fungi and parasites. It is a constituent of ointments and other preparations used in the treatment of skin disorders (such as psoriasis).
## Source[^2]
- Symbol S. A yellow nonmetallic element that is an essential element in living organisms, occurring in the amino acids cysteine and methionine and therefore in many proteins. It is also a constituent of various cell metabolites, e.g. coenzyme A. Sulphur is absorbed by plants from the soil as the sulphate ion $\left(\mathrm{SO}_{4}{ }^{2-}\right)$. See SULPHUR CYCLE.
