## Source [^1]
- (personal locator beacon) A radio transmitter carried by an individual for use in emergency situations to alert the search and rescue services to the emergency. The transmitted signal is usually an amplitude modulated signal at 121.5 megahertz, one of the international emergency distress frequencies. See also EPIRB.
