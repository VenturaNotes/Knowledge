## Source [^1]
- $n$. the SI unit of electrical conductance, equal to the conductance between two points on a conductor when a potential difference of 1 volt between these points causes a current of 1 ampere to flow between them. Symbol: S.
## Source[^2]
- (symbol: S) The SI unit of electrical conductance, suscetance, and admittance. An element possesses a conductance of one siemens if it has electrical resistance of one ohm. The siemens has replaced the mho.
