## Source [^1]
- A system of units in which the defining equations have been made to conform to the geometry of the system in a logical way. Thus equations that involve circular symmetry contain the factor $2\pi$, while those involving spherical symmetry contain the factor $4\pi$. SI units are rationalized; c.g.s. units are unrationalized.
