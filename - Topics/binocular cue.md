## Source [^1]
- $n$. Either of the cues (2) of distance, known as ocular convergence and stereopsis, that contribute towards visual depth perception and that depend on integration of information from both eyes. Compare MONOCULAR CUE.
## Source[^2]
- Binocular cues provide depth perception (due to retinal disparity)
	- #question What is meant by retinal disparity?
	- #question What are binocular cues?
- Retinal disparity is defined as the way that your left eye and your right eye view slightly different images
	- Important in gauging how far away objects are
	- The greater disparity between the image each eye has of the same object, the closer it is to you.
