## Source [^1]
- A lease that is not a finance lease, i.e. one in which the risks and rewards of owning the asset remain substantially with the lessor. In practice this is generally a lease under which an asset is hired out to a lessee for a period that is substantially shorter than its useful economic life. Section 30 of the Financial Reporting Standard Applicable in the UK and Republic of Ireland provides detailed guidance.
