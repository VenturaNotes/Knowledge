## Source [^1]
- the cells that lay down the myelin sheath around the axon of a medullated nerve fibre. Each cell is responsible for one length of axon, around which it twists as it grows, so that concentric layers of membrane envelop the axon. The gap between adjacent Schwann cells forms a node of Ranvier. \[T. Schwann (1810-82), German anatomist and physiologist]
