## Source [^1]
- A secondary cell that typically has a carbon material such as graphite as the anode, a lithium cathode, and lithium salts in an organic solvent as the electrolyte. They have largely replaced nickel-cadmium cells as the basis for rechargeable batteries for most devices, particularly mobile ones.
