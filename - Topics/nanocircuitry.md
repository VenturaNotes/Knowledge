## Source [^1]
- Circuits constructed from nanostructures. These are widely seen as the next step in integrated circuit manufacture when the current silicon-based technology reaches its size limit. A key focus is on how to connect nanowires to other nanostructures in order to produce memory cells and MOSFET-like switching elements.
