## Source [^1]
- $n$. The most common inherited cause of intellectual disability, involving an easily damaged X chromosome with a tip hanging by a narrow thread; the syndrome is often accompanied by attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder, with additional characteristics such as enlarged head, long face, prominent ears and (in males) enlarged testicles. It is caused by a single gene on the $\mathrm{X}$ chromosome that is invariably expressed in males but rarely in females$\textemdash$only if they inherit copies on both their X chromosomes$\textemdash$resulting in a greater preponderance of intellectual disability in males than females.
