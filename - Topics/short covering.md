## Source [^1]
- The purchasing of goods that have been sold short (see SHORT POSITION) so that the open position is closed. The dealer in commodities, securities, or foreign exchanges, hopes to cover the shorts at below the price at which they were sold in order to make a profit. Dealers cover their shorts when they expect the market to turn.
