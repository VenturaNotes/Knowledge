## Source[^2]
- $n$. Any of the gaps that occur at regular intervals along the myelin sheath of a myelinated nerve fibre, exposing the axon. Also called Ranvier's node. See also SALTATION (1). \[Named after the French histologist Louis-Antoine Ranvier (1835-1922) who drew attention to them]
