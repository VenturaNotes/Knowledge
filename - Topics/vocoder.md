## Source [^1]
- (voice coder) A device that enables a direct spectral representation of a speech signal to be obtained by finding the energy in each filter band of a bank of band-pass filters using a rectifier and smoothing circuit. This representation is coded and transmitted along with the excitation signal, which is either voiced (periodic), in which case a voicing flag is set and its fundamental frequency is coded, or voiceless (nonperiodic) when the voiced flag is unset.
