## Source [^1]
- $n$. any muscle that causes bending of a limb or other part.
## Source[^2]
- A muscle that causes a limb to bend by bringing the two parts of the limb together. An example is the biceps. Flexors work antagonistically with extensors. See SKELETAL MUSCLE.
