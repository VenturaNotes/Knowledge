## Source [^1]
- $n$. a rare malignant tumor of fat cells. It is most commonly found in the thigh and is rare under the age of 30 years. There are four main histological types: well-differentiated, myxoid, pleomorphic, and roundcell liposarcomas. Treatment is usually by surgery.
