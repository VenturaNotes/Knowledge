## Source [^1]
- $n$. A condition characterized by pain or discomfort from sounds or noises, usually resulting from damage to the cochlea. Also spelt dysacousia . Also called dysacusis, dysacusmia , or dysacousis . dysacusic adj. \[From Greek dys- bad or abnormal + akousis hearing, from akouein to hear + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
