## Source [^1]
- A transducer that can convert between electric signals and surface acoustic waves on piezoelectric crystals. The name derives from its structure, which is that of two comb-like metal patterns on the crystal surface with their teeth interleaved but not touching. The distance between adjacent teeth determines the frequency of the surface waves they generate.
