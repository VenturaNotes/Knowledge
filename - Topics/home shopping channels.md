## Source [^1]
- A form of television direct marketing in which television programmes or entire channels are dedicated to selling goods and services. While relatively small in money terms in the UK and Europe, in the USA it is estimated that more than $\$ 10$ billion is spent in this manner annually on goods and services. Compare DIRECT-RESPONSE TV ADVERTISING.
