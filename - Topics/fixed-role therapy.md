## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of psychotherapy based on the personal construct theory of the US psychologist George A(lexander) Kelly (1905-67), first explored by Kelly and his colleagues in 1939 and described in his book The Psychology of Personal Constructs (1955, Volume 1, pp. 360-451), in which the client begins with a self-characterization then modifies it in the direction of a desired alternative. See also personal constructs therapy. FRT abbrev.
