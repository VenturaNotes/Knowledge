## Source [^1]
- $n$. an infectious disease of animals that can be transmitted to humans. See anthrax; avian influenza; brucellosis; cat-scratch disease; cowpox; glanders; Q fever; Rift Valley fever; rabies; RAT-BITE FEVER; TOXOPLASMOSIS; TULARAEMIA; TYPHUS.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. zoonoses) An infectious disease of nonhuman vertebrates that can be transmitted to humans. Rabies and anthrax are well-known examples, and certain midges and the tsetse fly act as carriers for a variety of nematode-worm zoonoses.
