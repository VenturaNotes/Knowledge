## Source [^1]
- Resistance or failure of price to change instantaneously in response to a change in an economic environment. Possible causes of price stickiness are monopolistic competition and menu costs, money illusion, imperfect information with regards to price changes, and fairness concerns. See also IMPERFECT COMPETITION.
