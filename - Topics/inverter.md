## Source[^2]
- (1) A device or circuit that converts direct current into alternating current. 
- (2) (linear inverter) An amplifier that reverses the polarity of a signal, i.e. that introduces a $180^{\circ}$ phase shift. 
- (3) (digital inverter; NOT circuit) A logic circuit that inverts the level of the input signal, i.e. that produces a low output for a high input and vice versa.
