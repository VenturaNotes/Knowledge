## Source [^1]
- A worker who has a conditional arrangement with an employer, such as a temporary, agency, or casual worker. Contingent workers tend to be employed by organizations when they have temporary need for a particular service, technology, or skill at a particular time and place. See also AGENCY WORKER; TEMPORARY EMPLOYEE.
