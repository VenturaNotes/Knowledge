## Source [^1]
- (polychromatophilia) $n$. the presence of certain blue red blood cells (erythrocytes) seen in blood films stained with Romanowsky stains, as well as the normal pink cells. The cells that appear blue are juvenile erythrocytes (see RETICULOCYTE).
