## Source [^1]
- Responsible for supplying one of the extraocular muscles of the eye: the [[superior oblique muscle]]
## Source[^2]
- the fourth cranial nerve (IV), which supplies the superior oblique muscle, one of the muscles responsible for movement of the eyeball in its socket. The action of the trochlear nerve is coordinated with that of the oculomotor and the abducens nerves.
