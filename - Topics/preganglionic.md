## Source [^1]
- adj. describing fibers in a nerve pathway that end in a ganglion, where they form synapses with postganglionic fibers that continue the pathway to the effector organ, muscle or gland.
## Source[^2]
- adj. Pertaining to efferent neurons of the autonomic nervous system that synapse on to other neurons in peripheral ganglions. Compare postganglionic. \[From Latin prae in front or before + Greek ganglion a cystic tumour + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
