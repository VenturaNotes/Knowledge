## Source [^1]
- A type of television camera tube, little used now, in which electrons from each portion of a photosensitive plate are focused in turn on a collector electrode in order to produce a video signal. It differs from the iconoscope or image orthicon camera tubes in which the static charge pattern on the photosensitive plate is scanned by an electron beam.
