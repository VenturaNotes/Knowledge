## Source [^1]
- $n$. Surgical removal of portions of the frontal lobes of the brain, usually in the prefrontal cortex, and severing of the neural connections between the thalamus and the frontal lobes, as a method of treating intractable depression or pain. Also called prefrontal lobotomy or simply lobotomy. Compare FRONTAL LEUCOtomy. \[From Greek lobos a lobe + tome a cut]
