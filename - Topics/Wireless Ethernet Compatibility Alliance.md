## Source [^1]
- The Wireless Ethernet Compatibility Alliance (WECA), now known as the Wi-Fi Alliance, was formed in 1999 to certify the compatibility of computers and software with Wi-Fi. The alliance is also credited with promoting wireless LAN as the standard form of internet connection around the world.
