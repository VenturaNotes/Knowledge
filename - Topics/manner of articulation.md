## Source [^1]
- $n$. The way in which the airstream, usually from the lungs, is interfered with in order to produce a speech sound. In English, there are five or six manners of articulation: plosive, fricative, affricate, nasal (2), liquid, and semivowel, the last two usually being combined as approximant. See also articulation.
