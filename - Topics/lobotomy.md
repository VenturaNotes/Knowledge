## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) Surgical severing of one or more nerve fibres in the frontal lobe of the brain as a method of treating intractable mental disorders. More correctly called frontal lobotomy or frontal leucotomy. 
- (2) Surgical removal or disconnection of a lobe of any organ. \[From Greek lobos a lobe + tome a cut]
