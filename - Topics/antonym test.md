## Source [^1]
- $n$. A type of intelligence test item in which the respondent is presented with a word and is asked to supply a word with the opposite meaning to it, often by choosing from a set of response alternatives. Also called an opposites test. See also ANTONYm. Compare SYNONYm TEST.
