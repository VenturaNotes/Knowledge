## Source [^1]
- A microphone that utilizes the variation of contact resistance of granular carbon with applied pressure. There are several types of carbon microphone, including the telephone transmitter, in which sound-pressure variations are transmitted to the carbon granules through a diaphragm; the corresponding changes of resistance are detected by fluctuations in a current passed through the granules.
