## Source [^1]
- An anti-competitive practice is an action conducted by one or more businesses to make it difficult or impossible for other companies to enter or succeed in their market.

## Source[^2]
- Any practice that reduces the degree of competition in a market. Anti-competitive practices include price fixing, dumping, regulations, and monopolization. Both governments and firms can be responsible for anti-competitive practices.
