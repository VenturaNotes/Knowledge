## Source [^1]
- An astable multivibrator that is caused to operate by a trigger (i.e. it is clocked) rather than being allowed to run freely. Provided that the frequency of application of the trigger is high compared to the natural frequency of vibration of the free-running circuit it operates as a bistable circuit, i.e. a flip-flop.
