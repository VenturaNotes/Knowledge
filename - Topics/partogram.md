## Source [^1]
- $n$. a graphic record of the course of labor, designed to monitor its progress and enable early recognition of any problems. It includes information on the mother's blood pressure, heart rate, and temperature; length, frequency, and strength of contractions; and heart rate and amniotic fluid in the baby.
