## Source [^1]
- Lacking or not involving or requiring oxygen. For example, a culture of anaerobic microorganisms is called an anoxic culture.
## Source[^2]
- Describing a system or process in which oxygen is absent. For example, anoxic water is water in which there is no (or very little) oxygen dissolved. See also oxic.
