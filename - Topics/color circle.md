## Source [^1]
- $n$. A diagram showing the relationship between the hue and saturation (1) dimensions of colors, differences in hue being represented by different positions around the circumference of the circle in their natural spectrum order, with similar hues close together and complementary colors opposite one another, and saturation represented by the distance from the center, white light being situated at the center and saturation increasing radially towards the circumference, a special segment of which is occupied by the non-spectral color purple linking spectral blue with spectral red and completing the circle. Achromatic (1) color is not represented, and therefore neither is the lightness dimension of color. US color circle. See also METAMER. Compare COLOR SOLID, COLOR TRIANGLE.
