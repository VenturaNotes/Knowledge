## Source [^1]
- (1) A type of pin-and-socket fitting in which the base of a lamp or tube has two pins, diametrically opposite each other, that can be inserted into the socket and rotated in slots in the socket so that the device is held in position. 
- (2) A type of plug-and-socket BNC connector where the pins are on the socket.
