## Source [^1]
- A specification (ISO 13346) designed to apply to a wide range of CD and DVD types, including rewritable disks.
## Source[^2]
- (UDF) A file system for data storage, defined according to ISO/IEC standard 13346, that is open and independent of any particular storage vendor. Its most prevalent use is with DVD and more recent optical disk technology.
