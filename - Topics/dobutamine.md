## Source [^1]
- $n$. an inotropic sympathomimetic drug (a $\beta_{1}$ agonist) used to increase the force of contraction of the ventricles and improve the heart output, for example in patients who have had a myocardial infarction or in cases of septic shock. It is given by intravenous infusion.
