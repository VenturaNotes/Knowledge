## Source [^1]
- A standard set at the theoretical limit of performance. For example, in manufacturing, the theoretical quality standard of 'zero defects', which it is impossible to improve on, might be set as the absolute performance standard. Such standards may be achievable in practice or may form an ideal against which an organization may judge its progress.
