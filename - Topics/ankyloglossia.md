## Source [^1]
- (tongue-tie) $n$. a congenital anomaly in which an unusually short, thick, or tight fraenum restricts the mobility of the tongue. It is often first noticed in a newborn who has difficulty breastfeeding. No treatment is required unless the condition is extreme, in which case surgical division of the fraenum may be necessary.
