## Source[^2]
- Describing a nucleus, cell, or organism with a single set of unpaired chromosomes. The haploid number is designated as $n$. Reproductive cells, formed as a result of meiosis, are haploid. Fusion of two such cells (see FERTILIZATION) restores the normal (diploid) number.
