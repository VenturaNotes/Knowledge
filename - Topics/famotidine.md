## Source [^1]
- $n$. an $\mathrm{H}_{2}$-receptor antagonist (see ANTIHISTAMINE) used for the treatment of gastric and duodenal ulcers and reflux oesophagitis. Sideeffects are headache, diarrhoea, and dizziness.
