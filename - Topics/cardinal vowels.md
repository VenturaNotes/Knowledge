## Source [^1]
- $n$. A set of idealized speech sounds devised by the British phonetician Daniel Jones (1881-1967), usually represented by their location in a vowel quadrilateral representing the mouth (see vowel), used as reference points to classify the vowel sounds of any language. See also BACK VOWEL, CENTRAL VOWEL, CLOSE VOWEL, FRONT VOWEL, MID VOWEL, OPEN VOWEL.
