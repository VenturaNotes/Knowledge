## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in surgery) a piece of metal used to join up pieces of tissue. Staples can be used as an alternative to sutures for an anastomosis or to bring together the skin edges of an incision; stapling machines have been produced for this purpose. See also ENDOSTAPLER.
