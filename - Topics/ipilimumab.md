## Source [^1]
- n. a monoclonal antibody that activates the immune system by blocking the normal inhibition of cytotoxic T cells, which recognize and destroy cancer cells. It is used for the treatment of advanced melanoma. Side-effects include colitis, rash, and liver disorders.
