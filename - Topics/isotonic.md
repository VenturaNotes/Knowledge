## Source[^3]
- adj. 
- (1) Of, relating to, or comprising two or more muscles that are equally tense. Compare hypertonic (1), hypotonic (1). 
- (2) Of or relating to two or more solutions having equal concentration and hence exerting the same osmotic pressure as each other, such as a 0.9 per cent sodium chloride (salt) solution and the intracellular fluid of the body. See also osmosis. Compare hypertonic (2), hypotonic (2). \[From Greek isos equal or the same + tonos tension + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
