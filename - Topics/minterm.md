## Source [^1]
- (standard product term) A product (AND) of $n$ Boolean variables, uncomplemented or complemented but not repeated, in a Boolean function of $n$ variables. With $n$ variables, $2^{n}$ different minterms are possible. The complement of any minterm is a maxterm. See also STANDARD SUM OF PRODUCTS.
