## Source [^1]
- $n$. a tumor that contains APUD cells and may give rise to symptoms caused by excessive production of the hormones and other peptides that these cells produce. Carcinoids are a good example of this group of tumors, but there are many others (e.g. gastrinomas, somatostatinomas, and VIPomas).
