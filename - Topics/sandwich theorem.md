## Source [^1]
- Let $a_n$, $b_n$, $c_n$ be real sequences such that $a_n \le b_n \le c_n$ for all $n$; the theorem states that if $a_n$ and $c_n$ converge to some limit L, then so does $b_n$. A similar result holds for the limits of functions. Variously also called the sandwich rule or squeeze theorem.
