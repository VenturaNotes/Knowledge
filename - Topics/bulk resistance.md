## Source [^1]
- (1) Ohmic resistance of p and n material in a diode. 
- (2) Resistance of a diode when forward biased and with a current flowing, defined as the ratio $(V - 0.7)/I$, where $V$ is the forward voltage $V$ and $I$ is the measured current. The subtracted amount $(0.7~\mathrm{V})$ is an approximation of the forward voltage needed to overcome the barrier potential of the diode.
