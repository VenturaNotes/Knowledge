## Source [^1]
- the volume of air that remains in the lungs after the individual has breathed out as much as he or she can. This volume is increased in emphysema.
## Source[^2]
- The amount of air remaining in the lungs after maximum expiration, which cannot be expelled from the lungs voluntarily. An average human has a residual volume of about 1-1.2 litres. See also VITAL CAPACITY.
