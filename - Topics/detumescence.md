## Source[^2]
- $n$. Subsidence of a swelling or enlargement, especially of the penis during the resolution phase of the sexual response cycle. Compare tumescence. \[From Latin detumescere to cease swelling, from de away from + tumescere to begin to swell, from tumere to swell]
