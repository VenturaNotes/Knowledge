## Source[^3]
- $n$. Either of the two halves of the cerebrum, separated by the longitudinal fissure, having slightly different functions in humans. Also called simply a hemisphere . See also CEREBRAL DOMINANCE, LATERALITY. \[From Latin cerebrum brain + hemi- half + sphaira a sphere or globe]
