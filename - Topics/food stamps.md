## Source [^1]
- Documents issued to poor families in the US under the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) to entitle them to obtain free or cut-price foodstuffs. This is a working example of the use of vouchers, a method of ensuring the use of public assistance for an approved purpose, in this case the provision of basic foodstuffs, rather than providing assistance in cash which can be used for any purpose.
