## Source [^1]
- (electronic position-indicating radio beacon) A radio transmitter that is used in the event of an aviation or maritime emergency to alert the search and rescue services to the emergency and to provide some information about the carrier and its location. EPIRBs transmit a coded signal on one of the worldwide recognized emergency transmission frequencies. See also PLB.
