## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a personality type resulting from fixation (2) at the oral sadistic phase and sublimation of the impulses of that phase in later life. It is characterized by aggressiveness, exploitativeness, ambition, and envy. Also called an oral-aggressive personality.
