## Source [^1]
- The status of an individual who has never lived in a particular country for fiscal purposes or who has moved to another country, either for employment or permanently. This person's liability to tax in the first country is restricted to income from sources within that country. Interest on all British government stocks is exempt from UK tax for non-residents. See also DOUBLE TAXATION; RESIDENT. Compare NON-DOMICILED.
