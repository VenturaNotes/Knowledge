## Source [^1]
- $n$. any one of a group of winged insects (Reduviidae) whose mouthparts $\textendash$ adapted for piercing and sucking $\textendash$ take the form of a long proboscis that is tucked beneath the head when not in use. Some South American genera, notably Panstrongylus, Rhodnius, and Triatoma $\textendash$ the kissing bugs, are nocturnal bloodsucking insects that transmit the parasite causing Chagas' disease.
