## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) An acoustic property of a speech sound, such as a formant, used to identify the sound and to distinguish it from other speech sounds. See also distinCTIVE FEATURE, MINIMAL PAIR, NEUTRALIZATION. 
- (2) A cue (3) that prompts recall of a remembered word by evoking its sound.
