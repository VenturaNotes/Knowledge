## Source [^1]
- $n$. the study of the identification and distribution of chemical compounds within and between cells, by means of stains, indicators, and light and electron microscopy. $\textendash$histochemical adj.
## Source[^2]
- The study of the distribution of the chemical constituents of tissues by means of their chemical reactions. It utilizes such techniques as staining, light and electron microscopy, autoradiography, and chromatography.
