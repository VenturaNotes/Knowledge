## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug that inhibits the action of vascular endothelial growth factor A (VEGF-A) and thereby prevents the development of new blood vessels (see ANGIOGENESIS INHIBITOR). It is injected into the vitreous humor to stop abnormal blood vessel growth and leakage in the eye(s) in treating wet age-related macular degeneration and is given by intravenous infusion, in combination with chemotherapy drugs, to treat metastatic colorectal cancer.
