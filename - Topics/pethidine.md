## Source [^1]
- $n$. a potent opioid analgesic (see OPIATE) with mild sedative action, used to relieve moderate or severe pain of short duration (including that associated with childbirth). Side-effects may include nausea, dizziness, and dry mouth.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Meperidine hydrochloride, a synthetic, white, crystalline, narcotic analgesic drug with a morphine-like action, used as a narcotic analgesic and sedative. US meperidine. Also called Demerol (trademark). See also MPPP, OPIOID-RELATED DISORDERS. \[A blend of piperidine and methyl]
