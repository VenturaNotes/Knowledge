## Source[^2]
- The set of all possible outcomes of an experiment. For example, suppose that an experiment involves tossing a coin three times. Then the sample space could be expressed as $$\{HHH,HHT,HTH,HTT,THH,THT,TTH,TTT\}$$where, for example, 'HTT' indicates the tosses came up 'heads, tails, tails'. See probability space, random variable.
