## Source [^1]
- (pl. primordia) A group of cells that represents the initial stages in development of a plant organ. Root and shoot primordia are present in a young plant embryo while leaf primordia (or leaf buttresses) are seen as hornlike projections from the sides of the dome of the apical meristem, just below the shoot apex.
