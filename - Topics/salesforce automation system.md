## Source [^1]
- (SFA system) A computer application used to coordinate and analyze various types of information relevant to salespeople and salesforce managers. It will include a detailed customer database, product information, activity records, and an order management system. Most SFA systems can now be used via smartphones. See also CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT SYSTEM.
