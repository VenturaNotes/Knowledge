## Source [^1]
- a US system that integrates the financing and delivery of medical care by means of contracts with physicians and with hospitals. The goal is to cut costs through economic incentives, cost sharing, controlling the length of inpatient admissions, and careful management of high-cost cases. Providers of managed care take several different forms, including health maintenance organizations and preferred provider organizations.
