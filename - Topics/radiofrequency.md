## Source [^1]
- (r.f.; RF) Any frequency of electromagnetic radiation or alternating currents in the range 3 kilohertz to 300 gigahertz (see FREQUENCY BAND). An electronic device, such as an amplifier, choke, or transformer, that operates in this range is known as a radiofrequency device or radio device; similarly any associated effect such as distortion is termed a radiofrequency effect or radio effect.
