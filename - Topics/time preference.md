## Source [^1]
- The preference for immediate consumption over an equal amount of consumption at a time in the future. Time preference in this sense is a consequence of an individual's (or society's) preferences over allocations of consumption at different points in time. See also DISCOUNTING THE FUTURE; SOCIAL TIME PREFERENCE.
