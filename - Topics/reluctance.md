## Source [^1]
- (magnetic resistance) (symbol: $R$; unit: henry$^{-1}$) The ratio of the magnetomotive force, $F_{\mathrm{m}}$, to the total magnetic flux, $\Phi$,$$R = \frac{F_{\mathrm{m}}}{\Phi}$$
