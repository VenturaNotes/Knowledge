## Source [^1]
- Tuning of a very high frequency (microwave) oscillator by means of a ferrite rod in the cavity resonator of the oscillator. The magnetization of the rod is determined by an external steady magnetic flux density, and is varied to provide a different frequency in the cavity by altering the flux density.
