## Source [^1]
- A graph on which observations are plotted on the $y$-axis for events on the $x$-axis. For example, the wages incurred ($y$-axis) for each level of activity ($x$-axis) would produce a scatter graph from which a relationship can be established between the two variables, as an aid to predicting cost behavior.
