## Source [^1]
- (cytokine modulator) any one of a group of agents that inhibit the activity of cytokines, especially tumor necrosis factor alpha (TNF- $\alpha$ ). They include infliximab, etanercept, and adalimumab, known as anti-TNF drugs, which are used as disease-modifying antirheumatic drugs and to treat inflammatory bowel disease.
