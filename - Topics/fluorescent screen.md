## Source [^1]
- A type of screen that is used in various electronic devices, such as cathode-ray tubes and image converters, to convert the electron beam into a visible image. These screens consist of an array of many small-diameter (two to three micrometer) phosphor crystals that emit light when bombarded by high-energy radiation, such as X-rays, or by electrons.
