## Source [^1]
- In chartist analysis, the situation in which the prices of a security fall, then rise, then fall again to the same level, producing a ' $W$ ' pattern on a graph. This pattern is thought to indicate that the security has much support at the price at the bottom of the W. Compare DOUBLE TOP. See SUPPORT LEVEL.
