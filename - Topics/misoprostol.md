## Source [^1]
- $n$. a prostaglandin drug administered in the prevention and treatment of peptic ulcer, especially when caused by nonsteroidal antiinflammatory drugs (see NSAID), in which case it is given in conjunction with the NSAID (see DICLOFENAC; NAPROXEN). It is also used to terminate a pregnancy, being administered vaginally following mifepristone. Possible side-effects include diarrhoea.
