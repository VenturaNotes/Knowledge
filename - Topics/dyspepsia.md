## Source [^1]
- (indigestion) $n$. disordered digestion: usually applied to pain or discomfort in the lower chest or upper abdomen after eating and sometimes accompanied by nausea, vomiting, or a feeling of unease or fullness after eating. -dyspeptic adj.
