## Source [^1]
- $n$. A state of confusion arising from an inability to reconcile conflicting aspects of one's personality. The concept was popularized by the German-born ego psychologist Erik H. Erikson (1902-94) in his book Identity: Youth and Conflict (1968). See DeVELOPMENTAL CRISES, EGO IDENTITY. See also DISSOCIATIVE IDENTITY DISORDER, GENDER DYSPHORIA.
