## Source [^1]
- $n$. A gonadotrophin secreted by the placenta in humans and other mammals, stimulating the release of progesterone by the corpus luteum. A measure of its presence in the urine is used as a pregnancy test. Also spelt chorionic gonadotropin. \[From Greek chorion an afterbirth]
