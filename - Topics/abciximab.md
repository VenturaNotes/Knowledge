## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antiplatelet drug (a monoclonal antibody) that is used as an adjunct to lessen the chance of a heart attack during surgery to open blocked arteries of the heart (see CORONARY ANGIOPLASTY). Side-effects include bleeding, nausea, chest and back pain, and headache.
