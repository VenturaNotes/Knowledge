## Source [^1]
- $n$. any member of a group of organic compounds consisting of a carbonyl group (=CO) flanked by two alkyl groups. The ketones acetoacetic acid, acetone, and $\beta$-hydroxybutyrate (known as ketone (or acetone) bodies) are produced during the metabolism of fats. See also KETOSIS.
