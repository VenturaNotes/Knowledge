## Source [^1]
- (1) Denoting the gamete (sex cell) that, during sexual reproduction, fuses with a male gamete in the process of fertilization. Female gametes are generally larger than the male gametes and are usually immotile (see OOSPHERE; OVUM). 
- (2) (Denoting) an individual organism whose reproductive organs produce only female gametes. Compare HERMAPHRODITE.
