## Source [^1]
- The posterior region of the body trunk of animals. In vertebrates it contains the stomach and intestines and the organs of excretion and reproduction. It is particularly well defined in mammals, being separated from the thorax by the diaphragm. In many arthropods, such as insects and spiders, it may be segmented.
