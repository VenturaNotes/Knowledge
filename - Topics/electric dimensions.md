## Source [^1]
- Of an electronic circuit or structure, quantities that are measured in wavelengths at the frequency of interest. Electric dimensions are useful in determining the properties of a system or the method used to determine these properties. In particular, a system is considered to be electrically small if its dimensions are significantly less than a wavelength, typically a factor 10 to 100 times smaller.
