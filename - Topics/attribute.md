## Source [^1]
- In general, an attribute is a property, quality, or characteristic that something or someone has.
## Source[^2]
- A defined property of an entity, object, etc. In computer graphics it is a particular property that applies to a graphical output primitive; lines have attributes such as line width, color, and line style. See also ERA MODEL, INHERITANCE.
