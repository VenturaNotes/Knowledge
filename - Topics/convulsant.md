## Source [^1]
- adj. n. (Of or pertaining to) a drug such as picrotoxin, strychnine, or tetanus toxin that produces convulsions by blocking the inhibitory action of neurotransmitters such as GABA or acetylcholine in the nervous system. Compare ANTICONVULSANT.
