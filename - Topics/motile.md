## Source [^1]
- adj. being able to move spontaneously, without external aid: usually applied to a microorganism or a cell (e.g. a sperm cell).
## Source[^2]
- adj. 
- (1) Exhibiting or capable of motion. n. 
- (2) A person whose characteristic style of mental imagery is motor rather than visual, auditory, or tactile, taking the form of feelings of motion such as limb movements or muscular movements associated with the pronunciation of words. Compare AUDILE (2), TACTILE (2), VISUALIZER.
