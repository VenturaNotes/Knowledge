## Source [^1]
- $n$. a pharmaceutical preparation of the steroid hormone cortisol. Hydrocortisone is used in the treatment of adrenal failure (Addison's disease), shock, and inflammatory, allergic, and rheumatic conditions (including rheumatoid arthritis, colitis, and eczema). Possible side-effects include peptic ulcers, bone and muscle damage, suppression of growth in children, and the signs of Cushing's syndrome.
