## Source [^1]
- The form of advertising that consists of small typeset or semi-display advertisements grouped together in such categories as cars for sale, furniture for sale, flats to let, etc., usually in a paper or magazine. They are usually inserted by direct contact between the advertiser and the advertising department of the publication.
