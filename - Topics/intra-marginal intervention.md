## Source [^1]
- (in foreign exchange markets) Intervention in the foreign exchange market by the central bank or other monetary authorities before an exchange rate has reached the limit to which it is desired to restrict fluctuations. This is contrasted with intervention when the rate reaches its limit, to prevent it from moving any further.
