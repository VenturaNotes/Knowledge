## Source [^1]
- (1) The ratio of the average power dissipation in a line, circuit, or device to the power dissipated at peak load. 
- (2) The product of the power factor and the relative permittivity of a dielectric. For a given alternating field the power factor is proportional to the heat generated in the material.
