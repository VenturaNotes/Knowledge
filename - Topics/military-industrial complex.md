## Source [^1]
- An expression used to describe the combination of a country's armed forces and the parts of its industry related to military supply. The phrase is often used in a pejorative way in reference to the accusation that the military-industrial complex in major powers has been motivated by vested interests to influence policy in ways which promote international tension and thus lead to arms races or even wars.
