## Source [^1]
- An application of ultrasonics that measures the distance to an object by measuring the time it takes for an ultrasound pulse to travel out to that object and return as an echo. This technique is used in cars with sensors that assist with parking by detecting how close the car is to other objects. It is also used to enable robotic devices to avoid collisions with objects in their environment.
