## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) a unit of weight used in pharmacy. 1 drachm $=3.883 \mathrm{~g}$ ( 60 grains). 
- (2) a unit of volume used in pharmacy. 1 fluid drachm $=3.696 \mathrm{ml}$ ( $1 / 8$ fluid ounce).
