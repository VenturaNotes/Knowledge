## Source [^1]
- The directional growth of a plant organ in response to an external stimulus, such as light, touch, or gravity. Growth towards the stimulus is a positive tropism; growth away from the stimulus is a negative tropism. See also GEOTROPISM; HYdROTROPISM; ORTHOTROPISM; PHOTOTROPISM; PLAGIOTROPISM; THIGMOTROPISM. Compare NASTIC MOVEMENTS; TAXIS.
