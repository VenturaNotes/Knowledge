## Source [^1]
- (ABS) A braking system for wheeled vehicles that detects when a wheel is about to lock and reduces the braking force to ensure the wheel continues to rotate. This prevents the vehicle from skidding when braking hard, allowing the driver to apply full force to the brakes in an emergency without having to worry about losing traction and control. See also TRACTION CONTROL SYSTEM.
