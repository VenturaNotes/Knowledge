## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) treatment that involves exposure to ultraviolet or infrared radiation. For example, ultraviolet B radiation is used for treating severe psoriasis. See also PUVA. 
- (2) see PHOTODYNAMIC THERAPY.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Controlled exposure to extremely bright light (generally 10,000 lux), simulating daylight, used especially to treat seasonal affective disorder (SAD) and certain sleep-wake disorders. Also called bright light therapy. \[From Greek phos, photos light + therapeia service or treatment, from therapeuein to take care of or to heal]
