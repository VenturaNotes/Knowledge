## Source [^1]
- A generic term for a microwave circuit medium where the microwave signal flows as a wave along one or more conductors supported by a dielectric medium (which can be air). The stripline is a distributed circuit, possessing reactance as a part of its structure. Practical examples of striplines include the microstrip and coplanar waveguide.
