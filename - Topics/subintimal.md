## Source [^1]
- adj. underlying the inner layer (intima) of a blood vessel: commonly used in vascular and interventional radiology. When a blood vessel is occluded with atheroma, a channel is created in the subintimal plane with a wire and the vessel is opened up by angioplasty and/or stent insertion.
