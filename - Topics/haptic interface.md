## Source [^1]
- An interface that provides feedback to users via their sense of touch. Such interfaces range from the simple use of vibration in smartphones (to indicate incoming calls or give feedback regarding key presses) to force-feedback joysticks used for greater realism in flight simulators. A key application area is in virtual reality systems, where they can simulate the touch and feel of objects.
