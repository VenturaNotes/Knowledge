## Source [^1]
- (symbol: $Y$; unit: siemens) The reciprocal of impedance. It is a complex quantity given by$$Y = G + \mathrm{j} B$$where $G$ is the conductance, $B$ the susceptance, and $\mathrm{j} = \sqrt{-1}$. Since impedance, $Z$, is given by$$Z = R + jX$$where $R$ and $X$ are the resistance and reactance, respectively, then$$Y = \frac{1}{Z} = \frac{1}{(R + jX)} = \frac{(R - jX)}{(R^2 + X^2)}$$
