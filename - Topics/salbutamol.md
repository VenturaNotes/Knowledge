## Source [^1]
- n. a drug that stimulates $\beta_{2}$ adrenoceptors (see SYMPATHOMIMETIC): a short-acting beta agonist used as a bronchodilator to relieve asthma and other reversible obstructive airways diseases. Sideeffects may include dizziness, tremor, and fast heart rate, particularly after large doses.
