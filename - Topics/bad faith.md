## Source [^1]
- $n$. In the existentialism of the French philosopher Jean-Paul Sartre (1905-80), a form of self-deception and deception of others in which people refuse to acknowledge responsibility for their own freely chosen actions and perceive or depict themselves as the passive victims of circumstance. See also existential therapy.
