## Source [^1]
- A change in the business strategy of a company by diversification into new areas, which it considers more attractive. Alternatively, following poor performance, it may seek to withdraw from an area by divestment or closure of parts of its business, which it no longer considers to be part of its future strategic focus. See also DOWNSIZING; RIGHTSIZING.
