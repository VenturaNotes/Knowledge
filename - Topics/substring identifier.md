## Source [^1]
- substring identifier Let $\alpha=a_{1} a_{2} \ldots a_{n}$ denote a string in $\Sigma^{*}$ and let $\# \notin \Sigma$. The substring identifier for position $i$ in $\alpha \#$ is the shortest substring in $\alpha \#$ starting at position $i$ that identifies position $i$ uniquely. The existence of such a substring is guaranteed since$$a_{i} a_{i+1} \ldots a_{n}$$will always identify position $i$ uniquely. See also POSITION TREE.
