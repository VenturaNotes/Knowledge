## Source [^1]
- The maximum output of goods and services a firm or an economy is capable of producing. See also EXCESS CAPACITY; SPARE CAPACITY.

## Source[^2]
- (1) The amount of information that can be held in a storage device. The amount may be measured in words, bytes, bits, or characters. 
- (2) The maximum range of values that can be held in a register. 
- (3) of a transmission channel. See CHANNEL CODING THEOREM.

## Source[^3]
- (1) (of a cut) The sum of all the maximum flows allowable across all edges intersecting the cut (see network)
- (2) (of an edge) The maximum flow possible along an edge (see network)
