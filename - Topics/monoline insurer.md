## Source [^1]
- A company that provides guarantees to bond issuers as a form of credit enhancement. In recent years monoline insurers have been very active in the markets for collateralized debt obligations and other complex structured finance instruments. The subprime lending disaster of 2007 led to major losses for some monoline insurers.
