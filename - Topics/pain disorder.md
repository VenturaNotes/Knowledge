## Source [^1]
- $n$. In DSM-IV, a somatoform disorder characterized by pain, causing clinically significant distress or impairment in social, occupational, or other important areas of functioning, with psychological factors judged to play an important role in the onset or maintenance of the pain. This condition was relabelled somatic symptom disorder in DSM-5. Compare FACTITIOUS DISORDER, MALINGERING.
