## Source [^1]
- Prefix used with SI units to denote multiplication by $10^{-18}$. Abbreviated as a.
## Source[^2]
- Symbol a. A prefix used in the metric system to denote $10^{-18}$. For example, $10^{-18}$ second $=1$ attosecond (as).
## Source[^3]
- (symbol: a) A prefix to a unit, denoting a submultiple of $10^{-18}$ of that unit.
