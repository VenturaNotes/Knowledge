## Source [^1]
- The factors controlled by a company that can influence consumers' buying of its products. The four components of a marketing mix (often called the four Ps) are:
	- the product$\textemdash$quality, branding, packaging, and other features;
	- pricing$\textemdash$recommended retail price, discounts for large orders, and credit terms;
	- promotion$\textemdash$see SALES PROMOTION;
	- place$\textemdash$where to sell the product, which distributors and transport services to use, and desirable stock levels.
- The potential profitability of a particular marketing mix and its acceptability to its market are assessed by marketing research. See also EXTENDED MARKETING MIX.
