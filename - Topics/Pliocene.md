## Source [^1]
- The second epoch of the Neogene period. Preceded by the Miocene and followed by the Pleistocene, it extended from about 5.3 million years ago to 2.58 million years ago. Mammals similar to modern forms existed during the epoch and the australopithecines (see AUSTRALOPITHECUS), hominins that coexisted with early humans, appeared.
