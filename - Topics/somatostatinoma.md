## Source [^1]
- $n$. a rare tumor of the islets of Langerhans that produces excessive amounts of somatostatin. It is an example of an apudoma. In severe cases it can cause the somatostatinoma syndrome, consisting of diabetes, gall-bladder disease, and steatorrhoea due to malabsorption.
