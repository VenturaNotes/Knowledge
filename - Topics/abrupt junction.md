## Source [^1]
- A p-n junction in which the impurity concentration changes abruptly from acceptors to donors (see SEMICONDUCTOR). In practice such a junction may be approximately realized when one side of the junction is much more highly doped than the other, i.e. a $p^+\text{-}n$ or $n^+\text{-}p$ junction. This is a one-sided abrupt junction.
