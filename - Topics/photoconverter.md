## Source [^1]
- A photoelectric transducer that produces digital electrical signals from an optical pattern. It consists of an array of photocells each of which produces a current proportional to the intensity of light falling on it. The illumination may be due to an optical image produced on a transparent screen or a pattern resulting from an illuminated photographic mask or cut-out of opaque material.
