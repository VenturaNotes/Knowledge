## Source [^1]
- (digital business)
- In this context, a product refers to a broad collection of software or digital services which is ultimately used by an end consumer. A product is available for purchase or selling and can also have a variety of different sales models. For example, a one-time purchase is a standard way of buying a product. A subscription basis is another business model.
