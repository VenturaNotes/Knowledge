## Source [^1]
- A nanostructure in which two dimensions are in the nanometer range while the third is much larger (often a thousand times or more). Their electrical behaviour is dominated by quantum effects, owing to their small width. Applications include more efficient solar-cells, water pollution treatment, and flexible displays. See also QUANTUM WIRE.
