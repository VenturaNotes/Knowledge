## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) the regrowth of blood vessels following disease or injury so that normal blood supply to an organ, tissue, or part is restored. 
- (2) the restoration of blood flow to an organ by angioplasty/stenting or surgical bypass grafting. See CORONARY REVASCULARIZATION.
