## Source [^1]
- $n$. One of the carbamates, a central nervous system depressant that is prescribed as a sedative-hypnotic drug in the treatment of anxiety disorders and insomnia. Also called Doriden (trademark). See also SEDATIVE-, HYPNOTIC-, OR ANXIOLYTIC-RELATED DISORDERS. \[From its chemical name glut(aryl) + eth(yl) + imide]
