## Source [^1]
- $n$. an infectious disease of sheep and goats caused by a parapoxvirus. Those bottle-feeding infected lambs may develop a painful nodule, $2-3 \mathrm{~cm}$ across, on the fingers or hands; it resolves spontaneously.
