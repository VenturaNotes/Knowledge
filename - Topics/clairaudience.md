## Source [^1]
- $n$. A conjectural paranormal ability to sense sounds that are beyond the range of normal hearing. See also extra-sensory perception (ESP). Compare Clairvoyance. \[From French clair clear, from Latin clarus clear + audientia hearing, from audire to hear]
