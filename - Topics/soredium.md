## Source [^1]
- (pl. soredia) A small body, consisting of a core of algal cells surrounded by fungal hyphae, that functions as a structure of vegetative propagation in lichens. Soredia, which range from 25 to $100 ~\mu \mathrm{m}$ in diameter, are released like spores and are dispersed in air currents. Compare ISIDIUM.
