## Source [^1]
- (sustainable chemistry) The study of how chemistry can be used to prevent the production of dangerous or hazardous substances, improve the environment, make efficient use of energy, and dispose of waste in a harmless way. There has been a great deal of research into practical ways of implementing these principles since the 1970s.
