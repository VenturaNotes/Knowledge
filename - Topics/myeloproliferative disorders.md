## Source [^1]
- (MPD) a group of diseases in which there is excessive production of blood cells in the bone marrow. Myeloproliferative disorders include polycythaemia vera, essential thrombocythaemia, idiopathic myelofibrosis, and chronic myeloid leukaemia.
