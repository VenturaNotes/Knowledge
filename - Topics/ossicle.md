## Source [^1]
- $n$. a small bone. The auditory ossicles are three small bones (the incus, malleus, and stapes) in the middle ear. They transmit sound from the outer ear to the labyrinth (inner ear).
## Source[^2]
- Any small bony or chitinous structure found in various skeletal parts of animals. The term ossicle usually refers to any of the chain of bones in the mammalian ear (see EAR OSSICLES).
