## Source [^1]
- A discrete distribution with a probability function of the form$$P[X=x \mid p]=p(1-p)^{x-1}$$for $x=1,2, \ldots$ and $0 \leq p \leq 1$. The geometric distribution has mean $1 / p$ and variance $(1-p) / p^{2}$.
