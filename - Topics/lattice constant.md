## Source [^1]
- A parameter that describes the configuration of a crystal lattice. The lattice constant is given either as the lengths of the edges of a unit cell of a crystal or as the angle between the axes of the cell. The former description is also termed the lattice parameter or lattice spacing. The edge length of a cubic unit cell is usually given.
