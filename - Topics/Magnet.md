## Source [^1]
- A body that possesses the property of magnetism. The term is applied to those bodies that can produce an appreciable magnetic field external to themselves. Magnets are either temporary or permanent. See also PERMANENT MAGNET; ELECTROMAGNET; FERROMAGNETISM.
