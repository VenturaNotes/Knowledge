## Source [^1]
- (RCH) in the UK, a body authorized to act as a clearing house under the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000. There are currently five: LCH.Clearnet (see London Clearing House), Euroclear, ICE Clear Europe (see ICE Futures Europe and now including the LIFFE), CME Clearing Europe, and LME Clear (the clearing house for the London Metal Exchange).
