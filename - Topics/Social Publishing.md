## Source [^1]
- Social publishing allows individuals to store self-published content in a central shared repository that other people can access to view the content and provide feedback. An example of social publishing is the comments section of a blog, where people can give their opinions about a shared interest. Social publishing is a collaborative tool that enables the public to comment on topics.
