## Source [^1]
- Any building in which goods are stored; a public warehouse is one at or near a port in which goods are stored after being unloaded from a ship or before being loaded onto a ship (see also BONDED WAREHOUSE). When goods are taken into a public warehouse a warehouse warrant is issued, which must be produced before the goods can be removed.
