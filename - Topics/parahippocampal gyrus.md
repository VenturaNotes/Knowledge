## Source [^1]
- $n$. The gyrus of the cerebral cortex immediately adjacent to the hippocampus, considered as part of the limbic system. It receives a constant stream of information from the association areas that it transmits to the hippocampus, mainly via the entorhinal cortex, for consolidation in long-term memory, and it is also involved in the control of aggression.
