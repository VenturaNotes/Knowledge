## Source [^1]
- Acronym for Undertakings for Collective Investment in Transferable Securities: unit trusts or investment trusts that are permitted to operate throughout the EU on the basis of their admission by the domestic regulator in one member state. A set of rules making this possible was adopted in 1985 and widened in 2001, 2009 and 2014.
