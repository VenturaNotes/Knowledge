## Source [^1]
- Mathematical techniques for converting a rational function of the form$$\frac {f (x)}{g (x)}$$where both $f(x)$ and $g(x)$ are polynomials into a sum of simpler rational functions of the form$$\sum_ {i} \frac {f _ {i} (x)}{g _ {i} (x)}$$where the $g_{i}$ are of lower degree than $g$. These techniques are most useful as a way to integrate rational functions by converting them to a sum of simpler rational functions whose integrals are easy to determine.
