## Source [^1]
- Personal income actually available for spending. This is total or gross income less direct tax and social security contributions.
## Source[^2]
- (1) The income a person has available to spend after payment of taxes, National Insurance contributions, and other deductions, such as pension contributions. 
- (2) In national income accounts, the total value of income of individuals and households available for consumer expenditure and savings, after deducting income tax, National Insurance contributions, and remittances overseas.
