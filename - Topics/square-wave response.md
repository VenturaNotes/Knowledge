## Source [^1]
- In general, the response of any electronic circuit or device to a square-wave input signal, given as the wave shape and peak-to-peak amplitude of the output signal.
- In particular, it is the peak-to-peak amplitude obtained from a television camera tube in response to a test pattern of alternate black and white bars.
