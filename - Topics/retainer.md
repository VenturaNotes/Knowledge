## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in dentistry) 
- (1) the component of a partial denture that keeps it in place. 
- (2) an orthodontic appliance that holds the teeth in position at the end of active treatment. 
- (3) the component of a bridge that is fixed to a natural tooth.
