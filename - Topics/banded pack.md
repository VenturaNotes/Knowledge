## Source [^1]
- A special offer of goods in which two or more related (or sometimes unrelated) items are bound together to form a single pack. This pack is offered at a lower price than the combined price of the individual items. Banded packs are sometimes offered with the slogan 'buy one, get one free'. See PRICE-BUNDLING STRATEGY.
