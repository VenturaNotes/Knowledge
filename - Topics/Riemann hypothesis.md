## Source [^1]
- (Riemann zeta hypothesis) The hypothesis that the non-trivial zeros of the zeta function all have real part equal to $\frac 12$. It was both on Hilbert’s list of 23 problems in 1900 and on the list of Millennium Prizes in 2000.
