## Characteristics
- Periodic with period 2$\pi$ [^1]
- Even Function[^2]
- For Right Triangles [^3]
	- $cos\theta = \frac {\text{adj}}{\text{hyp}}$
-  For [[Unit Circle]] [^4]
	- $cos\theta = \frac {\text{x}}{\text{r}}$
- Limit [^5]
	- $lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1 - cosx}{x} = 0$
- Getting Derivative [^6]
	- $y = cosx$
	- $y' = -sinx$
