## Source [^1]
- (investment manager) An employee of one of the larger institutions, such as an insurance company, investment trust, or pension fund, who manages its investment fund. The fund manager decides which investments the fund shall hold, in accordance with the specified aims of the fund, e.g. high income, maximum growth, etc.
