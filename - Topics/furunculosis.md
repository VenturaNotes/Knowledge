## Source [^1]
- $n$. the occurrence of several boils (furuncles) at the same time, usually caused by Staphylococcus aureus infection. Treatment includes thorough daily disinfection of the skin and incision (lancing), which may be more effective than antibiotic therapy. Diabetes mellitus should be excluded.
