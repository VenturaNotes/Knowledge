## Source [^1]
- Two haploid nuclei in the centre of the embryo sac of flowering plants. These nuclei fuse with a male gamete nucleus to form a triploid endosperm nucleus, which subsequently divides to form the endosperm. See also DOUBLE FERTILIZATION.
