## Source [^1]
- $n$. poisoning due to an overdose of aspirin or other salicylate-containing compounds. The main symptoms are headache, dizziness, ringing in the ears (tinnitus), disturbances of vision, vomiting, and $\textendash$ in severe cases $\textendash$ delirium and collapse. There is often severe acidosis.
