## Source [^1]
- $n$. a procedure in which the blood supply to a tumor is blocked with a synthetic embolic agent and cytotoxic drugs are then administered directly into the tumor. It is most frequently used for the treatment of liver cancer. See also TRANSARTERIAL CHEMOEMBOLIZATION.
