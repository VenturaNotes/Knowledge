## Source [^1]
- a technique for creating a mechanical bond between resin-based materials and the enamel of teeth; it is used to retain and seal the margins of composite fillings, to retain brackets of fixed orthodontic appliances, and to retain resin-based fissure sealants and adhesive bridges. Phosphoric acid is used to create a porous surface into which a bonding agent can flow.
