## Source [^1]
- $n$. The leaf of the betel pepper Piper betle or the nut of the betel palm Areca catechu, sometimes chewed, especially by people of the Indian subcontinent and South-East Asia, as a digestive and for its psychotropic effects of mild euphoria. See also euphoriant. \[Portuguese, from Malayalam vettila]
