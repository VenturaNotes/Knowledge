## Source [^1]
- $n$. an agent that reduces the normal activity of any body system or function. Drugs such as general anaesthetics, barbiturates, and opioids are depressants of the central nervous system and respiration. Cytotoxic drugs, such as azathioprine, are depressants of the levels of white blood cells.
