## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of a number of genetically determined and highly variable proteins, including the major histocompatibility antigens, that are displayed on the surface of almost every body cell, defining tissue type, and are involved in antigen recognition. \[From Greek histos tissue + Latin compatibilis agreeable]
