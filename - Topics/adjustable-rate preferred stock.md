## Source [^1]
- (ARP) Stock or cumulative preference shares in the USA whose dividends are linked to Treasury Bill interest rates. Minimum and maximum rates are specified by the application of a collar. Convertible adjusted-rate preferred stock can be converted to common stock at a fixed price at specified dates.
