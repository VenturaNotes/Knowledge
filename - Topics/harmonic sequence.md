## Source [^1]
- AKA harmonic progression
- A sequence $a_1, a_2, a_3, ...$ such that $\frac {1}{a_1}, \frac {1}{a_2}, \frac {1}{a_3},...$ is an arithmetic sequence. The most commonly occurring harmonic sequence is the sequence $1, \frac 12 \frac 13 \frac 14, ...$
