## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug that causes the pupil of the eye to dilate. Examples are atropine, cyclopentolate, and phenylephrine. Mydriatics in the form of eye drops or ointments are used to aid examination of the eye and to treat some eye inflammations such as iritis and cyclitis.
