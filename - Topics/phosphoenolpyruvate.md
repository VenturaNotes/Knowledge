## Source [^1]
- (PEP) A three-carbon compound that is the substrate for carbon dioxide fixation during photosynthesis in $\mathrm{C}_{4}$ plants (see c). PEP is also an intermediate in glycolysis (being the immediate precursor of pyruvate) and the glyoxylate cycle.
