## Source [^1]
- $n$. A psychophysical technique in which an observer adjusts a variable stimulus until it stands in some prescribed relation of magnitude to a standard stimulus, as when an observer adjusts a sound stimulus until it appears equal in loudness, twice as loud, or half as loud as a comparison sound. Also called the production method. See also CROSS-MODAL MATCHING. Compare MAGNITUDE ESTIMATION.
