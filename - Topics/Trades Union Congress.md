## Source[^2]
- (TUC) The UK national organization representing trade unions. Its main functions are to lobby the government on issues concerning workers, particularly legislation affecting employment and social security, and to settle disputes between member unions. The TUC has a fair amount of moral influence but no legal authority over its member unions.
- http://www.tuc.org.uk/
