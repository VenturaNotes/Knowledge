## Source [^1]
- n. An inability, sometimes classified as a form of apraxia, to perform rapid rhythmic movements such as finger tapping, often indicative of damage to the cerebellum. Compare Dysdiadochokinesia. \[From Greek $a$ - without + diadochos a successor + kinesis movement + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
