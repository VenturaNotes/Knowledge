## Source [^1]
- (1) Any of various particulate structures formed in plant and animal cells due to viral infection. Inclusion bodies sometimes become embedded in cellular proteins. 
- (2) A storage granule found in cells of bacteria or archaea. They may consist of energy compounds (e.g. poly-$\beta$-hydroxybutyric acid or elemental sulphur) or serve as reservoirs of structural components.
