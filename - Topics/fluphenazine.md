## Source [^1]
- $n$. a phenothiazine antipsychotic drug used for the treatment of schizophrenia and other psychotic disorders. Side-effects include abnormal muscular movements (see EXTRAPYRAMIDAL EFFECTS).
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Fluphenazine hydrochloride, or fluphenazine decanoate, or fluphenazine enanthate, different forms of a neuroleptic (1) drug belonging to the group of phenothiazines, frequently used in the treatment of schizophrenia. Also called Prolixin (trademark).
