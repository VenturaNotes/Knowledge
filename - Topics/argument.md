## Source[^2]
- A value or address passed to a procedure or function at the time of call. Thus in the Basic statement$$\mathrm{Y}=\operatorname{SQR}(\mathrm{X})$$X is the argument of the SQR (square root) function. Arguments are sometimes referred to as actual parameters.
