## Source [^1]
- A letter of credit that guarantees a loan or other form of credit facility. The bank that issues it promises to refund the amount borrowed if the borrower defaults on repayment. It calls for a certificate of default by the applicant. In a note issuance facility a standby credit is a third-party guarantee to honor an issue to an investor who may have a low credit rating. See also REVOLVING BANK FACILITY.
