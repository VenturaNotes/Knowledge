## Source [^1]
- (1) A measure of the purity (fineness) of gold. Pure gold is defined as 24 carat; 14-carat gold contains 14/24ths gold, the remainder usually being copper. 
- (2) A unit for measuring the weight of a diamond or other gemstone, equal to 0.2 gram.
