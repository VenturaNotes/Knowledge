## Source [^1]
- Firms that refer clients to outside consultants with expertise in their fields in return for a fee. Expert networks are often used by financial professionals to obtain specialized information relevant to investment decisions, for example whether or not to back a new product or company. However, such advice has the potential to cause conflicts of interest when confidential or proprietary information is involved and may give rise to allegations of insider dealing.
