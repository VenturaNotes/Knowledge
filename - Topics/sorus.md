## Source [^1]
- (pl. sori) 
- (1) Any of the spore-producing structures on the undersurface of a fern frond, visible as rows of small brown dots. 
- (2) A reproductive area on the thallus of some algae, e.g. Laminaria. 
- (3) Any of various spore-producing structures in certain fungi.
