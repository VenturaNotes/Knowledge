## Source [^1]
- A signal, often a voltage or current, whose amplitude may have any value between limits defined by the associated circuitry. It may represent a measured physical quantity, such as temperature or position, or may carry information as audio or video waveforms, etc. See also A/D CONVERTER, D/A CONVERTER, DISCRETE AND CONTINUOUS SYSTEMS.
