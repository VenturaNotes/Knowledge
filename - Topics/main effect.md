## Source [^1]
- $n$. In statistics, in data obtained from an analysis of variance (ANOVA) or the transformed scores of a log-linear analysis, the mean change in scores resulting from a change in the level of a single independent variable or factor, averaged over all combinations of levels of the other factor(s). Compare INTERACTION EFFECT.
