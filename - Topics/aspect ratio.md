## Source [^1]
- The ratio of width to height for a specified rectangular area such as a display surface or window.
## Source[^2]
- (1) The ratio of the width of a television picture to the height. Most countries, including the UK and US, have adopted an aspect ratio of 4:3. See also HDTV. 
- (2) The ratio of the width to the length of the channel in field-effect transistors.
