## Source [^1]
- The regular and systematic pattern of growth such that the mass or size of any organ or part of a body can be expressed in relation to the total mass or size of the entire organism according to the allometric equation: $Y=b x^{\alpha}$, where $Y=$ mass of the organ, $x$ $=$ mass of the organism, $\alpha=$ growth coefficient of the organ, and $b=$ a constant. See also KLEIBER'S LAW.
