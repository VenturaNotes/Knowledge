## Source [^1]
- Indexing information providing an online directory of services on a network. In contrast, the information of the [[white pages]] provides an online index of users on a network. In both cases the services or users indexed will normally be those of local interest, typically those on a local area network. As the yellow pages are used directly by the computer systems on the network to access services provided by other systems, there is a high premium on accuracy
