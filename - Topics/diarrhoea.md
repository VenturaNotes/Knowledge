## Source [^1]
- $n$. frequent bowel evacuation or the passage of abnormally soft or liquid faeces. It may be caused by intestinal infections, inflammation (such as ulcerative colitis or Crohn's disease), malabsorption, anxiety, drugs, and irritable bowel syndrome. Severe or prolonged diarrhoea may lead to excessive loss of water, salts, and nutrients.
