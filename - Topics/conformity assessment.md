## Source [^1]
- Refers to any activity that determines whether a product, system, service and sometimes people fulfill the requirements and characteristics described in a standard or specification. 
- Such requirements can include
	- Performance
	- Safety
	- Efficiency
	- Effectiveness
	- Reliability
	- Durability
	- Environmental Impacts
		- Pollution
		- Noise
- Verification done through testing or/and inspection
- May or may not include on-going verification
