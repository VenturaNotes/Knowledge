## Source [^1]
- a method for checking the regeneration of a nerve: usually used in patients with carpal tunnel syndrome. Direct tapping over the sheath of the nerve elicits a distal tingling sensation (see PARAESTHESIA), which indicates the beginning of regeneration. \[J. Tinel (1879-1952), French neurosurgeon]
