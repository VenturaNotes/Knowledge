## Source [^1]
- $n$. abnormally large size. In fetal macrosomia the baby is large for its gestational age. This condition is associated with poorly controlled maternal diabetes, the increased size being due to excessive production of fetal insulin and thence to increased deposition of glycogen in the fetus, and maternal obesity.
