## Source [^1]
- $n$. a tetracycline antibiotic that is used to treat infections caused by Chlamydia, rickettsiae, and mycoplasmas. It is also used to treat syndrome of inappropriate secretion of antidiuretic hormone. Common side-effects are nausea, diarrhoea, and symptoms resulting from the growth of organisms not sensitive to the drug.
