## Source [^1]
- $n$. the front part of the embryonic gut, which gives rise to the oesophagus (gullet), stomach, and part of the small intestine (from which the liver and pancreas develop).
## Source[^2]
- (1) The anterior region of the alimentary canal of vertebrates, up to the anterior part of the duodenum. 
- (2) The anterior part of the alimentary canal of arthropods. See also HINDGUT; MIDGUT.
