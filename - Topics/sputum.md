## Source [^1]
- $n$. material coughed up from the respiratory tract. Its characteristics, including color, consistency, volume, smell, and the appearance of any solid material within it, often provide important information affecting the diagnosis and management of respiratory disease. Pathological examination of sputum for microorganisms and for abnormal cells may add further information.
