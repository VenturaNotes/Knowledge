## Source [^1]
- (also lean sensei)
- A lean sensei is an expert in lean management who helps companies implement lean processes. Lean management is a set of techniques that can improve the efficiency of business operations. These techniques are based on the idea of continuous improvement, meaning businesses should always be looking for ways to make their operations faster and more efficient.
