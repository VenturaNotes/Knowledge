## Source [^1]
- $n$. sensation experienced in the deeper parts of the body, such as the joints and muscles.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Sensation from receptors inside the body. US bathyesthesia. Also called bathaesthesia or bathesthesia . Compare bathyanaesthesia. \[From Greek bathys deep + aisthesis sensation, from aisthanesthai to feel + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
