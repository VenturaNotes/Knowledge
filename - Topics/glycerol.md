## Source [^1]
- (glycerine; propane-1,2,3,-triol) A trihydric alcohol, $\mathrm{HOCH}_{2} \mathrm{CH}(\mathrm{OH}) \mathrm{CH}_{2} \mathrm{OH}$. Glycerol is a colorless sweet-tasting viscous liquid, miscible with water but insoluble in ether. It is widely distributed in all living organisms as a constituent of the glycerides, which yield glycerol when hydrolysed. Glycerol itself is used as an antifreeze molecule by certain organisms.
