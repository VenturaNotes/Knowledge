## Source [^1]
- $n$. a butyrophenone antipsychotic drug used to relieve anxiety and tension in the treatment of schizophrenia and other psychiatric disorders and also to treat tics and related movement disorders. Muscular incoordination and restlessness are common side-effects.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A central nervous system depressant and neuroleptic (1) agent, belonging to the class of butyrophenones, that blocks dopamine neuroreceptors and is used in the treatment of schizophrenia and other psychotic disorders and in the control of symptoms in Tourette's disorder. Also called Haldol (trademark). Compare DROPERIDOL. \[From hal- indicating a halogen, from Greek hals salt + English (pi)perid(ine) + -ol denoting a compound containing a hydroxyl group, from (alcoh)ol]
