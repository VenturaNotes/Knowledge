## Source [^1]
- $n$. a synthetic male sex hormone with anabolic effects. It is used in the treatment of aplastic anaemia and high doses may cause signs of virilization in women.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. An anabolic steroid with tissue-building properties and a weak androgenic action, closely related to testosterone, prescribed for the treatment of osteoporosis (brittle bones) and female breast cancer, and used to stimulate growth, weight gain, and production of red blood cells. It is frequently abused by athletes as a performance enhancer. Formula $\mathrm{C}_{18} \mathrm{H}_{26} \mathrm{O}_{2}$. nandrolone decanoate $n$.
