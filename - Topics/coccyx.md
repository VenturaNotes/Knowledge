## Source [^1]
- n. (pl. coccyges or coccyxes) the lowermost element of the backbone: the vestigial human tail. It consists of four rudimentary coccygeal vertebrae fused to form a triangular bone that articulates with the sacrum. See also VERTEBRA. $\textemdash$ coccygeal adj.
