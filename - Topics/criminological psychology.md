## Source [^1]
- $n$. A field of applied psychology devoted to psychological problems associated with criminal behavior, criminal investigation, and the treatment of criminals. The term is sometimes used synonymously with forensic psychology, but this is avoided in careful usage. See also CRIMINAL PROFILING, INVESTIGATIVE PSYCHOLOGY.
