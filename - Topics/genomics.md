## Source [^1]
- $n$. the branch of genetics concerned with the study of genomes. It includes both the mapping of genomes $\textendash$ and ultimately producing a DNA sequence for any particular organism or individual $\textendash$ and understanding how gene expression is controlled and gene products change under different conditions, including disease states.
