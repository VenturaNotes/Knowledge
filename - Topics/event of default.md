## Source [^1]
- In a loan agreement, a specified occurrence that will make the loan repayable immediately. The breaching of any covenant clause will be an event of default. Events of default also include failure to pay, failure to perform other duties and obligations, false representation and warranty, material adverse change, bankruptcy, and alienation of assets.
