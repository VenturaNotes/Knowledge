## Source [^1]
- Restrictions by a government on the prices of consumer goods, usually imposed on a short-term basis as a measure to control inflation. See also PRICES AND INCOME POLICY.
## Source[^2]
- The setting of maximum or minimum prices by law. This may affect particular markets, for example domestic rents, or apply in an economy overall.
