## Source [^1]
- Any activities undertaken by an organization as part of its business and any related activities in which it engages in furtherance of, incidental to, or arising from, these activities. Ordinary activities include the effects on the reporting entity of any event in the various environments in which it operates, including the political, regulatory, economic, and geographical environments, irrespective of the frequency or unusual nature of the events. See also EXTRAORDINARY ITEMS.
