## Source [^1]
- Wages above the market-clearing level paid by employers in order to increase the productivity, or efficiency, of their employees. The various explanations of efficiency wages include nutritional reasons (especially in developing economies), prevention of shirking, reduction in labor turnover, the adverse selection problem, and traditions.
