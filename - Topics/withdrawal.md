## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) (social withdrawal) (in psychology and psychiatry) the removal of one's interest from one's surroundings. See also BEREAVEMENT. 
- (2) see COITUS INTERRUPTUS. 
- (3) see THOUGHT WITHDRAWAL. 
- (4) (of treatment) the correct change to palliative care when someone is dying.
