## Source [^1]
- A type of intermediate filament found in the axons of nerve cells. Neurofilaments serve as elements of the cytoskeleton supporting the axon cytoplasm.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Any of the minute threadlike fibers found in all parts of a neuron, with side-arms that keep them apart and evenly spaced. See also MICROTUBULE, NEUROFIBRIL. \[From Greek neuron a nerve + filum a thread]
