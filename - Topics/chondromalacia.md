## Source [^1]
- $n$. softening, inflammation, and degeneration of cartilage at a joint. Chondromalacia patellae is the most common kind, affecting the undersurface of the kneecap; it results in pain in the front of the knee and grating ( crepitus), which is made worse by kneeling, squatting, and climbing stairs. Treatment includes physiotherapy, ice packs, antiinflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), weight loss, and avoidance of aggravating factors, such as running and jumping.
