## Source [^1]
- $n$. Impairment or loss of sensation from receptors within the body. US bathyanesthesia . Also called bathanaesthesia or bathanesthesia . See also anAesthesia. Compare bathyaesthesia. \[From Greek bathys deep + anaisthesia absence of sensation, from an- without + aisthesis feeling + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
