## Source [^1]
- $n$. the body of the eye, which is roughly spherical, is bounded by the sclera, and lies in the orbit. It is closely associated with accessory structures $\textendash$ the eyelids, conjunctiva, and lacrimal (tear-producing) apparatus $\textendash$ and its movements are controlled by three pairs of extrinsic eye muscles (see illustration).
