## Source [^1]
- Describing a vascular plant in which the sporophyte generation produces a single type of spore. This spore develops into a single type of gametophyte, which gives rise to both male and female reproductive organs. Most seedless plants exhibit homospory. Compare HETEROSPOROUS. See also ALTERNATION OF GENERATIONS.
