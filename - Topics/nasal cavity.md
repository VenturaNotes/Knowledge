## Source [^1]
- the space inside the nose that lies between the floor of the cranium and the roof of the mouth. It is divided into two halves by a septum: each half communicates with the outside via the nostrils and with the nasopharynx through the posterior nares.
## Source[^2]
- The cavity in the head of a vertebrate that is lined by a membrane rich in sensitive olfactory receptors (see olfaction). It is connected to the exterior by external nostrils and (in air-breathing vertebrates) to the respiratory system by internal nares.
