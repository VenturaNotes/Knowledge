## Source [^1]
- (internal schema) A specification of how the data relationships and rules specified in the logical schema of a database will be mapped to the physical storage level in terms of the available constructs, such as aggregation into records, clustering on pages, indexing, and page sizing and caching for transfer between secondary and primary storage. Storage schema facilities vary widely between different DBMS.
