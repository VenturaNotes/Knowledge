## Source [^1]
- a noninflammatory condition of the cornea resulting in yellow-white nodules under the epithelium in the central area. These may cause symptoms if the epithelium over them breaks down or if they are located along the visual axis. \[M. Salzmann (1862-1954), German ophthalmologist]
