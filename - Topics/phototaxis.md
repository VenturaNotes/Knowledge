## Source [^1]
- $n$. movement of a cell or organism in response to a stimulus of light.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. phototaxes) The movement of a cell (e.g. a gamete) or a unicellular organism in response to light. For example, certain algae (e.g. Chlamydomonas) can perceive light by means of a sensitive eyespot and move to regions of higher light concentration to enhance photosynthesis. See taxis.
