## Source [^1]
- A type of graph used by chartists in which each trading period is represented by a separate histogram. The blocks of the diagram represent the opening and closing prices, while the high and low periods are represented by vertical lines from the opening and closing prices, which in the case of the highs resemble candlewicks.
