## Source [^1]
- $n$. a solution containing $40 \%$ formaldehyde in water, used as a sterilizing agent and, in pathology, as a fixative. It is lethal to bacteria, viruses, fungi, and spores and is used to treat wools and hides to kill anthrax spores. Heating the solution produces the irritating vapour of formaldehyde, which is also used for disinfection.
