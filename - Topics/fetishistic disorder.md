## Source [^1]
- $n$. A paraphilic disorder in DSM-5 characterized by intense, recurrent sexual arousal from either non-living objects or nongenital body parts, or associated fantasies or urges over a period of at least six months. To meet the diagnostic criteria, the fantasies, urges, or behaviour must have caused clinically significant distress or impairment in social, occupational, or other areas of life, and the fetish objects must not be limited to opposite-sex clothing or sex toys or devices. See also fetishism.
