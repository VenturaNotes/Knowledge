## Source [^1]
- (magnetizing force) (symbol: $\pmb{H}$ ; unit: ampere/meter) The strength of a magnetic field at a point in the direction of the line of force at that point. It is defined in a vacuum from the equation$$\boldsymbol {B} = \mu_ {0} \boldsymbol {H}$$where $\pmb{B}$ is the magnetic flux density and $\mu_0$ is a constant, the permeability of free space. See also AMPERE'S LAW.
