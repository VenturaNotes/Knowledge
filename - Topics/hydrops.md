## Source [^1]
- $n$. an abnormal accumulation of fluid in body tissues or cavities. For example, corneal hydrops is the sudden painful accumulation of fluid in the cornea seen in keratoconus. It results in a sudden reduction of vision. See also HYDROPS FETALIS; MÉNIÈRE'S DISEASE.
