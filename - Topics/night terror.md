## Source [^1]
- the condition in which a child (usually aged 2-7 years), soon after falling asleep, starts screaming and appears terrified. The child cannot be comforted because he or she remains mentally inaccessible; the attack ceases on fully wakening and is never remembered. Attacks sometimes follow a stressful experience.
