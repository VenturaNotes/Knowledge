## Source [^1]
- Entries made at a balance-sheet date under an accrual accounting system to ensure that the income and expenditure of the business concerned are included in the correct period. Examples of adjustments include those made for depreciation, payments in advance, accruals, and closing stock (items that will not be sold until future periods).
