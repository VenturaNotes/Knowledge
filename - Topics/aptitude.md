## Source [^1]
- $n$. Suitability, natural ability, or capacity to learn; especially (in psychology) potential rather than existing capacity to perform some function, whether physical, mental, or a combination of the two, given the necessary education or training. See also aptitude test. Compare ability. \[From Latin aptus suitable or adapted, from apere to fasten + -tudo indicating a state or condition]
