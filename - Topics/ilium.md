## Source [^1]
- $n$. the haunch bone: a wide bone forming the upper part of each side of the hip bone (see also PELVIS). There is a concave depression (iliac fossa) on the inside of the pelvis; the right iliac fossa provides space for the vermiform appendix. $\textendash$iliac adj.
