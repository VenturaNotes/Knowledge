## Source [^1]
- Same as 10th percentile, or 0.1th quantile.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Any of the ten equal groups into which a distribution of scores can be divided, or the value of a variable such that a specified proportion of scores arranged in order of magnitude, divided into tenths, falls below that value, the sixth decile, for example, being the value of the variable such that 60 per cent of the other scores fall below that value. The fifth decile is the median. See also partile. Compare percentile, quartile. \[From Latin decem ten]
