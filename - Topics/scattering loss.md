## Source [^1]
- Energy loss from a beam of electromagnetic radiation because of deflection of the radiation by scattering. The individual particles or photons in the beam can interact with the nuclei or electrons present in the medium through which they are propagated, with photons of another field of radiation, or irregularities in a reflective surface.
