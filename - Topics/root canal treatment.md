## Source [^1]
- the procedure of fully devitalizing and removing the remnants of the pulp of a tooth, cleaning and shaping the canal inside the tooth, and filling the root canal (see ROOT FILLING). The treatment may extend over several visits. It is used to treat toothache and apical abscesses. See also APICECTOMY; ENDODONTICS.
