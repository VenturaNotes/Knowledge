## Source [^1]
- $n$. A dreamlike image, often vivid and resembling a hallucination, generally accompanied by sleep paralysis, experienced by a person in the transition state from wakefulness to sleep. See also IsAKOWER Phenomenon. Compare HYpnOPOMPIC IMAGE. \[From French hypnagogique hypnagogic, from Greek hypnos sleep + agogos bringing + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
