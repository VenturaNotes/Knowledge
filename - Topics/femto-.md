## Source [^1]
- Symbol f. A prefix used in the metric system to denote $10^{-15}$. For example, $10^{-15}$ second $=1$ femtosecond (fs).
## Source[^2]
- (symbol: f) A prefix to a unit, denoting a submultiple of $10^{-15}$ of that unit.
