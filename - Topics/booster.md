## Source [^1]
- (1) A generator or transformer inserted in a circuit in order to increase (positive booster) or decrease (negative booster) the magnitude or to change the phase of the voltage acting in the circuit. 
- (2) A repeater station that amplifies and retransmits a broadcast signal received from a main station, with or without a change of frequency.
