## Source [^1]
- $n$. tissue in the medulla of the adrenal gland consisting of modified neural cells containing granules that are stained brown by chromates. Adrenaline and noradrenaline are released from the granules when the adrenal gland is stimulated by its sympathetic nerve supply. See also NEUROHORMONE.
