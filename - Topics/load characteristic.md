## Source [^1]
- A characteristic curve for an electronic device, such as a transistor, showing the dynamic relationship between the instantaneous values of two variables when all the supply voltages are maintained constant. Emitter current, $I_{\mathrm{e}}$, plotted against emitter voltage, $V_{\mathrm{e}}$, provides an example.
