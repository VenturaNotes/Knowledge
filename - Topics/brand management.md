## Source [^1]
- A marketing function that is concerned with maintaining and increasing the value of a company's brands. Successful brand management involves creating strong positive associations for the branded product or service, largely through design, advertising, and publicity; it also requires vigilant attention to the quality of the product and the customer experience provided. The aim is to create a popular, trusted brand with a distinct identity, thereby promoting both brand recognition and brand loyalty.
