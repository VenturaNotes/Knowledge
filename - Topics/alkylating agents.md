## Source [^1]
- a class of chemotherapy drugs that includes cyclophosphamide, ifosfamide, and melphalan. These drugs bind to DNA and prevent complete separation of the two DNA chains during cell division. Side-effects are those of other cytotoxic drugs; in addition, alkylating agents interfere with gametogenesis and may cause sterility in men.
