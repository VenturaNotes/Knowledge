## Source [^1]
- $n$. A bundle of nerve fibres belonging to the trigeminal nerve, facial nerve, and hypoglossal nerve, leading from the primary motor cortex to nuclei in the medulla oblongata. A lesion in this pathway can lead to pseudobulbar palsy. \[From cortex + Greek bolbos an onion, alluding to the onion-shaped medulla oblongata]
