## Source [^1]
- (1) The transport of goods by aircraft, either in a scheduled airliner or chartered airliner carrying passengers (an all-traffic service) or in a freight plane (an all-freight service). Air cargo usually consists of goods that have a high value compared to their weight. 
- (2) The cost of transporting goods by aircraft. It is usually quoted on the basis of a price per kilogram.
