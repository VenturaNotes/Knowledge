## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in psychiatry) the failure to recall a specific event, or to explore a specific train of thought, because of its unpleasant associations.
## Source[^2]
- In telecommunication systems, the inability of a connection to be made between the sender and the receiver. Blocking, or congestion, can result from a busy line where the receiver is already connected to another sender. In system blocking, all the connection routes between the sender and receiver are already servicing other connections.
