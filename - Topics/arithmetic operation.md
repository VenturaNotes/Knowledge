## Source [^1]
- An operation that forms a function of two numbers. This function is usually one of the class of operations: add, subtract, multiply, and divide. These operations may be carried out on integers, fractional numbers, or floating-point numbers and are normally performed in the ALU. See also floating-POINT NOTATION.
