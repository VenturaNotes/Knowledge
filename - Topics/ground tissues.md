## Source [^1]
- All the plant tissues formed by the apical meristems except the epidermis and vascular tissue. The principal ground tissues are the cortex, pith, and primary medullary rays, and they consist chiefly of parenchyma. See also COLLENCHYMA; SCLERENCHYMA.
