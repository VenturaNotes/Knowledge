## Source [^1]
- a test used to assess a patient's capacity to absorb vitamin $\mathrm{B}_{12}$ from the bowel. Radioactive vitamin $\mathrm{B}_{12}$ is given by mouth and urine collected for 24 hours. A normal individual will excrete at least $10 \%$ of the original dose over this period; a patient with pernicious anaemia will excrete less than 5\%. \[R. F. Schilling (1919), US physician]
