## Source [^1]
- A process for producing certain metals by reducing the chloride with magnesium metal. It can be used for titanium, e.g., $$TiCl_4+2Mg \to Ti + 2MgCl_2$$It is named after William Kroll (1889-1971), who devised the process in 1940.
