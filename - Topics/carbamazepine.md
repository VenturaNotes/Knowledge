## Source [^1]
- $n$. an anticonvulsant drug used in the treatment of epileptic tonic-clonic seizures, the prophylaxis of bipolar affective disorder, and to relieve the pain of trigeminal neuralgia. Common side-effects include drowsiness, dizziness, and muscular incoordination; abnormalities of liver and bone marrow may occur with long-term treatment.
