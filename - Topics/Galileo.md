## Source [^1]
- A satellite navigation system currently being deployed by the European Space Agency. When complete, it will consist of thirty satellites in medium Earth orbit at a height of $23\,222\,\mathrm{km}$, inclined at $56^{\circ}$ to the equator. The first satellite was launched in 2011 and the system is expected to be completed by 2020. Working with dual frequencies, it will offer positioning accuracy measured in meters. See also Beidou; GLONASS; GPS.
