## Source [^1]
- (s-parameters) Two-port parameters (see NETWORK) that relate the incident and reflected power waves of a network. If the incident and reflected power waves at the ports are denoted by $\pmb{a}$ and $\pmb{b}$ respectively, then for a two-port network the relationship is$$\boldsymbol {b} _ {1} = s _ {1 1} \boldsymbol {a} _ {1} + s _ {1 2} \boldsymbol {a} _ {2}$$$$\boldsymbol {b} _ {2} = s _ {2 1} \boldsymbol {a} _ {1} + s _ {2 2} \boldsymbol {a} _ {2}$$or, in matrix form,$$\left( \begin{array}{c} \boldsymbol {b} _ {1} \\ \boldsymbol {b} _ {2} \end{array} \right) = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} s _ {1 1} & s _ {1 2} \\ s _ {2 1} & s _ {2 2} \end{array} \right] \left( \begin{array}{c} \boldsymbol {a} _ {1} \\ \boldsymbol {a} _ {2} \end{array} \right)$$
