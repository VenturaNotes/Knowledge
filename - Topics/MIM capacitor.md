## Source [^1]
- (metal-insulator-metal capacitor) A thin-film capacitor consisting of two metal plates separated by a dielectric material and used in integrated circuits. MIM capacitors are the most commonly used type of monolithic capacitor in MMICs (monolithic microwave integrated circuits).
