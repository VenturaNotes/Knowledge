## Source [^1]
- (frequency converter; conversion transducer) 
- (1) A device that converts alternating current of one frequency to alternating current of another frequency. The conversion gain ratio of a frequency changer is defined as the ratio of signal power available at the output to that available at the input; the conversion voltage gain is the ratio of the output voltage to the input voltage. 
- (2) See MIXER.
