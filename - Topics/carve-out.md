## Source [^1]
- (equity carve-out) A form of corporate restructuring in which a parent firm sells shares in a subsidiary by means of an initial public offering (IPO). A minority stake is usually sold in the subsidiary, leaving the parent company with a degree of control. Compare SPIN-OFF; SPLIT-OFF.
