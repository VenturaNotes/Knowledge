## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antiviral drug used to treat severe cytomegalovirus (CMV) infections, including CMV retinitis, in immunocompromised patients and also dendritic ulcers. Possible side-effects include nausea, vomiting, diarrhoea, infertility, confusion, seizures, and disturbance of bone marrow blood-cell production.
