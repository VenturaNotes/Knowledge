## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a set of technical procedures presented by the Hungarian psychoanalyst Sandor Ferenczi (1873-1933) to the 1920 Psychoanalytic Congress in The Hague, with the approval of Sigmund Freud (1856-1939), in which the analyst is encouraged to go beyond interpretation (2) and on occasions to issue instructions and prohibitions to patients.
