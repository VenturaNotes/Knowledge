## Source [^1]
- $n$. the combination of pregnancy-induced hypertension and proteinuria ($>0.3 \mathrm{g}$ in 24 hours) with or without oedema. The condition usually resolves after delivery, but the risk of eclampsia remains for up to the eleventh day postpartum. See also eclampsia; HELLP syndrome.
