## Source [^1]
- A series $s_{n}(x)=a_{0}+\frac{a_{1}}{x}+\frac{a_{2}}{x^{2}}+\ldots+\frac{a_{n}}{x^{n}}$ is called an asymptotic series for a function $f(x)$ if $\lim_{|x| \to \infty}\{x^{n}\left[f(x)-s_{n}(x)\right]\}=0$ for every $n$ or equivalently if $f(x)=s_{n}(x)+$ $o\left(x^{-n}\right)$ in the little o notation.
