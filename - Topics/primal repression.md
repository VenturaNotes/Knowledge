## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a form of repression, when wishes emanating in the id are blocked from reaching consciousness. Sigmund Freud (1856-1939) introduced the concept in the Schreber case in 1911. Compare PRIMARY REPRESSION, SECONDARY REPRESSION.
