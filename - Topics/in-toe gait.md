## Source [^1]
- (in-toeing, pigeon toe) a common foot deformity, noted at birth, in which the forefoot points inward (see TIBIAL TORSION). Most cases are self-correcting during childhood, but in severe cases surgery may be indicated. Medical names: metatarsus adductus, metatarsus varus.
