## Source [^1]
- (of a node in a tree) Any node on the unique path from the root of the tree to the node in question. A [[proper ancestor]] of a node, $A$, is a node, $B$, such that $B$ is an ancestor of $A$ and $A \neq B$. See also PARENT.
