## Source [^1]
- a nonmedical term applied to a range of emotional crises varying from a brief attack of 'hysterical' behavior to a major mental illness. The term is also sometimes used as a euphemism for a frank psychiatric illness, such as schizophrenia.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A non-technical term for any severe mental disorder involving a marked impairment in functioning and impaired reality testing.
