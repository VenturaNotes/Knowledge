## Source [^1]
- A sequence of binary digits. Such a sequence, produced randomly or pseudorandomly (see RANDOM NUMBERS) and generally of known statistical properties, may be employed either as a model of noise affecting a binary channel or as a means of controlling synchronization between transmitter and receiver.
