## Source [^1]
- (statement of changes in equity; statement of movements in shareholders' funds) A financial statement bringing together the performance of an organization in a financial period, as shown in the statement of total recognized gains and losses, with all other changes in shareholders' equity in the period, including capital contributed by or repaid to shareholders. Under the Financial Reporting Standard Applicable in the UK and Republic of Ireland, companies are required to publish such a statement as part of the annual accounts.
