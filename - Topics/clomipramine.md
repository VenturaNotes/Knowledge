## Source [^1]
- $n$. a sedative tricyclic antidepressant taken by mouth to treat depressive illness, phobias, and obsessional states. Common sideeffects are dry mouth and blurred vision.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Clomipramine hydrochloride, a tricyclic antidepressant drug, closely related to imipramine, that is a potent selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor, and is used chiefly in the treatment of obsessivecompulsive disorder. Also called Anafranil (trademark). \[From $c(h) l o(r o)$ denoting chlorine + (i)mipramine]
