## Source [^1]
- $n$. a pharmaceutical preparation used to treat preterm labour by stopping uterine contractions for long enough to allow administration of corticosteroids to enhance fetal lung maturity or to enable transfer to a unit with adequate neonatal facilities. Tocolytics include atosiban.
