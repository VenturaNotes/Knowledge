## Source [^1]
- n. In language acquisition, the extension of a grammatical rule beyond its range of applicability in standard usage, as in the use by a child of such formations as foots instead of feet and goed instead of went, or in response to a request to behave, I am being have instead of I am behaving. Compare MISMATCH, OVEREXTENSION, UNDEREXTENSION.
