## Source [^1]
- (GPA) an autoimmune disease, formerly known as Wegener's granulomatosis, predominantly affecting the sinuses, lungs, and kidneys, and characterized by blood-vessel inflammation and the formation of necrotizing granulomas. It is associated with the presence of antineutrophil cytoplasmic antibodies (ANCA). Untreated the disease is usually fatal, but it can be controlled with corticosteroids, cyclophosphamide, or rituximab.
