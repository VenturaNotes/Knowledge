## Source [^1]
- Where the payoff to the player in a game is dependent on chance outcomes, the emv is the average gain the player would achieve per game in the long run. It is calculated as the sum of: each payoff multiplied by the probability of that payoff.
- For example: 
	- ![[Screenshot 2025-03-11 at 12.01.52 AM.png]]
	- Showing possible payoffs and chances
	- The emv is $3 \times 0.2+0 + 0.3+(-1) \times 0.5 = 0.1$ as written in the circle.
