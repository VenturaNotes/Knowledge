## Source [^1]
- (PRF, pulse repetition rate) (of a pulse train) The average number of pulses that occur per second, expressed in hertz. It is equal to the reciprocal of the period.
## Source[^2]
- (PRF; pulse rate) The rate at which pulses are transmitted in a pulse train. It is measured in hertz. See PULSE.
