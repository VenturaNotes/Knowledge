## Source [^1]
- $n$. the use of information technology in the diagnosis and treatment of patients. It includes telephone conversations between physicians or between physicians and patients; tele- or videoconferencing among members of a patient's health-care team; and telepathology, in which (for example) digital pictures of microscope slides can be sent by the Internet to a pathologist for a second opinion. Using telemedicine, a paramedic can access a consultant in an A & E department for the appropriate emergency treatment in difficult cases.
