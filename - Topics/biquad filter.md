## Source [^1]
- (biquadratic filter) A general term for a second-order filter (see ORDER) based on a transfer function that is the ratio of two quadratic equations – hence the term biquadratic. The filter transfer function $H(s)$, from which low-pass, high-pass, band-pass, and band-stop filters can be realized, is given by:$$H(s) = \frac{(a_2 s^2 + a_1 s + a_0)}{(s^2 + b_1 s + b_0)}$$where $s$ is the complex frequency variable (see LAPLACE TRANSFORM).
