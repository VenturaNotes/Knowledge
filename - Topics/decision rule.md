## Source [^1]
- $n$. A formal or mechanical principle or formula for choosing a course of action in response to any given input data. A typical example is a formula for deciding whether a student passes or fails a course on the basis of a set of marks; another is a significance test for deciding whether or not to reject a null hypothesis.
