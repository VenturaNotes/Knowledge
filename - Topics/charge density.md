## Source[^2]
- (1) (volume charge density) (symbol: $\rho$ ; unit: coulomb per meter cubed) The electric charge per unit volume of a medium or body. 
- (2) (surface charge density) (symbol: $\sigma$ ; unit: coulomb per square meter) The electric charge per unit area at the surface of a medium or body.
