## Source [^1]
- (MPA) an autoimmune disease characterized by inflammation of small blood vessels, leading to reduced kidney function and breathlessness. It is associated with the presence of antinuclear cytoplasmic antibodies (ANCA) and can be treated with corticosteroids, cyclophosphamide, or rituximab.
