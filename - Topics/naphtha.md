## Source [^1]
- Any liquid hydrocarbon or mixture obtained by the fractional distillation of petroleum. It is generally applied to higher alkane fractions with nine or ten carbon atoms. Naphtha is used as a solvent and as a starting material for cracking into more volatile products, such as petrol.
