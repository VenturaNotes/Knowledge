## Source [^1]
- $n$. a sense of suffocation or suffocating pain. See ANGINA PECTORIS; LUDWIG's ANGINA.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Short for angina pectoris, a sudden and intense chest pain, generally following physical exertion, often radiating towards the left shoulder and arm and accompanied by feelings of suffocation, caused by an inadequate blood supply to the heart muscle. See also referred pain. \[From Latin angina quinsy, from Greek anchone a strangling]
