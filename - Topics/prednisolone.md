## Source [^1]
- $n$. a synthetic corticosteroid used to treat rheumatic diseases, inflammatory and allergic conditions (e.g. inflammatory bowel disease and severe asthma), and some cancers (e.g. leukaemia, lymphoma). Side-effects are those of cortisone.
