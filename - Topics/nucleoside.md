## Source [^1]
- $n$. a compound consisting of a nitrogen-containing base (a purine or pyrimidine) linked to a sugar. Examples are adenosine, guanosine, cytidine, thymidine, and uridine.
## Source[^2]
- An organic compound consisting of a nitrogen-containing purine or pyrimidine base linked to a sugar (ribose or deoxyribose). An example is adenosine. Compare NUCLEOTIDE.
