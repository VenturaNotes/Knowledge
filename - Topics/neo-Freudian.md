## Source [^1]
- adj. n. (Of or relating to) any of the theorists in the US who reformulated psychoanalysis on a more sociological and humanistic basis, notably the German-born US psychoanalysts Karen Horney (1885-1952), Erich Fromm (1900-80), and Erik H. Erikson (1902-94), and the US psychiatrist Harry Stack Sullivan (1892-1949).
