## Source [^1]
- (l/f noise) Noise superimposed on shot noise at very low frequencies. Flicker noise voltage is inversely proportional to frequency, but this may change as the frequency falls. The origins of flicker noise include effects resulting from generation-recombination of electron-hole pairs in semiconductors, but l/f noise is observed in many natural systems, including biological systems.
