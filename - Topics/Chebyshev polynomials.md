## Source [^1]
- Polynomials $T_n(x)$ and $U_n(x)$ which uniquely satisfy $T_n(\cos\theta) = \cos n\theta$ and $U_n(\cos\theta)\sin\theta = \sin(n+1)\theta$ for all $\theta$. They are orthogonal polynomials on the interval $(-1, 1)$ with weight functions $w(x) = (1 - x^2)^{1/2}$ and $w(x) = (1 - x^2)^{-1/2}$ respectively.
