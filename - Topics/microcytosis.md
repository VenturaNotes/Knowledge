## Source [^1]
- $n$. the presence of abnormally small red cells (microcytes) in the blood. Microcytosis is a feature of certain anaemias (microcytic anaemias), including iron-deficiency anaemias, certain haemoglobinopathies, anaemias associated with chronic infections, etc.
