## Source [^1]
- A group of countries with free trade between members and a common external tariff on trade with non-members. These trading rules govern a large proportion of trade, but may exclude particular sectors of the economy, such as agricultural products. The European Union is the prime example of a customs union. See also FREE-TRADE AREA.
## Source[^2]
- A union of two or more states to form a region in which there are no import or export duties between members but goods imported into the region bear the same import duties. The European Union is an example.
