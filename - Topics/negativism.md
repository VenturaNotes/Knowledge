## Source [^1]
- $n$. behavior that is the opposite of that suggested by others. In active negativism the individual does the opposite of what is asked for (for example, screws the eyes up when asked to open them). In psychosis it is usually associated with other features of catatonia. In passive negativism the person fails to cooperate (for example, stops eating). This occurs in schizophrenia and depression.
