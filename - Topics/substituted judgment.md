## Source [^1]
- a decision made by someone on behalf of a patient lacking capacity that is judged to reflect what the patient would have wanted had he or she had the mental capacity to decide for him- or herself. This judgment is best made by someone close to the patient who has a good knowledge of the patient's beliefs, opinions, and character, provided that there are no potentially conflicting and partial interests at play. See also POWER OF ATTORNEY; PROXY DECISION.
