## Source [^1]
- $n$. The application of scientific principles to the study of the behavior of organisms. The term is applied to disciplines such as psychology, anthropology, sociology, behavioral zoology, and any other discipline devoted to studying behavior scientifically. The term was coined by the US psychologist James G(rier) Miller (1916-2002) in a privately circulated memo in 1949. US behavioral science.
