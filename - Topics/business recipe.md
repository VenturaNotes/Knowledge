## Source [^1]
- An approach to business that seeks to identify the 'ingredients' (factors and resources) that have enabled a particular company to succeed, and to apply these lessons to new or established firms operating in a similar environment. The business recipe approach is thus based on an assumption that success can be explained and replicated.
