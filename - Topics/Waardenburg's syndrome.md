## Source [^1]
- an inherited form of deafness accompanied by a characteristic white forelock of hair and multiple colors within the irises of the eyes. It is inherited as an autosomal dominant disease, i.e. the children of an affected parent have a $50 \%$ chance of inheriting the disorder, although severity is variable. The gene responsible has been identified. \[P. J. Waardenburg (1886-1979), Dutch ophthalmologist]
