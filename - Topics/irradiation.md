## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) exposure of the body to ionizing radiation (see IONIZATION). For humans the source may be background radiation, diagnostic X-rays, radiotherapy, or nuclear accidents. 
- (2) exposure of a substance or object to ionizing radiation. Irradiation of food with gamma rays, which kill bacteria, is a technique used in food preservation.
