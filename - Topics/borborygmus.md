## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. borborygmi) an abdominal gurgling sound due to movement of fluid and gas in the intestine. Excessive borborygmi can occur when intestinal movement is increased, for example in the irritable bowel syndrome and in intestinal obstruction, or when there is more intestinal gas than normal.
