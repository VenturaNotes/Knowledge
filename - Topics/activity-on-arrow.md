## Source [^1]
- (AOA) One of two alternative conventions for the visual representation of network diagrams (see NETWORK MODELLING). In AOA the activities, which make up the network, are depicted as arrows, with durations attached. The direction of the arrow indicates the direction of time. Compare ACTIVITY-ON-NODE.
