## Source [^1]
- A computational paradigm based on large numbers of computing nanostructures that can physically interact and link with one another. The individual devices are referred to as claytronic atoms (c-atoms). Currently, most prototype c-atoms are much larger than nanoscale, with sizes of the order of millimetres; numerous small electromagnets are used to connect them. See also MEMS.
