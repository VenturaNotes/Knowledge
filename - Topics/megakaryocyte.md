## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cell in the bone marrow that produces platelets. It is large ($35-160 ~\mu \mathrm{m}$ in diameter), with an irregular multilobed nucleus, and with Romanowsky stains its abundant cytoplasm appears pale blue with fine reddish granules. See also THROMBOPOIESIS.
