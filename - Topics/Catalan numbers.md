## Source [^1]
- Numbers of the form $C_{n}=\frac{1}{n+1}\binom{2 n}{n}=\frac{(2 n)!}{(n+1)!n!}$ for $n \geq 0$. The first few Catalan numbers are $1,2,5,14,42 \ldots$. These numbers arise in various combinatorial ways.
