## Source [^1]
- $n$. A type of cell in the retina that conveys signals across the middle layer from the inner plexiform layer to the outer plexiform layer, in the opposite direction to the rest of the neural information flow in the retina, and whose functions are poorly understood. \[From Latin inter between plexus a network, from plectere to weave + forma a shape]
