## Source [^1]
- n. Knowledge or beliefs about one's own memory, its strengths and weaknesses, whether one has remembered particular items, and so on. The word is also used to denote regulation or control of memory, as described under metacognition. See also MNEMONIC, REALITY MONITORING. \[From Greek meta beside or beyond + English memory]
