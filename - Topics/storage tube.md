## Source [^1]
- A vacuum tube that can receive and retain information. The data can be erased and new data entered as required. The data may be graphical and visible on the face of the tube or it may be retrieved as an electric signal. Storage tubes are now obsolete. See also ELECTROSTATIC STORAGE DEVICE.
