## Source [^1]
- A pension scheme whose members are not required to contribute to the cost of their pensions, which is entirely met by their employer. This is contrasted with a contributory pension scheme, in which the members are required to contribute to meet at least part of the cost. See also DEFINED CONTRIBUTION.
