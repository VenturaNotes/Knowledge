## Source [^1]
- (1) In communications, a device that performs the reverse function to a multiplexer.
- (2) A combinational circuit that converts from $n$ inputs to 1 of $m$ outputs, where $m \leq 2^{n}$.
- See also DECODER/DEMULTIPLEXER.
