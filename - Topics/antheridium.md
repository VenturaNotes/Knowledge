## Source [^1]
- (pl. antheridia) The male sex organ of algae, fungi, bryophytes, clubmosses, horsetails, and ferns. It produces the male gametes (antherozoids). It may consist of a single cell or it may have a wall that is made up of one or several layers forming a sterile jacket around the developing gametes. Compare ARCHEGONIUM.
