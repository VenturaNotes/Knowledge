## Source [^1]
- A logic circuit in which input devices are AND gates and output devices are OR gates; it thus has the form AND-to-OR. This represents Boolean algebra functions of the form$$ABC'D + ABCD' + A'BC'D + AB'CD$$expressing a sum of product terms, i.e. an OR of AND terms, containing complemented (negative) or uncomplemented variables (' after a variable denoting the complemented form).
