## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in dentistry) 
- (1) tooth death, normally as a result of disease, trauma, or (less commonly) orthodontic treatment. 
- (2) the removal of a diseased pulp from a tooth. In primary teeth the coronal pulp alone may be removed, while the pulp in the root is kept alive or root canal treatment undertaken. For permanent teeth, see ROOT CANAL TREATMENT.
