- When a policy can evolve over time in response to new information. The converse is pre-commitment where a policy rule is set at the outset and cannot be revised. The literature on 'rules versus discretion' investigates which of these forms of policy is preferable. Discretion is related to the closed-loop equilibrium in policy games.
