## Source [^1]
- The use of the silicon dioxide layer (see OXIDATION) on the surface of a silicon chip to provide a mask for selective processing of the chip. The oxide formed by oxidation of the surface of the silicon is selectively etched to expose the underlying silicon prior to diffusion or metallizing.
