## Source[^2]
- adj. Of unknown or uncertain cause. Hence terms such as idiopathic epilepsy, idiopathic convulsion, idiopathic seizure exclude cases resulting from known head injury or other identifiable causes. \[From Greek idios separate + pathos suffering + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
