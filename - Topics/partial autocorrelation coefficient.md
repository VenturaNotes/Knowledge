## Source [^1]
- For lag $k$, the correlation coefficient between the variable and its $k$-th lag after removing the effect of all intermediate lags. In a sample, it is estimated as the last coefficient in the linear regression of $Y_{t}$ on $Y_{t-1}, \ldots, Y_{t-k}$.
