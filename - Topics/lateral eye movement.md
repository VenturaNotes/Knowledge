## Source [^1]
- $n$. A deflection of gaze to the left or right, sometimes claimed to indicate an increase of activity in the contralateral cerebral hemisphere, so that a person will tend to show a rightward deflection of gaze when preparing to answer a question requiring verbal processing and a leftward deflection when thinking about a spatial problem.
