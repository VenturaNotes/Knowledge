## Source [^1]
- The range of wavelengths over which electromagnetic radiation extends. The longest waves $\left(10^{5}\textendash10^{-3}\right.$ metres) are radio waves, the next longest $\left(10^{-3}\textendash10^{-6} \mathrm{~m}\right)$ are infrared waves, then comes the narrow band $\left(4\textendash7 \times 10^{-7} \mathrm{~m}\right)$ of visible light, followed by ultraviolet radiation $\left(10^{-7}\textendash10^{-9} \mathrm{~m}\right)$, X-rays $\left(10^{-9}\textendash10^{-11} \mathrm{~m}\right)$, and gamma rays $\left(10^{-11}\textendash10^{-14} \mathrm{~m}\right)$.
