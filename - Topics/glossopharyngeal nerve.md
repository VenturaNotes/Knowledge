## Source [^1]
- the ninth cranial nerve (IX), which supplies motor fibers to part of the pharynx and to the parotid salivary glands and sensory fibers to the posterior third of the tongue and the soft palate.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Either of the ninth pair of cranial nerves, carrying sensations from the pharynx, the back of the tongue, and the middle ear, and supplying the muscles of the pharynx, hence involved in controlling swallowing and, together with the vagus nerve, the gag reflex. \[From Greek glossa a tongue + pharynx, pharyngos the throat]
