## Source [^1]
- A regression with two or more explanatory variables. Variable $y$, for example, may be explained by variables $x_{i}, i=1$, $2, \ldots, K$. The multiple linear regression equation is then$$y_{t}=\alpha+\beta_{1} x_{1 t}+\beta_{2} x_{2 t}+\ldots+\beta_{K} x_{K t}+\varepsilon_{t}$$
