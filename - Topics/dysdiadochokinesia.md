## Source [^1]
- n. Impaired ability to perform rapidly alternating movements, such as rapid rhythmic tapping of the fingers. Also called dysdiadochokinesis . Compare adiadochokinesia. \[From Greek dys- bad or abnormal + diadochos working in turn + kinesis movement + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
