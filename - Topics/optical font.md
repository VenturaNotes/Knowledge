## Source [^1]
- A printing font or style of character specially designed to be accurately read by reading machines and by people. The most widely used are OCR A and OCR B and they are defined in internationally recognized standards. Although reading machines are available that can process a wide variety of fonts, provided that they are clearly printed, lower-cost machines and lower error rates can be achieved if the specialized optical fonts are used. See also OCR.
