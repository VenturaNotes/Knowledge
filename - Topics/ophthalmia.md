## Source [^1]
- $n$. inflammation of the eye, particularly the conjunctiva (see CONJUNCTIVITIS). Sympathetic ophthalmia is a granulomatous uveitis affecting all parts of the uveal tract of both eyes that may develop after perforating trauma or (more rarely) after intraocular surgery.
