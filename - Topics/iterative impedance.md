## Source [^1]
- The impedance that when connected to one pair of terminals of a two-port network produces a like impedance at the other pair of terminals. In general a two-port network has two iterative impedances, one for each pair of terminals. If the two iterative impedances are equal, their common value is termed the characteristic impedance of the network. Compare IMAGE IMPEDANCE.
