## Source [^1]
- A range of frequencies with a lower limit and an upper limit, such that all frequencies between these limits (but not necessarily excluding other frequencies) are passed, with little attenuation, by a filter or a channel. See also BAND-PASS FILTER, BANDWIDTH, FILTERING.
