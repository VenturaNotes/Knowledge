## Source [^1]
- The requirement often included in trade agreements that governments treat the products of foreign firms on an equal basis with those of domestic firms. This implies that once goods have been subjected to border measures such as tariffs and quotas, they should be treated in the same way as domestic goods in matters such as taxes and excise duties.
