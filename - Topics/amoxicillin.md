## Source [^1]
- $n$. a semisynthetic penicillin used to treat infections caused by a wide range of bacteria and other microorganisms (see also BETA-LACTAM ANTIBIOTIC). Side-effects include nausea, vomiting, diarrhoea, rashes, and anaemia. Sensitivity to penicillin prohibits its use.
