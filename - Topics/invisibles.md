## Source [^1]
- International trade in services. Invisible exports are sales of services to non-residents; invisible imports are purchases of services from non-residents. Invisible items include the services of airlines and shipping, hotels and other tourist facilities, banking, insurance, medical services and education, and various forms of consultancy.
