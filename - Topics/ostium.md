## Source [^1]
- n. (pl. ostia) (in anatomy) an opening. The ostium abdominale is the opening of the Fallopian tube into the abdominal cavity.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. ostia) 
- (1) (in anatomy) A small opening, especially into a tube or hollow organ. 
- (2) Any one of the pores in the body wall of a sponge (see PORIFERA) through which water enters the body cavity. 
- (3) Any one of the apertures in the arthropod heart through which blood enters from the haemocoel.
