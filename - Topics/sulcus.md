## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. sulci) 
- (1) one of the many clefts or infoldings of the surface of the brain. The raised outfolding on each side of a sulcus is termed a gyrus. 
- (2) any of the infoldings of soft tissue in the mouth, for example between the cheek and the alveolus.
