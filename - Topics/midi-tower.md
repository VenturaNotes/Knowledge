## Source [^1]
- A computer system box (see DESKTOP) that is small enough to stand on a desk usually alongside the monitor. The dimensions are such that $\text{height} > \text{depth} > \text{width}$. Peripherals such as CD-ROM disk drives are usually mounted horizontally in the front surface one above the other. See also TOWER.
