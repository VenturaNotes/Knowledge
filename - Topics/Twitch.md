## Source [^1]
- Twitch is a live video streaming platform that can be accessed through on the official Twitch website or the Twitch mobile application. Twitch is typically used by gamers to broadcast their gameplay with subscribers who want to watch them play various video and computer games in real time. Twitch stream subscribers can interact with twitch streamers using channel chats and donation systems. Twitch offers partner and affiliate advertising programs for popular streamers, paying them a set fee to share advertisements with their subscribers.
