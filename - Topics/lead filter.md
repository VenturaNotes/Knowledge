## Source [^1]
- A filter whose Laplace transform is of the form $(s - z) / (s - p)$, where $z$ and $p$ are negative and the magnitude of $z$ is less than that of $p$. Such filters can help to improve the responsiveness and stability of a closed-loop control system. They are often used in conjunction with lag filters to improve frequency response.
