## Source [^1]
- (1) A systematic and disciplined approach to assessing the significance in terms of safety of the complete set of risks that may occur with a system
- (2) An assessment in quantitative terms of the damage that would be sustained if a computer system were exposed to postulated threats. A quantitative risk analysis may ascribe a probable financial loss if each specified threat successfully exploited each possible vulnerability of the system
