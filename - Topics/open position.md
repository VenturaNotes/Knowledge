## Source [^1]
- (naked position) A trading position in which a dealer has commodities, securities, or currencies bought but unsold or unhedged (see HEDGE), or sales that are neither covered nor hedged. In either case the dealer is vulnerable to market fluctuations until the position is closed or hedged.
