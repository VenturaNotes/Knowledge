## Source [^1]
- $n$. a system of treating diseases by manipulation, mainly of the vertebrae of the backbone. It is based on the theory that nearly all disorders can be traced to the incorrect alignment of bones, with consequent malfunctioning of nerves and muscle throughout the body. Practitioners in the UK are required to register at the General Chiropractic Council, set up in 1997 to regulate the profession.
