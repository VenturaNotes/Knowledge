## Source [^1]
- $n$. an abnormal high-pitched (sibilant) or low-pitched sound heard $\textendash$ either by the unaided human ear or through the stethoscope $\textendash$ mainly during expiration. Wheezes occur as a result of narrowing of the airways, such as results from bronchospasm or increased secretion and retention of sputum; they are commonly heard in patients with asthma or chronic bronchitis.
