## Source [^1]
- In economic theory, a hypothetical state in which each participant in a market has complete up-to-date information about products and prices and can therefore make perfectly rational choices. Perfect information is a precondition of perfect competition. See also ASYMMETRIC INFORMATION; BOUNDED RATIONALITY.
