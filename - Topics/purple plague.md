## Source [^1]
- The formation of purple-colored areas on the bonds of a silicon integrated circuit. The purple color is caused by the formation of an unwanted aluminium-gold eutectic mixture ($AuA1_2$) at the bond between the gold connecting wire and the aluminium bonding pads. The resulting bond is mechanically weak, since the eutectic is very brittle, and is therefore susceptible to failure.
