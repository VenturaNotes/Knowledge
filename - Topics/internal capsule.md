## Source [^1]
- $n$. Either of two neural pathways in the brain between the thalamus and the lenticular nucleus containing nerve fibers running from the thalamus to the left or right cerebral cortex and from the cerebral cortex to the thalamus, brainstem, and spinal cord. It is a common site for a stroke.
