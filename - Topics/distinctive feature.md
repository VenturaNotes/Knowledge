## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any feature (1) or attribute of speech or writing that is relevant or significant inasmuch as it enables a contrast to be made between phonological, syntactic, or semantic units of a language, especially the phonological attributes that distinguish minimal pairs and that therefore underlie the definition of a phoneme. See also acoustic cue (1).
