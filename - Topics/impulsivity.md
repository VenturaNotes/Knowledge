## Source [^1]
- $n$. Acting on the spur of the moment without thinking, planning, or considering the consequences It is frequently accompanied by an inability to stick to plans and a continual sense of urgency. See ANTISOCIAL PERSONALITY DISORDER, ATTENTION-DEFICIT/HYPERACTIVITY DISORDER, BORDERLINE PERSONALITY DISORDER, PSYCHOPATHY, REFLECTION-IMPULSIVITY. \[From Latin impulsus, a pushing against, from impellere to strike against + -ivus indicating a tendency + itas indicating a state or condition]
