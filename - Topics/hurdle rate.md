## Source [^1]
- The rate of interest in a capital budgeting study that a proposed project must exceed before it can be regarded worthy of consideration. The hurdle rate is often based on the cost of capital or the weighted average cost of capital, adjusted by a factor to represent the risk characteristics of the projects under consideration.
