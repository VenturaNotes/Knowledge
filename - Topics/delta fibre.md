## Source [^1]
- $n$. A small-diameter, thinly myelinated type of afferent nerve fiber with low conduction velocity, such as any of the afferent fibers from certain pain, temperature, and touch receptors. US delta fiber. Also written $\delta$ fiber. Compare alpha fiber, Ia fiber, Ib fiber, II fiber, C fiber, gamma fiber. \[From delta ( $\delta$ ) the fourth letter of the Greek alphabet]
