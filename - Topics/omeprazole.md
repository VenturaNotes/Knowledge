## Source [^1]
- $n$. a proton-pump inhibitor used to treat gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastro-oesophageal reflux disease, and the Zollinger-Ellison syndrome. Omeprazole can be effective in cases that have failed to respond to $\mathrm{H}_{2}$-receptor antagonists, such as ranitidine. Possible side-effects include nausea, diarrhoea, headache, constipation, and skin rashes.
