## Source [^1]
- The ratio of enhanced current flow in the collector of a transistor as a result of minority carriers entering from the base with sufficient energy to create extra electron-hole pairs, to the current carried by minority carriers at the collector voltage. This ratio is usually unity but under high field conditions increases rapidly as the avalanche breakdown voltage is reached.
