## Source [^1]
- (hydroxyurea) $n$. a drug that prevents cell growth and is used mainly to treat chronic myeloid leukaemia but also to reduce the frequency of sickle-cell crises (see SICKLE-CELL DISEASE). Hydroxycarbamide may lower the white cell content of the blood due to its effects on the bone marrow.
