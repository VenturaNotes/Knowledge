## Source [^1]
- A process in which one bit is stored by one single magnetic domain particle. The integrity of the data can be maintained at densities higher than 1 trillion bits per square inch. The key issue is to produce the media at a low cost. One way is to self-assemble the nano-scale magnetic bits by chemical routes. Other ways are by electron-beam lithography and nanoimprinting lithography.
