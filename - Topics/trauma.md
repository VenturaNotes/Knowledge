## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) a physical wound or injury, such as a fracture or blow. Trauma scores are numerical systems for assessing the severity and prognosis of serious injuries. 
- (2) (in psychology) an emotionally painful and harmful event. Post-traumatic stress disorder may follow an overwhelmingly stressful event, such as battle, assault, or serious injury. $\textemdash$traumatic adj.
