## Source [^1]
- an ironic 'law' stating that where good health care is most needed, it is usually least available. It was first stated by the Welsh GP Tudor Hart to underline the great difficulty in bringing areas of health-care poverty up to acceptable levels. See HEALTH INEQUALITIES; JUSTICE; NEED; RATIONING.
