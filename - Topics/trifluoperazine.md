## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antipsychotic drug (a phenothiazine) used in the treatment of schizophrenia and other psychoses, severe anxiety, and severe nausea and vomiting. Common side-effects include drowsiness, dryness of mouth, dizziness, abnormal facial and body movements, and tremor.
