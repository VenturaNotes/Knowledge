## Source [^1]
- $n$. an abnormal discharge of fluid from the prostate gland. This occurs in some patients with acute prostatitis, who complain of a profuse discharge from the urethra. The discharge is usually thin and watery and is often sterile on culture. The discharge usually subsides when the underlying prostatitis is controlled.
