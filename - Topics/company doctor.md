## Source [^1]
- (1) An executive or accountant with wide commercial experience, who specializes in analyzing and rectifying the problems of ailing companies. He or she may either act as a consultant or may be given executive powers to implement the policies recommended. 
- (2) A medical doctor employed by a company, either full-time or part-time, to look after its staff, especially its senior executives, and to advise on medical and public-health matters.
