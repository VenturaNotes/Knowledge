## Source [^1]
- The risk that a transaction will not be settled as expected (i.e. that an agreed delivery or payment will not be made). It is sometimes referred to as Herstatt risk after a bank of that name, which failed to honor its foreign exchange transactions in 1974. See also COUNTERPARTY RISK.
