## Source [^1]
- The permanent absence of vital functions of the brain, which is marked by cessation of breathing and other reflexes (including the pupillary reflex) controlled by the brainstem and by a zero reading on an electroencephalogram. Organs may be removed for transplantation when brain death is established, which may not necessarily be associated with permanent absence of heartbeat.
