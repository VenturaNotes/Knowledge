## Source[^2]
- $n$. Abnormal slowing of bodily movements, notably as a feature of Parkinson's disease and of parkinsonism. See also neurolepticINDUCEd. Compare AKinesia. \[From Greek bradys slow + kinesis movement + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
