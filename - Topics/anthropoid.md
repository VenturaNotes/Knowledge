## Source [^1]
- Any member of the clade of primates that includes the New World and Old World monkeys, gibbons, orangutans, African apes, and humans, and comprises the superfamily Anthropoidea.
## Source[^2]
- adj. 
- (1) Resembling the human species in form, applied especially to the Anthropoidea or higher primates (humans, apes, and monkeys) or more narrowly to the highest apes (gorillas, chimpanzees, orangutans, and gibbons). n. 
- (2) An anthropoid primate or ape. Compare prosimian. \[From Greek anthropos man or the human species + -oeides denoting resemblance of form, from eidos shape]
