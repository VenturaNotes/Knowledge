## Source [^1]
- $n$. A neuron that is located in the visual system and is excited by blue light and inhibited by yellow light or vice versa. Such opponent-process cells are not found in the retina, where the trichromatic theory applies, but at higher levels in the visual system. See also RED-GREEN CELL. Compare CONE.
