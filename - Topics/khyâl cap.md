## Source [^1]
- $n$. A culture-bound syndrome included in DSM-5, found among Cambodians in Cambodia and the US, involving panic attacks, with dizziness, palpitations, shortness of breath, and cold hands and feet, accompanied by catastrophic thoughts and images that a wind-like substance (khyâl) will rise in the body causing serious bodily effects. \[From Cambodian khyâl cap wind attacks]
