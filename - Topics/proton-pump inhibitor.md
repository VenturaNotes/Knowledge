## Source [^1]
- a drug that reduces gastric acid secretion by blocking the proton pump. Proton-pump inhibitors include esomeprazole, lansoprazole, omeprazole, pantoprazole, and rabeprazole sodium; they are used for treating gastro-oesophageal reflux disease, peptic ulcer, and acid hypersecretion associated with gastrinoma.
