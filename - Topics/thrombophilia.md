## Source [^1]
- $n$. an inherited or acquired condition that predisposes individuals to thrombosis. Inherited thrombophilias are established causes of systemic thrombosis due to defects in proteins involved in regulating blood coagulation. Causes include Factor V Leiden gene mutation, a prothrombin gene mutation, deficiencies in proteins C and S and antithrombin III, and hyperhomocysteinaemia (see HOMOCYSTEINE). $\textemdash$thrombophilic adj.
