## Source [^1]
- $n$. The learning of contingencies between events such as occurs in conditioning (1). In classical conditioning, associations between conditioned stimuli and unconditioned stimuli are learned; in operant conditioning, associations between responses and reinforcers are learned. See also AUTOSHAPING, EVALUATIVE CONDITIONING, SHAPING (1).
