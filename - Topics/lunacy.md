## Source [^1]
- $n$. An obsolete term for any severe mental disorder, thought to be associated with phases of the moon. Empirical studies have consistently failed to find any significant correlation between phases of the moon and admissions to mental hospitals, suicides, or any other objective index of mental disorder. lunatic $n$. One afflicted with lunacy. \[From Latin luna the moon]
