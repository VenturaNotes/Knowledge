## Source [^1]
- $n$. a small vascular tumor of the inner region (medulla) of the adrenal gland. Many tumors function by their uncontrolled and irregular secretion of the hormones adrenaline and noradrenaline. They cause attacks of raised blood pressure, increased heart rate, palpitations, and headache.
