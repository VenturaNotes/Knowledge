## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) (in anatomy) a communication between two blood vessels without an intervening capillary network. See ARTERIOVENOUS ANASTOMOSIS. 
- (2) (in surgery) the surgical joining of two hollow organs, such as different parts of the intestines or blood vessels, in order to bypass disease or resected tissue and to restore continuity of the affected organ. See also SHUNT.
