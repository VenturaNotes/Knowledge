## Source [^1]
- (1) A measure of how much of the outside world can be imaged by a camera in a single exposure. It is typically expressed in terms of the angle of view, which is the angle at the camera lens between the two most extreme opposite visible points. 
- (2) A measure of how much of a virtual world is visible onscreen.
