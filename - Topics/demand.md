## Source [^1]
- Demand is an economic concept that relates to a consumer's desire to purchase goods and services and willingness to pay a specific price for them

## Source[^2]
- (1) The desire and ability to acquire a good or service.
- (2) The quantity of a good or service that economic agents are willing to buy at a given price. See also AGGREGATE DEMAND; CROSS-PRICE ELASTICITY OF DEMAND; DERIVED DEMAND; EFFECTIVE DEMAND; EXCESS DEMAND; INCOME ELASTICITY OF DEMAND; INELASTIC DEMAND.
