## Source [^1]
- $n$. A sexual dysfunction in DSM-5 characterized by persistent or recurrent inability to attain or maintain an adequate erection of the penis until completion of sexual activity, causing significant distress or problems of interpersonal interaction, believed to affect between 13 per cent and 21 per cent of men aged $40\text{-}80$ years on at least some occasions. It is not diagnosed if it is attributable to a drug or a general medical condition. Also called erectile dysfunction (ED), erectile impotence, or impotence. See also cyclic GMP, de-reflection, impotence (2), Viagra. Compare male ORGASMIC DISORDER. ED abbrev.
