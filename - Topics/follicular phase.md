## Source [^1]
- The phase of the ovarian cycle during which the Graafian follicles mature and the lining of the uterus thickens under the influence of oestradiol.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. The first phase of the oestrous cycle or menstrual cycle, initiated by the secretion of follicle-stimulating hormone and ending with ovulation. Also called the proliferative phase. Compare LUTEAL PHASE, MENSTRUAL PHASE. \[Named after the Graafian follicles]
