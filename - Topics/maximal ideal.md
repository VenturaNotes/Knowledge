## Source [^1]
- A proper (see PROPER SUBSET) ideal $I$ of a ring $R$ which is maximal with respect to containment, that is, if $J$ is an ideal such that $I \subseteq J \subseteq R$, then $I=J$ or $I=R$. An ideal $I$ is maximal if and only if the quotient ring $R/I$ is a field.
