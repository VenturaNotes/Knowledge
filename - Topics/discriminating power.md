## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychometrics, the degree to which a test yields different scores when applied to different criterion groups; specifically, the degree to which an item in an attitude scale, or the scale as a whole, yields different scores when it is applied to people holding different attitudes towards the attitude object in question.
