## Source [^1]
- On financial markets, the tendency for a security, commodity, currency etc. to jump (or fall) suddenly in value and then to drop (or rise) slowly until it finds a new equilibrium. Overshooting has been used as an explanation of exchange-rate movements arising from adjustment problems in economies.
