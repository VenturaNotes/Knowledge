## Source [^1]
- (aminodeoxysugar) Any sugar containing an amino group in place of a hydroxyl group. The hexosamines are amino derivatives of hexose sugars and include glucosamine (based on glucose) and galactosamine (based on galactose). The former is a constituent of chitin and the latter occurs in cartilage.
