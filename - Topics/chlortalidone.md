## Source [^1]
- (chlorthalidone) $n$. a thiazide diuretic used to treat fluid retention (oedema), ascites due to cirrhosis, high blood pressure (hypertension), heart failure, and diabetes insipidus. It may cause a fall in blood pressure on standing up, dizziness, and reduced blood potassium levels.
