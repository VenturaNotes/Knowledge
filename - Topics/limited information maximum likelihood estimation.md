## Source [^1]
- A method of estimation of a single equation in a linear simultaneous equations model based on the maximization of the likelihood function, subject to the restrictions imposed by the structure. The LIML estimator is efficient among the single equation estimators when the errors are normally distributed. See also FULL INFORMATION MAXIMUM LIKELIHOOD ESTIMATION.
