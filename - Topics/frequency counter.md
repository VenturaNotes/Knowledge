## Source [^1]
- A device that measures the frequency of a periodic signal by counting the number of times the signal repeats in a given time interval, or the time taken for a fixed number of repetitions of the signal. Because highly accurate timing is required, frequency counters use very stable clocks based on a quartz-crystal oscillator.
