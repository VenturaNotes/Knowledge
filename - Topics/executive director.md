## Source [^1]
- (working director) A director of a company who is also an employee (usually full-time) of that company. An executive director will often have a specified role in the management of the company, such as finance director, marketing director, production director, etc. By contrast a non-executive director is involved in planning and policy-making, but not in the day-to-day management of the company.
