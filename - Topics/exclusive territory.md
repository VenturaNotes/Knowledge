## Source [^1]
- (1) An area, defined by geographical boundaries or population, assigned by a manufacturer to a retailer, wholesaler, or other dealer with the understanding that no other distributors will be allowed to sell the manufacturer's products in that area. 
- (2) A practice used by some manufacturers in which intermediaries may only sell their products within a designated geographical area.
