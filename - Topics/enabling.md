## Source [^1]
- Activating a particular circuit or group of circuits from a larger set of circuits in order to effect their operation. An enabling pulse or signal is often used to select the desired circuit. Examples include selecting a particular input stage from several inputs in parallel, or selecting a particular semiconductor chip from an array of several similar chips.
