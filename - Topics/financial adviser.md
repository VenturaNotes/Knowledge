## Source [^1]
- (1) Anyone who offers financial advice to someone else, especially one who advises on investments. See also INDEPENDENT FINANCIAL ADVISER. 
- (2) An organization, usually a merchant bank, who advises the board of a company during a takeover (see TAKEOVER BID).
