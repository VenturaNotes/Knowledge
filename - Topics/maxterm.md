## Source [^1]
- (standard sum term) A sum (OR) of $n$ Boolean variables, uncomplemented or complemented but not repeated, in a Boolean function of $n$ variables. With $n$ variables, $2^{n}$ different maxterms are possible. The complement of any maxterm is a minterm. See also STANDARD PRODUCT OF SUMS.
