## Source [^1]
- The innermost of the three membranes (meninges) that surround the brain and spinal cord of vertebrates. The pia mater lies immediately adjacent to the central nervous system, and the choroid plexus, which secretes cerebrospinal fluid, is an extension of it.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A delicate vascular membrane enclosing the brain and spinal cord, forming the innermost of the three meninges, below the arachnoid space. Compare arachnoid MEMbRANE, DURA MATER. \[Latin pia mater tender mother, a literal translation of the Arabic name umm raqiqah]
