## Source [^1]
- (1) The collection of items from a cargo or ship that is itself floating on the sea after a shipwreck. In British waters, it can be claimed by the ship's owners for 366 days after the shipwreck; thereafter it belongs to the Crown. Compare JETSAM. 
- (2) In marine insurance, items or rights that are lost as a result of breach of warranty. In such cases the policyholder has no right to make an insurance claim.
