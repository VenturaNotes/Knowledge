## Source [^1]
- A plot of a transfer function in which, for each frequency, the real part is plotted on the horizontal $x$-axis, while the imaginary part is plotted on the vertical $y$ -axis. Nyquist plots are a useful means of visually assessing the stability of a closed-loop control system with negative feedback. See also BODE DIAGRAM.
