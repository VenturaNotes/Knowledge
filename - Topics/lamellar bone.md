## Source [^1]
- mature bone, in which the collagen fibers are arranged parallel to each other to form multiple layers (lamellae) with the osteocytes lying between the lamellae. It exists in two structurally different forms: cortical (compact) and cancellous (spongy) bone. See also woven bone.
