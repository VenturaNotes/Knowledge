## Source [^1]
- pathological imitation of the actions of another person. It may be a symptom of catatonia or of latah. It is sometimes called echokinesis.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Automatic or uncontrollable imitation of other people's movements, gestures (1), or postures, symptomatic of some mental disorders, including catatonic schizophrenia, latah, and pibloktoq. Compare echolalia. \[From Greek echo a sound + praxis a deed or action + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
