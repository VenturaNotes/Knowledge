## Source [^1]
- A perceived rapid periodic change of a displayed image. This is mainly a problem with cathode-ray tube displays. Flicker is perceived by users when the refresh frequency is below the CFF. It is claimed to induce headaches and eyestrain with prolonged usage. See also INTERLACING.
