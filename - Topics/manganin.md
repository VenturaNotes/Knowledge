## Source [^1]
- An alloy that consists of 70–86% copper, 15–25% manganese, and 2–5% nickel and has a high coefficient of electrical resistivity, low temperature coefficient of resistance, and low contact potential (see CONTACT). It is used in the manufacture of precise wire-wound resistors.
