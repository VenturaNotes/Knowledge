## Source [^1]
- (1) To reduce the value of an asset to zero in a balance sheet. An expired lease, obsolete machinery, or an unfortunate investment would be written off. 
- (2) To reduce to zero a debt that cannot be collected (see BAD DEBT). Such a loss will be shown in the profit and loss account of an organization.
