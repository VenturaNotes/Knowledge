## Source [^1]
- An object considered as having a 2-dimensional shape and density but having no thickness. It is used in a mathematical model to represent an object such as a thin plate or sheet
## Source[^2]
- $n$. (pl. laminae) 
- (1) a thin membrane or layer of tissue. 
- (2) the section of the posterior arch of a vertebra located between the central spinous process and the transverse process on each side.
