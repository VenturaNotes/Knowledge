## Source [^1]
- $n$. A storage system for declarative knowledge, involving structures in the temporal lobes, especially the hippocampus. Information contained in it is acquired by a form of learning that requires conscious awareness and that occurs quickly, often in a single trial, generally involving the formation of associations among multiple stimuli. Also called explicit memory. See also ACT, EPISODIC MEMORY, MIRROR DRAWING, SEMANTIC MEMORY. Compare PROCEDURAL MEMORY.
