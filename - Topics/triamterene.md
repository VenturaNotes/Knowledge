## Source [^1]
- $n$. a potassium-sparing diuretic given, usually in combination with a thiazide or loop diuretic, for the treatment of various forms of fluid retention (oedema). Common side-effects include nausea, vomiting, weakness, reduced blood pressure, and dry mouth.
