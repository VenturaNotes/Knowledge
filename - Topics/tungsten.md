## Source [^1]
- (symbol: W) A heavy metal, atomic number 74, that has an extremely high melting point and is extensively used to form lamp filaments. It has also been widely used to form thermionic cathodes. It is resistant to electromigration and is used as an interconnection on some IC processes.
