## Source [^1]
- $n$. A compound reinforcement schedule in which reinforcement (1) is delivered after an organism has completed two or more simple reinforcement schedules in succession, a signal being given to indicate to the organism which schedule is operating at any particular time. Also called a chained schedule . See also REINFORCEMENT SCHEDULE. Compare TANDEM REINFORCEMENT SCHEDULE.
