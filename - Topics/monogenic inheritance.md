## Source [^1]
- $n$. Inheritance of a characteristic through a single gene, such as occurs in the inheritance of haemophilia or phenylketonuria. See also qualitative inHERITANCE. Compare POLYGENIC INHERITANCE, QUANTITATIVE INHERITANCE. \[From Greek monos single + German Gen a gene, from Greek genein to produce]
