## Source[^3]
- An extension of a company's intranet allowing public access to part of the network for commercial or public-relations purposes.
## Source[^4]
- An intranet extended beyond a company to its customers, suppliers, collaborators, or even competitors. Like an intranet, an extranet is password-protected to prevent access by general Internet users.
