## Source [^1]
- A six-channel digital audio-coding format for consumer formats, such as DVD and digital television. The channels comprise five full channels $\textendash$ front-left, centre, front-right, left surround, and right surround $\textendash$ along with a low-frequency channel of approximately one-tenth the bandwidth of the full channels, hence '5.1’.
