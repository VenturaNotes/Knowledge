## Source [^1]
- $n$. any structure or agent that brings about activity in a muscle or gland, such as a motor nerve that causes muscular contraction or glandular secretion. The term is also used for the muscle or gland itself.
## Source[^2]
- A cell or organ that produces a physiological response when stimulated by a nerve impulse. Examples include muscles and glands.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. A cell or organ that is specialized to perform a specific function in response to a signal from the nervous system, the most important examples being a muscle, gland, cilium, or electric organ.
