## Source [^1]
- the system of nerve pathways in the brain concerned with the level of consciousness $\textendash$ from the states of sleep, drowsiness, and relaxation to full alertness and attention. The system integrates information from all of the senses and from the cerebrum and cerebellum and determines the overall activity of the brain and the autonomic nervous system and patterns of behavior during waking and sleeping.
