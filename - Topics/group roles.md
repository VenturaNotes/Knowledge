## Source [^1]
- Certain identifiable roles (i.e. coherent sets of behaviors) that tend to be adopted by the different members of a group, partly as a matter of personal inclination but also as a response to the expectations of others. These will include relationship roles, such as the group encourager or comedian, as well as task roles, such as the initiator or summarizer.
