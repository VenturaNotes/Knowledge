## Source [^1]
- (1) (functional packing density) A measure of the number of electronic devices per unit area contained on one integrated circuit. 
- (2) (recording density) A measure of the amount of information in a given dimension of a storage medium.
## Source[^2]
- (1) (functional packing density) The number of devices or logic gates per unit area of an integrated circuit. 
- (2) The amount of information that is contained in a given dimension of a storage system of a digital computer, e.g. the number of bits per inch of magnetic tape.
