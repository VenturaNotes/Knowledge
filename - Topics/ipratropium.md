## Source [^1]
- n. an antimuscarinic drug used as a bronchodilator in the treatment of chronic reversible airways obstruction (see BRONCHOSPASM) and to relieve rhinorrhoea resulting from rhinitis. Side-effects include dry mouth and nasal dryness.
