## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug used to treat infections of the urinary, genital, and digestive systems (such as trichomoniasis, Helicobacter pylori infection, amoebic dysentery, and giardiasis), acute ulcerative gingivitis, fungating wounds, and rosacea. Side-effects include digestive upsets and an unpleasant taste in the mouth.
