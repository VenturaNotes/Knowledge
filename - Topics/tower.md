## Source [^1]
- A piece of equipment, such as a computer system box, whose dimensions are such that height $>$ depth $>$ width. A large tower might be free-standing on the floor, while a smaller one might be a deskside unit and the smallest ones stand on the desktop. See also MIDI-TOWER.
