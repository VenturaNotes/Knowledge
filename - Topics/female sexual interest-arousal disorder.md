## Source [^1]
- $n$. A sexual dysfunction in DSM-5 characterized by reduced or absent interest in sex and erotic thoughts or fantasies, reduced or absent sexual excitement or pleasure during sex, unresponsiveness to sexual cues, and absent genital sensations in all or most sexual encounters ($75\text{-}100$ per cent). It is usually accompanied by a persistent or recurrent inability to attain or maintain adequate lubrication and swelling of the external sexual organs until completion of sexual activity.
