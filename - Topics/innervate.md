## Source [^1]
- $v b$. 
- (1) To supply an organ, structure, or body part with nerves or with nerve impulses. 
- (2) To excite or stimulate a nerve. When used by the Austrian neurologist Sigmund Freud (1856-1939), the word always had this meaning. innervation $n$. \[From Latin in in or into + nervus a nerve]
