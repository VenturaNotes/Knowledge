## Source [^1]
- $n$. the material of which living cells are made, which includes the cytoplasm and nucleus. $\textemdash$protoplasmic adj.
## Source[^2]
- Formerly, the material comprising the living contents of a cell, i.e. all the substances in a cell except large vacuoles and material recently ingested or to be excreted. The term is no longer used; the material of the cell is now referred to as the cytoplasm, apart from the nucleoplasm inside the nucleus.
