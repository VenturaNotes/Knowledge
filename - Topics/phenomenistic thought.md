## Source [^1]
- $n$. A term introduced by the Swiss psychologist Jean Piaget (1896-1980) to characterize the reasoning of young children, whose cognitive processes focus on the physical appearances of objects in the environment. One important aspect of phenomenistic thought is phenomenistic causality.
