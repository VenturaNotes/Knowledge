## Source [^1]
- A US name for the sale and purchase by a single investor, or a group in collusion, of a block of securities, either simultaneously or in a short space of time, to establish a loss or a gain; it may also be used to create the impression that the security in question is trading actively. It is similar to the practice known in the UK as bed and breakfast.
