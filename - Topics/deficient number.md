## Source [^1]
- An integer that is larger than the sum of its positive divisors (not including itself). Any prime number must be deficient by definition but so is any power of any prime, as well as numbers like 10 whose divisors are 1, 2, 5 giving a sum of 8. See abundant number, perfect number.
