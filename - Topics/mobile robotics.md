## Source [^1]
- The branch of robotics concerned with vehicular robot systems that are able to locomote within an environment or terrain. Mobile robots are used in research on navigation, exploration, and autonomous agents. Recent research in behavior-based systems and biomimetic robotics has created legged robots and other mechanical analogues of insects and animals.
