## Source [^1]
- The equivalent temperature that is often used as a figure of merit for the power associated with noise in a device or system. If there is power $P$ associated with the noise, and the system is operating with bandwidth $B$, then$$P = k T B$$where $T$ is the noise temperature and $k$ is the Boltzmann constant.
