## Source [^1]
- $n$. A class of mental disorders with disturbance of mood (1) as the predominant feature. This term was used in DSM-IV but not in DSM-5. See BIPOLAR DISORDERS, CYCLOTHYMIC DISORDER, DEPRESSIVE DISORDER, MANIA (1), MOOD EPISODES, SEASONAL AFFECTIVE DISORDER (SAD), SUBSTANCE-INDUCED DISORDERS, UNIPOLAR DEPRESSION. See also SCHIZOAFFECTIVE DISORDER.
