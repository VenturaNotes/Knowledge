## Source [^1]
- $n$. the five bones of the hand that connect the carpus (wrist) to the phalanges (digits).
## Source[^2]
- (pl. metacarpi) The hand (or corresponding part of the forelimb) in terrestrial vertebrates, consisting of a number of rod-shaped bones (metacarpals) that articulate with the bones of the wrist (see CARPUS) and those of the fingers (see PHALANGES). The number of metacarpals varies between species: in the basic pentadactyl limb there are five, but this number is reduced in many species.
