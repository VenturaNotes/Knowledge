## Source [^1]
- A linear amplifier in which the output current flows for more than half but less than the whole of the input cycle, i.e. the angle of flow is between π and 2π. At low input-signal levels class AB amplifiers tend to operate as class A and at high input-signal levels as class B amplifiers.
