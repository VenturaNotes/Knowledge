## Source [^1]
- $n$. pretending to be ill, usually in order to avoid work or gain attention. It may be a sign of mental disorder (see also MUNCHAUSEN's SYNDROME).
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Intentional feigning or exaggeration of physical or psychological symptoms, motivated by external incentives such as avoidance of work or military service, receipt of financial compensation, evasion of criminal prosecution, or procurement of prescription drugs. See also Stenger test. Compare COMPENSATION NEUROSIS, FACTITIOUS DISORDER, Munchausen SYndrome, somatic SYMPTOM Disorder. malinger $v b$. \[From French malingre sickly, perhaps from mal badly + Old French haingre feeble]
