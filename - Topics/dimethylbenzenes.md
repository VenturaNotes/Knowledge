## Source [^1]
- (xylenes) Three compounds with the formula $\left(\mathrm{CH}_{3}\right)_{2} \mathrm{C}_{6} \mathrm{H}_{4}$, each having two methyl groups substituted on the benzene ring. 1,2-dimethylbenzene is ortho-xylene, etc. A mixture of the isomers is obtained from petroleum and is used as a clearing agent in preparing specimens for optical microscopy.
