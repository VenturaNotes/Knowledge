## Source [^1]
- (xiphoid cartilage) the lowermost section of the breastbone (see STERNUM): a flat pointed cartilage that gradually ossifies until it is completely replaced by bone, a process not completed until after middle age. It does not articulate with any ribs. Also called: ensiform process or cartilage, xiphisternum.
