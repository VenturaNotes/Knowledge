## Source [^1]
- (wharfinger's receipt) A receipt for goods given by a dock warehouse or wharf, acknowledging that the goods are awaiting shipment. A more formal document is a dock warrant or wharfinger's warrant (see WARRANT), which gives the holder title to the goods.
