## Source [^1]
- $n$. surgical removal of the thyroid gland. In partial thyroidectomy, only the diseased part of the gland is removed (e.g. one lobe); in subtotal thyroidectomy, a method of treating thyrotoxicosis due to Graves' disease, the surgeon removes approximately $90 \%$ of the gland.
