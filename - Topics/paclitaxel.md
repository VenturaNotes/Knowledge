## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cytotoxic anticancer drug (see TAXANE). It is used mainly to treat ovarian cancer, breast cancer, and non-small-cell lung cancer. Typical side-effects include damage to bone marrow (see MYELOSUPPRESSION), loss of hair, and peripheral neuropathy.
