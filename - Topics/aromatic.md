## Source [^1]
- adj. Spicy or fragrant, belonging to the class of benzene derivatives or closed-chain organic compounds. Aliphatic and aromatic hydrocarbons in petrol or gasoline, glue, paint thinners, and some aerosol propellants are psychotropic when inhaled. See also inhalant-related disorders, solvent abuse. \[From Greek aroma, aromatos spice + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
