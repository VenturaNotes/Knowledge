## Source [^1]
- (Informal) Someone who visits a chat room, blog, file-sharing website, etc. but does not submit any material.
## Source[^2]
- A lurker is someone who reads online discussions boards but doesn't participate by commenting or liking the content. This applies to anyone lurking on a platform where others can leave comments and interact.
