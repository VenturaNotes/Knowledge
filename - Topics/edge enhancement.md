## Source [^1]
- Techniques to sharpen and strengthen the contrast of boundaries in an image. Edge enhancement makes an image appear sharper when viewed at a distance, but does not increase the true level of detail; it can also introduce halo effects adjacent to sharpened boundaries, particularly on large high-quality screens. See also EDGE DETECTION.
