## Source [^1]
- (1) A payment made for goods or services before the goods or services are finally billed. See also DEPOSIT. 
- (2) A payment towards meeting a liability that is not the full amount of the liability. The sum paid will be credited to the account of the payer in the books of the payee as a part-payment of the ultimate liability.
