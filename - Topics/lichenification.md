## Source [^1]
- $n$. thickening of the epidermis of the skin with exaggeration of the normal creases, thought to resemble tree bark. The cause is abnormal scratching or rubbing of the skin. It is one of the cardinal features of chronic eczema, together with erythema (flushing), pruritus (itching), and xerosis (dryness).
