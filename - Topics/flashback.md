## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) vivid involuntary reliving of the perceptual abnormalities experienced during a previous episode of drug intoxication, including hallucinations and derealization, most commonly experienced with LSD. 
- (2) a symptom of post-traumatic stress disorder characterized by the reliving of a traumatic experience.
