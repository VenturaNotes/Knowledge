## Source [^1]
- A postal service offered by the UK Post Office for urgent, valuable, or important items. Next-day delivery is guaranteed and the progress of the parcel can be tracked online or via a mobile phone. The recipient's signature on delivery can also be viewed electronically. Compare RECORDED DELIVERY.
