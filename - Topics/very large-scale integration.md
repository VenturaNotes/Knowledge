## Source [^1]
- Integrated-circuit fabrication technology that allows over 100000 transistors to be integrated on a single chip. All modern chips are VLSI. See also INTEGRATED CIRCUIT.
## Source[^2]
- (very large scale integration) Describing all integrated circuits that are larger than a few thousand gates: this includes most microprocessor, memory, and digital signal processing ICs currently available. Modern commercial processor circuits have gate counts that number in the billions.
