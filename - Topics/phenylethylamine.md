## Source [^1]
- $n$. An amine that resembles amphetamine chemically and pharmacologically and that functions as a neurotransmitter in the nervous system. PEA abbrev. \[From phenyl (as in phenylalanine) + ethyl, from Greek aither ether, from aithein to burn + hyle wood or substance + English amine, from am(monium) + Greek -ine indicating an organic compound]
