## Source [^1]
- $n$. a radioactive gaseous element that is produced during the decay of radium. Sealed in small capsules called radon seeds, it is used in radiotherapy for the treatment of cancer, but has largely been replaced by newer agents and techniques. It emits alpha and gamma radiation. Symbol: Rn.
