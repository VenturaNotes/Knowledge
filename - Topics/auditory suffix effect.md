## Source [^1]
- $n$. The impairment of memory for a list of words caused by the presentation at the end of the list, during the learning process, of further words or speech sounds that need not be committed to memory. Non-speech sounds do not normally produce such an effect. See also PRECATEGORICAL ACOUSTIC STORE.
