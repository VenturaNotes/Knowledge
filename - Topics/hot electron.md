## Source [^1]
- A conduction electron in a solid that has an energy such that the electron is not in thermal equilibrium with the crystal lattice, i.e. it is more than a few $kT$ above the Fermi level, where $k$ is the Boltzmann constant and $T$ the thermodynamic temperature of the lattice. A hot hole is a mobile hole that is similarly not in thermal equilibrium with the lattice.
