## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug used in the treatment of benign prostatic hyperplasia (see PROSTATE GLAND). It is a $5 \alpha$-reductase inhibitor, which blocks the conversion of testosterone to dihydrotestosterone. It has the same side-effects as finasteride.
