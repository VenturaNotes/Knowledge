## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) flaking off of the upper layers of the skin. 
- (2) separation of a surface epithelium from the underlying tissue. 
- (3) the natural shedding of primary teeth due to the eruption of their permanent successors. $\textemdash$exfoliative adj.
