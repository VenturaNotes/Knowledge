## Source [^1]
- The arrangement of business and financial activities by an individual or a firm so as to minimize tax liabilities in legal ways. Tax planning is encouraged by the government when it offers incentives, such as tax-free savings accounts, to cause desirable behaviour change. See also TAX AVOIDANCE.
