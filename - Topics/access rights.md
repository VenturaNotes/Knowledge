## Source [^1]
- Also known as access privileges
- A classification of the modes of access to an object granted to particular subjects, or groups of users. Thus, the owner of a file will typically have rights to read, write, or delete the file. Some or all these rights may also be granted to other users on the system. See ACCESS CONTROL
