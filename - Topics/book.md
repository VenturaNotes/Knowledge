## Source [^1]
- (1) The totality of the purchases and sales that make up the position of a trader on financial markets. The terms long book, short book, and open book are used synonymously with long position, short position, and open position. 
- (2) To record an item in the accounts, usually a sale. 
- (3) Short for book value.
