## Source [^1]
- $n$. a small branch of an artery, leading into many smaller vessels $\textendash$ the capillaries. By their constriction and dilation, under the regulation of the sympathetic nervous system, arterioles are the principal controllers of blood flow and pressure.
## Source[^2]
- A small muscular blood vessel that receives blood from the arteries and carries it to the capillaries. See microcirculation.
