## Source [^1]
- A regular payment, usually monthly, made by an employer, under a contract of employment, to an employee.
## Source[^2]
- Fixed compensation for the supply of labour services paid on a regular basis. Salaries are typically quoted on an annual basis and paid monthly or semi-monthly, in the same amount per pay period. Employees on salaries do not receive overtime payments for working more than the contractual number of hours, although exceptions may be in place for employees on low salaries. See also WAGE(S).
