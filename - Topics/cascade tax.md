## Source [^1]
- A turnover tax that is applied at every stage in the supply chain without the provision for any deduction of the tax paid at earlier stages. A cascade tax provides an incentive for vertical integration to reduce the number of stages at which the tax can be levied. The valueadded tax permits the tax paid on inputs to be reclaimed so does not distort the vertical integration decision.
