## Source [^1]
- n. Impairment of ability to do arithmetic. See also AGRAPHIA, alEXIA, SPECIFIC DISORDER OF ARITHMETIC SKILLS, SPECIFIC LEARNING DISORDER. Compare DYSCALCULIA. \[From Greek $a$ - without + Latin calculare to count, from calculus a stone, diminutive of calx limestone + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
## Source[^2]
- $n$. an acquired inability to make simple mathematical calculations. It is a symptom of disease in the dominant parietal lobe of the brain. See Gerstmann's Syndrome.
