## Source [^1]
- $n$. A graph showing the performance of one task plotted against the performance of a different task, when both tasks are performed at the same time. In general, it shows a trade-off, with relatively good performance on one task being associated with relatively poor performance on the other. See also attention operating CHARACTERISTIC. POC abbrev.
