## Source [^1]
- An electronic circuit, usually an integrated circuit, whose output has two stable states to which it is directed by the input signal or signals. It is more usually known as a flip-flop. The term is also applied to other devices with two stable states, such as certain switches and relays. See also MULTIVIBRATOR.
## Source[^2]
- (1) Having two stable states. 
- (2) (bistable multivibrator) A circuit having two stable states. See FLIP-FLOP.
