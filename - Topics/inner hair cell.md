## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of approximately 3,500 hair cells, situated in the organ of Corti, specialized as transducers of sound waves into nerve impulses and believed to encode information about the frequency of sound. Compare outer hair cell. IHC abbrev. \[So called because they are further from the outside of the cochlea than are the outer hair cells]
