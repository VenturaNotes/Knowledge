## Source [^1]
- $n$. Phenmetrazine hydro-chloride, a sympathomimetic central nervous system stimulant used as an appetite suppressant for weight control and sometimes taken as a street drug. Also called Preludin (trademark). Formula: $\mathrm{C}_{11} \mathrm{H}_{15} \mathrm{NO}$. \[From its chemical name phen(yl) + me(thyl) + (te)tra + (oxa)zine]
