## Source [^1]
- A form of prohibited discrimination on grounds of sex, race, age, gender reassignment, sexual orientation, religion or belief, or disability that occurs where someone is subjected to unwanted conduct that has the purpose or effect of violating their dignity or creating an intimidating, hostile, degrading, humiliating, or offensive environment. Sexual harassment occurs where the unwanted conduct is of a sexual nature.
