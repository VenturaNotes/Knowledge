## Source [^1]
- A type of voltmeter that has now generally been superseded by the digital voltmeter. It consists of an amplifier of extremely high input impedance and containing one or more thermionic valves, with a measuring instrument in the output circuit. Direct voltage or alternating voltage may be measured with this instrument.
