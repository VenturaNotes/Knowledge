## Source [^1]
- $n$. Impaired ability to discriminate by touch such physical properties as weight, density, or texture, with intact ability to recognize size and shape. Also spelt ahylagnosia . See also agnosia. Compare AMORPHOGNOSIA, TACTILE AGNOSIA. \[From Greek $a$ - without + hyle matter + gnosis knowledge + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
