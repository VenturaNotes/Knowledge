## Source [^1]
- $n$. a simple sugar and a constituent of the milk sugar lactose. Galactose is converted to glucose in the liver. The enzyme necessary for this is missing in infants with the rare metabolic disease galactosaemia.
## Source[^2]
- A simple sugar, $\mathrm{C}_{6} \mathrm{H}_{12} \mathrm{O}_{6}$, stereoisomeric with glucose, that occurs naturally as one of the products of the enzymic digestion of milk sugar (lactose) and as a constituent of gum arabic.
