## Source [^1]
- $n$. A term introduced by the English psychologist Edward Bradford Titchener (1867-1927) for the type of sensory clarity distinguishing a sensation that is the focus of attention from one that is not. \[From at(tributive) + tensity stretching, from Latin tendere, tensus to stretch]
