## Source [^1]
- The means whereby an electronic musical instrument can communicate with other MIDI instruments and computers. Many sound cards provide MIDI input and output facilities.
## Source[^2]
- Musical Instrument Digital Interface (MIDI) is a technical standard that allows production hardware components, such as synthesizers, electronic musical instruments, and computers, to communicate effectively.
- MIDI translates musical notes generated from various instruments into readable data for digital use. It is often used by music production companies to capture and store musical notes in an easily editable data format.
