## Source [^1]
- $n$. A non-probability sample drawn haphazardly from a population by a procedure such as stopping the first hundred passers-by on a particular street corner. See also CONVENIENCE SAMPLE, OPPORTUNITY SAMPLE, QUOTA SAMPLE, SELF-SELECTED SAMPLE, SNOWBALL SAMPLE. Compare CLUSTER SAMPLE, PROBABILITY SAMPLE, RANDOM DIGIT DIALLING, SIMPLE RANDOM SAMPLE, STRATIFIED RANDOM SAMPLE.
