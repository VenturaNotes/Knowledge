## Source [^1]
- $n$. a patient who receives treatment at a hospital, either at a single attendance or at a series of attendances, but is not admitted to a bed in a hospital ward. Large hospitals have clinics at which out-patients with various complaints can be given specialist treatment. Compare IN-PATIENT.
