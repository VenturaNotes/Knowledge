## Source [^1]
- $n$. muscular weakness caused by disease of the nervous system. It implies a lesser degree of weakness than paralysis, although the two words are often used interchangeably.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Partial paralysis, as in general paralysis (paresis) of the insane. \[From Latin paresis relaxation, from para beside or beyond + hienai to release]
