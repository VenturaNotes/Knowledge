## Source [^1]
- An obsolete term for invertebrate animals with a radially symmetrical body, a body wall consisting of two cell layers, and a central gastrovascular cavity, including Cnidaria (hydras, jellyfish, sea anemones, and corals) and Ctenophora (comb jellies).
