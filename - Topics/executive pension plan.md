## Source [^1]
- (EPP) A pension for a senior executive or director of a company in which the company provides a tax-deductible contribution to the premium. An executive pension plan may be additional to any group pension scheme provided by the company, as long as the pension limit of two-thirds of the working salary is not exceeded.
