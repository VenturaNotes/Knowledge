## Source [^1]
- The units of measurement in use in the USA. They are based on Imperial units but the US gallon is equal to 0.8327 Imperial gallons and the hundredweight is taken as 100 lb , making the ton $2,000 \mathrm{lb}$ (instead of the Imperial 2,240 lb). The USA has fallen behind Europe in converting to metric units, but intends to complete the process.
