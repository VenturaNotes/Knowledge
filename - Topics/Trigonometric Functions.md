## Source [^1]
- Mathematical functions that relate an angle to the lengths of the sides of right-angled triangles in which it occurs. The two most common are the sine function, $\sin (\theta)$, and cosine function, $\cos (\theta)$, as shown in the diagram. The other four can be obtained as reciprocals or ratios of these two. Trigonometric functions have a wide range of uses in electronics, notably for dealing with AC waveforms and signal frequency analysis. See also INVERSE TRIGONOMETRIC FUNCTIONS.
