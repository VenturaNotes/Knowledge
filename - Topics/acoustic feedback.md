## Source [^1]
- Unwanted feedback of the sound output of an audiofrequency loudspeaker to a preceding part of a sound-reproduction system via a microphone. The sound waves can be detected and amplified by the electronic circuits in the system; above a critical level oscillations are produced that are heard as an unpleasant and self-sustaining howling noise from the loudspeaker.
