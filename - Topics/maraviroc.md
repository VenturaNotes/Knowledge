## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antiretroviral drug (ARV) used, in combination with other ARVs, for treating refractory HIV infection. It acts by binding to and antagonizing CCR5, a receptor for a class of chemokines on the host cell needed by some HIV viruses to enter the cell. Side-effects include gastrointestinal upsets, cough, and dizziness.
