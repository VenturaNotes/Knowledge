## Source [^1]
- $n$. A consonant speech sound articulated by completely blocking the airstream through the mouth and nose for a moment, as in the initial phonemes in the words bee, dear, gear, key, pea, tea. See also MANNER OF articulation. \[French, from (ex)plosif]
