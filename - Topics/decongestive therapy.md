## Source [^1]
- an approach that combines many of the different treatments (such as compression and physiotherapy) used to minimize the pain and swelling associated with lymphoedema following breast surgery and radiotherapy. There are two phases: reduction (which lasts up to 12 weeks) and maintenance (which continues for life).
