## Source [^1]
- $n$. a monoclonal antibody that interferes with the growth of new blood vessels (see ANGIOGENESIS) by inhibiting the action of vascular endothelial growth factor. It is licensed for the treatment of metastatic colorectal cancer, breast cancer, ovarian cancer, cervical cancer, and non-small-cell lung cancer. Side-effects include hypertension, bowel perforation, and bleeding; there may be a risk of osteonecrosis of the jaw.
