## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug (a monoclonal antibody) that acts as an inhibitor of epidermal growth factor receptor (EGFR), preventing tumor-cell growth. It is used, in combination with other agents, in the treatment of metastatic colorectal cancer and, in combination with radiotherapy, in the treatment of squamous cell carcinoma of the head and neck.
