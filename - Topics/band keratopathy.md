## Source [^1]
- the deposition of calcium in the superficial layers of the cornea, usually as a horizontal band starting peripherally and moving centrally. It is associated with chronic eye disease, e.g. chronic uveitis, particularly juvenile chronic uveitis. It is treated by application of EDTA (see EDETATE) or with an excimer laser.
