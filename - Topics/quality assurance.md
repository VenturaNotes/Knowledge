## Source[^3]
- The systematic process of verifying whether a product or service meets specified requirements. In software development, QA involves various forms of testing to ensure the software is free of defects and meets the quality standards set by stakeholders.
## Source[^4]
- The design and implementation of systematic activities aimed at preventing quality problems; British Standard 5750 provides a classic example. The approach lies somewhere between the process performance orientation of quality control and the overall approach of total quality management.
