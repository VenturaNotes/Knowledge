## Source [^1]
- $n$. The tendency for the perceived intensity of an odor to remain the same despite differences in the volume of air being drawn into the nose and therefore the amount of stimulation of the olfactory receptors. US odor constancy. See also PERCEPTUAL CONSTANCY.
