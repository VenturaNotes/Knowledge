## Source [^1]
- (1) The predetermined proportions in which a mixture of different materials are intended to be used in a manufacturing process. It is set as a standard for the purposes of calculating any variances arising from differences in the proportions or quantity of direct materials used. 
- (2) The budgeted total volume of sales of an organization expressed in predetermined proportions of its range of related products. It is set as a standard for the purposes of calculating any variances arising from differences in the actual sales mix or volume of sales achieved.
