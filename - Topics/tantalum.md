## Source [^1]
- $n$. a rare heavy metal used in surgery as it is easily moulded and does not corrode. For example, tantalum sutures and plates are used for repair of defects in the bones of the skull. Symbol: Ta.
## Source[^2]
- (symbol: Ta) A metal, atomic number 73, that has an extremely high resistance to corrosion and is used for applications where this property is desirable.
