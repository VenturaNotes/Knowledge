## Source [^1]
- A high-speed electronic link in the USA between the 12 Federal Reserve Banks and the Treasury, used to move large sums of money for themselves and their customers. The transactions are generally completed within minutes of being initiated. With the Clearing House Inter-Bank Payments System (CHIPS) it constitutes the main US network for large-value payments.
