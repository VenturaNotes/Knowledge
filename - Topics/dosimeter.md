## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) a device used to measure the intensity of a radiation source. 
- (2) a device to record the amount of radiation received by workers exposed to X-rays or other radiation. See THERMOLUMINESCENT DOSIMETER.
## Source[^2]
- Any device used to measure absorbed dose of ionizing radiation. Methods used include the ionization chamber, photographic film, or the rate at which certain chemical reactions occur in the presence of ionizing radiation.
