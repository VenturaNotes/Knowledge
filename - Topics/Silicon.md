## Source [^1]
- (symbol: Si) A semiconductor element, atomic number 14. It is very abundant in nature in the form of silicon dioxide (silica) and is the most widely used semiconductor in solid-state electronics. It is cheap and extremely versatile and rapidly replaced germanium except for a very few specialized applications. See also AMORPHOUS SILICON; POLYSILICON.
