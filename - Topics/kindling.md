## Source [^1]
- $n$. A progressively increasing response of a group of neurons when exposed to a repetitive electrical stimulation of unchanging strength, sometimes leading to a psychomotor seizure or convulsion, a phenomenon mediated by neurons in the hippocampus and amygdala. \[From Old Norse kyndill to set fire to or inflame, from Latin candela a candle]
