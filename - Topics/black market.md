## Source [^1]
- Trading which violates rationing or price control laws, or both. Black markets can by definition exist only when governments attempt to control prices or ration quantities.
## Source[^2]
- An illegal market for a particular good or service. It can occur when regulations control a particular trade (as in arms dealing) or a particular period (as in wartime). Compare GREY MARKET.
