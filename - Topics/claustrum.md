## Source [^1]
- $n$. a thin vertical layer of grey matter in each cerebral hemisphere, between the surface of the insula and the lenticular nucleus (see BASAL GANGLIA).
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Either of two thin sheets of grey matter, composed chiefly of spindle cells, separating the internal capsule from the insula, situated on either side of the lenticular nucleus at the base of the brain. \[From Latin claustrum a bar]
