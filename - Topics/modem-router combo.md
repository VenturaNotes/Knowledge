## Source [^1]
- A modem-router combo is a single device that combines the functionalities of a modem and a router. 
	- The [[modem]] aspect allows it to connect to and receive Internet signals from your [[Internet Service Provider]] (ISP)
		- #question What is meant by internet signals?
	- The [[routers|router]] aspect enables it to distribute the Internet connection to multiple devices in your home or office using Wi-Fi or Ethernet connections.
		- #question what is the difference between Wi-FI and Ethernet connections?
- Essentially, it serves as both the entry point for the Internet signal and the hub for sharing that signal among various devices within your network.
