## Source [^1]
- $n$. A technique of psychotherapy based on the personal construct theory of the US psychologist George A(lexander) Kelly (1905-66) in which the client or patient is encouraged to formulate hypotheses and try out new constructs for interpreting experience. Compare COGNITIVE THERAPY, FIXED-ROLE THERAPY.
