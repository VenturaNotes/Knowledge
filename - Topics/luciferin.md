## Source [^1]
- $n$. A chemical compound involved in bioluminescence, present in certain organisms such as deep-sea fishes, fireflies, and glow-worms. It is oxidized by the enzyme luciferase and emits light on decaying to its ground state. \[From Latin lucifer light-bearer + -in(a) indicating an organic compound]
