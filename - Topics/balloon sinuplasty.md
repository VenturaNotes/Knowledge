## Source [^1]
- a surgical procedure to open or enlarge a blocked ostium of the paranasal sinuses in patients with chronic rhinosinusitis. A small balloon is inserted into the ostium in a deflated state under endoscopic control. It is then inflated to enlarge the ostium and finally deflated and removed.
