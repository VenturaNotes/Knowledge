## Source [^1]
- $n$. A bitter alkaloid present in capsicums, hot peppers, and chillis that excites peripheral nerve endings of the trigeminal nerve and causes certain neurons to release substance P. See also COMMON CHEMICAL SENSE. \[From Latin capsa a box or case + -in(a) indicating an organic compound]
