## Source [^1]
- $n$. Retrieval of information from memory with the help of cues (3), typical examples of such cues being the first letter of a word or name to be remembered, or the category to which the item of information belongs (such as, a kind of food). It is often contrasted with free recall. See also PRIMING (3), RECALL.
