## Source [^1]
- (butobarbitone) $n$. an intermediate-acting barbiturate, taken for the treatment of severe insomnia that has not responded to other drugs. Prolonged administration may lead to dependence and its use with alcohol should be avoided; overdosage has serious effects (see BARBITURISM).
