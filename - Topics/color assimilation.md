## Source [^1]
- $n$. A tendency for a color to become tinged with a different color that is juxtaposed with it, as when an area of color such as grey is surrounded by an area of another color such as red that is not its complementary color. US color assimilation. See also ASSIMILATION (6). Compare COLOR CONTRAST.
