## Source [^1]
- The quantum mechanics equivalent of a plasma oscillation. Plasmons can occur in conductors where the free electrons oscillate with respect to the fixed positive metal ions. They have a strong influence on the visible properties of conductors and semiconductors, such as their color and reflectivity.
