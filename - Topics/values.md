## Source [^1]
- pl. n. (in ethics) the moral standards and principles that govern personal and institutional behavior. They derive from asking first what makes a good clinician or caring institution and then identifying the attributes, behaviors, actions, and aims appropriate to this end. They include concepts such as truthfulness, kindness, tolerance, and integrity.
