## Source [^1]
- Any phenotypic trait that shows continuous variation and can be measured quantitatively in terms of length, weight, concentration, test score, etc. Such traits, which include height, intelligence, and obesity, are determined by the cumulative effects of numerous genes at quantitative trait loci.
