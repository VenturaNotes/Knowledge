## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) an operation in which tissue is cut away from a wound to form a saucer-like depression. It is carried out to facilitate healing and is commonly used to treat injuries or disorders in which bone is infected. 
- (2) the concave appearance of the upper surface of a vertebra that has been fractured by compression.
