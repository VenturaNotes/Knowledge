## Source [^1]
- $n$. A large-diameter, thickly myelinated type of efferent somatic nerve fibre, such as the fibre supplying a skeletal muscle, composed of alpha motor neurons and having high conduction velocity. US alpha fiber. Also written $\alpha$ fibre . Compare C fibre, gamma fibre, Ia fibre, Ib fibre, II fibre. \[From alpha $(\alpha)$ the first letter of the Greek alphabet]
