## Source [^1]
- (store card) A card attached to each site or bin in which individual items of stock are stored to record the receipts, issues, and balances of each item of stock in units. The bin card balance should indicate the physical stock available at any time; regular reconciliations with the physical quantities should be made to ensure accuracy.
