## Source [^1]
- (plugboard) A matrix of sockets that can be interconnected manually by means of [[patchcord|patchcords]], i.e. cables with plugs attached to each end. Thus one socket can be patched to another. Patchcords were used in some early computers and punched-card machines to make temporary connections between devices, to program analogue computers, and to connect different peripheral devices to computer lines.
