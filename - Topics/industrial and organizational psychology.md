## Source [^1]
- (industrial psychology; organizational psychology) The application of psychological concepts to the issues and problems that arise in business, industry, and other organizational settings. An understanding of individual and group psychology is particularly relevant to such areas as motivation, decision making, leadership (see LEADERSHIP THEORIES), team work, human-resource management, and personnel selection.
