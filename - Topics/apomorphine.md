## Source [^1]
- $n$. a dopamine receptor agonist used in the treatment of Parkinson's disease that is poorly controlled by levodopa. Side-effects include involuntary movements and instability of posture.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. An alkaloid that is prepared by dehydrating morphine and that acts as a dopaminomimetic drug, used as a powerful emetic, expectorant, and hypnotic. \[From Greek apo from + English morphine]
