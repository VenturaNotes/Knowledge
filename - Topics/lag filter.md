## Source [^1]
- A filter whose Laplace transform is of the form $(s - z) / (s - p)$, where $z$ and $p$ are negative and the magnitude of $z$ is greater than that of $p$. Such filters can help to reduce the steady-state error in a closed-loop control system. They are often used in conjunction with lead filters to improve frequency response. See also S-DOMAIN CIRCUIT ANALYSIS.
