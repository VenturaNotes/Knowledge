## Source [^1]
- $n$. any of the thickened areas of ectoderm in the embryo that will develop into nerve ganglia or the special sensory structures of the eye, ear, or nose.
## Source[^2]
- An area of thickening in the ectoderm of an early embryo that is the forerunner of a particular organ or structure. For example, the inner ear develops from the otic placodes, located on either side of the hindbrain. Likewise, hair follicles arise from individual epithelial placodes.
