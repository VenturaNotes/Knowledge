## Source [^1]
- (gonocyte) any of the embryonic cells that have the potential to develop into spermatozoa or ova. The term is also applied to any of the cells undergoing gametogenesis and to the gametes themselves.
## Source[^2]
- Any cell in the series of cells (the germ line) that eventually produces gametes, especially the first cell in such a series. In mammals the germ cells are the oogonia (in the ovaries) and the spermatogonia (in the testes). See OOGENESIS; SPERMATOGENESIS.
