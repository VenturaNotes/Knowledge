## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of thought disorder characterized by a disturbing sense of discontinuity with respect to time and person; a tendency to interpret experiences according to symbols and meanings rather than facts; and an impaired ability to generalize from one situation to another analogous one. It is most commonly referred to as a characteristic of schizotypy or schizotypal (personality) disorder.
