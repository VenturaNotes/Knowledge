## Source [^1]
- Data security refers to the protection of electronic information. Popular data security methods include antivirus software, social engineering training, and computer firewalls. Data security is a critical component of efforts to maintain the integrity and privacy of data. Any business may suffer data breaches due to inadequate data security. Like many other potential problems, a little bit of investment in data security can go a long way. All businesses should look into getting the appropriate data security tools in order to appropriately protect their customer data.
