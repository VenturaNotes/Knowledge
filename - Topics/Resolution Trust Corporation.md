## Source [^1]
- (RTC) A US federal agency set up in 1989 to wind up bankrupt thrifts. The RTC was funded by the federal government, and supervised by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC). In 1995 its duties were transferred to the Savings Association Insurance Fund, now the Deposit Insurance Fund, of the FDIC.
