## Source [^1]
- a form of ultraviolet laser that can remove very thin sheets of tissue. This can be used to alter the curvature of the corneal surface, for example to treat long- or short-sightedness (see LASEK; LASIK), or to remove diseased (e.g. calcified) tissue from the corneal surface. See KERATECTOMY.
