## Source [^1]
- (SEM) An econometric model of a relationship among two or more endogenous variables and a set of exogenous or predetermined variables, formulated as a system of equations. An example is the system of the demand and supply equations, where price and quantity are determined endogenously in the equilibrium. See also INDIRECT LEAST SQUARES; REDUCED FORM; STRUCTURAL EQUATION.
