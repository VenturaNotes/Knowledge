## Source [^1]
- any of various methods in which the application of an agreed numerical scale is used as a means of estimating the degree of a clinical situation, e.g. the severity of an injury, the degree of patient recovery, or the extent of malignancy. Examples include the Glasgow Coma Scale (or scoring system) and the injury scoring system.
