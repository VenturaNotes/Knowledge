## Source [^1]
- suffix denoting 
- (1) a diseased condition. Examples: nephrosis (of the kidney); leptospirosis (caused by Leptospira species). 
- (2) any condition. Example: narcosis (of stupor). 
- (3) an increase or excess. Example: leucocytosis (of leucocytes).
