## Source [^1]
- A set of waves that are associated with a moving particle and represent its behavior in certain situations, as when a beam of particles undergoes diffraction. The wavelength is given by$$\lambda = \frac {h}{m v}$$where $h$ is the Planck constant and $m$ and $v$ the mass and velocity of the particle.
