## Source [^1]
- (leucoplakia) n. a thickened white patch on a mucous membrane, such as the mouth lining or vulva, that cannot be rubbed off. It is not a specific disease and is present in about $1 \%$ of the elderly. Occasionally leukoplakia can become malignant. Hairy leukoplakia, with a shaggy or hairy appearance, is a marker of AIDS. See also KHAT.
