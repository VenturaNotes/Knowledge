## Source [^1]
- (1) A magnetic disk consisting of a rigid substrate coated or plated$\textemdash$usually on both sides$\textemdash$with a magnetic material. Hard disk is used as a generic term, but today is generally used to mean the magnetic disks used in Winchester technology. Compare FLOPPY DISK, OPTICAL DISK. 
- (2) A disk permanently mounted in its drive; in certain circumstances disk + drive may be demountable.
