## Source [^1]
- A wireless Ethernet link operating in the 2.4 GHz band and conforming to IEEE 802.11b. It has a range of up to 100m, but this is very dependent on the location.
## Source[^2]
- A wireless technology that enables a computer or mobile device to link to a network or access the Internet using radio waves in the 2.4 GHz band. WiFi has a range of up to 100 m but this is very dependent on the location.
