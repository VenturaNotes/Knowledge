## Source [^1]
- (paper throw) A rapid and continuous movement of the paper in a line or serial printer such that the space of several line pitches occurs without printing. In high-performance line printers the slew rate may be 75 inches per second ( $190 \mathrm{~cm} / \mathrm{s}$ ).
