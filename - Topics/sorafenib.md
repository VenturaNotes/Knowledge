## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug used in the management of advanced renal cell carcinoma and resistant thyroid cancer. It is an inhibitor of multiple kinases (see PROTEIN KINASE; TYROSINE KINASE INHIBITOR), preventing the mediation of cell growth and proliferation, and blocks vascular endothelial growth factor receptors on tumor cells, preventing new vessel formation. Side-effects include rash, diarrhoea, and hypertension.
