## Source [^1]
- (r.d.) The ratio of the density of a substance to the density of some reference substance. For liquids or solids it is the ratio of the density (usually at $20^{\circ} \mathrm{C}$) to the density of water (at its maximum density). This quantity was formerly called specific gravity.
