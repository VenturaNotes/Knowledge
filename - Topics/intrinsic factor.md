## Source [^1]
- a glycoprotein secreted in the stomach. The secretion of intrinsic factor is necessary for the absorption of vitamin $\mathrm{B}_{12}$; a failure of secretion of intrinsic factor leads to a deficiency of the vitamin and the condition of pernicious anaemia.
