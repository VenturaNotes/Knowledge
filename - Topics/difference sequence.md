## Source [^1]
- If $\{x_i\}$ is a sequence of numbers then $\{x_{i+1}-x_i\}$ is the difference sequence, obtained by subtracting successive terms. If the sequence $\{x_i\}$ is quadratic, the difference sequence will be linear, i.e. an arithmetic sequence.
