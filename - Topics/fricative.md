## Source [^1]
- $n$. A consonant speech sound articulated by narrowing the mouth passage to make the airflow turbulent but to allow it to pass through without interruption, as in the initial phonemes in the words fire, high, see, shoe, thee, thigh, vow, zoo, and the second phoneme in azure. See also MANNER OF ARTICULATION.
