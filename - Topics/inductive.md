## Source [^1]
- Denoting any electric circuit, device, or winding having an appreciable self-inductance (see ELECTROMAGNETIC INDUCTION). In practice it is extremely difficult to produce a total lack of inductance and the term is usually applied to a circuit, etc., in which for a particular application the effect of inductance is not negligible.
