## Source [^1]
- $n$. The range of light intensity that is sufficiently high to stimulate rods but not cones in the retina of the eye and that therefore produces vision without colour. See also scotopic. \[From Greek phos, photos light + chroma, chromatos a colour + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
