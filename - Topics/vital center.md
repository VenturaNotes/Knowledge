## Source [^1]
- any of the collections of nerve cells in the brain that act as governing centers for different vital body functions $\textendash$ such as breathing, heart rate, blood pressure, temperature control etc. $\textendash$ making reflex adjustments according to the body's needs. Most lie in the hypothalamus and brainstem.
