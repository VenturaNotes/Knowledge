## Source [^1]
- The faculties that enable animals to perceive information about their external environment or about the state of their bodies in relation to this environment (see SENSE ORGAN; VISION; HEARING; BALANCE; OLFACTION; TASTE; TOUCH). Specific receptors are sensitive to pain, temperature, chemicals, etc.
