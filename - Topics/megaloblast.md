## Source [^1]
- $n$. an abnormal form of any of the cells that are precursors of red blood cells (see ERYTHROBLAST). Megaloblasts are unusually large and their nuclei fail to mature in the normal way; they are seen in the bone marrow in certain anaemias (megaloblastic anaemias) due to deficiency of vitamin $\mathrm{B}_{12}$ or folate. $\textemdash$megaloblastic adj.
