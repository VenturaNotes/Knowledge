## Source [^1]
- A group of 39 countries, most of them in sub-Saharan Africa, identified by the International Monetary Fund and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (World Bank) as potentially eligible for debt relief. A country must meet three criteria in order to qualify for the debt relief: unsustainable debt, a high poverty level, and an established track record of reforms aimed at preventing future debt crises.
