## Source [^1]
- the electrical potential generated by the cochlea in response to an acoustic stimulus. It can be detected by auditory brainstem response audiometry or electrocochleography and is useful in the diagnosis of auditory neuropathy spectrum disorder.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. The summated electrical activity of large numbers of neurons in the cochlea, recorded from flat metal electrodes attached to the skin in the vicinity of the ear, analogous to the EEG or the electroretinogram. Also called a cochleogram . CM abbrev.
