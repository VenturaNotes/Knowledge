## Source [^1]
- (jobber) A wholesale dealer on the London Stock Exchange prior to the Big Bang (October 1986). Stockjobbers were only permitted to deal with the general public through the intermediary of a stockbroker. This single capacity system was replaced by the dualcapacity system of market makers after the Big Bang.
