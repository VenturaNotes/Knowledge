## Source [^1]
- The requirement that all workers of certain grades in a given business or establishment belong to a recognized trade union.
## Source[^2]
- A method of running a computing facility such that the design, development, writing, testing, and running of programs is carried out by specialist computing staff and not by the originators of the problem. Closed shop operation is the operation of a computing system, excluding terminals, by specialist computer operators and not by other computing staff or computer users. Compare OPEN SHOP.
