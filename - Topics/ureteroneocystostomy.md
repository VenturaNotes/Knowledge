## Source [^1]
- $n$. the surgical reimplantation of a ureter into the bladder. This is most commonly performed to cure vesicoureteric reflux. The ureter is reimplanted obliquely through the bladder wall to act as a valve and prevent subsequent reflux. The operation is usually referred to as an antireflux procedure or simply reimplantation of ureter.
