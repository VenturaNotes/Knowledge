## Source [^1]
- adj. n. (Of or relating to) electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths longer than the longest radiation in the visible spectrum but not as long as radio waves, hence within the range 750 nanometres to 1 millimetre, such radiation being perceived as heat. See also infra-red vision. Compare ultraviolet. IR abbrev. \[From Latin infra below or under + English red]
