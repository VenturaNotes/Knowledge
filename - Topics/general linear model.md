## Source [^1]
- A linear statistical model of the form $Y=X \mathrm{B}+U$ where $Y$ and $X$ are matrices of multivariate observations, $B$ is a matrix of parameters to be estimated, and $U$ is a matrix of random errors, usually assumed to have multivariate normal distribution.
