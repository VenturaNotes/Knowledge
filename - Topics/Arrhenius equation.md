## Source [^1]
- A physical model that expresses the variation, $R(T)$, of some device parameter or material property as a function of operating temperature, $T$:$$R(T) = A \exp \left(- \frac {A _ {\mathrm {E}}}{k T}\right)$$where $k$ is the Boltzmann constant. The constant $A_{\mathrm{E}}$ is a measure of the average behavior of a population of items and is the 'empirical' activation energy of the physical process.
