## Source [^1]
- adj. Beyond the normal; supernatural or inexplicable by the laws of science or reason. See also parapsychology. Also called psychic or psychical. \[From Greek para beside or beyond + English normal, from Latin normalis conforming to a carpenter's square or rule, from norma a carpenter's square or a rule + -alis of or relating to]
