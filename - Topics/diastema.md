## Source [^1]
- $n$. a gap between two teeth.
## Source[^2]
- The gap that separates the biting teeth from the grinding teeth in herbivores. It creates a space in which food can be held in readiness for the grinding action of the teeth. This space is filled by large canine teeth in carnivores.
