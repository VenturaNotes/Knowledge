## Source [^1]
- Technical License Management refers to the management of a software program. On one hand, it ensures that the management of a software system occurs in line with the intended use of the software, which in turn ensures that any computer software's usage occurs in line with its design. Secondly, it ensures that users of the software understand the operation of that system.
