## Source [^1]
- Investment in durable capital equipment, such as buildings, machinery, or vehicles. Fixed investments are expected to have a working life of several years, but will suffer from depreciation. This can be contrasted with investment in stocks, which are goods expected to be used up quickly without time to depreciate.
