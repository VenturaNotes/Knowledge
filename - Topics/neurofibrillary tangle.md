## Source [^1]
- $n$. A knotty mass of neurofibrils and insoluble fibers composed chiefly of breakdown products of the tau protein, occurring in the brains of most people over 70 years old and found abundantly in the hippocampi and amygdalae of patients with Alzheimer's disease and other disorders. See also AMYLOID PLAQUE, PROGRESSIVE SUPRANUCLEAR PALSY. NFT abbrev.
