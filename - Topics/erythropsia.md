## Source [^1]
- $n$. red vision: a symptom sometimes experienced after removal of a cataract and also in snow blindness.

## Source[^2]
- $n$. A visual defect in which everything appears reddish, often caused by overexposure to bright light. Also spelt erythropia. \[From Greek erythr(os) red + ops an eye + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
