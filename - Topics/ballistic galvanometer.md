## Source [^1]
- A galvanometer that measures the quantity of electricity $Q$, flowing during the passage of a transient current $I$, where$$Q = \int_{0}^{\infty} I \, dt$$The value of $Q$ is deduced from the deflection, $\theta$, of the moving part of the instrument. For a moving-coil instrument, $Q \propto \theta$.
