## Source [^1]
- $n$. A colorless strong-smelling liquid obtained from acetaldehyde and sulphuric acid that acts as a central nervous system depressant and is used as a sedative-hypnotic drug. Formula $\mathrm{C}_{6} \mathrm{H}_{12} \mathrm{O}_{3}$. \[From Greek para beside or beyond + English aldehyde, from Latin al(cohol) dehyd(rogenatum) dehydrogenated alcohol]
