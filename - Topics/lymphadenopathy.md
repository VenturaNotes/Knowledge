## Source [^1]
- $n$. enlargement of the lymph nodes. This is usually due to infection (e.g. viral or bacterial), when the nodes are painful and tender, but may alternatively be caused by malignancy (e.g. leukaemias, lymphomas), autoimmune disease (e.g. systemic lupus erythematosis), or adverse drug reactions.
