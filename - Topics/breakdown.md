## Source [^1]
- (1) A sudden catastrophic change in the properties of a device rendering it unfit for its purpose. 
- (2) A sudden disruptive electrical discharge in an insulator or between the electrodes of an electron tube. 
- (3) A sudden change from a high dynamic resistance to a much lower value in a semiconductor device.
- See also AVALANCHE BREAKDOWN; SECOND BREAKDOWN; THERMAL BREAKDOWN; ZENER BREAKDOWN.
