## Source [^1]
- A mineral consisting of a mixed iron-nickel sulphide, $(Fe,Ni)_9S_8$, crystallizing in the cubic system; the chief ore of nickel. It is yellowish-bronze in color with a metallic lustre. The chief occurrence of the mineral is at Sudbury in Ontario, Canada.
