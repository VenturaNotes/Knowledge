## Source [^1]
- A material consisting of two or more metals (e.g. brass is an alloy of copper and zinc) or a metal and a nonmetal (e.g. steel is an alloy of iron and carbon, sometimes with other metals included). Alloys may be compounds, solid solutions, or mixtures of the components. They can have structures and properties that are different from any of their constituents.
