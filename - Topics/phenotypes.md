## Source [^1]
- The phenotype is the set of observable characteristics or traits of an organism

## Source[^2]
- Physical appearance of an organism based on traits
- Examples
	- Hair color
	- Height

| Symbol | Description    |
| ------ | -------------- |
| B      | Black Fur      |
| b      | White Fur      |
| H      | Coarse Hair    |
| h      | Fine Hair      |
| L      | Short Tail     |
| l      | Long Tail      |
| A      | Normal Pigment |
| a      | Albinism       |
- The genotype above of `bb` will have the phenotype of
	- white fur
- The genotype of AA will have the phenotype of
	- normal pigment
- The genotype of heterozygous course hair will have the alleles of 
	- Hh
- The genotype of albino carrier will have the alleles of
	- Aa
