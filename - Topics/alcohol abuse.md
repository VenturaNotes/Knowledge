## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of substance abuse associated specifically with drinking alcohol, leading to serious problems of functioning at home, school, or work. When there is evidence of tolerance (3), withdrawal, or compulsive drinking, alcohol dependence rather than alcohol abuse may be diagnosed. Preferred usage alcohol misuse.
