## Source [^1]
- $n$. Substance-related and addictive disorders in DSM-5 associated specifically with the consumption of caffeine, including caffeine intoxication, caffeine-induced anxiety disorder, and caffeine-induced sleep-wake disorder. DSM-5 distinguishes caffeine intoxication and caffeine withdrawal as separate disorders. See also SUBSTANCE-INDUCED DISORDERS, SUBSTANCE-RELATED AND ADDICTIVE DISORDERS, SUBSTANCE USE DISORDERS.
