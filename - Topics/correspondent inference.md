## Source [^1]
- $n$. Another name for the fundamental attribution error. The term was introduced in 1965 by the US psychologists Edward Ellsworth Jones (1926-93) and Keith Eugene Davis (born 1936). Also called correspondence bias , although this is avoided in careful usage.
