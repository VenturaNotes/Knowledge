## Source [^1]
- A type of sclerenchyma cell that is shorter than a fibre; its lignified walls typically contain branched pits. Sclereids provide support and the hard texture typical of seed coats and nut shells. Clumps of them also occur in pear fruits, giving them their characteristic gritty texture.
