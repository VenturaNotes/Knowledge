## Source [^1]
- An equation relating the signal power returned to a radar system to the output power of the radar, its operating frequency, the distance to the target, the radar cross section of the target, and a number of other factors depending on the design of the radar and environmental conditions. The precise form of the equation can vary depending on which design and environmental aspects are being considered.
