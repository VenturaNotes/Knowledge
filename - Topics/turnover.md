## Source [^1]
- (1) The total sales figure of an organization for a stated period. Turnover is defined in the Companies Acts as the total revenue of an organization derived from the provision of goods and services, less trade discounts, VAT, and any other taxes based on this revenue. 
- (2) More generally, the rate at which some asset is sold and replaced by one of the same class. See CAPITAL TURNOVER; LABOUR TURNOVER RATE; STOCK TURNOVER. See also RATE OF TURNOVER. 
- (3) The total value of the transactions on a market or stock exchange in a specified period.
