## Source [^1]
- A method of applying bias to an electrode of an active device so that the bias is applied across the same impedance in which the signal current flows (Fig. a).
- Parallel supply is a method of applying the bias so that the bias is supplied across an impedance in parallel with that in which the signal current flows (Fig. b).
