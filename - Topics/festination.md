## Source [^1]
- $n$. short tottering steps that become more rapid, due to a loss of postural reflexes and stooped posture. Festination is found in patients with parkinsonism.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A manner of walking with an involuntary acceleration of gait, as if trying to catch up with one's displaced center of gravity, often shown by people with Parkinson's disease. \[From Latin festinare to hurry + -ation indicating a process or condition]
