## Source [^1]
- (NEMS) Nanostructure devices that combine both electrical and mechanical components. This technology is still very much at the research stage, with current work focusing on improving the manufacturability of the devices. The technology is largely carbon-based with diamond showing promise as a manufacturing substrate.
