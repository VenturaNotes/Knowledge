## Source [^1]
- $n$. a thin layer of grey matter covering the upper surface of the corpus callosum between the two cerebral hemispheres.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. indusia) The kidney-shaped covering of the sorus of certain ferns that protects the developing sporangia. It withers when the sorus ripens to expose the sporangia.
