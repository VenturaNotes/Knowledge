## Source [^1]
- (1) Symbol for base pair(s), used as a unit at the molecular level for measuring distances along a duplex polynucleotide and corresponding to the number of paired bases in a particular segment of DNA (or duplex RNA). See also KILobASE. 
- (2) (in medicine) Abbreviation for blood pressure.
