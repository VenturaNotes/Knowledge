## Source [^1]
- (syndicated selling) An Internet marketing model in which a merchant uses a network of so-called 'affiliate' websites, often belonging to businesses with a similar customer base, to generate traffic to its own site via links, advertisements, special offers, etc. An affiliate earns commission on any sales made to customers who arrive at the merchant's site via the affiliate site. Compare AGGREGATOR MODEL.
