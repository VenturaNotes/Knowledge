## Source [^1]
- a rolling survey carried out annually (1971-2007) in Great Britain by the Office for National Statistics. It included questions about the household and questions to be completed by all individuals aged over 16 within the household. It covered a wide variety of topics, such as health, employment, pensions, education, and income. It was succeeded (2007-12) by the general lifestyle survey. See also CENSUS.
