## Source [^1]
- A polyploid organism in which the multiple sets of chromosomes are all derived from the same species. For example, doubling of the chromosome number during mitotic cell division, possibly induced by colchicine, gives rise to a tetraploid known as an autotetraploid. Compare ALLOPOLYPLOID.
