## Source [^1]
- Control techniques based on the use of feedback control loops, with mathematical modelling carried out in the frequency domain rather than the time domain. Laplace transforms are the main technique used, along with a variety of graphical approaches such as Bode diagrams or Nyquist plots. See also MODERN CONTROL.
