## Source [^1]
- (per pro; p.p.) Abbreviations for per procurationem (Latin: by procuration): denoting an act by an agent, not acting on his own authority but on that of his principal. The abbreviation is often used when signing letters on behalf of a firm or someone else, if formally authorized to do so. The firm or person giving the authority accepts responsibility for documents so signed.
