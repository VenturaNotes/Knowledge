## Source [^1]
- (1) Any of the skills, talents, and traits required to be able to perform a particular task to a given standard. The assessment of competencies is central to various key aspects of human resources management: setting standards of performance that can be expected from employees and appraising them against such standards; assessing the training and development needs of individual employees; and identifying the skills, abilities, and characteristics needed when recruiting and selecting new employees.
