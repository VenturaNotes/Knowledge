## Source [^1]
- Documents that provide evidence of ownership, for a limited period, of unregistered shares. An allotment letter (see ALLOTMENT) sent to shareholders when a new issue is floated is an example. Ownership of the shares can be passed to someone else by renunciation at this stage.
