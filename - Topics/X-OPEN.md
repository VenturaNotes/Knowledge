## Source [^1]
- A joint initiative, launched in 1988 by some of the world's leading computer manufacturers, to endorse and integrate evolving standards in order to encourage applications portability, and to give a seal of approval to conforming products. In 1996, X/Open Company Ltd and the OSF merged to form The Open Group.
