## Source [^1]
- Data recorded for each of the four quarters of each year (that is, January-March, April-June, July-September, and OctoberDecember). Quarterly data are produced for many components of the national income accounts. They are often presented both before and after seasonal adjustment, that is, the removal of the estimated effects of seasonal factors.
