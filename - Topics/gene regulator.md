## Source [^1]
- $n$. A protein that can become attached to the binding site of a region of DNA, thereby affecting the expression of a structural gene by increasing or repressing the synthesis of its protein product. See also GENE ACTIVATOR, GENE OPERATOR, GENE REPRESSOR, REGULATOR GENE.
