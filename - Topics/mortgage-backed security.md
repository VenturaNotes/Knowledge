## Source [^1]
- (MBS) A security in which cash flows derive from an underlying pool of mortgages. They are very widely used in the USA. See also SECURITIZATION; SUBPRIME LENDING.
## Source[^2]
- Debt obligations that represent a claim on the cash flow from a pool of mortgage loans. Most commonly, the mortgages are on residential property. See also SECURITIZATION.
