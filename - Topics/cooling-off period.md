## Source [^1]
- (1) The 14 days that begins when a life-assurance policy, credit agreement, etc., is effected, during which new policyholders, borrowers, etc., can change their minds. During this period any policies or agreements entered into can be cancelled with a full refund of any premiums, arrangement fees, etc. 
- (2) The time that must elapse after a failed takeover bid before a new bid can be made.
