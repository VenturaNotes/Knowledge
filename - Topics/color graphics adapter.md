## Source [^1]
- A general-purpose graphics adapter used in early IBM-compatible PCs but now superseded. It could generate a $320 \times 200$ four-color screen and a $640 \times 200$ two-color screen.
## Source[^2]
- (color graphics adapter) A color video display standard introduced by IBM for its first PC, with a resolution of $320 \times 200$ pixels and four colors.
