## Source [^1]
- (1) Techniques often used at the end of a financial year to enhance a cash position and reduce borrowing. This is achieved by arranging for the settlement of outstanding obligations to be accelerated (leading) or delayed (lagging). 
- (2) The use of similar techniques to benefit from the expected change in a currency value.
