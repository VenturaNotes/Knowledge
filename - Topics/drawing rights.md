## Source [^1]
- The right of members of the International Monetary Fund (IMF) to acquire foreign currency from the IMF in exchange for their own currency, to an extent proportional to the size of their quota. Drawing rights were extended after 1970 by the creation of special drawing rights, which enabled members to obtain further amounts of foreign currency.
