## Source [^1]
- $n$. an incision to allow access to the abdominal cavity. All of the abdominal organs are examined in order to make a diagnosis and then proceed to treatment. An exploratory laparotomy may be performed in abdominal trauma or in the investigation of patients with emergency intraabdominal conditions.
