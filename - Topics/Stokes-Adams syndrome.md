## Source [^1]
- (Adams-Stokes syndrome) attacks of temporary loss of consciousness (syncope) that occur when blood flow ceases due to severe bradycardia. This syndrome may complicate heart block. \[W. Stokes (1804-78) and R. Adams (1791-1875), Irish physicians]
