## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, the withdrawal of cathexis from an idea or instinctual object, as occurs in narcissistic neurosis, when cathexis is withdrawn from external instinctual objects and turned on the ego. Also called withdrawal of cathexis . See also ISOLATION OF AFFECT.
