## Source [^1]
- An annual longitudinal survey of UK households started in 2009. The questionnaire covers economic and social circumstances, attitudes, behavior, and health. It builds on the British Household Panel Survey and can be linked to a number of other data sets, including the administrative data collected by UK government departments.
