## Source [^1]
- A plastic card issued by a store or retail chain that entitles the holder to purchase goods or services up to a prescribed limit from that store or outlets in the chain. Customers receive monthly statements from the retailer, or a finance company appointed by the retailer; they may settle the bill in full without interest, or make a specified minimum payment and pay interest on the outstanding balance. See also CHARGE CARD.
