## Source [^1]
- A form of image rectification in which one image is taken as a reference, and other sensed images of the same scene are transformed to overlay that reference. Often the images come from a variety of sensors, different viewpoints, or different times. Applications include computer vision, medical imaging, and satellite data analysis.
