## Source [^1]
- An electrostatic voltage generator that can produce potentials of millions of volts. An electric charge from an external source is applied to a continuous insulated belt at points A (see diagram). The belt travels vertically up into a large hollow metallic sphere and the charge is collected at points B. The charge resides on the exterior of the sphere and the possible voltage is limited only by leakage.
