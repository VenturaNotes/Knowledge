## Source [^1]
- $n$. An umbrella term introduced in DSM-5 for mental disorders associated with disturbance of eating or eating-related behavior with significant impairment of physical health or psychosocial functioning. See anOREXIA NERVOSA, avoidant/RESTRICTIVE FOOD INTAKE DISORDER, BINGE-EATING DISORDER, BULIMIA NERVOSA, PICA, RUMINATION DISORDER. See also ACTIGRAPHY.
