## Source [^1]
- (1) A prefix denoting very small size; e.g. microgamete, micronucleus. 
- (2) Symbol $\mu$. A prefix used in the metric system to denote one millionth. For example, $10^{-6}$ metre $=1$ micrometre $(\mu \mathrm{m})$.
