## Source [^1]
- $n$. sleep-walking: walking about and performing other actions in a semiautomatic way during sleep without later memory of doing so. It is common during childhood and may persist into adult life. It can arise spontaneously or as the result of stress or hypnosis; it may also be a side-effect of a hypnotic drug. $\textemdash$somnambulistic adj.
