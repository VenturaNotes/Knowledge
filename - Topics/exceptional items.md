## Source [^1]
- Costs or income affecting a company's profit and loss account that arise from the normal activities of the company but are of exceptional magnitude, either large or small. Under current UK rules they are included in the calculation of the normal trading profit or loss but disclosed separately. Neither exceptional nor extraordinary items are recognized under International Financial Reporting Standards.
