## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) Of or relating to knowledge or epistemology. 
- (2) Of or relating to the use of a modal verb, such as can, may, shall, or will in a context in which the truth or falsity of a proposition (1) is at issue, as in Can she drive a car? \[From Greek episteme knowledge + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
