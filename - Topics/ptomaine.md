## Source [^1]
- $n$. any of various substances produced in decaying foodstuffs and responsible for the unpleasant taste and smell of such foods. These compounds $\textendash$ which include putrescine, cadaverine, and neurine $\textendash$ were formerly thought to be responsible for food poisoning, but although they are often associated with toxic bacteria they themselves are harmless.
