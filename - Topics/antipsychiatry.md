## Source [^1]
- $n$. A radical critique of traditional (especially medical) approaches to mental disorders, influenced by existentialism and sociology, popularized by the Scottish psychiatrist Ronald D(avid) Laing (1927-89), the South African psychiatrist David (Graham) Cooper (1931-86), and others during the 1960s and 1970s. \[The term was first used, though not defined, by Cooper in his book Psychiatry and Anti-Psychiatry (1967)]
