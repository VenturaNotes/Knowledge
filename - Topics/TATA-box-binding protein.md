## Source [^1]
- (TBP) A protein that binds to the TATA box in the promoter of eukaryotic genes and is necessary for initiation of transcription. It bends the DNA molecule and associates with many other transcription factors to assemble a pre-initiation complex, thereby enabling binding of RNA polymerase.
