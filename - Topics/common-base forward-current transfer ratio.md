## Source [^1]
- (alpha current factor; collector efficiency; common-base current gain) (symbol: $\alpha$) The ratio of the collector current $I_{\mathrm{c}}$ to the emitter current $I_{\mathrm{e}}$ of a bipolar junction transistor operated in common-base connection.
