## Source [^1]
- A survey technique that uses a probe with electric terminals to measure the electrical resistance of the ground. A series of measurements are taken according to a regular grid of surface points, and these are then converted into an image of the resistivity. As different subsurface features have different resistance values, images produced in this way enable these features to be identified. Applications include archaeology and locating buried pipes and drains.

| Material       | Resistivity (ohm meters) |
| -------------- | ------------------------ |
| Conductors     | $10^{-8} - 10^{-6}$      |
| Semiconductors | $10^{-6} - 10^{-7}$      |
| Insulators     | $10^{-7} - 10^{-23}$     |
