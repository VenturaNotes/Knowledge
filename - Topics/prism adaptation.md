## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of adaptation (2) in which a person wearing wedge-shaped prism spectacles, resulting in displacement of the visual field usually about 10 degrees to one side, becomes accustomed to the distortion, making normal body movements and no longer reaching out for objects at incorrect positions in space.
