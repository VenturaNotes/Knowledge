## Source [^1]
- $n$. the fourth stage in the first prophase of meiosis, in which crossing over occurs between the paired chromatids of homologous chromosomes, which then begin to separate.
## Source[^2]
- The period in the first prophase of meiosis when paired homologous chromosomes begin to move apart. They remain attached at a number of points (see CHIASMA).
