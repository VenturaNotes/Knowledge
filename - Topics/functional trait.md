## Source [^1]
- Any characteristic of an organism that influences its performance (e.g. growth rate, competitive ability), and so ultimately affects its ability to survive and reproduce (i.e. its fitness). Examples include leaf photosynthetic rate, leaf size, stomatal density, and herbivore resistance in plants and beak size, feeding rate, and milk yield in animals.
