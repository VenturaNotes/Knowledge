## Source [^1]
- $n$. the space between the two vocal folds. The term is often applied to the vocal folds themselves or to that part of the larynx associated with the production of sound.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. glottides) The opening from the pharynx to the trachea (windpipe). In mammals it also serves as the space for the vocal cords. See also EPIGLOTTIS; LARYNX.
