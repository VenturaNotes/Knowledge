## Source [^1]
- $n$. Substance-related and addictive disorders in DSM-5 induced by consuming opiates or opioids, including natural opiates such as morphine, semi-synthetic compounds such as heroin, and fully synthetic compounds with morphine-like effects such as codeine, methadone, and meperidine. DSM-5 distinguishes between opioid use disorder, opioid intoxication, and opioid withdrawal. See also Substance ABUSE, SUBSTANCE DEPENDENCE, SUBSTANCE-INDUCED DISORDERS, SUBSTANCE-RELATED AND ADDICTIVE DISORDERS, SUBSTANCE USE DISORDERS.
