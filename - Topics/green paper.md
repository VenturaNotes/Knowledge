## Source [^1]
- A UK government publication, intended to stimulate public discussion on an issue, without necessarily committing the government to legislation, or to the lines this might take. This is contrasted with a white paper, which is a UK government publication generally intended as a prelude to legislation, and giving some indication of its likely form.
