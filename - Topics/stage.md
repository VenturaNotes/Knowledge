## Source [^1]
- $v b$. (in oncology) to classify a primary tumor by its size and the presence or absence of metastases. In addition to clinical examination, a variety of imaging and surgical techniques may be employed to provide a more accurate assessment. Staging tumors is of importance in prognosis and defining appropriate treatment. See also TNM CLASSIFICATION.
