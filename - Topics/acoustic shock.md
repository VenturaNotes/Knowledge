## Source [^1]
- an adverse reaction to a sudden unexpected noise (an acoustic incident). The condition is characterized by symptoms that may include pain, dizziness, tinnitus, anxiety, and depression. It is not usually associated with permanent reduction of hearing, in comparison to noiseinduced hearing loss (see DEAFNESS).
