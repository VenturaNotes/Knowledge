## Source [^1]
- (PPI) A measure of the rate of inflation among goods purchased and manufactured by UK industry (replacing the former wholesale price index). It measures the movements in prices of about 10,000 goods relative to the same base year. Compare Consumer Price Index; Retail Price Index.
