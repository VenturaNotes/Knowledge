## Source [^1]
- $n$. a disorder of speech in which a word spoken by the individual is rapidly and involuntarily repeated. It is seen, with other tics, in Tourette's. It is also encountered when encephalitis or other processes damage the extrapyramidal system of the brain.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A speech abnormality symptomatic of some forms of tic disorder and other mental disorders, characterized by repetition of words or speech fragments with a characteristic acceleration during each cluster of repeated speech. Compare echolalia. \[From Greek palin back or again + lalia speech]
