## Source [^1]
- Government intervention in the labour market to assist the unemployed to find work. Examples include job centres and labour exchanges that help with job search, training schemes to provide vocational skills, and employment subsidies to create jobs. Many governments employ active labour market programmes, for example the Work Programme in the UK and workfare programmes in the US.
