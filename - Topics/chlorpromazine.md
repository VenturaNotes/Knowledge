## Source [^1]
- $n$. a phenothiazine antipsychotic drug used in the treatment of schizophrenia and mania; it is also used to control nausea and vomiting in terminal illness. Common side-effects are drowsiness and dry mouth. It also causes abnormalities of movement, especially dystonias, tardive dyskinesia, and parkinsonism.
