## Source [^1]
- (gamma richness) (in ecology) The overall species diversity (see BIODIVERSITY) of a range of habitats or communities within a region. It reflects the diversity of species occupying individual habitats (alpha diversity) combined with a measure of the heterogeneity between the habitats (i.e. beta diversity).
