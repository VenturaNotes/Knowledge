## Source [^1]
- $n$. an awareness of the heartbeat. This is normal with fear, emotion, or exertion. It may also be a symptom of neurosis, arrhythmias, heart disease, and overactivity of the circulation (as in thyrotoxicosis).
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Abnormally rapid or pounding heartbeat. \[From Latin palpitare to throb, from palpare to stroke + -ation indicating a process or condition]
