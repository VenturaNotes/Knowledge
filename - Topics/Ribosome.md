## Source [^1]
- $n$. a particle, consisting of RNA and protein, that occurs in cells and is the site of protein synthesis in the cell (see TRANSLATION). Ribosomes are either attached to the endoplasmic reticulum or free in the cytoplasm as polysomes. $\textemdash$ribosomal adj.
