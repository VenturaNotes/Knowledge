## Source [^1]
- (pointer) Any means of passing spatial information to a computer system. The computer is usually programmed to display the current position by means of crosshairs or a cursor on the screen. The mouse, trackerball, joystick, data tablet, digitizer, light pen, and human finger are all examples of input devices.
