## Source [^1]
- An animal that eats both animal and vegetable matter. Pigs, for example, are omnivorous. Compare CARNIVORE; HERBIVORE.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. An animal that eats both flesh and plants; more generally, a person or animal with indiscriminate eating habits. Compare CARNIVORE, HERBIVORE. omnivorous adj. \[From Latin omnis all + vorare to devour]
