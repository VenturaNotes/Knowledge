## Source [^1]
- a residential home for a group of people where some professional supervision is available. It is used as a stage in the rehabilitation of recovering drug addicts and people who are mentally ill, usually when they have just been discharged from hospital or a rehabilitation facility and are able to work but are not yet ready for independent life.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A residential centre offering temporary accommodation and rehabilitation to people recently released from prison or recovering from a mental disorder not requiring full hospitalization.
