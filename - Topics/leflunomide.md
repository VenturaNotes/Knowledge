## Source [^1]
- $n$. an immunosuppressant drug used to treat rheumatoid and psoriatic arthritis that have not responded to methotrexate or other diseasemodifying antirheumatic drugs. Side-effects, including blood disorders caused by impairment of bone-marrow function, may be severe.
