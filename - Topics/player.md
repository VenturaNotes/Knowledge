## Source [^1]
- The term used in game theory for any participant in a strategic situation being modeled. A player is any individual, firm, or government that has to make a choice among different strategies.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. 
- (1) A person who takes part in a game; more specifically a decision maker in an interactive decision problem of the type analyzed within game theory. A player in this sense may be a person, a committee or other decision making group, an animal, or any other entity that functions as a decision maker in a strategic game (1). 
- (2) An actor or one who plays a musical instrument.
