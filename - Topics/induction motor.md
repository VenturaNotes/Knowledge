## Source [^1]
- An alternating-current motor in which the current in the secondary winding, usually the rotor, is produced by electromagnetic induction when alternating current is supplied to the primary winding, usually the stator. Mechanical movement results from the torque produced by the interaction between the rotor current and the magnetic field due to the current in the stator.
