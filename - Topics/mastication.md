## Source [^1]
- $n$. the process of crushing, grinding, and chewing food.
## Source[^2]
- The process of chewing food, which involves movements of the jaws and teeth. Mastication breaks up the food into small particles, which provides a greater surface area for digestion and enables the formation of a bolus, which is small enough to pass through the oesophagus.
