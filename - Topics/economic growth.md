## Source [^1]
- Economic growth refers to the increase in a country's real GDP over time, indicating an expansion of the productive capacity and [[standard of living]].
## Source[^2]
- The expansion of the output of an economy, usually expressed in terms of the increase of national income. Nations experience different rates of economic growth mainly because of differences in population growth, investment, and technical progress.
