## Source [^1]
- A liability to pay all the debts incurred by a business. For a sole proprietor or general partner (see PARTNERSHIP), liability is not limited to the amount he or she has agreed to invest. All debts of the business must not only be paid out of the assets of the business but also, if necessary, out of personal assets.
