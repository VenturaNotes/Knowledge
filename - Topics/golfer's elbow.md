## Source [^1]
- inflammation of the origin of the common flexor tendon on the medial epicondyle of the humerus (medial epicondylitis), caused by overuse of the forearm muscles. Treatment is by rest, a brace, antiinflammatory medication, or steroid injection. Avoidance of repetitive injury is also important. Compare TENNIS ELBOW.
