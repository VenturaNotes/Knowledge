## Source [^1]
- A form of cable with two conductors per signal path. Equal and opposite signals are applied simultaneously to each conductor. Balanced cables are used where high-speed, long distance, and minimum EMI are essential. See also CABLE, TwISTED PAIR. Compare UNBALANCED CABLE.
