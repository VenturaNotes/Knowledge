## Source [^1]
- (pl. thalli) A relatively undifferentiated vegetative body with no true roots, stems, leaves, or vascular system. It is found in the algae, fungi, mosses, liverworts, and lichens, and in the gametophyte generation of clubmosses, horsetails, and ferns.
