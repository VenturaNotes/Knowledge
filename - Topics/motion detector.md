## Source [^1]
- A device that detects moving objects in its vicinity, often used in security systems to identify intruders. Sensors typically use infrared radiation, ultrasonics, or microwaves, and work by sensing specific changes in the signal they receive from the area being observed, due either to different levels of signal reflection, or to changes in measured heat levels. Video cameras with suitable image processing software can also be used to detect motion.
