### Fraction Rules
- $\frac{\frac ab}{c} = \frac {a}{bc}$
	- This is because $\Huge \frac{\frac ab}{c} = \frac{\frac ab}{\frac c1} = \frac ab * \frac 1c = \frac {a}{bc}$
### Examples
- $\frac{\left(\frac{3}{4}\right)}{5} = \frac{3}{20}$
