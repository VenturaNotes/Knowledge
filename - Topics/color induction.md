## Source [^1]
- $n$. The generation of illusory sensations of color without direct stimulation of the corresponding cones in the retina, by any of a number of different devices. US color induction. See also AFTERIMAGE, Benham's top, Bidwell's ghost, chromaesthesia, color assimilation, COLOR CONTRAST, FLIGHT OF COLORS, LAND EFFECT, PATTERN-INDUCED FLICKER COLOR, PURKINJE IMAGE.
