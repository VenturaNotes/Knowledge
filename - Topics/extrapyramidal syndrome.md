## Source [^1]
- $n$. A condition arising from a lesion in the extrapyramidal system or as a side-effect of medication with psychotropic drugs, including such signs and symptoms as tremor, parkinsonism, akathisia, dystonia, akinesia, myotonia, drooling, and absence of facial expression.
