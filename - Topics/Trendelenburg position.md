## Source [^1]
- a special operating-table posture for patients undergoing surgery of the pelvis or for patients suffering from shock to reduce blood loss in operations on the legs. The patient is laid on his or her back with the pelvis higher than the head, inclined at an angle of about $45^{\circ}$. \[F. Trendelenburg (1844-1924), German surgeon]
