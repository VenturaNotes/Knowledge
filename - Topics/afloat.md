## Source [^1]
- Denoting goods, especially commodities, that are on a ship from their port of origin to a specified port of destination; for example, 'afloat Rotterdam' means the goods are on their way to Rotterdam. The price of such goods will usually be between the price of spot goods and goods for immediate shipment from origin.
