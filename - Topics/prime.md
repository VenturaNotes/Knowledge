## Source [^1]
- $v b$. (in chemotherapy) to administer small doses of a cytotoxic drug prior to high-dose chemotherapy and/or radiotherapy. This causes proliferation of the primitive bone marrow cells and aids subsequent regeneration of the bone marrow.
