## Source [^1]
- $v b$. (in chemotherapy) to administer small doses of a cytotoxic drug prior to high-dose chemotherapy and/or radiotherapy. This causes proliferation of the primitive bone marrow cells and aids subsequent regeneration of the bone marrow.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. 
- (1) A cue (3) given to facilitate a particular response. See priming (1). 
- (2) A prime number, that is, any number that is evenly divisible only by itself and 1. 
- (3) A typographical symbol (') affixed to a mathematical symbol as a modifier, as in $d^{\prime}$$\textemdash$see D PRIME.
