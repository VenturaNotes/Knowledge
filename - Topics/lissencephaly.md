## Source [^1]
- (smooth brain) $n$. a condition in which the brain develops abnormally $\textendash$ it has no grooves on the outside, large ventricles, and generally is smaller than it should be. Lissencephaly causes severe learning disabilities and failure to thrive. See also Miller-DiEKER SYndrome.
