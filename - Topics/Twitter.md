## Source [^1]
- A very popular online microblogging service; the maximum size of the messages that can be posted, referred to as tweets, is 140 characters. Originally developed as a short messaging service in 2006, it has expanded into a global web-based system for spreading news and opinion. As such it has benefited greatly from the emergence of the smartphone. Currently (2014) it has some 500 million users.
