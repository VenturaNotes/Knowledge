## Source [^1]
- $n$. the middle portion of the embryonic gut, which gives rise to most of the small intestine and part of the large intestine. Early in development it is connected with the yolk sac outside the embryo via the umbilicus.
## Source[^2]
- (1) The middle section of the alimentary canal of vertebrates, which is concerned with digestion and absorption. It comprises most of the small intestine. 
- (2) The middle section of the alimentary canal of arthropods. See also FOREGUT; HINDGUT.
