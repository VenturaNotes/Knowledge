## Source [^1]
- Exercises used in personnel training in which teams of employees compete against one another in attempting to solve typical business or management problems. The games, which often involve role play, encourage participants to develop their problem-solving and decision-making abilities, to modify their ideas and attitudes, and to bond with other team members. See also TEAM-BUILDING EXERCISES.
