## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) a protuberance of bone to which a tendon is attached. It ossifies separately from the rest of the bone and fuses with it at maturity. 
- (2) a projection of any other part, e.g. of the brain (apophysis cerebri: the pineal gland).
