## Source [^1]
- $n$. a malignant tumor, usually of childhood, composed of embryonic nerve cells. It may originate in any part of the sympathetic nervous system, most commonly in the medulla of the adrenal gland, and secondary growths are often widespread in other organs and in bones. It can nevertheless be very responsive to systemic chemotherapy.
