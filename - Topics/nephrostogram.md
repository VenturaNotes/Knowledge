## Source [^1]
- $n$. X-ray imaging (see FLUOROSCOPY) of the interior of the kidney and ureter after injecting a radiographic contrast medium through a catheter placed inside the renal pelvis (see NEPHROSTOMY). This procedure is done to check for any problems with the drainage of urine from the pelvis to the ureter.
