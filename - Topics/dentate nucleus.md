## Source [^1]
- $n$. A large wrinkled horseshoe-shaped strip of nerve cell bodies deep within each hemisphere of the cerebellum, involved in the control of rapid skilled movements. Often confused with the dentate gyrus. \[From Latin dens, dentis a tooth + nucleus a kernel, from nux, nucis a nut, alluding to its toothed appearance]
