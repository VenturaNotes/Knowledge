## Source [^1]
- (pl. sclerotia) 
- (1) In certain fungi, such as Sclerotium and Claviceps, a compact mass of fused hyphae produced as a resistant resting structure when conditions are unfavorable. 
- (2) In plasmodial slime moulds, an irregular compact mass of cellular components formed to survive unfavorable conditions.
