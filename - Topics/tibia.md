## Source [^1]
- $n$. the shin bone: the inner and larger bone of the lower leg (see illustration). It articulates with the femur above, with the talus below, and with the fibula to the side (at both ends); at the lower end is a projection, the medial malleolus, forming part of the articulation with the talus. $\textemdash$tibial adj.
