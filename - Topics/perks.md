## Source [^1]
- An informal word for perquisites, the benefits arising as a result of employment, in addition to regular renumeration. Perks are privileges that are expected mainly by senior employees (e.g. a company car, private health insurance, gym membership). See BENEFITS IN KIND; SHAREHOLDERS' PERKS.
