## Source [^1]
- $n$. a fine curved needle with a hooked tip or a small knife with a tiny curved or hooked blade, used to create an opening in the lens capsule in the type of operation for cataract in which the capsule is left behind (extracapsular cataract extraction; see PHACOEMULSIFICATION).
