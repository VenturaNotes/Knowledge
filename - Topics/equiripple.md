## Source [^1]
- (equal ripple) (1) A magnitude response of a filter circuit that is characterized by equal variations or ripples in the pass band and/or stop band. 
- (2) (equiripple filter) A type of filter where equiripple in the filter response is permitted, resulting in a steeper roll-off or narrower transition band. Chebyshev filters, inverse Chebyshev, and elliptic filters all possess equiripple responses.
