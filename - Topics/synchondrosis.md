## Source [^1]
- $n$. a slightly movable joint (see AMPHIARTHROSIS) in which the surfaces of the bones are separated by hyaline cartilage, as occurs between the ribs and sternum. This cartilage may become ossified in later development, as between the epiphyses and shaft of a long bone.
