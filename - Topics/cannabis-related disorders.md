## Source [^1]
- $n$. Substance-related and addictive disorders in DSM-5 associated specifically with the use of cannabis. DSM-5 distinguishes cannabis use disorder, cannabis intoxication, and cannabis withdrawal as separate disorders. See also SUBSTANCE-INDUCED DISORDERS, SUBSTANCE-RELATED AND ADDICTIVE DISORDERS, SUBSTANCE USE DISORDERS.
