## Source [^1]
- $n$. The distance between the center of a lens or curved mirror and its focal point. See also dioptre.
## Source[^2]
- The distance from a lens to the focus point where parallel rays of light hitting the lens are brought together. The focal length of a lens determines its magnification and its field of view, with a shorter length meaning lower magnification but a larger field of view.
