## Source [^1]
- adj. Of or relating to the generation of energy by a form of metabolism requiring oxygen or air. See also glycolysis, Krebs cycle. Compare anaerobic. \[From Greek aer air + bios life + -ikos of, relating to, or resembling]
## Source[^2]
- adj. 
- (1) of or relating to aerobes: requiring free oxygen for life and growth. 
- (2) describing a type of cellular respiration in which foodstuffs (carbohydrates) are completely oxidized by atmospheric oxygen, with the production of maximum chemical energy from the foodstuffs.
