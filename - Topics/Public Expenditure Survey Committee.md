## Source [^1]
- (PESC) A UK government interdepartmental committee that reviewed expenditure plans in the process leading to the annual Public Expenditure White Paper. The plans referred both to the coming financial year and to forward projections for the following four years. The PESC ceased operation as a working committee in 1984.
