## Source [^1]
- (in animal behavior) The tendency to exhibit displacement behavior at a point of equilibrium in situations where there are two or more conflicting drives. See DISPLACEMENT ACTIVITY.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. The reappearance of a conditioned response that has been inhibited, usually by extinction (2), following the presentation of a novel or strong stimulus. Also called inhibition of inhibition . Compare inhibition (3).
