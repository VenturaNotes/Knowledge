## Source [^1]
- (sulphur bridge) A covalent bond (S-S) formed between the thiol groups $(-\mathrm{SH})$ of two cysteine residues, usually in the polypeptide chains of proteins. Easily hydrolysed and prone to rearrangement, these bonds contribute to the tertiary structure of proteins.
