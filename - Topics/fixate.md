## Source [^1]
- $v b$. 
- (1) To train one's eyes on an object or point in the visual field so that its image falls on the foveas of the eyes. See also Donders' law, reading SPAN. 
- (2) To engage or indulge in fixation (3); to become preoccupied or obsessed with something. \[From Latin fixus fixed]
