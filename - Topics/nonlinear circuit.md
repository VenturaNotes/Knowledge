## Source [^1]
- A circuit in which the relation between the input and output is not linear. In general, a circuit with a mixture of both linear and nonlinear devices will itself be nonlinear. However, much electronic design focuses on building circuits with nonlinear components that exhibit near-linear behaviour over a wide range of inputs, known as the operational range. Examples of such circuits include audio amplifiers and operational amplifiers. See also LINEAR CIRCUIT.
