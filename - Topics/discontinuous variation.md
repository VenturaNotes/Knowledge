## Source [^1]
- (qualitative variation) Clearly defined differences in a characteristic that can be observed in a population. Characteristics that are determined by different alleles at a single locus show discontinuous variation, e.g. garden peas are either wrinkled or smooth. Compare CONTINUOUS VARIATION.
