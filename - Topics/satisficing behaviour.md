## Source [^1]
- Behavior by a company or an individual that is aimed at achieving satisfactory returns rather than optimum profit or growth. Given the limitations on any individual's knowledge of the total market situation, this is often a more rational approach than an abstract commitment to maximizing returns. See BOUNDED RATIONALITY. See also BEHAVIOURAL FINANCE.
