## Source [^1]
- the study of the relationships between the human genome, nutrition, and health. There are two main branches of study: nutrigenetics investigates how an individual's genes affect their response to certain dietary nutrients, whereas nutrigenomics investigates how nutrients ingested can modulate gene expression. In time, nutrigenomics may help to identify the aetiology of certain chronic diseases.
