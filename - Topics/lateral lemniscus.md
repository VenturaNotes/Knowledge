## Source [^1]
- $n$. Either of the two bands of nerve fibers in the lemniscus connecting the cochlear nuclei to the inferior colliculi and the thalamus on each side of the brain, involved in the processing of auditory signals. Compare MEDIAL LEMNISCUS, TRIGEMINAL LEMNISCUS.
