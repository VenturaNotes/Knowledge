## Source [^1]
- A wide display capable of showing images with a very wide field of view, often $270^{\circ}$ or even a full wrap-around $360^{\circ}$. Panoramic displays are usually constructed from an array of standard large displays aligned with little or no gap between their screens; relevant portions of the full image are then sent to each display.
