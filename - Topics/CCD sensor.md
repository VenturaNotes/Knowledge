## Source [^1]
- An imaging sensor built using charge-coupled device technology. They are very efficient in terms of light capture, with pixel sensor elements capable of detecting a single photon. This leads to the capture of high-quality images with very little noise. Because of the charge-coupled design and the limited number of amplifiers, they are limited in the number of images they can take in any time interval. This also means they have relatively high power consumption. See also CMOS SENSOR.
