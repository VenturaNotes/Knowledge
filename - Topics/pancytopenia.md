## Source [^1]
- $n$. a simultaneous decrease in the numbers of red cells (anaemia), white cells (neutropenia), and platelets (thrombocytopenia) in the blood. It occurs in a variety of disorders, including aplastic anaemias, hypersplenism, and tumors of the bone marrow. It may also occur after chemotherapy or total body irradiation.
