## Source [^1]
- (foetus) n. a mammalian embryo during the later stages of development within the uterus. In human reproduction it refers to an unborn child from its eighth week of development. $\textendash$fetal adj.
## Source[^2]
- (foetus) The embryo of a mammal, especially a human, when development has reached a stage at which the main features of the adult form are recognizable. In humans the embryo from eight weeks to birth is called a fetus.
