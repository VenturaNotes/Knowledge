## Source [^1]
- (pyridoxine) a vitamin of the B complex from which the coenzyme pyridoxal phosphate, involved in the transamination of amino acids, is formed. The vitamin is found in most foods and a deficiency is therefore rare. The adult RNI (see Dietary Reference Values) is 15 $\mu \mathrm{g} / \mathrm{g}$ dietary protein.
