## Source [^1]
- n. One of the Big Five personality factors, characterized by traits such as organization, thoroughness, reliability, and practicality, and the relative absence of carelessness, negligence, and unreliability. Also called dependability . See also alPHA PERSONALITY FACTOR, GENERAL FACTOR OF PERSONALITY, HEALTH PSYCHOLOGY. conscientious adj. \[From Latin con with + scire to know]
