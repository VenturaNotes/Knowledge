## Source [^1]
- $n$. A protein produced in the liver of a human foetus. Unusually low levels in the amniotic fluid or blood of an expectant mother may indicate Down's syndrome in the foetus, and unusually high levels of spina bifida. See also amniocentesis. Also spelt alpha-fetoprotein. afp abbrev.
