## Source [^1]
- The insurance of ships or their cargo against specified causes of loss or damage that might be encountered at sea. The definition has widened over the years to include the transit of cargo over land at each end of the voyage and the term 'vessel' now extends to include ships under construction or repair and drilling rigs.
