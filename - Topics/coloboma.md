## Source [^1]
- $n$. a defect in the development of the eye causing abnormalities ranging in severity from a notch in the lower part of the iris, making the pupil pear-shaped, to defects in the retina, choroid, and optic nerve fundus. A coloboma of the eyelid is a congenital notch in the lid margin.
