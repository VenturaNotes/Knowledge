## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) Without hue, or represented in black, white, and grey. An achromatic color, also called a neutral grey or neutral color, is a mixture of black and white without any chroma and is the lightness component of any color. See also Hering Grey, saturation (1). 
- (2) Capable of refracting or reflecting light without chromatic aberration (3). \[From Greek $a$ - without + chroma color]
