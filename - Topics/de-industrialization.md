## Source [^1]
- A substantial fall in the importance of the manufacturing sector in the economy of an industrialized nation as it becomes uncompetitive with its neighbors. This may result from bad industrial relations, poor management, inadequate investment in capital goods, or shortsighted government economic policies. In many cases each of these factors contributes to de-industrialization.
