## Source [^1]
- A written document from the owner of goods to the holder of the goods (e.g. a warehouse company) instructing them to release the goods to the firm named on the delivery order or to the bearer (if made out to 'bearer'). A delivery order backed by a dock or warehouse warrant may be accepted by a bank as security for a loan.
