## Source [^1]
- $n$. a rare condition of inflammation of the pituitary gland (hypophysis). The main cause is an infiltration by lymphocytes, most commonly during or just after pregnancy. This usually presents as a mass lesion of the pituitary with visual-field loss and headache or with anterior hypopituitarism, which may be total or just involve particular hormone systems. Around $50 \%$ of cases are associated with other autoimmune endocrine diseases, and antipituitary antibodies have been identified.
