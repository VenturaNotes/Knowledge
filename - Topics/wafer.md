## Source [^1]
- A large single crystal of semiconductor, usually silicon, that is used as the substrate on which integrated circuits are manufactured. [[Wafer-scale integration]] is a technique that utilizes a very large area of the silicon wafer to implement a VLSI circuit.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. a thin sheet made from moistened flour, formerly used to enclose a powdered medicine that is taken by mouth.
## Source[^3]
- (slice) A large single crystal of semiconductor material that is used as the substrate during the manufacture of a number of chips. Very large single crystals are grown and then sliced into wafers before processing.
