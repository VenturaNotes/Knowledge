## Source [^1]
- The process of performing computations on a set of measured or presented data (believed to be) from a physical, i.e. natural, source in such a way as to develop a 'best' parametric model of that physical source, i.e. one that best fits the observed data according to some error criterion. See also ADAPTIVE-CONTROL SYSTEM, SELF-ORGANIZING SYSTEM.
