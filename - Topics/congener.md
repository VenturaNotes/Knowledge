## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) A member of the same genus as another organism. 
- (2) A drug that belongs to the same category, class, or group as its parent compound.
- (3) Any additional substance apart from alcohol that is present in an alcoholic beverage and that is partly responsible for its effects.
