## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) In genetics, the proportion of monozygotic twins (or relatives of other known degrees of genetic relatedness) who display different forms of a particular characteristic or different phenotypes. See also map distance, twin study. Compare concordance (1). 
- (2) In statistics, the degree of disagreement between ranks. See gamma statistic, Kendall coefficient of concordance, Kendall's tau, Somers' D. Compare CONCORDANCE (2). concordant adj.
