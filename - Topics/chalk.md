## Source [^1]
- A very fine-grained white rock composed of the fossilized skeletal remains of marine plankton known as coccoliths and consisting largely of calcium carbonate $\left(\mathrm{CaCO}_{3}\right)$. It is the characteristic rock of the Cretaceous period. It should not be confused with blackboard 'chalk', which is made from calcium sulphate.
