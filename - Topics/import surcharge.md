## Source [^1]
- An extra tax applied by a government on certain imports in addition to the normal tariff. It is usually applied as a temporary measure by a government that is in difficulty with its balance of payments, but does not wish to violate its international tariff agreements.
## Source[^2]
- A temporary additional tax on imports, imposed in addition to normal tariffs, in response to balance-of-payments problems. Such a temporary tax may be very effective in reducing imports, as it creates an incentive to postpone them until it has been removed.
