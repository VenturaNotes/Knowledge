## Source [^1]
- 1. (in chemistry) A compound, molecule, ion, etc., to which electrons are donated in the formation of a coordinate bond. 
- 2. (in biochemistry) A receptor that binds a hormone without any apparent biological response.
## Source[^2]
- The drawee of a bill of exchange after acceptance of the bill, i.e. the acceptor has accepted liability by signing the face of the bill.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. A chemical substance that receives and combines with another substance, as in an oxidation-reduction reaction where the oxygen is the acceptor of the substance that is oxidized and hydrogen is the acceptor of the substance that is reduced.
## Source[^4]
- $n$. (in biochemistry) a substance that helps to bring about oxidation of a reduced substrate by accepting hydrogen ions.
