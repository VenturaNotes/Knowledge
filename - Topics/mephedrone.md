## Source [^1]
- $n$. A synthetic stimulant drug and euphoriant 4-methylmethcathinone (4-MM-CAT) or 4-methylephedrone, closely related to amphetamine, first manufactured in 2007, supplied as a white powder to be snorted through the nostrils or as pills to be swallowed, and often sold as plant food. Street name: meow meow. Formula $\mathrm{C}_{11} \mathrm{H}_{15} \mathrm{NO}$. Often confused with methadone. \[A contraction of the chemical name of the variant $m$(ethyl)ephedrone]
