## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of thirst triggered by decreased blood volume sensed by baroreceptors. US hypovolemic thirst. Also called extracellular thirst or volumetric thirst. Compare intracellular thirst. \[From Greek hypo under + English volume + Greek haima blood]
