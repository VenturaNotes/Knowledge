## Source [^1]
- (1) A form, often attached to an allotment letter, on which a person who has been allotted shares in a new issue renounces any rights to them, either absolutely or in favor of someone else (during the renunciation period). 
- (2) A form on the reverse of some unit-trust certificates, which the holder completes to dispose of the holding. The holder sends the completed certificate to the trust managers.
