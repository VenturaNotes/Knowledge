## Source [^1]
- $n$. A measure of visual acuity, usually determined with an acuity grating, equal to the visual angle corresponding to the finest grating in which separate bars can be distinguished. A person with normal 20/20 vision according to the Snellen fraction has a minimum separable of approximately $1 / 60$ of a degree or one minute of arc. Compare MINIMUM VISIBLE.
