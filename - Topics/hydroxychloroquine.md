## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug similar to chloroquine, used mainly to treat lupus erythematosus and rheumatoid arthritis (see DISEASE-MODIFYING ANTIRHEUMATIC DRUG). Side-effects such as skin reactions, headache, and digestive upsets may occur and prolonged use can lead to eye damage.
