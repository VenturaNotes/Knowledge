## Source [^1]
- $n$. A variation of random digit dialing (RDD) in which a probability sample of telephone subscribers is drawn by selecting telephone numbers randomly from directories and adding one to the final digit of each number before dialling it, the purpose being to ensure that ex-directory subscribers are adequately represented in the sample.
