## Source [^1]
- (Best's disease) degeneration of the macula of the eye that is inherited as a dominant characteristic and usually starts in childhood. There is widespread abnormality of retinal pigment epithelium (see RETINA) with the accumulation of a yellowish material, especially in the macular area.
