## Source [^1]
- adj. Pro-bulimia, loosely descriptive of a subculture, literature, and websites that provide support for people with bulimia nervosa and that sometimes (controversially) promote the disorder as a lifestyle choice. Compare Pro-ana. \[From Latin pro in favour of + (buli)mia, from Greek bous ox + limos hunger + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
