## Source [^1]
- Denoting a display with a horizontal resolution of approximately 4000 pixels. For consumer TV sets, this means that they conform to the minimum UHD standard. The 4K digital projectors used in cinemas have a horizontal resolution of 4096 pixels, this being the resolution used in most digital film production.
