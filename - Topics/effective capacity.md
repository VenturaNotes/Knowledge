## Source [^1]
- The capacity of a system in units per time, taking into account such factors as staffing decisions (e.g. whether to run 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, or to operate one 8 -hour shift, 5 days a week), set-up times, maintenance, and an allowance for unforeseeable failures.
