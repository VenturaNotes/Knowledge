## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of perception found in all snakes of the subfamily Crotalinae, the pit vipers, including rattlesnakes and pythons, in which infra-red radiation emitted by warm objects such as prey animals is detected by a snake's pit organs, a pair of deep cavities in the sides of its head below its eyes.
