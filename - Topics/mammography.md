## Source [^1]
- $n$. the study of the breast by imaging techniques, most commonly X-rays. The breast is usually compressed between radiolucent plates before being exposed to relatively low-energy X-rays to emphasize differences in the soft tissues. Mammography is mainly concerned with the detection and diagnosis of breast cancer.
