## Source [^1]
- A method of error recovery in which the packet received in error, and all information transmitted since that packet was transmitted, is retransmitted. It requires very little complexity in the receiver, since the receiver can discard all packets arriving out of order. Compare SELECTIVE REPEAT ARQ.
