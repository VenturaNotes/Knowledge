## Source [^1]
- (SAR) Mobile imaging radar that sends a stream of radar pulses at right angles to its motion. The timing of the return echoes gives the radar an effective aperture that is much larger than its physical size. This results in much higher-resolution images than would be obtained by looking at echoes from a single pulse. See also SIDE-LOOKING AIRBORNE RADAR.
