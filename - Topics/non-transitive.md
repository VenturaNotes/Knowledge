## Source [^1]
- (of a relation) Neither transitive nor intransitive. The transitive relationship has to hold for some triples and not for others, i.e. there exists elements $a, b, c, d, e, f$ for which $a \sim b, b \sim c, a \sim c$, whereas $d \sim e, e \sim f,$ but $d \sim f$ does not hold.
