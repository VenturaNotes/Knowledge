## Source [^1]
- (AMOLED) An organic light-emitting diode in an active-matrix display, i.e. one with a backplane that consists of two thin-film transistors per pixel. These transistors enable the display to operate at higher speed and with much lower power consumption than is possible with a simpler passive matrix. AMOLED systems offer the thinnest possible display, combined with very high image quality.
