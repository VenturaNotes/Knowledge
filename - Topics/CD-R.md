## Source [^1]
- (compact disc recordable) A type of compact disc that can be used to store music or data. Once written, the data cannot be erased, but multiple sessions can be appended to the same disc. A dye core is used rather than the metal core found in a conventional compact disc. A laser etches pits into the dye to store the data.
