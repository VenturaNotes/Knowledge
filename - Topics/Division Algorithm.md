## Source [^1]
- For integers $a$ and $b$, with $b > 0$, there exist unique integers $q$ and $r$ such that $a = bq + r$, where $0 \le r < b$. In the division of $a$ by $b$, the number $q$ is the quotient and $r$ is the remainder. See EUCLIDEAN DOMAIN.
