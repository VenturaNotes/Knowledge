## Source [^1]
- Cooperation between representatives in national or local legislatures to support other members' bids for public money in return for support for measures to benefit their own constituents. Log-rolling is blamed for wasteful public expenditures: a coalition formed on this basis may push through a group of measures most of which each member would have opposed if not voting strategically.
