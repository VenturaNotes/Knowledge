## Source[^2]
- (pl. halluces) The innermost digit on the hindlimb of a tetrapod vertebrate. In humans it is the big toe and contains two phalanges. The hallux is absent in some mammals and in many birds it is directed backwards as an adaptation to perching. Compare POLLEX.
