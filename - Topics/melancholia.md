## Source[^2]
- $n$. A severe form of depression; more specifically, a major depressive episode. involutional melancholia $n$. Another name for involutional depression. \[From Greek melas black + chole bile + -ia indicating a condition or quality, referring to the ancient doctrine of the four temperaments, according to which depression was attributed to an excess of black bile]
