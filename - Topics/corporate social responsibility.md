## Source [^1]
- The intention of a business to ensure active compliance with the spirit of the law, uphold high ethical standards, and conform to international standards of behavior. Corporate social responsibility is adopted to ensure that a business has a positive impact upon its stakeholders, the environment, and the wider community. 
## Source[^2]
- (CSR) The notion that a company has responsibilities to society that go beyond its legal obligations and its duties to shareholders. Corporate social responsibility considerations include the company's impact on the environment, any ethical issues arising from its trading or investment practices, and the policies of the company internally, for instance with regard to transparency and the fair and equal treatment of employees.
