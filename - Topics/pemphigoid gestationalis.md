## Source [^1]
- (pemphigoid gestationis) a rare autoimmune condition ( 1 in 10,000-1 in 60,000 pregnancies) that usually starts in the second trimester with itching preceding a widespread polymorphic eruption with vesicles and blisters. It is associated with intrauterine growth restriction and preterm delivery.
