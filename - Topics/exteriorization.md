## Source [^1]
- $n$. a surgical procedure in which an organ is brought from its normal site to the surface of the body. This may be done as a temporary or permanent measure; for example, the intestine may be brought to the surface of the abdomen (see COLOSTOMY). The process is also sometimes used in physiological experiments on animals.
