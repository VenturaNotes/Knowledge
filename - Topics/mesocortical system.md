## Source [^1]
- $n$. A system of dopamine-secreting neurons with cell bodies in the ventral tegmental area of the midbrain (mesencephalon) and terminal boutons in the cerebral cortex, involved in experiences of pleasure and reward. See also MESOLIMBIC SYSTEM, MESOTELENCEPHALIC PATHWAY.
