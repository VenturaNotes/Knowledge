## Source [^1]
- (SD) 
- (1) Describing a display with a typical resolution of $640 \times 480$ pixels and a 4:3 aspect ratio. In practice, this means any display that is not high-definition. 
- (2) Conforming to any of various analogue television broadcast standards, such as PAL, SECAM, and NTSC.
