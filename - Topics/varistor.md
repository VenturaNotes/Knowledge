## Source [^1]
- (variable resistor) A two-terminal device whose resistance varies with the magnitude of the applied voltage, in particular increasing rapidly at high voltages. Such a device can be used to suppress large voltage spikes, although the switching time is not rapid. See TRANSIENT SUPPRESSOR.
