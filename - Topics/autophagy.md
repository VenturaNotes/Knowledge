## Synthesis
- A cellular process in which cells recycle their own components, such as damaged proteins, organelles, and cellular debris
- A natural and essential mechanism for maintaining [[cellular homeostasis]] and adapting to stress conditions
### Process
- (1) Initiation
	- Triggered by various signals, including nutrient deprivation, stress, and cellular damage
- (2) Formation of an [[autophagosome]]
