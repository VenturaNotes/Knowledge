## Source [^1]
- A [[sensory system]] responsible for providing our brain with information about motion, head position, and spatial orientation
## Source[^2]
- those parts of the body involved in balance. The peripheral vestibular system, or vestibular apparatus, is in the inner ear. The central vestibular system comprises those parts of the brain that are involved in balance. The central vestibular system receives other inputs as well as from the ear, in particular inputs from the eyes and proprioceptors.
