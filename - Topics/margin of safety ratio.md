## Source [^1]
- The margin of safety expressed as a percentage of a given level of activity. For example, if the sales level achieved is $£ 500,000$ and the sales level breakeven point is $£ 400,000$, the margin of safety is $£ 100,000$ and the margin of safety ratio will be: $$(£ 100,000 \times 100) / £ 500,000=20 \%$$
