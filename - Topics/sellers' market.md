## Source [^1]
- A market in which the demand exceeds the supply, so that sellers can increase prices. At some point, however, buyers will cease to follow the price rises and the sellers will be forced to drop prices in order to make sales, i.e. the supply will have exceeded the demand. See BUYERS' MARKET.
