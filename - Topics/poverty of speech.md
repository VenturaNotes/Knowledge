## Source [^1]
- Brief hesitant speech using few words (often monosyllables) and lacking spontaneity. It can be observed in patients with schizophrenia, dementia, and depression.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Marked deficit in spontaneous speech, and replies to questions that are perfunctory, monosyllabic, or unforthcoming. Also called hypologia, hypophrasia. Compare POVERTY OF CONTENT.
