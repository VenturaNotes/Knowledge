## Source [^1]
- In management and leadership, the use of rewards and penalties to motivate followers and achieve compliance with organizational goals and norms. Reliance on contingent reinforcement is a hallmark of transactional leadership. The term is associated with behaviouristic schools of psychology that emphasize the correlation of stimulus and response. Compare HUMAN RELATIONS THEORY.
