## Source [^1]
- A practice that has been used in a particular trade for a long time and is understood to apply by all engaged in that trade. Such customs or practices may influence the way in which a term in a contract is interpreted and courts will generally take into account established customs of a trade in settling a dispute over the interpretation of a contract.
