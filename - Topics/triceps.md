## Source [^1]
- $n$. a muscle with three heads of origin, particularly the triceps brachii, which is situated on the back of the upper arm and contracts to extend the forearm. It is the antagonist of the brachialis.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. triceps) A muscle that runs parallel to the humerus and works antagonistically with the biceps, causing the arm to extend. See ANTAGONISM; SKELETAL MUSCLE.
