## Source [^1]
- $n$. Something or someone that adapts or adjusts to a changed situation or helps a person or other organism to adjust, especially (in social psychology) a gesture (1), usually involving self-touching, that helps one to cope with an emotional reaction, typical examples being self-grooming behavior, covering the eyes for fear, or putting a hand over the mouth for surprise. See also DISPLACEMENT ACTIVITY, KINESICS.
