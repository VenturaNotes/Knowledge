## Source [^1]
- The time required for a periodic waveform-i.e. a waveform recurring at fixed intervals-to repeat itself.
## Source[^2]
- (1) The time taken for one complete cycle of an oscillating system or wave. 
- (2) See periodic table
## Source[^3]
- (periodic time) (symbol: $T$) The time required to complete a single cycle of regularly recurring events. The period of an oscillation is related to the frequency, $f$, and the angular frequency, $\omega$, by$$T = \frac{1}{f} = \frac{2\pi}{\omega}$$
