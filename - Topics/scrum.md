## Synthesis
- A structured and incremental agile framework for managing complex work. It organizes work into sprints and defines specific roles, events, and artifacts (like backlogs) to implement agile values. 
### Scrum Roles
- Three core roles of scrum
	- [[Product Owner]]
	- [[Scrum Master]]
	- [[Development Team]]

### Scrum Events
- Five formal events in scrum:
	- [[Sprint planning]]
	- [[Daily scrum]] (stand-ups)
	- [[Sprint review]]
	- [[Sprint retrospective]]
	- [[Sprint]] (encapsulating the other events)
