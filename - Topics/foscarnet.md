## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antiviral drug used in the treatment of herpes simplex and cytomegalovirus retinitis that are resistant to aciclovir, especially in patients with AIDS. Possible side-effects include thirst and increased urine output, nausea and vomiting, fatigue, headache, and kidney damage.
