## Source [^1]
- $n$. the concentration of body fluids (e.g. plasma, urine) measured in terms of the amount of dissolved substances per unit mass of water. It is usually given in units of $\mathrm{mOsm} \mathrm~{kg}^{-1}$ or $\mathrm{Osm} / \mathrm{l}$. Symbol: Os or osmol.
