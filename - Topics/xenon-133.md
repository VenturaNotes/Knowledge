## Source [^1]
- n. a radioactive isotope that has a half-life of about five days and is used in ventilation scanning of the lungs in nuclear medicine (see VENTILATION-PERFUSION SCANNING). It gives off beta particles, which are responsible for the relatively high radiation dose compared to krypton81m. Symbol: Xe-133.
