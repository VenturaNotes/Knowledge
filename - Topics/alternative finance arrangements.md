## Source [^1]
- A term applied in the UK Finance Acts to certain lending arrangements that comply with Islamic law (see ISLAMIC FINANCE). The Finance Act 2005 introduced a tax code under which tax is levied on the lender and relief is granted to the borrower as if (broadly) a proportion of each payment were interest.
