## Source [^1]
- A number whose square is a negative real number. Imaginary numbers are written as the product of a real number coefficient $r$ and the unit imaginary number $i$, where $i^2 = -1$. This means that $(ir)^2 = -(r^2)$. See also COMPLEX NUMBER.
