## Source [^1]
- small groups of endocrine cells scattered through the material of the pancreas. There are three main histological types: alpha ( $\alpha$ ) cells, which secrete glucagon; beta ( $\beta$ ) cells, which produce insulin; and D cells, which release somatostatin and pancreatic polypeptide. \[P. Langerhans (1847-88), German physician and anatomist]
