## Source [^1]
- A nonmotile reproductive cell of certain filamentous cyanobacteria. An akinete is an enlarged resting cell with a thick wall and large amounts of food reserves and DNA. After cell division has occurred within the akinete the cell wall ruptures, releasing a filament of cells.
