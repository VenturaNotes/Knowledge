## Source [^1]
- A transaction in which the parties intend to create one set of rights and obligations but perform acts or enter into documents that they intend should give parties (often HM Revenue and Customs) the appearance of creating different rights and obligations. Tax benefits arising from sham transactions may be denied by the courts.
