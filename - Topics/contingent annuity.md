## Source [^1]
- (reversionary annuity) An annuity in which the payment is conditional on a specified event happening. The most common form is an annuity purchased jointly by a husband and wife that begins payment after the death of one of the parties (see JOINT-LIFE AND LAST-SURVIVOR ANNUITIES).
