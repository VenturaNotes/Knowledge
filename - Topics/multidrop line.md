## Source [^1]
- A single communication line that connects multiple stations-terminals or computers. Typically, one station will use polling to coordinate access to the line and prevent collisions (multiple stations transmitting at once). A control protocol such as SDLC is used. See also MULTIPOINT CONNECTION. Compare POINT-TO-POINT LINE.
