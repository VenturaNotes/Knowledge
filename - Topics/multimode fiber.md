## Source [^1]
- An optical fibre with a core diameter many times the wavelength of the light, so that multiple modes of the optical transmission line are excited. This type of fibre has much greater dispersion than single-mode fibre, but due to the larger diameter, it is much easier to launch significant quantities of light into multimode fibre and easier to connect the fibre. Most fibre to a desktop system uses multimode fiber.
