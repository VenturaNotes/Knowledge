## Source[^2]
- adj. 
- (1) Positioned off centre.
- (2) Of or relating to a retinal image cast by a visual stimulus off the centre of the fovea.
- (3) Odd or unconventional. eccentricity $n$. \[From Greek $e k$ out of + kentron centre]
