## Source [^1]
- (HIA) a systematic process that generally uses existing scientific literature and local geographic and demographic knowledge to judge the potential impact of a project or policy on the health of a population. HIAs are used to assess the likely health impact of many different types of projects or policies, from large construction projects to taxation policies.
