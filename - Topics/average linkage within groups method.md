## Source [^1]
- $n$. In statistics, a technique of cluster analysis in which clusters are combined in such a way that the average distance between all individuals or cases in the resulting cluster is minimized. Also, the distance between two clusters is defined as the average distance between all possible pairs of individuals in the cluster that would result if they were combined.
