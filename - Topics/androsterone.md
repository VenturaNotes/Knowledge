## Source [^1]
- $n$. a steroid that is formed in the liver as a metabolite of testosterone. See also ANDROGEN.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. An androgen steroid hormone, less active than testosterone, produced in the testes and detectable in men's urine, synthesized artificially from cholesterol. \[From andro(gen) + ster(ol) + (horm)one]
