## Source [^1]
- (body schema) the individual's concept of the disposition of his limbs and the identity of the different parts of his body. It is a function of the association areas of the brain. See also GERSTMANN's SYNDROME.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A mental representation of one's own physical appearance, based partly on self-observation and partly on the reactions of others. See also IMAGE (3), SCHEMA.
