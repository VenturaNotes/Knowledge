## Source [^1]
- $n$. A form of psychotherapy, developed by the US psychologist Arthur Janov (born 1924) in 1970, in which clients or patients are encouraged to scream about their parents and their painful re-experiencing of birth and infancy. Also called primal scream therapy or scream therapy. See also BIRTH TRAUMA, BODY THERAPIES, REBIRTHING.
