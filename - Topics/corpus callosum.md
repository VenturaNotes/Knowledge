## Source [^1]
- Carries information received in one hemisphere over to the other and in general allows for communication between two hemispheres
- It's a large C-shaped nerve fiber bundle that stretches across the midline of the brain
	- It connects the left and right cerebral hemispheres
- Makes up largest collection of white matter found in the brain
- Patients have had their corpus callosum surgically severed to stop the spread of epileptic seizures
