## Source [^1]
- (amplitude; crest value) 
- (1) The maximum positive or negative value of any alternating quantity, such as current or voltage, during a given time interval. The positive and negative values are not necessarily equal in magnitude. 
- (2) (peak pulse amplitude) The maximum value of an impulse voltage or current.
