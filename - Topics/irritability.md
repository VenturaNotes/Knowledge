## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) (in physiology) the property of certain kinds of tissue that enables them to respond in a specific way to outside stimuli. Irritability is shown by nerve cells, which can generate and transmit electrical impulses when stimulated appropriately, and by muscle cells, which contract when stimulated by nerve impulses. 
- (2) impatience and lack of empathy. This has been found to be a major factor in patients' dissatisfaction with their medical advisors.
