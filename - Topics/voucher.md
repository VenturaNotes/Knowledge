## Source [^1]
- A receipt for money or any document that supports an entry in a book of account.
## Source[^2]
- A certificate usable in place of money, but only for a specific purpose. For example, in some school districts in the US parents or guardians receive education vouchers to cover the cost of educating their children in the school of their choice. Such a system is aimed at combining the advantages of universal state funding of education with competition in its provision.
