## Source [^1]
- $n$. a bile-acid sequestrant used, in conjunction with dietary reduction of cholesterol, to lower cholesterol levels in the blood in patients with hyperlipidaemia and primary hypercholesterolaemia that have not responded to diet. Constipation is a common side-effect.
