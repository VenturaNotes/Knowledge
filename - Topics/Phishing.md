## Synthesis
- It's when someone receives a deceptive message claiming to be from a trusted source, but are just trying to retrieve sensitive information

## Source [^1]
- Attackers send fraudulent emails or messages that appear to come from trusted sources, tricking recipients into revealing sensitive information, such as login credentials or financial details.
