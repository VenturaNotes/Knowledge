## Source [^1]
- (DC scheme) A pension scheme in which the benefits are based on the value of the contributions paid in by each member. The rate of contribution is normally specified; the amount of pension an individual will receive will depend on the size of the fund accumulated and the annuity that can be obtained from it at the date of retirement. Compare DEFINED-BENEFIT PENSION SCHEME.
