## Source [^1]
- A creditor who has obtained a court judgment against a debtor (the judgment debtor), ordering him or her to pay the sum owed. If the judgment debtor fails to pay, the judgment creditor must return to the court asking for the judgment to be enforced. See also THIRD-PARTY DEBT ORDER.
