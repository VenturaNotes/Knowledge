## Source [^1]
- A carboxylic acid having two carboxyl groups in its molecules. In systematic chemical nomenclature, dicarboxylic acids are denoted by the suffix -dioic; e.g. hexanedioic acid, $\mathrm{HOOC}\left(\mathrm{CH}_{2}\right)_{4} \mathrm{COOH}$.
