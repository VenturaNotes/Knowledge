## Source [^1]
- $n$. A structure that is a mirror image of another, being exactly the same shape as the other except for the reversal of left and right. Some pairs of molecules have this relational property. See also handedness (2), MENTAL ROTATION, MIRROR REVERSAL PROBLEM. enantiomorphic adj. \[From Greek enantios opposite + morphe a form or shape]
