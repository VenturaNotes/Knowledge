## Source [^1]
- Denoting an export price in which the exporter pays all the charges to the port of destination and also pays the landing charges but not any dock dues or onward carriage charges to the importer's factory or warehouse. The corresponding Incoterm is Delivered Ex Quay (DEQ); see Delivered Ex SHIP. Compare FRANCO.
