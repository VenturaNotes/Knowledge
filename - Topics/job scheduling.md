## Source[^2]
- Job scheduling tools handle queues of jobs or tasks in a logical order. They prioritize jobs effectively for speed and efficiency. Most operating systems have some sort of built-in job scheduling. Businesses can also buy additional pieces of software to handle jobs and related batches of instructions.
