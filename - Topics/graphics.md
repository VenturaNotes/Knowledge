## Source [^1]
- Any computer output except alphanumeric characters, especially the drawings, graphs, and symbols that can be produced on computers. Most business software packages include a graphics program that can produce graphs, histograms, and piecharts. More powerful graphics programs are used for computer-aided design and animation.
