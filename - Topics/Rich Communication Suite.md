## Source [^1]
- A rich communication suite (RCS) is a part of a multimedia communication protocol used by certain phone and data carriers. A participating individual could use a variety of advanced messaging tools via an RCS. This included advanced messaging, sending multimedia files during calls, and enhanced encryption options.
