## Source [^1]
- (1) To fail to pay a cheque when the account of the drawer does not have sufficient funds to cover it. When a bank dishonours a cheque it marks it 'refer to drawer' and returns it to the payee through his or her bank. 
- (2) To fail to accept a bill of exchange (dishonour by non-acceptance) or to fail to pay a bill of exchange (dishonour by non-payment). 
- (3) To fail to honour any other financial obligation.
