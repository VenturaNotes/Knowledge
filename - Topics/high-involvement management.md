## Source [^1]
- An approach to management that emphasizes the need to develop organizational commitment amongst employees, on the grounds that this will lead to improved motivation, better performance, and a lower labour turnover rate. It argues that employee commitment can be enhanced through training, incentives, sharing of information, and participation in decision making.
