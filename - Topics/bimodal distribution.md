## Source [^1]
- A distribution with two distinct peaks and a dip between them. An example is the empirical distribution of human death rates, which are higher in infancy and in old age than in the years between.
## Source[^2]
- n. Any probability distribution or frequency distribution with two modes, therefore appearing visually with two peaks, indicating a clustering of scores around two different values.
