## Source [^1]
- (1) n. any drug or chemical agent used to destroy parasitic worms (helminths), e.g. tapeworms, roundworms, and flukes, and/or remove them from the body. Anthelmintics include albendazole, mebendazole, niclosamide, and praziquantel. 
- (2) adj. having the power to destroy or eliminate helminths.
