## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) a complex pattern of behavior innately determined, which is characteristic of all individuals of the same species. The behavior is released and modified by environmental stimuli, but its pattern is relatively uniform and predetermined. 
- (2) an innate drive that urges the individual towards a particular goal (for example, libido in psychoanalytic psychology).
