## Source [^1]
- $n$. The deep horizontal cleft in the outside wall of each cerebral hemisphere, separating the frontal lobe and part of the parietal lobe from the temporal lobe. Also called the fissure of Sylvius, the lateral fissure, or the Sylvian fissure. See also insula, operculum.
