## Source [^1]
- An average obtained by calculating the $n$th root of a set of $n$ numbers. For example the geometric mean of 7, 100, and 107 is $\sqrt[3]{74,900}$ $=42.15$, which is considerably less than the arithmetic mean of 71.3.
