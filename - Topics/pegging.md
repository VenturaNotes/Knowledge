## Source [^1]
- (1) (pegging the exchange) The fixing of the value of a country's currency on foreign exchange markets. See CRAWLING PEG; FIXED EXCHANGE RATE. 
- (2) The fixing of wages or prices at existing levels by government order to prevent them rising during a period of inflation.
