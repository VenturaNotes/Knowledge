## Source [^1]
- drum printer (barrel printer) A type of solid-font line printer, first marketed in 1955 but now obsolete. The font was etched or engraved on the outer surface of a cylinder$\textemdash$known as the drum (or barrel)$\textemdash$that extended across the full width of the line to be printed.
