## Source [^1]
- adj. (in anatomy) lower in the body in relation to another structure or surface.
## Source[^2]
- Describing a structure that is positioned below or lower than another structure in the body. For example, in flowering plants the ovary is described as inferior when it is located below the other organs of the flower (see EPIGYNY). Compare SUPERIOR.
