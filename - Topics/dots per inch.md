## Source [^1]
- (dpi) A measure of the resolution of images used in displays, image capture, and printing. The more dots per inch, the better the resolution. Printer resolution of 600 dpi is common and means that the image has 600 printed dots per inch horizontally and 600 dots per inch vertically.
