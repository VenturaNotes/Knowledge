## Source [^1]
- A system in which two or more sites, each equipped with a video camera and screen, are interconnected by a network so that participants at each site can both see and hear their opposite numbers at the other sites. There are now a number of commercial services offering videoconferencing facilities that can operate over digital networks.
## Source[^2]
- Video conferencing is way of communicating among individuals or groups using systems that support image, voice, and data transfer over digital networks or telephone circuits. This online technology allows people in different locations to talk and see each other using live audio and video, and hold face-to-face meetings virtually. Videoconferencing allows for convenient collaboration across the globe.
