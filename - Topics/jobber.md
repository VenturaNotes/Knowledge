## Source[^2]
- A dealer in shares or commodities who holds a stock of the asset and trades as a principal. Jobbers are distinguished from brokers, who operate in the same markets, but put people who want to buy and sell in touch with each other, and do not trade for themselves. The modern name for a jobber is a market-maker.
