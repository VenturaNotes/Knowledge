## Source[^1]
### Definition
- 1 amu = mass of $\frac {1}{12}$ Carbon atom $(\ce{^{12}_{6}C})$
	- 1 amu $\approx$ mass of 1 proton or 1 neutron

## Source[^2]
- The atomic mass unit (AMU or amu) of an element is a measure of its atomic mass.
