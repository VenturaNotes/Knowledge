## Source [^1]
- failure of the foramen ovale to close shortly after birth resulting in an atrial septal defect. A patent foramen ovale is present in everyone before birth but closes off in about $80 \%$ of people. It usually causes no symptoms; an isolated patent foramen ovale without any other structural heart defect is usually of no haemodynamic significance.
