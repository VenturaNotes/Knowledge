## Source [^1]
- $n$. paralysis of the iris, which is usually associated with cycloplegia and results from injury, inflammation, or the use of pupil-dilating eye drops. In the case of injury, the pupil is usually larger than normal and moves little, if at all, in response to light and drugs.
