## Source [^1]
- A biological rhythm whose period is less than a day. Numerous physiological processes undergo rhythmical changes of less than 24 hours' duration, such as appetite, heart rate, blood pressure, and growth hormone secretion. Compare CIRCADIAN RHYTHM; INFRADIAN RHYTHM. See BIORHYTHM.
