## Source [^1]
- A photosynthetic pigment that traps light energy and channels it to chlorophyll $a$, the primary pigment, which initiates the reactions of photosynthesis. Accessory pigments include the carotenoids, phycobiliproteins, and chlorophylls $b, c$, and d.
