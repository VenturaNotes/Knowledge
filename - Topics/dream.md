## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) The mental experiences, usually in the form of a sequence of ideas, images, and imagined events, often accompanied by emotions, occurring during REM sleep. See also dream analysis, dreamwork, INTERPRETATION (2), LUCID DREAM, NIGHTMARE, SLEEP TERRORS. vb. 
- (2) To experience a dream or dreams; hence, figuratively, to imagine or fantasize. \[From Old English dream a song]
