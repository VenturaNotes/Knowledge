## Source [^1]
- Describing a type of dentition that is characterized by two successive sets of teeth: the deciduous (milk) teeth, which are followed by the permanent (adult) teeth. Mammals have a diphyodont dentition. Compare MONOPHYODONT; POLYPHYODONT.
