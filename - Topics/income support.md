## Source [^1]
- Government payments to maintain individuals' incomes at some prescribed minimum level, in the event of illness, old age, disability, or unemployment making them unable to earn sufficient income. A negative income tax is a system in which the income support is delivered through a combined tax and social security system, which collects tax if income is above a certain level and pays out if income is below it.
