## Source [^1]
- $n$. A term introduced in DSM-5 to denote a disorder characterized by clearly excessive preoccupation, for at least six months, with the possibility of having or contracting a serious illness, with at most mild or minimal somatic symptoms (this distinguishes it from somatic symptom disorder), intense anxiety about health, and excessive health-related activities, either repeatedly checking for symptoms or fearfully avoiding doctor's appointments. Compare BODY DYSMORPHIC DISORDER, GENERALIZED ANXIETY DISORDER, OBSESSIVE-COMPULSIVE DISORDER, PANIC DISORDER.
