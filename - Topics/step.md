## Source [^1]
- The international standard, ISO 10303: Standard for the Exchange of Product Model Data, covering all aspects of product life cycle in all industries. It is a neutral way of representing product data throughout its life, independent of any particular computeraided system. STEP includes a textual conceptual schema language, EXPRESS, which is based on the ERA (entity-relationship-attribute) model.
