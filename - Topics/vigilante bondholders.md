## Source [^1]
- (bond vigilantes) Investors who react to government policies they believe likely to cause inflation, such as expansion of the money supply and high budget deficits, by selling government bonds and thereby pushing up bond yields. The term was widely used in the USA in the 1990s and revived after the crisis of 2008.
