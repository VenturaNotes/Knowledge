## Source [^1]
- An equitable legal remedy requiring the parties to carry out the contract they have entered into. This will be granted by a court only if damages are an inadequate remedy and if it is possible to perform the contract. It is the usual remedy in cases in which there is a breach of contract for the sale of land.
