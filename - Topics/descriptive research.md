## Source [^1]
- Marketing research aimed at providing good descriptions of marketing problems, situations, or markets. Descriptive research explores the market potential for a product, the demographics of a market, and the attitudes of consumers. Most marketing research in the UK is of this type.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Research that has no other purpose than to describe phenomena and is not intended to explain, predict, or control them. Compare ACTION RESEARCH, APPLIED RESEARCH, BASIC RESEARCH, EVALUATION RESEARCH, EXPLORATORY RESEARCH.
