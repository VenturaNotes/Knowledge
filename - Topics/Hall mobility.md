## Source [^1]
- Hall mobility (symbol: $\mu_{\mathrm{H}}$) A value for the drift mobility of charge carriers determined from measurements of the Hall effect. The Hall mobility is the product of the Hall coefficient, $R_{\mathrm{H}}$, and the conductivity, $\sigma$ (measured in the absence of a magnetic field):$$\mu_{\mathrm{H}} = R_{\mathrm{H}} \sigma$$Hall and drift mobilities are usually very nearly equal. Small differences arise due to the details of the carrier-scattering processes in the material.
