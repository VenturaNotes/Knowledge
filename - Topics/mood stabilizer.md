## Source [^1]
- a drug used in the treatment of bipolar affective disorder to reduce the severity of manic and depressive episodes. Mood stabilizers include lithium and antiepileptics. More recently antipsychotics have been marketed as mood stabilizers but evidence about their efficacy for this purpose is scarce and their clinical usefulness needs further evaluation.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Any drug, such as lithium carbonate, that is used in the treatment of bipolar disorders. Compare ANTIMANIC.
