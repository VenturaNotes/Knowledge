## Source [^1]
- $n$. a bone cell: an osteoblast that has ceased activity and has become embedded in the bone matrix.
## Source[^2]
- Any of the cells, found in bone, that perform the cellular activities, such as respiration and exchange of materials with the blood, that are required for maintenance of the bone tissue. They are derived from osteoblasts.
