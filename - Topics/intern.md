## Source [^1]
- $n$. in the USA and some other countries, a trainee doctor, serving in a hospital, who does not yet have a full licence to practice (compare RESIDENT). The UK equivalent is the so-called foundation doctor, a postgraduate who has embarked on the two-year Foundation Programme of training (see DOCTOR).
