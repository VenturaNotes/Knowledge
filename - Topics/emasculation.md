## Source [^1]
- $n$. strictly, surgical removal of the penis. The term is often used to mean loss of male physical and emotional characteristics, either as a result of removal of the testes (castration) or of emotional stress.
## Source[^2]
- (in plant breeding) The removal of the anthers of a flower in order to prevent self-pollination or the undesirable pollination of neighboring plants.
