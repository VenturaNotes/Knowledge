## Source [^1]
- An exogenous variable that is correlated with an endogenous variable used as an explanatory variable in a regression. Instrumental variables are used to obtain consistent estimators when an ordinary least squares estimator suffers from the endogeneity problem. See also PREDETERMINED VARIABLE; TWO-STAGE LEAST SQUARES.
