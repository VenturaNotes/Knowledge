## Source [^1]
- a surgical procedure used to treat dysfunction of the Eustachian tube. A small balloon is inserted from the nasopharynx into the Eustachian tube in a deflated state under endoscopic control. It is then inflated to widen the Eustachian tube before being deflated and removed.
