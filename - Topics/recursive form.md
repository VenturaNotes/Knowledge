## Synthesis
- Given the [[Fibonacci Numbers|Fibonacci sequence]]
	- Recursive Form
		- $F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2)$
	- Base Cases
		- $F(0) = 0,\quad F(1) = 1$
- A recursive formula does not always need two base cases. It mainly depends on how many previous terms it depends on
