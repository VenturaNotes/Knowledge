## Source [^1]
- In the UK, a special capital allowance against corporation tax that is granted in the year of purchase of an asset in place of the standard writing-down allowance of $25 \%$. Various first-year allowances, often targeted at smaller businesses wishing to invest in new technology, have been made available at various times. See also Annual Investment Allowance.
