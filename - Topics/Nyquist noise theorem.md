## Source [^1]
- The law relating the power $P$ dissipated in a resistor due to thermal noise with the frequency $f$ of the signal. At ordinary temperatures the law is expressed as:$$\mathrm {d} P = k T \mathrm {d} f$$where $k$ is the Boltzmann constant and $T$ the thermodynamic temperature.
