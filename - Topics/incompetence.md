## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) impaired function of the valves of the heart or veins, which allows backward leakage of blood. See AORTIC REGURGITATION; MITRAL REGURGITATION; VARICOSE VEINS. 
- (2) (incapacity) impairment of mental functioning such that a person is unable to understand, retain, and weigh up information so as to communicate a choice or preference. 
- (3) inability to fulfil the responsibilities and meet the standards of care expected of individuals working in a professional role in health care.
