## Source [^1]
- Any form of coordinated action in an industrial dispute by employees, with or without the support of a trade union, that seeks to force employers to agree to their demands relating to wages, terms of employment, working conditions, etc. It may take the form of a go slow, overtime ban, work-to-rule, sit-down strike, or strike.
