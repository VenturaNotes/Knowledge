## Source [^1]
- $n$. sexual attraction to children (of either sex) that may cause deviant behavior. Sexual activity with any children under the age of 16 is illegal in the UK for adults. Paedophiles who have been arrested may be forced to undergo treatment: behavior therapy can be used, although evidence of its efficacy is controversial. Occasionally, the offender's sexual drive can be reduced by drug treatment. —paedophile $n$. —paedophilic adj.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A paraphilia characterized by recurrent, intense sexual fantasies, urges, or behaviour involving sexual activity with prepubescent girls or boys. US pedophilia. See also PAEDOPHILIC DISORDER. Compare GERONTOPHILIA. paedophile $n$. A person who manifests paedophilia. US pedophile. \[From Greek pais, paidos a boy or child + philos loving, from phileein to love + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
