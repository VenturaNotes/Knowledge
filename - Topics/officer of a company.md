## Source [^1]
- A person who acts in an official capacity in a company. Company officers include the directors, managers, the company secretary, and in some circumstances the company's auditors and solicitors. The Companies Act empowers a court dealing with a liquidation to investigate the conduct of the company's officers with a view to recovering any money they may have obtained illegally or incorrectly.
