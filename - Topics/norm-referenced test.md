## Source [^1]
- $n$. A test that measures an attribute of a respondent relative to norms (2) derived from a standardization sample rather than by reference to any predefined absolute standard. Modern IQ tests are among the best known examples of norm-referenced tests. Compare CRITERION-REFERENCED TEST, DOMAIN-REFERENCED TEST.
