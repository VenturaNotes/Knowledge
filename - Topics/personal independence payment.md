## Source [^1]
- (PIP) a tax-free benefit replacing (from April 2013) disability living allowance (DLA) for people aged 16-64 (DLA is still paid to children). It has two components $\textendash$ a daily living component (including help with washing, dressing, using the toilet, preparing and eating food, and taking medicines) and a mobility component (for help with walking).
