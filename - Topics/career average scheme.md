## Source [^1]
- A form of defined benefit pension in which the pension is determined by the average salary during membership of the pension scheme. The annual pension on retirement is given by the formula 
	- Pension $=$ Average Salary $\times$ Accrual Rate $\times$ Years of Pensionable Service.
- See also ACCRUAL RATE; DEFINED CONTRIBUTION; FINAL SALARY SCHEME.
