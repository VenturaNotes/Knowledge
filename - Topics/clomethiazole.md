## Source [^1]
- (chlormethiazole) $n$. a hypnotic and sedative drug used to treat severe insomnia, agitation, and restlessness in the elderly, and alcohol withdrawal symptoms. The most common side-effects are tingling sensations in the nose and sneezing.
