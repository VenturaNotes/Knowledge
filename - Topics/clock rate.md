## Source [^1]
- Also known as clock speed
- In computing, it typically refers to the frequency at which the clock generator of a processor can generate pulses used to synchronize the operations of its components. 
- It is used as an indicator of the processor's speed
- Clock rate is measured in the SI unit of frequency hertz (Hz)
