## Source [^1]
- (services) Services paid for to one body, for example a UK local authority, but provided under contract by an outside supplier. This is contrasted with services provided by direct labour, employed by the authority. Examples of contracted-out services include refuse collection, office cleaning, and the provision of school meals.
