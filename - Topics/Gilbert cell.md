## Source [^1]
- A transistor circuit, devised by Barrie Gilbert, that is based on coupled long-tail pair circuits and performs four-quadrant multiplication of two input signals; it is used frequently as a mixer circuit, in heterodyne radio receivers for example (see diagram).
