## Source [^1]
- A civil wrong other than one relating to a contract. The law of tort is concerned with providing damages for personal injury and damage to property resulting from negligence. It is also concerned with protecting against defamation of character and preserving personal freedom, enjoyment of property, and commercial interests. A person who commits a tort is called a tortfeasor.
