## Synthesis
- A system that connects multiple devices within a residence, like computers, smartphones, tablets, and smart TVs, allowing them to communicate and share resources like internet access, files, and printers, usually facilitated by a [[modem]] and [[routers|router]] at the core of the network
## Source[^1]
- A home network or home area network is a type of computer network, specifically a type of local area network, that facilitates communication among devices within the close vicinity of a home.
## Source[^2] 
- A home network or home area network (HAN) is a type of [[LAN|local area network]] with the purpose to facilitate communication among digital devices present inside or within the close vicinity of a home
## Source[^3]
- If you only have one-device in your home network and your internet is working, that means you have a [[modem-router combo|modem-router combination device]].
