## Source [^1]
- a life-threatening syndrome seen after starting antipsychotic medication. It is characterized by confusion, muscle rigidity, fever, pallor and sweating, urinary incontinence, and a high level of creatine kinase. Its symptoms can appear similar to catatonia. Treatment in a high-dependency unit with high-dose benzodiazepines and immediate cessation of antipsychotic drugs is usually indicated.
