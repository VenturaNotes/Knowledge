## Source [^1]
- A process that results in an enhancement of the field strength at a given receiver due to the focusing that arises from either small-scale or large-scale curvature of the ionospheric layers. Ionospheric defocusing results from a reduction of the field strength at a receiver due to the defocusing that arises from the curvature of the layers.
