## Source [^1]
- The smaller firms that coexist with a dominant firm, or a small number of dominant firms, in an imperfectly competitive industry. The firms in the competitive fringe have no market power so take market price as given. The dominant firm does have market power, and takes the behavior of the competitive fringe into account when setting price.
