## Source [^1]
- $n$. an enzyme that catalyses the hydrolysis of compounds. Examples are the peptidases.
## Source[^2]
- Any of a class of enzymes that catalyze the addition of water to, or the removal of water from, a molecule. Hydrolases play an important role in the construction and breakdown of storage materials, such as starch.
