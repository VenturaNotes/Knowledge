## Source [^1]
- n. A breathing-related sleep disorder in DSM-5 involving sleep apnoea or hypopnoea in which the recurrent episodes of breathing cessation or diminution during sleep are associated with obstruction to the upper airway and are followed by loud snores or gasps as breathing resumes. Sleep is unrefreshing and the condition is accompanied by daytime sleepiness or hypersomnia. US obstructive sleep apnea hypopnea. Compare CENTRAL SLEEP APNOEA, PICKWICKIAN SYNDROME. OSAH abbrev.
