## Source [^1]
- A situation in which the futures price (or forward price) of a commodity is higher than the spot price. See also BACKWARDATION.
## Source[^2]
- (1) (forwardation) The situation in which (or the extent to which) the price for future delivery is greater than the spot price. Compare BACKWARDATION. 
- (2) The former practice of carrying the purchase of stocks and shares over from one account day on the London Stock Exchange to the next.
