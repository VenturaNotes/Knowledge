## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) An individual cut off from other members of a group or community. In a sociogram, an individual who is not chosen by any other group member. 
- (2) An organism or group of organisms separated from a specimen. \[From Latin insulatus made into an island, from insula an island]
