## Source [^1]
- A term coined by the US founder of Microsoft, Bill Gates (1955-), to refer to the enhanced efficiency of markets resulting from the widespread use of the Internet; this is held to have abolished such traditional market imperfections as physical distance, lack of information, lack of choice, and local regulation.
