## Source [^1]
- (auditory neuropathy, auditory dyssynchrony) a form of hearing loss characterized by normal cochlear function as measured by otoacoustic emissions or detection of cochlear microphonics but abnormal or absent middle ear reflexes and abnormal auditory brainstem responses.
