## Source [^1]
- $n$. A type of neuron in the lateral geniculate nucleus that responds maximally to light of one particular range of wavelengths and is maximally inhibited by light of a different range of wavelengths. Also called an opponent neuron. Compare NON-OPPONENT CELL. \[From Latin opponere to oppose, from $\text{ob}$ against + ponere to place]
