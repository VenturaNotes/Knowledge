## Source [^1]
- $n$. the layer of the eyeball between the retina and the sclera (see also BRUCH's MEMBRANE). It contains blood vessels and a pigment that absorbs excess light and so prevents blurring of vision. See EYE. $\textemdash$choroidal adj.
