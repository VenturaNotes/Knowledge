## Source [^1]
- A test used in marketing research to ascertain how much consumers can remember about an advertisement. Spontaneous tests reveal which advertisements a respondent can remember without guidance or assistance. Prompted (or aided) tests indicate which advertisements respondents can remember from a series they have seen in a campaign.
