## Source [^1]
- n. 
- (1) A generally accepted standard of behavior within a society, community, or group. See also social influence. 
- (2) A typical or most common value, pattern, state, condition, or event. In psychometrics, an average score on a test determined by applying the test to a standardization sample. See also standardized test. \[From Latin norma a carpenter's square or a rule]
