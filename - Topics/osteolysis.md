## Source [^1]
- (osteoclasia) $n$. dissolution of bone through disease, commonly by infection or by loss of the blood supply (ischaemia) to the bone. In acroosteolysis the terminal bones of the fingers or toes are affected: a common feature of some disorders involving blood vessels (including Raynaud's disease), scleroderma, and systemic lupus erythematosus.
