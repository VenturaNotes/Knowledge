## Source [^1]
- Evolution on a relatively large scale, involving, for example, the emergence of entire groups of organisms, such as the flowering plants or the mammals. Compare MICROEVOLUTION.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A relatively slow change brought about by natural selection, leading to the emergence of a genus, family (2), order (3), or class (2) of organisms. Compare microevolution. \[From Greek makros large + English evolution]
