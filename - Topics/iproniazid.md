## Source [^1]
- $n$. An antidepressant drug, the first of the monoamine oxidase inhibitors (MAOIs), originally developed in 1951 for the treatment of tuberculosis. Also called Marsilid (trademark). \[From i(so)niazid an earlier compound used for treating tuberculosis + pro(pyl) indicating a compound containing the monovalent group $\mathrm{C}_{3} \mathrm{H}_{7}$]
