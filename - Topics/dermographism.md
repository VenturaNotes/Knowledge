## Source [^1]
- $n$. a common local reaction caused by stroking the skin, which develops itchy weals. People with such highly sensitive skin can 'write' on it with a finger or blunt instrument. A rare delayed form exists. Dermographism can be confirmed with a calibrated instrument called a dermographometer. Antihistamines are first-line treatment.
