## Source [^1]
- A rare mineral form of sodium aluminofluoride, $Na_3AlF_6$, which crystallizes in the monoclinic system. It is usually white but may also be colorless. The only important occurrence of the mineral is in Greenland. It is used chiefly to lower the melting point of alumina in the production of aluminium.
