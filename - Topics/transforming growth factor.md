## Source [^1]
- (TGF) a protein $\textendash$ a cytokine $\textendash$ that controls growth, proliferation, and other functions in most cells. It plays a role in asthma and other respiratory diseases and is also involved in diseases elsewhere in the body. There are two classes: TGF- $\alpha$ and TGF-$\beta$.
