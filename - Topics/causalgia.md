## Source [^1]
- $n$. an intensely unpleasant burning pain felt in a limb where there has been partial damage to the sympathetic and somatic sensory nerves.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A condition in which burning pain is experienced along the course of a peripheral nerve without the application of heat. Also called thermalgia. \[From Greek kausos fever + algos pain + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
