## Source [^1]
- $n$. a colloidal suspension that has set to form a jelly. Some insoluble drugs are administered in the form of gels.
## Source[^2]
- A lyophilic colloid that has coagulated to a rigid or jelly-like solid. In a gel, the disperse medium has formed a loosely held network of linked molecules through the dispersion medium. Examples of gels are silica gel and gelatin.
