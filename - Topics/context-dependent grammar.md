## Source [^1]
- $n$. A type of generative grammar in which a rule can be conditional on the linguistic context$\textemdash$the linguistic units adjacent or nearby those to which the rule applies directly. For example, Rewrite $X$ as $X+Y$ in the context of $Z$ is a typical context-dependent rule in such a grammar. Also called a context-sensitive grammar. Compare CONTEXT-FREE GRAMMAR.
