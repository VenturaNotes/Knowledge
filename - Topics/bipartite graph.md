## Source [^1]
- A graph $G$ whose vertices can be split into two disjoint sets, $U$ and $V$, in such a way that the only edges of $G$ join a vertex in $U$ and a vertex in $V$. Bipartite graphs tend to provide a convenient graphical representation of relations and therefore functions.
