## Source [^1]
- A work measurement technique for adjusting data from a series of time studies against a common level of performance, corresponding to standard performance.
## Source[^2]
- Stipulating or the stipulation of operating conditions for a machine, transformer, or other device or circuit and stating the performance limitations of such equipment. Rating is carried out by the manufacturer of such equipment. The designated limits to the operating conditions within which the device or equipment functions satisfactorily are the rated conditions (current, load, voltage, etc.). If the rated conditions are not adhered to the device is likely not to produce its rated performance.
