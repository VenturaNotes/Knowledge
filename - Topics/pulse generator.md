## Source [^1]
- A circuit or instrument that generates a sequence of pulses, usually (but not necessarily) of uniform height, width, and pulse repetition frequency.
## Source[^2]
- An electronic circuit or device that generates voltage or current pulses of a desired waveform. It can be designed to produce either single pulses or a pulse train. A pulser is a particular type of pulse generator that produces fast (short-duration) pulses of high voltage.
