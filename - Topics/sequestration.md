## Source [^1]
- The confiscation of the property of a person or organization who has not complied with a court order. This is a very drastic remedy, and will be ordered in serious cases only. It is most commonly used against companies, trade unions, and in matrimonial cases.
## Source[^2]
- (1) A UK procedure by which assets can be temporarily frozen by court order.
- (2) The US term for mandatory spending cuts in the budget proposed under the Gramm-Rudman-Hollings law on the deficit.
- (3) See CARBON SEQUESTRATION.
