## Source [^1]
- (thermalgesia) $n$. an abnormal sense of pain that is felt when part of the body is warmed. It is a type of dysaesthesia and is a symptom of partial damage to a peripheral nerve or to the fibre tracts conducting temperature sensation to the brain.
