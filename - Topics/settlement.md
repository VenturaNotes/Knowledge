## Source [^1]
- (1) The payment of an outstanding account, invoice, charge, etc. 
- (2) A disposition of land, or other property, made by deed or will under which a trust is set up by the settlor. The settlement names the beneficiaries and the terms under which they are to acquire the property. 
- (3) The document in which such a disposition is made. 
- (4) The voluntary conclusion of civil litigation or an industrial dispute, as a result of agreement between the parties.
