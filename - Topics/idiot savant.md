## Source [^1]
- $n$. A person with intellectual disability who can perform at a high level in some restricted domain of intellectual functioning, such as memorizing vast bodies of information, musical or artistic performance, or calendar calculating$\textemdash$naming the day of the week for a specified date in the past or future. It is not unusual for an idiot savant to suffer from autism spectrum disorder. Also called a savant. idiots savants pl. \[From French idiot an idiot + savant wise or knowledgeable]
