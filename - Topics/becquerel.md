## Source [^1]
- $n$. the SI unit of activity of a radioactive source, being the activity of a radionuclide decaying at a rate of one spontaneous nuclear transition per second. It has replaced the curie. Symbol: Bq.
## Source[^2]
- Symbol Bq. The SI unit of activity (see radiation units). The unit is named after the discoverer of radioactivity, A. H. Becquerel (1852-1908).
