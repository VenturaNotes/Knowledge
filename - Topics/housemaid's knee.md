## Source [^1]
- (prepatellar bursitis) inflammation and resultant swelling of the bursa in front of the kneecap, usually due to repetitive friction and pressure over the kneecap, as from frequent episodes of prolonged kneeling. Treatment includes pressure bandaging, NSAIDs, and avoidance of kneeling. See BURSITIS.
