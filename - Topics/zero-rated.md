## Source [^1]
- (VAT) Goods or services included in the value-added tax system, with a value-added tax (VAT) rate of zero. Firms can reclaim VAT on inputs to zero-rated goods. These are distinguished from VAT-exempt goods and services which are outside the VAT system, so that VAT on inputs cannot be reclaimed.
