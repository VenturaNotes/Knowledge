## Synthesis
- Number of goods and services produced and sold at a given market price
## Source [^1]
- The number of goods or services that suppliers will produce and sell at a given market price
## Source[^2]
- The number of units of a good or service that producers are willing and able to sell
## Source[^3]
- The quantity of a good supplied at a given price. The quantity supplied traces out the supply curve as the given price is changed.
