## Source [^1]
- $n$. Substance-related and addictive disorders associated specifically with amphetamine and amphetamine-like substances such as dextroamphetamine and methamphetamine (speed). Included among these disorders are amphetamine misuse, amphetamine dependence, and a range of amphetamine-induced disorders. See also SUBSTANCE-INDUCED DISORDERS.
