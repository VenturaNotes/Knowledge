## Source [^1]
- an element that is required in minute concentrations for normal growth and development; the body contains a total of $<5 \mathrm{~g}$ of the element. Trace elements include fluorine (see FLUORIDE), manganese, zinc, copper, iodine, cobalt, selenium, molybdenum, chromium, and silicon. They may serve as cofactors or as constituents of complex molecules (e.g. cobalt in vitamin $\mathrm{B}_{12}$ ).
