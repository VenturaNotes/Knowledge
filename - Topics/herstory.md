## Source [^1]
- $n$. Feminist history, or history viewed from a female or feminine perspective. The word was coined by militant feminists in the US in 1970, although the standard word history is not, in fact, a compound of his + story but derives from the Greek and Latin historia, meaning narrative. See also POLITICAL CORRECTNESS, PSEUDOMORPHEME. herstorian $n$. A writer of herstory. \[Coined in 1970 by the US poet and radical feminist Robin Morgan (born 1941) in her book Sisterhood is Powerful as a deliberate folk etymology (1), from her + story, intended to resemble history]
