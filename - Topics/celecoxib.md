## Source [^1]
- $n$. an anti-inflammatory drug (see NSAID) that selectively inhibits cyclo-oxygenase 2 (see COX-2 inhibitor). It is used in the treatment of osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and ankylosing spondylitis.
