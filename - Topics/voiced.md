## Source [^1]
- $(\mathbf{V}^{+})$ Denoting a speech sound, such as all the sounds in 'does', 'love', and 'judge', that involves vibrating vocal folds. The time waveform of a voiced sound is quasi-periodic, and the sound has a pitch associated with it. Compare VOICELESS.
