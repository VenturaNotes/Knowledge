## Source [^1]
- (polycrystalline silicon) Silicon in polycrystalline form is most often used to form the gate electrodes in silicon-gate MOS integrated circuits and charge-coupled devices. In this application the silicon is doped with a sufficiently high doping concentration so that it becomes degenerate and exhibits metallic properties.
