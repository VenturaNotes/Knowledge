## Source [^1]
- (e-shopping) The buying of goods or services over the Internet. Payment is generally by credit card or PayPal, although various types of electronic cash have been devised (see BITCOIN). Delivery may be by shipping to a designated address, downloading of digital products (e.g. music files or computer software), printing out of coupons etc., or customer pick-up (e.g. of theatre or concert tickets at the box office; see also CLICK AND COLLECT).
