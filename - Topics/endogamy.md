## Source [^1]
- The fusion of reproductive cells from closely related parents, i.e. inbreeding. Compare EXOGAMY.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. The cultural practice or custom of marriage between people within the same clan, tribe, or other group of related individuals; more generally, the fusion of gametes from closely related parents. Compare EXOGAMY. \[From Greek endon within + gameein to marry]
