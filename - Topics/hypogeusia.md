## Source [^1]
- $n$. a condition in which the sense of taste is abnormally weak. See also HYPOAESTHESIA.
## Source[^2]
- n. Abnormally diminished taste sensitivity. Compare AGEUSIA, CACOGEUSIA, DYSGEUSIA, HYPERGEUSIA, PARAGEUSIA, TASTE BLINDNESS. hypogeusic adj. \[From Greek hypo under + geusis taste + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
