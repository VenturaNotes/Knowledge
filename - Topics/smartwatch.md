## Source [^1]
- A watch that has a screen instead of a dial and various computing facilities; typically it connects to the Internet, either directly or indirectly via a wireless connection to a smartphone. Many smartwatches are fitted with sensors for pulse and gait, and are marketed as fitness aids and monitors. These are an example of the emerging trend for wearable technology.
