## Source [^1]
- $n$. Diminished sensation of pain during exposure to loud sounds; also, the use of music to distract a person's attention from pain, as during dentistry. See also analgesia. \[From Latin audio I hear, from audire to hear + Greek an- without + algos pain + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
