## Source [^1]
- $n$. A culture-bound syndrome included in DSM-5, found among Haitian communities, involving a belief that illnesses such as depression or psychosis, or that academic failure or problems of daily living, especially when these have followed economic, occupational, or other successes or gains, have been caused by the envy and hatred of enemies. Compare mal de ojo. \[From Haitian creole maladi moun humanly caused illness]
