## Source [^1]
- A document issued by HM Revenue and Customs as part of the PAYE system showing the amount of income tax allowances to be set against an individual's taxable pay. The total of allowances is reduced to a code by dropping the last digit and adding a letter depending on the taxpayer's circumstances.
