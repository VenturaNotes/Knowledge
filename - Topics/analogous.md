## Source [^1]
- adj. 
- (1) describing organs or parts that have similar functions in different organisms although they do not have the same evolutionary origin or development. Compare HOMOLOGous 
- (2) (in ethics) describing a case, justification, or argument that is similar to the case in question and so may be used to help to reach a conclusion. See also ANALYSIS; CASUISTRY.
