## Source [^1]
- adj. Of or relating to an adjective that is non-self-descriptive, such as the adjective long (which is not long), Russian (which is not Russian), and monosyllabic (which is not monosyllabic). See also Grelling's paradox. Compare autological. \[From Greek heteros other + logos a word + Latin -icalis of, relating to, or resembling]
