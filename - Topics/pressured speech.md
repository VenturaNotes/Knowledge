## Source [^1]
- $n$. Continuous, excessive, rapid, loud, and sometimes incoherent speech that is difficult to interrupt and may continue even when no one is listening, often seen in manic episodes and other mental disorders. Also called pressure of speech. See also LOGORRHOEA.
