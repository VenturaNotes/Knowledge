## Synthesis
- Pronounced (hydroxy - apatite)
- Mineral crystals composed of calcium and phosphate that form essential component of the bone matrix.
	- Primary mineral component responsible for hardness and strength of bones and teeth
- Creates a [[crystalline structure]]
## Source [^1]
- Chemical Formula: $Ca_{10}(PO_4)_6(OH)_2$
