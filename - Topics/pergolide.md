## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug that stimulates dopamine receptors in the brain and is now rarely used in the treatment of Parkinson's disease. Possible sideeffects include confusion, hallucinations, sleepiness, heart irregularity, nausea, breathing difficulties, and double vision (see also BROMOCRIPTINE).
