## Source [^1]
- $n$. One of the sexual dysfunctions in DSM-5, characterized by persistent or recurrent absence or deficiency of sexual fantasy and desire in men, for at least six months, causing significant distress or problems of interpersonal interaction. It is not diagnosed if it is attributable to a drug or another medical condition. Compare PERSISTENT SEXUAL AROUSAL SYNDROME. \[From Greek hypo under + Latin activus in a state of action, from actus a doing + -ivus tending]
