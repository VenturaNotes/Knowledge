## Source [^1]
- When two quantities X and x are approximately equal, written $X \approx x$, one of them may be used in suitable circumstances in place of, or as an approximation for, the other. For example, $\pi \approx \frac {22}{7}$ and $\sqrt{2} \approx 1.414$
