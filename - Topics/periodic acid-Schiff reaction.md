## Source [^1]
- (PAS reaction) a test for the presence of glycoproteins, polysaccharides, certain mucopolysaccharides, glycolipids, and certain fatty acids in tissue sections. The tissue is treated with periodic acid, followed by Schiff's reagent. A positive reaction is the development of a red or magenta coloration.
