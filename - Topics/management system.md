## Source [^1]
- A computer application that enables the owner of a franchise network to access and coordinate information about the business activities of the franchisees. Such systems provide data about marketing, salesforce activity, customer relationships, and royalties across the network and thereby allow analysis of the operations of the business as a whole and comparison of individual units.
