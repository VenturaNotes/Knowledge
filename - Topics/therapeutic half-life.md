## Source [^1]
- (in pharmacology) The time taken for half the dose of a drug to be excreted: used to calculate the most effective and nontoxic dosing intervals. It can be determined by administering a therapeutic dose of the drug labelled with a radioisotope (see LABELLING) and measuring the time for half of it to be excreted in the urine.
