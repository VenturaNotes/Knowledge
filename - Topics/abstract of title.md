## Source [^1]
- A document used in conveyancing land to show how the vendor derived good title. For registered land, the abstract of title consists of official copies of the entries in the register. For unregistered land, the abstract generally comprises duplicate copies of those documents required to trace ownership back to a document that is at least 15 years old and that is held to show good title.
