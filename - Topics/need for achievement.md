## Source [^1]
- $n$. A social form of motivation involving a competitive drive to meet standards of excellence, traditionally measured with a projective test such as the TAT. The concept was introduced by the US psychologist Henry Alexander Murray (1893-1988) in his book Explorations in Personality (1938). Also called achievement motivation. N Ach or nAch abbrev.
