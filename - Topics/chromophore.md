## Source [^1]
- Any group of atoms in a molecule that includes an unsaturated chemical group (such as $\mathrm{C}\text{=}\mathrm{C}$ ) capable of absorbing ultraviolet radiation; the chromophore is responsible for the color of the compound. For example, retinal is the chromophore of rhodopsin, the light-sensitive pigment in the rod cells of the retina.
