## Source [^1]
- (EoP) The use of electrical power wiring to transmit digital data, rather than a dedicated Ethernet cable. Special plug adapters are used that have a socket for a standard Ethernet cable, which is used to connect to devices such as a smart TV or a broadband router. These devices can then communicate over the power wiring in the building.
