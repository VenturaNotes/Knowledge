## Source [^1]
- (1) The financial institution, usually a merchant bank or investment bank, that handles the flotation of a company. It will supervise the preparation of the prospectus and make sure that the company is aware of the benefits and obligations of being a public company. 
- (2) The lender in a project financing agreement.
