## Source [^1]
- $n$. mottled purplish discoloration on the legs and thighs, usually of adolescent girls or obese boys before puberty. The disorder sometimes occurs in older women and is worse in cold weather. Weight loss is the best treatment because it reduces the insulating effect of a thick layer of fat.
