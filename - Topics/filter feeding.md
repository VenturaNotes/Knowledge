## Source [^1]
- A method of feeding in which tiny food particles are strained from the surrounding water by various mechanisms. It is used by many aquatic invertebrates, especially members of the plankton, and by some vertebrates, notably baleen whales. See also CILIARY FEEDING; WHALEBONE.
