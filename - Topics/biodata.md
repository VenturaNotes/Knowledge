## Source [^1]
- In personnel selection, items of biographical information about the candidates for a job that can be objectively assessed, e.g. qualifications, years of experience, positions of responsibility, etc. Each candidate is scored on the answers given and those whose total scores fall below a certain level are eliminated. This can be an effective way of selecting a short list. See STANDARD APPLICATION BLANK.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. 
- (1) Biographical information, such as the personal details about research participants or subjects recorded by researchers, or similar information recorded by personnel officers about employees. 
- (2) Sometimes, especially in Indian English usage, a curriculum vitae. \[From bio(graphical) data, from Greek bios life + graphein to write, + Latin data things given, from dare to give]
