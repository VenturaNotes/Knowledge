## Source [^1]
- A method of running a computing facility such that the design, development, writing, and testing of computer programs is carried out by the problem originator and not by specialist computing staff. Open shop operation is the operation of a computing system by the writer or user of a program and not by specialist computer operators. Compare CLOSED SHOP.
