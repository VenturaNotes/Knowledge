## Source [^1]
- $n$. either the maxilla (upper jaw) or the mandible (lower jaw). The jaws form the framework of the mouth and provide attachment for the teeth.
## Source[^2]
- The part of the vertebrate skeleton that provides a support for the mouth and holds the teeth. It consists of the upper jaw (maxilla) and the lower jaw (mandible; see also DENTARY). Agnathans lack jaws.
