## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) The act or experience of hearing. 
- (2) A trial performance by a musician or other performing artist applying to join a group, gain a place on a course, or take part in a performance. \[From Latin auditio, -onis a hearing, from audire to hear + -ion indicating an action, process, or state]
