## Source [^1]
- $n$. inflammation of the ureter. This usually occurs in association with inflammation of the bladder (see CYSTITIS), particularly if caused by vesicoureteric reflux. Tuberculosis of the urinary tract can also cause ureteritis, which progresses to stricture formation.
