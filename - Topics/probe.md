## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) a thin rod of pliable metal, such as silver, with a blunt end. The instrument is used for exploring cavities, wounds, fistulae, and sinus channels. 
- (2) (dental probe) a hand instrument designed for specific exploratory examination procedures (e.g. for dental caries, endodontics, or periodontics). 
- (3) (ultrasound probe) see ULTRASOUND; TRANSDUCER. 
- (4) (gene probe) a radioactively labelled cloned section of DNA that is used to detect identical sections of nucleic acid by means of pairing between complementary bases. See Northern blot analysis; Southern blot ANALYSIS.
