## Source [^1]
- (LFO) An oscillator found in musical synthesizers operating at frequencies up to approximately 20 hertz. It is used to modulate the fundamental frequency or amplitude of the output sound, referred to as ‘vibrato’ or ‘tremolo’ respectively.
