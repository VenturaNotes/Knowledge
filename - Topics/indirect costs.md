## Source [^1]
- (indirect expenses) Expenses that cannot be traced directly to a product or cost unit and are therefore overheads (compare DIRECT COSTS). Some indirect product costs may, however, be regarded as cost center direct costs; the indirect cost center costs are usually those costs requiring apportionment to cost centers in an absorption costing system.
