## Source [^1]
- A measure of how visible a target is to radar, expressed as an area. It is proportional to the cross-sectional area of the target as seen from the radar position, adjusted by factors that account for the reflectivity and scattering properties of the target. It is very hard to calculate with any accuracy and so is usually determined by experimental measurement.
