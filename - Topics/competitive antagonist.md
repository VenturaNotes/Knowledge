## Source [^1]
- $n$. An antagonist (3) drug that interacts with a class of neuroreceptors, without itself eliciting any response, by producing a change in the neuroreceptors that prevents the agonist (3) from acting on them. Also called a receptor blocker . Compare NON-COMPETITIVE ANTAGONIST.
