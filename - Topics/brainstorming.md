## Source [^1]
- A group discussion in order to invoke ideas and solve business problems. No idea is rejected, no matter how irrelevant it appears, until it has been thoroughly discussed and evaluated. A major rule of brainstorming is that the discussion of ideas should not be inhibited. Compare DELPHI TECHNIQUE.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. A method of generating ideas and solving problems through the encouragement of intensive spontaneous group discussion.
