## Source [^1]
- $n$. the fluid between the bony and membranous labyrinths of the ear.
## Source[^2]
- The fluid of the inner ear that fills the space between the bony labyrinth and the membranous labyrinth. See also COChLeA. Compare ENDOLYMPH.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. The fluid contained within the scala tympani and the scala vestibuli in the space between the membranous labyrinth and bony labyrinth of the inner ear. Compare endolymph. \[From Greek peri around + Latin lympha water, alluding to the fact that it lies outside the membranous labyrinth, unlike endolymph]
