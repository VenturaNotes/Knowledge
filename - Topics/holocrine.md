## Source [^1]
- adj. describing a gland or type of secretion in which the entire cell disintegrates when the product is released.
## Source[^2]
- adj. Of or relating to a gland, such as the sebaceous gland, whose secretions contain the whole of the secreting cell. Compare APOCRINE, MEROCRINE. \[From Greek holos whole + krinein to separate]
