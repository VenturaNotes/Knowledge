## Source [^1]
- (eformoterol) $n$. a sympathomimetic drug (a $\beta_{2}$ agonist) used, with inhaled corticosteroids (see also BUDESONIDE), as a long-acting bronchodilator to control chronic asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. Side-effects include tremor, palpitations, and headache.
