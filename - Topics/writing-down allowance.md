## Source [^1]
- (WDA) A capital allowance available to a UK trader. Any additions to plant and machinery are added to the written-down value of assets acquired in previous years and the writing-down allowance is calculated as $18 \%$ of the total. For certain long-life assets it is calculated at $8 \%$ in the same way. See also Annual Investment Allowance.
