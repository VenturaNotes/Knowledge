## Source [^1]
- $n$. The quality of a food or drink perceived in the mouth, determined by a combination of sensory effects including its taste, odor, texture, temperature, color, irritability, and sound (when chewed). US flavor. See also taste. \[From Old French flaour flavour, probably influenced by savour taste, from Latin sapor taste, from sapere to taste]
