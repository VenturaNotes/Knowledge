## Source [^1]
- The provision of risk capital by one company, either directly or by means of a venture-capital fund, for another company. This may be undertaken as a means of obtaining information about the company requiring venture capital or its market, of taking a first step towards acquiring the company, or of moving into a new market cheaply and without needing to acquire the relevant expertise and personnel.
