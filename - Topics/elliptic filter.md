## Source [^1]
- (Cauer filter) A filter that is characterized by a magnitude response with equal ripples (equiripple) in both the pass-band and stop-band, producing a filter with a steeper roll-off or narrower transition band compared to Chebyshev and Butterworth filters. See also ELLIPTIC FUNCTION.
