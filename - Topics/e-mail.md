## Source [^1]
- (electronic mail) Software that enables messages (e.g. letters, memos, documents) between individuals or from individuals to groups to be exchanged by computer. Its operation can be restricted to a local area network or it can be open to a wide area network, such as the Internet. Many systems now support the delivery of multimedia mail, which can combine text, graphics, voice, and other forms of information in a single message.
