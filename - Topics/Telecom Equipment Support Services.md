## Source [^1]
- The phrase telecom equipment support services refers to any telecoms-based service that provides communications assistance to businesses. Telecoms include landlines, cell phones, portable radios, and internet services. VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) is an example of internet-based telecoms. This is a service that uses a wired or wireless internet connection to initiate a voice call.
