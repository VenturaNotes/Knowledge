## Source [^1]
- n. a genus of tropical and subtropical fleas, with some 40 species. The rat flea, $X$. cheopis, occasionally attacks humans and can transmit plague from an infected rat population; it also transmits murine typhus and two tapeworms, Hymenolepis nana and H. diminuta.
