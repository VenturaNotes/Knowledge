## Source [^1]
- (in electrocardiography) the superimposition of an R wave (ventricular depolarization) on the T wave (ventricular repolarization) of the preceding heartbeat. It may trigger ventricular fibrillation. See QRS COMPLEX; Q-T INTERVAL; S-T SEGMENT.
