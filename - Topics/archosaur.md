## Source [^1]
- Any member of a clade of reptiles including the crocodilians, pterosaurs, dinosaurs, and birds. They are distinguished from other amniote vertebrates by having a single opening in the skull behind each eye socket, a high narrow skull with pointed snout, and teeth set in sockets.
