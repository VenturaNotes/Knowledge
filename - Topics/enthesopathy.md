## Source [^1]
- $n$. any rheumatic disease resulting in inflammation of entheses. Ankylosing spondylitis, psoriatic arthritis, and reactive arthritis are examples. In these conditions the Achilles tendon is commonly involved, with swelling at its insertion on the calcaneus. Calcification within the tendon insertion may be seen on X-ray in patients with longstanding enthesopathy.
