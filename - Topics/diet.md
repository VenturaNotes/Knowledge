## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) the mixture of foods that a person eats. A balanced diet contains the correct proportions of all the nutrients, i.e. vitamins, minerals (calcium, phosphorus, potassium, sodium, chlorine, sulphur, magnesium, the trace elements), and dietary fibre, as well as water, carbohydrates and fats (which provide energy), and proteins (required for growth and maintenance). 
- (2) a restrictive regime of food intake used by an individual to lose weight.
