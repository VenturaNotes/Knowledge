## Source [^1]
- $n$. the removal of the whole or part of the vitreous humour of the eye. Anterior vitrectomy is removal of the front portion of the vitreous humour. Vitrectomy is often required to remove a nonresolving vitreous haemorrhage and also as a part of surgical procedures on the retina, including repair of a detached retina.
