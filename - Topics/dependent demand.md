## Source [^1]
- The situation arising when the demand for one product is derived from or depends on the demand for other items. If a car manufacturer, for example, knows how many vehicles to produce in a month, this will fix how many engines, wheels, doors, seats, etc., have to be ordered. Compare INDEPENDENT DEMAND.
