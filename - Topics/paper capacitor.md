## Source [^1]
- A capacitor of medium loss and medium capacitance-stability that is used in high-voltage a.c. and d.c. applications. These capacitors are manufactured by winding together aluminium foils interleaved with layers of tissue paper. The moisture content of the paper is removed by impregnation with a suitable oil or wax. Metallized paper capacitors use an evaporated metal film as the electrode instead of aluminium.
