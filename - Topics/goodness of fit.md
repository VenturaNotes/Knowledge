## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) In statistics, the closeness of agreement between observed and expected frequencies, usually measured by the chi-square test or the Kolmogorov-Smirnov test. 
- (2) In fitting a line or a curve to a set of data points, the degree of closeness of the points to the curve, often measured by the least-squares method or maximum likelihood method.
