## Source [^1]
- (SSAP) Any of a series of accounting standards issued by the Accounting Standards Committee between 1971 and 1990. Many were subsequently replaced by the later Financial Reporting Standards and all have now been superseded by the Financial Reporting Standard Applicable in the UK and Republic of Ireland.
