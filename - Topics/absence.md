## Source [^1]
- $n$. The state or condition of being away from a place. In abnormal psychology and neurology, a brief period of loss of attention or consciousness, often followed by amnesia for the period of absence, characteristic of minor (formerly called petit mal) epilepsy. Compare TONIC-CLONIC.
