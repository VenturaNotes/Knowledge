## Source [^1]
- The time and date of an operation or event when automatically added to a screen display, log file, or output file of a computer procedure. It is a valuable aid to the tracking down of errors and can be used as part of an auditing process. The time and date are derived from the computer's internal real-time clock.
## Source[^2]
- A field added to a communications packet that provides information about when the packet was transmitted. It provides information to the receiver about the current delays in the channel, which is important in the transmission of streaming audio and video over a packet-switched network. See PACKET SWITCHING.
