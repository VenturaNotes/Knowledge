## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any systematic distortion in the assignment of causes to behavior or in inferring the causes of behavior. The most important ones so far discovered are the actor-observer difference, base-rate fallacy, fundamental attribution error, positivity bias (1), and self-serving bias. See also attribution, attribution theory.
