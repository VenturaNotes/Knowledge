## Source [^1]
- (survey under the guise of research) The unethical practice of deliberate deception by telemarketers masquerading as market researchers. Respondents are often invited to take part in a survey as a leadin to a sales presentation or as an attempt to get information that could be used for sales leads or mailing lists. Compare FRUGGING.
