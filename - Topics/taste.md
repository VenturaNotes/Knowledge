## Source [^1]
- $n$. the sense for the appreciation of the flavor of substances in the mouth. The sense organs responsible are the taste buds on the surface of the tongue, which are stimulated when food dissolves in the saliva in the mouth. It is generally held that there are four basic taste sensations $\textendash$ sweet, bitter, sour, and salt $\textendash$ but two others $\textendash$ alkaline and metallic $\textendash$ are sometimes added to this list.
