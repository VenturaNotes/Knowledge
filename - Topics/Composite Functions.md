### Example
- Given $f(x) = \frac{x}{x+3}$ and $g(x) = \frac{2}{x}$, find the [[Domain (Math)|domain]] of (f $\circ$ g)(x)
	- $(f \circ g)(x) = f(g(x))$
		- Domain of $g(x)$ is $x \ne 0$
		- Domain of $f(g(x))$ = $\frac {2}{3x+2}$ is $x \ne \frac {-2}{3}$
			- $\therefore D = \{x | x \ne 0, x \ne \frac {-2}{3}\}$
