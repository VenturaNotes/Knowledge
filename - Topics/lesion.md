## Source [^1]
- $n$. a zone of tissue with impaired function as a result of damage by disease or wounding. Apart from direct physical injury, examples of primary lesions include abscesses, ulcers, and tumors; secondary lesions (such as crusts and scars) are derived from primary ones.
