## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any drug or treatment that improves an athlete's level of achievement. Among the drugs that have been used for this purpose are anabolic steroids, creatine, erythropoietin, testosterone, growth hormone, stimulant drugs, beta-blockers, and smart drugs. Also called a performance-enhancing drug.
