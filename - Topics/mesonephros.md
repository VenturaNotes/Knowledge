## Source [^1]
- (Wolffian body) $n$. the second area of kidney tissue to develop in the embryo. Its excretory function only lasts for a very brief period before it degenerates. However, parts of it become incorporated into the male reproductive structures. Its duct $\textendash$ the Wolffian (or mesonephric) duct $\textendash$ persists in males as the epididymis and vas deferens, which conduct sperm from the testis. $\textemdash$mesonephric adj.
