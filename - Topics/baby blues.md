## Source [^1]
- (postpartum blues) a colloquial name for the brief episodes of low mood, tearfulness, poor sleep, and irritability that affect up to half of all women during the first week after delivery. Reassurance and support are the mainstay of management. Compare POSTNATAL DEPRESSION; PUERPERAL PSYCHOSIS.
