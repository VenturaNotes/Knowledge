## Source [^1]
- Investors who acquire an equity stake in a publicly traded company as a means of attempting to influence the company's practices or policies. Shareholders can be ethically motivated, such as wanting an improvement in the environmental or social impact of a business, or interested mainly in changing its business strategy or management.
