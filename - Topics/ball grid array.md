## Source [^1]
- (BGA) An integrated circuit package in which all the leads are brought out on the underside in a regular array grid and connections are made using surface mount technology, with small balls of solder at each array position. BGAs are particularly suited to chips with more than 100 input/output pins. The shorter connections result in better performance, as there is less inductance and heat dissipation than with other package types.
