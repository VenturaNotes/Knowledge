## Source [^1]
- The cytoplasm of a muscle fiber. Sarcoplasm contains chemicals that are required for muscle contraction, including glycogen, ATP, and phosphocreatine. In addition the sarcoplasm of active muscles tends to be rich in mitochondria.
## Source[^2]
- (myoplasm) $n$. the cytoplasm of muscle cells.
