## Source [^1]
- a group of inherited conditions transmitted through mitochondrial DNA (see MITOCHONDRION), which can affect any organ and can present at any age. Most of these conditions are very rare; examples of those that are less rare include Leigh syndrome, congenital lactic acidosis, MELAS, and Pearson syndrome.
