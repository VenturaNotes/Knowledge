## Source [^1]
- A semiconductor nanocrystal that is small enough for its properties to be dominated by quantum-mechanical effects and to be influenced strongly by parameters such as shape and size. Quantum dots are capable of emitting narrowband light at specific, tunable centre frequencies and hence are well suited to flatscreen display applications in which they can potentially offer both increased brightness and a wider range of colours. They are also capable of absorbing light of specific frequencies and are being investigated as a possible photovoltaic for use in solar cells.
