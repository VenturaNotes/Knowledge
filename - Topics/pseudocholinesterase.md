## Source [^1]
- $n$. an enzyme found in the blood and other tissues that $\textendash$ like cholinesterase $\textendash$ breaks down acetylcholine, but much more slowly. Not being localized at nerve endings, it plays little part in the normal breakdown of acetylcholine in synapses and at neuromuscular junctions.
