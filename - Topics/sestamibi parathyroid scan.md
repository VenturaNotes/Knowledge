## Source [^1]
- (in nuclear medicine) a scan that can help to localize the site of a parathyroid adenoma before surgical removal, to treat primary hyperparathyroidism. The tracer is technetium-99m-labelled sestamibi (a small protein), which is selectively absorbed by overactive parathyroid glands.
