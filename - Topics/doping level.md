## Source [^1]
- The amount of doping necessary to achieve the desired characteristic in a semiconductor. Low doping levels (p, n) give a high-resistivity material; high doping levels ($p^+$, $n^+$) give a low-resistivity material. See also SEMICONDUCTOR.
