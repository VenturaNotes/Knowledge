## Source [^1]
- (symbol: Al) A metal, atomic number 13, that is extensively used in electronic equipment, including power transmission lines, heat sinks, and optical disk layers. It is a good conductor and is ductile, malleable, resistant to corrosion, lightweight, easily evaporated onto surfaces, and abundant.
