## Source [^1]
- (AVC) Additional pensionscheme contributions that employees can make, at their discretion, in order to increase the benefits available from their pension fund on retirement. Additional voluntary contributions can be paid into an employers' scheme or to a scheme of the employee's choice (a free-standing AVC).
