## Source [^1]
- A social profile refers to an individual's online persona that is presented when using an online tool. The social profile is developed by analyzing the information that they share on a variety of social media platforms and then highlighting this information in a summarized manner. Some of the most common information shared on social profiles include things such as geolocation and professional affiliations.
