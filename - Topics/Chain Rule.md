## Source[^1]
### Description
- Used to calculate the derivative of a composite function

## Source[^2]
### Formula
- $\frac{d}{dx}[(f(x))^n] = nf(x)^{n-1}*f'(x)$

## Source[^3]
### Example
$$ \begin{align*} f(x) &= (x^2-3x)^2 \\ f'(x) &= 2(x^2-3x)(2x-3)  \end{align*}$$
