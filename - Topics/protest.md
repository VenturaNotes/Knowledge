## Source [^1]
- A certificate signed by a notary public at the request of the holder of a bill of exchange that has been refused payment or acceptance. It is a legal requirement after noting the bill (see also ACCEPTANCE SUPRA PROTEST). The same procedure can also be used for a promissory note that has been dishonoured.
