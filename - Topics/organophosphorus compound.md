## Source [^1]
- A compound that has at least one carbon-phosphorus bond. Organophosphorus compounds are used as pesticides (as in malathion and parathion) and as catalysts and solvents. Phosphate esters, sometimes included in this category, are employed for fire-proofing textiles.
