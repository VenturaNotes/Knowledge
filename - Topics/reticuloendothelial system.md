## Source [^1]
- (RES) a community of cells $\textendash$ phagocytes $\textendash$ spread throughout the body. It includes macrophages and monocytes. The RES is concerned with defence against microbial infection and with the removal of worn-out blood cells from the bloodstream. See also SPLEEN.
