## Source [^1]
- $n$. a preparation of zinc carbonate used as a mild astringent on the skin in the form of a lotion, cream, or ointment. It is also included in a coal tar preparation for the treatment of psoriasis.
## Source[^2]
- A mineral form of zinc carbonate, $ZnCO_3$ (smithsonite), although in the USA the same name is given to a hydrated zinc silicate (hemimorphite). The calamine used medicinally in lotions for treating sunburn and other skin conditions is basic zinc carbonate colored pink with a trace of iron(III) oxide.
