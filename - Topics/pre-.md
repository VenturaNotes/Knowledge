## Source [^1]
- prefix denoting 
- (1) before; preceding. Example: premenstrual (before menstruation); prenatal (before birth). 
- (2) (in anatomy) in front of; anterior to.
- Example: precardiac (in front of the heart); prepatellar (in front of the patella).
