## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any corticosteroid that maintains the body's electrolyte and fluid balance, especially by promoting the retention of sodium in the kidney tubules, the most significant one in humans being aldosterone. See also zona glomerulosa. Compare glucocorticoid. \[From mineral + cortico(stero)id]
