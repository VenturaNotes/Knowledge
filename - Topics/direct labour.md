## Source [^1]
- The use by a UK local authority of its own employees for work such as refuse collection or maintenance of its housing stock. This is an alternative to having such work contracted out to independent firms.
## Source[^2]
- Workers directly concerned with the production of a product, service, or cost unit, such as machine operators, assembly and finishing operators, etc. Compare INDIRECT LABOUR.
