## Source [^1]
- A small lamp that uses a glow discharge in low-pressure neon as the source of light (see GAS-DISCHARGE TUBE). The light is red. Neon lamps consume very little power. They can be made extremely large for use in illuminated signs or extremely small for use as indicators and voltage regulators.
