## Source [^1]
- adj. n. (Of or relating to) any substance that diminishes sexual desire. Many therapeutic drugs, including the selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors such as Prozac, have anaphrodisiac side-effects. Compare aphrodisiac. \[From Greek an- without + aphrodisiakos aphrodisiac, from Aphrodite the Greek goddess of love]
