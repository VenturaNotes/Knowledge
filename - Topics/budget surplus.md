## Source [^1]
- The excess of a government's total income over its expenditure. This can be used to repay government debt. Budget surpluses can be calculated for any level of government-central, local, state in federal countries such as Germany or the United States-or for general government, which is all these levels combined. See also BUDGET DEFICIT.
