## Source [^1]
- (stock transfer) A change in the ownership of a share or stock. Formerly, a stock transfer form had to be executed by the seller of registered securities to legalize such a transaction on the London Stock Exchange. The whole process can now be carried out instantaneously in electronic form, using the CREST system.
