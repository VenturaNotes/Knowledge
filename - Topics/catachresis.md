## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any misuse or wrong application of a word, often by assuming its meaning to be that of a word with a similar sound, as in the utterance I suffer from prostrate trouble, seeming to imply that I can't get up from a lying position, when prostate is meant. Compare MALAPROPISM. \[From Greek katachresis a misuse, from kata down + chresthai to use]
