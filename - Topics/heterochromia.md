## Source [^1]
- $n$. color difference in the iris of the eye, which is usually congenital but is occasionally secondary to inflammation of the iris (as in Fuchs' heterochromic cyclitis). In heterochromia iridis one iris differs in colour from the other; in heterochromia iridum one part of the iris differs in colour from the rest. $\textemdash$ heterochromic adj.
