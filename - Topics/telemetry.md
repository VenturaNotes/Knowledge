## Source [^1]
- Measurement at a distance. Data is transmitted over a particular telecommunication channel from the measuring point to the recording apparatus. A measuring instrument that measures a quantity and transmits the measured data as an electrical signal to a distant recording point is known as a telemeter. Space exploration and physiological monitoring in hospitals both require the use of telemetry.
