## Source [^1]
- $n$. an apparatus for testing hearing at different sound frequencies, so helping in the diagnosis of deafness. -audiometry $n$.
## Source[^2]
- An instrument that generates a sound of known frequency and intensity in order to measure an individual's hearing ability.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. A device for measuring a person's auditory acuity across a range of sound intensities and frequencies. See also audIOGRAM. audiometric adj. \[From Latin audio I hear, from audire to hear + Greek metron a measure]
