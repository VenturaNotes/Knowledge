## Source [^1]
- $n$. the presence of abnormally large red cells (macrocytes) in the blood. Macrocytosis is a feature of certain anaemias (macrocytic anaemias), including those due to deficiency of vitamin $\mathrm{B}_{12}$ or folate, and also of anaemias in which there is an increase in the rate of red cell production.
