## Source [^1]
- Any of a class of cell organelles that typically are spherical vesicles, 0.2-1.5 $\mu \mathrm{m}$, bounded by a single membrane. Microbodies contain enzymes responsible for the oxidation of various materials and they originate from the endoplasmic reticulum. They include peroxisomes and glyoxysomes (see GLYOXYLATE CYCLE).
