## Source [^1]
- (PCO) the presence of more than 12 follicles in each ovary ( $2-9 \mathrm{~mm}$ in diameter), or increased ovarian volume ( $>10 \mathrm{ml}$ ), or both. The presence of enlarged ovaries with multiple small cysts and a hypervascularized androgen-secreting stroma (connective tissue) is associated with signs of androgen excess (see POLYCYSTIC OVARY SYNDROME).
