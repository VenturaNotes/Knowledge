## Source [^1]
- (symbol: $j$ or $J$; unit: amperes per square meter) A vector quantity that is equal to the ratio of the current to the cross-sectional area of the current-carrying medium. The medium may be either a conductor or a radiation beam. The current density is defined either at a point or as the mean current density.
