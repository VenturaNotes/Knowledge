## Source [^1]
- The implementation of economic policy, in particular monetary and fiscal policy, by means of small, incremental policy changes. Doubt has been cast on any real possibility of fine-tuning economies, given their lack of short-term forecastability. Critics argue that, if an economy is impossible to forecast in detail, then it follows that it is also impossible to determine appropriate short-term incremental policies.
