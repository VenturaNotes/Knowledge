## Source [^1]
- n. a device that enables severely paralyzed patients to use typewriters, adding machines, telephones, and a wide variety of other machines. Modern Possums are operated by micro-switches that require only the slightest movement in any limb. The original device worked by blowing and sucking a mouthpiece. The name derives from PatientOperated Selector Mechanism (POSM).
