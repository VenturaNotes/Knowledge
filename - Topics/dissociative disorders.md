## Source [^1]
- n. A category of mental disorders with dissociation as a prominent feature. In DSM-5 it includes depersonalization/derealization disorder, dissociative amnesia, and dissociative identity disorder. See also dissociative amnesia, dissociative CONVULSIONS, DISSOCIATIVE FUGUE, DISSOCIATIVE MOVEMENT DISORDERS, DISSOCIATIVE STUPOR, DISSOCIATIVE TRANCE DISORDER, GANSER SYNDROME. In some taxonomies of mental disorders, including ICD-10, but not in DSM-5, conversion disorder is also classified as a dissociative disorder. DD abbrev.
