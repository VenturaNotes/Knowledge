## Source [^1]
- (SUR) An approach to operations management that seeks to reduce the set-up time of a process by better process or product design, by using parallel rather than sequential operations, or by undertaking at least some of the set-up while the process is running, e.g. refuelling nuclear power stations online. See also JUST IN TIME.
