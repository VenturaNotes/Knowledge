## Source [^1]
- (1) (in immunology) An attribute or specificity that is unique to a particular clone of cells, especially the structure of the surface receptors of a T cell or of the secreted antibodies of a plasma cell. 
- (2) (in botany) A plant, or line of plants, that has been propagated by cloning a type specimen.
