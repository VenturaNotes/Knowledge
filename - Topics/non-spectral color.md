## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any color that does not occur in the visible spectrum and that cannot be produced by light of a single wavelength, for example purple, which can be generated only by a mixture of red and blue (see COMPLEMENTARY COLORS). US non-spectral color. Also called an extra-spectral colour/hue or extraspectral color/hue. Compare SPECIAL COLOR.
