## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug effective against protozoans and used in the treatment of Pneumocystis pneumonia in AIDS patients, leishmaniasis, and trypanosomiasis. Possible side-effects include low blood pressure, heart irregularity, low blood sugar (hypoglycaemia), low white blood cell count, and kidney damage.
