## Source [^1]
- $n$. a disturbance of normal heart rhythm that $\textendash$ like fibrillation $\textendash$ may affect the atria or ventricles. However, the arrhythmia is less rapid and less chaotic. The causes and treatment are similar to those of fibrillation. See also CARDIAC ARREST; DEFIBRILLATION.
