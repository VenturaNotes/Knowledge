## Source [^1]
- (1) A third party who acts on behalf of a bond issuer to pay subscribers to the issue and generally assist the issuer. 
- (2) An agent for one of the national financial institutions in the USA who acts as an adviser, for example, to the National Mortgage Association for its debt securities. 
- (3) In the USA, an agent empowered to collect taxes, revenues, and duties on behalf of the government. For example, the Federal Reserve Banks perform this function for the US Treasury.
