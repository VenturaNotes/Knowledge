## Source [^1]
- $n$. an incision made in the capsule of the lens. In posterior capsulotomy a hole is made in the center of the posterior capsule using a YAG laser, thus providing a clear path for light rays to reach the retina. The laser light is aimed using a modified slit-lamp microscope from outside the eye.
