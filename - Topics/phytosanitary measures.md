## Source [^1]
- Restrictions on trade designed to protect the health of humans, animals, or plants. While some restrictions on trade are necessary to prevent the spread of diseases such as BSE or foot-and-mouth disease, there is a danger that restrictions purporting to be phytosanitary will be used to protect domestic producers of the goods concerned.
