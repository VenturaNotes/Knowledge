## Source [^1]
- (symbol: VA) The SI unit of apparent power, defined as the product of the root-mean-square values of voltage and current (as measured by a voltmeter and an ammeter) in an alternating-current circuit. See POWER; ACTIVE VOLT-AMPERES; REACTIVE VOLT-AMPERES; VAR.
