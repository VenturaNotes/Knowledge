## Source [^1]
- A system of personal taxation in which individuals are taxed separately on their income and capital gains, independent of their marital status and the income of their spouse. In the UK the independent taxation of husbands and wives was introduced in 1990; from December 2005 it was extended to same-sex couples registered under the Civil Partnership Act.
