## Source [^1]
- An area subject to special treatment by the government with the purpose of encouraging investment and employment. The special treatment may include government grants, relaxation of planning regulations, or reduction in taxation. This device has been adopted, for example, in both the UK and the US to try to stimulate recovery in decayed urban areas
