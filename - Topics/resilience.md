## Source [^1]
- A capacity of an economy, a community, or an individual to cope with economic losses caused by an extraordinary adverse shock, such as a natural disaster, a terrorist attack, or a large-scale economic crisis. The concept of resilience includes technological, institutional, and behavioral components, and implies an active response to mitigate the consequences of the shock.
