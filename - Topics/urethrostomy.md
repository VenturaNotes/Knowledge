## Source [^1]
- $n$. the operation of creating an opening of the urethra in the perineum in men. This can be permanent, to bypass a severe stricture of the urethra in the penis, or it can form the first stage of an operation to cure a stricture of the posterior section of the urethra (urethroplasty).
