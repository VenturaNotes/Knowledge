## Source [^1]
- (manufacturing statement) An accounting statement forming part of the internal final accounts of a manufacturing organization; for a particular period, it is constructed to show, inter alia, direct cost of sales, manufacturing overhead, total production cost, and cost of goods manufactured. In some cases a manufacturing profit is also computed.
