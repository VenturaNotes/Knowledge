## Source [^1]
- $n$. A pattern of speech, associated with thought disorder in general and loosening of associations in particular, in which the speaker hops from one topic to another via homonyms, as in: The pain is hard to bear, doctor, a koala bear came to the zoo from China cups and saucers for the goose is sauce for the gander.
