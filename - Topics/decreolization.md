## Source [^1]
- $n$. A process whereby speakers of a creole language, coexisting in a community with a standard language that is associated with higher status, prestige, and wealth, come under social pressure to alter their speech in the direction of the standard. See also creolization. Compare HYPERCREOLIZATION.
