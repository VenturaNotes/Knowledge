## Source [^1]
- adj. describing an agent that breaks up blood clots (thrombi). Thrombolytic drugs are used to unblock arteries in the treatment of myocardial infarction. See FIBRINOLYTIC; TISSUE-TYPE PLASMINOGEN ACTIVATOR.
