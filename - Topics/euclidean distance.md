## Source [^1]
- (Cartesian distance) The distance between two points in Euclidean or Cartesian space. In two dimensions this is $\sqrt{(x_1-x_2)^2+(y_1-y_2)^2}$ and in three dimensions it is $\sqrt{(x_1-x_2)^2+(y_1-y_2)^2+(z_1-z_2)^2}$ with the obvious notation for the coordinates of the points.
