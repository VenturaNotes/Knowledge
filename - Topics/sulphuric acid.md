## Source [^1]
- a powerful corrosive acid, $\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{SO}_{4}$, widely used in industry. Swallowing the acid causes severe burning of the mouth and throat and difficulty in breathing, speaking, and swallowing. The patient should drink large quantities of milk or water or white of egg; gastric lavage should not be delayed. Skin or eye contact should be treated by flooding the area with water.
