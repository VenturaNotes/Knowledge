## Source [^1]
- (snoRNA) Any of a class of small RNA molecules, generally 60-300 nt in length, that guide enzymes to modify the chemical structure of ribosomal RNAs, either by adding a methyl group or by changing the nucleoside uridine to pseudouridine. They are associated chiefly with the nucleolus.
