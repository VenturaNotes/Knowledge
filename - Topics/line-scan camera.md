## Source [^1]
- A camera that records a single one-dimensional line of pixels, rather than a two-dimensional image frame. The main application is high-speed high-resolution scanning of long bands of material in order to use image processing techniques to check for defects. The use of line-scan cameras avoids a lot of technical issues that arise if a conventional 2D camera is used, mostly to do with lighting quality and accurate time synchronization.
