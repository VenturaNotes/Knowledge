## Source [^1]
- The use of an organization by a group of individuals to protect them against monopolistic exploitation by others. Examples include the formation of cooperatives to protect consumers from suppliers and the formation of trade unions to protect workers against monopsonistic employers. The alternative is control of the monopolists by public regulatory authorities.
