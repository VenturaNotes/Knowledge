## Source [^1]
- $n$. a mass of swallowed indigestible material within the stomach. The material, which is usually swallowed by patients with psychiatric disorders, or children, may accumulate and lead to gastric obstruction. Its removal often requires surgery. See also TRICHOBEZOAR.
