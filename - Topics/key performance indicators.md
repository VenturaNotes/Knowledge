## Source [^1]
- (KPIs) Specific measures of the performance of an individual, team, or department in defined key performance areas (KPAs). In the case of an individual employee, these will be based on that person's job description and may be specified in his or her contract of employment. See EMPLOYEE EVALUATION; PERFORMANCE MEASUREMENT.
