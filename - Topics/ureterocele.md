## Source [^1]
- $n$. a cystic swelling of the wall of the ureter at the point where it passes into the bladder. It may be associated with stenosis of the opening of the ureter and it may cause impaired drainage of the kidney with dilatation of the ureter and hydronephrosis. Ureteroceles may also be complicated by infection, prolapse, and bladder outflow obstruction.
