## Source [^1]
- A stock whose value tends to move in the opposite direction to prevailing economic trends, i.e. one that rises in a recession and falls during periods of economic growth. Such stocks could include those in firms that sell low-priced essential goods or inexpensive 'feel-good' items (see LIPSTICK EFFECT).
