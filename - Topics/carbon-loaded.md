## Source [^1]
- Describing foams and rubbers that have been impregnated with carbon particles in order to increase their conductivity and reduce the dielectric properties. This results in flexible materials that are easy to cut or mould, and that can be used for electric shielding and preventing unwanted reflection of electromagnetic waves.
