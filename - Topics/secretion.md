## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) the process by which a gland isolates constituents of the blood or tissue fluid and chemically alters them to produce a substance that it discharges for use by the body or excretes. The principal methods of secretion $\textendash$ apocrine, holocrine, and merocrine $\textendash$ are illustrated in the diagram. 
- (2) the substance that is produced by a gland.
