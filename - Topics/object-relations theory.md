## Source [^1]
- $n$. A theory of object relations developed in the UK by the British-based Austrian psychoanalyst Melanie Klein (1882-1960), the English psychoanalyst Donald Woods Winnicott (1896-1971), the Scottish psychoanalyst W. Ronald D. Fairbairn (1889-1964), the British-based Hungarian psychiatrist Michael Balint (1896-1970), and others. Also called object theory.
