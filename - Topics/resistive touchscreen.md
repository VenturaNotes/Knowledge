## Source [^1]
- A touchscreen that uses the effect of the pressure of a finger on the resistance of the screen sensor to identify its location. Resistive touchscreens will work with gloves or any kind of stylus, but can be slow, and reduce the sharpness of the display. See also CAPACITIVE TOUCHSCREEN.
