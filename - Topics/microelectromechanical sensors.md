## Source [^1]
- (MEMS) Sensors involving very small moving parts that are constructed within an integrated circuit along with the required sensor electronic circuits. The size of the mechanical components is in the range of 1-100 microns. Applications include gyroscopic sensors, pressure sensors, and accelerometers. See also NANOELECTROMECHANICAL SYSTEMS.
