## Source [^1]
- A product (AND) of $n$ Boolean variables, uncomplemented or complemented but not repeated, in a function of $n$ variables. With $n$ variables, $2^n$ different standard product terms are possible. The complement of any standard product term is a standard sum term, or maxterm
