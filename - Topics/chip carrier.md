## Source [^1]
- An integrated circuit package in which the signal leads from the chip inside are brought out on all four sides of a flat square pack. The leads may project out and are usually bent into a J-shape; alternatively they can be simple solder bumps. The term is sometimes used more loosely to refer to any style of chip packaging.
