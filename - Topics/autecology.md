## Source [^1]
- The study of ecology at the level of the species. An autecological study aims to investigate the ecology of populations or individuals of a particular species, including habitat, distribution, life cycle, etc. This should enable a full description of the ecological niche of the organism to be made. Compare SYNECOLOGY.
