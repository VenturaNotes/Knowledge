## Synthesis
- Marketing products or services from one business to another, not individual consumers. 
- For example, NVIDIA's core business model and primary focus is business-to-business marketing and sales
	- #question Is there a difference between marketing and sales in this context?
	- #question Who is NVIDIA mostly doing business with?
## Source [^1]
- (B2B marketing) Marketing aimed at selling a product for any use other than personal consumption. The buyer may be a manufacturer, a reseller, a government body, a non-profit-making institution, or any organization other than an ultimate consumer.
