## Source [^1]
- $n$. Literally wordless, but usually (in relation to one of the negative symptoms of schizophrenia, for example) taken to mean impoverishment of thought inferred from poverty of speech or poverty of content. \[From Greek $a$ - without + logia words, from logos word + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
