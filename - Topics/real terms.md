## Source [^1]
- A representation of the value of a good or service in terms of money, taking into account fluctuations in the price level.
## Source[^2]
- A way of measuring economic variables to remove or minimize the effect of nominal changes, such as changes in the level of prices. Because of continual changes in the type and quality of goods and services available, measurement of changes in real terms becomes increasingly unreliable as longer time intervals are considered. See also REAL BALANCES; REAL GDP; REAL INCOME; REAL INTEREST RATE; REAL WAGES.
