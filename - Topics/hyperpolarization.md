## Source [^1]
- $n$. An increase in the membrane potential of a neuron relative to its resting potential, reducing its propensity to generate a nerve impulse, such as occurs in an inhibitory postsynaptic potential. Compare dePolarization. \[From Greek hyper over + English polarization]
