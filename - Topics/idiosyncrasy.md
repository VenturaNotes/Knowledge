## Source [^1]
- $n$. an unusual and unexpected sensitivity exhibited by an individual to a particular drug or food. Drug idiosyncrasy commonly takes the form of undue susceptibility or hypersensitivity, so that the standard dose causes an excessive effect; the normal effect is produced by a small fraction of the standard dose. $\textendash$idiosyncratic adj.
## Source[^2]
- An abnormal reaction to a drug or other foreign substance shown by an individual, which is usually genetically determined. An individual that shows immunological idiosyncrasy is said to be hypersensitive to a particular substance, agent, etc.
