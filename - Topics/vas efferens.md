## Source [^1]
- (pl. vasa efferentia) Any of various small ducts carrying sperm. In reptiles, birds, and mammals they convey sperm from the seminiferous tubules of the testis to the epididymis; in invertebrates they carry sperm from the testis to the vas deferens.
