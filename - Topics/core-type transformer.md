## Source [^1]
- A transformer in which most of the core is enclosed by the windings. The core is made from laminations; usually the yoke is built up from a stack of laminations and the windings are formed around this (see diagram). Once the windings have been formed extra laminations are added to form limbs around each winding and thus complete the core. Compare SHELL-TYPE TRANSFORMER.
