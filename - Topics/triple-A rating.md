## Source [^1]
- The highest grading available from credit rating agencies. A triple-A rating (AAA) means that delay or default in payments of principal or interest on the security concerned is regarded as extremely unlikely. Any institution with a triple-A rating on its securities can borrow easily and on favorable terms.
