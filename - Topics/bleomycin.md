## Source [^1]
- $n$. an antibiotic with action against cancer cells (see CYTOTOXIC DRUG), used in the treatment of squamous cell carcinoma, germ-cell cancer, and non-Hodgkin's lymphoma. It can cause toxic side-effects in the skin and lungs.
