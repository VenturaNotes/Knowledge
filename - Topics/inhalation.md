## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) (inspiration) the act of breathing air into the lungs through the mouth and nose. See BREATHING. 
- (2) a gas, vapor, or aerosol breathed in for the treatment of conditions of the respiratory tract. $\textemdash$inhaler $n$.
