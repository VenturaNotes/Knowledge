## Source [^1]
- $n$. A word such as a definite or indefinite article (the, a), a quantifier (2) (all, some, every, many, any), or a demonstrative or possessive adjective or pronoun (this, that, my, our, your, his, her, their) that limits or determines the meaning of a noun or noun phrase, as in the sentence I received your letter, in which your determines the meaning of letter.
