## Source [^1]
- (prototype filter) A low-pass filter with a normalized cut-off frequency of one radian per second. From this normalized prototype filter, conversions to other frequencies or to other types (such as high-pass or band-pass) is achieved through denormalization and frequency transformation respectively.
