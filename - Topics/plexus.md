## Source [^1]
- $n$. a network of nerves or blood vessels. See BRACHIAL PLEXUS.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. plexus or plexuses) A compact branching network of nerves or blood vessels, such as the brachial plexus$\textemdash$a network of spinal nerves that supply branches to the forelimbs in vertebrates. See also CHOROID PLEXUS.
## Source[^3]
- $n$. Any complex network of nerves, ganglia, blood vessels, or lymphatic vessels, such as the choroid plexus. \[From Latin plexus a weaving, from plectere to weave]
