## Source [^1]
- (bond pads) Metal pads arranged on a semiconductor chip (usually around the edge) to which wires may be bonded so that electrical connection can be made to the component(s) or circuit(s) on the chip. Bonding is usually effected by thermocompression or ultrasonic bonding. See also TAPE AUTOMATED BONDING; WIRE BONDING.
