## Source [^1]
- (electric circuits) For any physical linear system, the positions of an ideal voltage source and an ideal ammeter can be interchanged without affecting their readings. This can be generalized to situations involving electric fields and current sources. A system is said to possess reciprocity if it obeys the reciprocity theorem. Reciprocity can be useful in solving the electrical properties of that system.
