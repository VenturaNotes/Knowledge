## Source[^2]
- If $x$ is a real number, then ceiling $(x)$, also written as $\lceil x\rceil$, is the smallest integer greater than or equal to $x$.
## Source[^3]
- (least integer function) The smallest integer $\lceil x\rceil$ not less than a given real number $x$. So $\lceil 3.2\rceil=4$ and $\lceil 5\rceil=5$. Compare FLOOR.
