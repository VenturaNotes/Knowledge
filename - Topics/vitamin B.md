## Source [^1]
- any one of a group of water-soluble vitamins that, although not chemically related, are often found together in the same kinds of food (milk, liver, cereals, etc.) and all function as coenzymes. See VitAmins $\mathrm{B}_{1}$, $\mathrm{B}_{2}, \mathrm{~B}_{6}, \mathrm{~B}_{12}$, BIOTIN; FOLATE; NICOTINIC ACID; PANTOTHENIC ACID.
