## Source [^1]
- $n$. the use of a transmembrane hydrostatic pressure to induce filtration of plasma water across the membrane of a haemofilter. Solutes dissolved in the plasma water accompany their solvent to a greater or lesser extent dependent on molecular weight and the characteristics of the filter membrane (pore size).
