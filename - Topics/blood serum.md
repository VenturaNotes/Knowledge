## Source [^1]
- Blood plasma from which the fibrin and clotting factors have been removed by centrifugation after allowing the plasma to clot. Serum containing a specific antibody or antitoxin may be used in the treatment or prevention of certain infections. Such serum is generally derived from a nonhuman mammal (e.g. a horse).
