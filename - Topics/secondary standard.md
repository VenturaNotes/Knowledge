## Source [^1]
- (1) A copy of a primary standard that has a known difference from the primary standard. 
- (2) A quantity that is accurately known in terms of a primary standard and that may be used as a unit. 
- (3) A measuring instrument that has been accurately calibrated in terms of a primary standard and that can be used for accurate measurements or calibration of other equipment.
