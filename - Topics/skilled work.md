## Source [^1]
- Work that can be satisfactorily performed only by somebody with appropriate technical qualifications, experience, or both. Skilled workers are usually paid more than unskilled, and their jobs are typically more secure, because they would be difficult to replace, so that employers do not lay them off in the face of temporary falls in demand.
