## Source [^1]
- A computer or other system that is claimed by its manufacturer or supplier to behave in exactly the same way as a system from another company, i.e. it will produce identical results from an identical program. [[PC clone|PC clones]] were personal computers that behaved like one of IBM's personal computer range.
