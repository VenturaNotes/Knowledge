## Source [^1]
- The structure in seed plants in which pollen is produced. In angiosperms there are usually four pollen sacs in each anther; they contain the microspore mother cells (microsporophytes). In gymnosperms variable numbers of pollen sacs are borne on the microsporophylls that make up the male cone.
