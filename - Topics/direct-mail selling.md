## Source [^1]
- (direct-mail marketing) A form of direct marketing in which sales literature or other promotional material is mailed directly to selected potential purchasers. The seller may be the producer of the products or a business that specializes in this form of marketing. The seller may build up his own list of potential customers or he may buy or rent a list (see LIST RENTING). See also MAIL-ORDER HOUSE.
