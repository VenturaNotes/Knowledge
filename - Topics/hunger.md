## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) Appetite, craving, desire, or need for food; figuratively, a strong desire for anything else. $v b$. 
- (2) To have a hunger for something. See also 2-deoxyglucose, glucostatic theory, HPA axis, hypothalamus, limbic SYSTEM, LIPOSTATIC THEORY, MEDIAL FOREBRAIN BUNDLE, SPECIFIC HUNGER. hungry adj. \[From Old English hungor a hunger, hyngran to hunger]
