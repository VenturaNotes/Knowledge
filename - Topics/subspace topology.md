## Source [^1]
- For a topological space $X$ and subset $A$, the sets $A \cap U$, where $U$ is an open set of $X$, form a topology on $A$ called the subspace (or relative or induced) topology. When $X$ is a metric space, the subspace topology on $A$ is that induced by the metric restricted to pairs of points of $A$
