## Source [^1]
- $n$. the first stage of mitosis and of each division of meiosis, in which the chromosomes become visible under the microscope. The first prophase of meiosis occurs in five stages (see LEPTOTENE; ZYGOTENE; PACHYTENE; DIPLOTENE; DIAKINESIS).
