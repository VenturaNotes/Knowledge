## Source [^1]
- $n$. a large grinding tooth at the back of the mouth. In the permanent dentition, the sixth, seventh, and eighth teeth from the midline on each side in each jaw (see also WISDOM TOOTH). Permanent molars do not replace primary teeth. In the primary dentition, molars are the fourth and fifth teeth from the midline on each side in each jaw. Molars commonly have two or three roots.
