## Source [^1]
- (limited recourse financing) An arrangement in which the money or loans put up for a particular project (often a major property development) are secured on that project and its foreseen earnings rather than forming part of the general borrowing of the company concerned. In case of default, the lender has no recourse to the other assets of the company.
