## Source [^1]
- The side of an interface that represents the user of the data communication services in a standard such as RS232C or X25. DTEs are usually computers or computer terminals. Compare DCE.
## Source[^2]
- (DTE) Equipment that can be used as a data transmitter or data receiver in a communication system. Data terminal equipment is normally connected to data communications equipment (DCE).
