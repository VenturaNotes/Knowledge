## Source [^1]
- (cough suppressant) $n$. a drug, such as pholcodine (a weak opioid), that suppresses coughing, possibly by reducing the activity of the cough centre in the brain and by depressing respiration. Some opioid analgesics also have antitussive activity, e.g. codeine, diamorphine, and methadone.
