## Source [^1]
- (stimulated emission) The emission of a photon by an excited atom or molecule induced by an incident photon of suitable energy. The relation between induced emission and spontaneous emission is given by the Einstein coefficients. The process of induced emission is essential for the operation of lasers and masers.
