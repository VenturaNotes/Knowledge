## Source [^1]
- Denoting a (usually simpler) value that in the limit gets closer and closer, indeed arbitrarily close, to a given value; for example,$$n^{*} n+4$$is asymptotic to $n^{*} n$ as $n$ tends to infinity.
## Source[^2]
- Approaching a given value or condition. A straight line that approaches a two-dimensional curve but never actually touches it is said to be asymptotic to the curve.
