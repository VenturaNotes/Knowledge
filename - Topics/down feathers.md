## Source [^1]
- (plumules) Small soft feathers that cover and insulate the whole body of a bird. In nestlings they are the only feathers; in adults they lie between and beneath the contour feathers. Down feathers have a fluffy appearance as their barbs are not joined together to form a smooth vane.
