## Source [^1]
- A room equipped with audiovisual apparatus for the study of foreign languages. The object is to acquire a proficiency in the spoken language as quickly as possible and to this end extensive use is made of recording equipment, pre-recorded CDs, and interactive computer software. A supervisor can plug into recordings made by students, to monitor their progress and correct errors, especially of pronunciation.
