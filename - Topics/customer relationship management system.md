## Source [^1]
- Business software designed to support the interaction between a business and its customers. It would normally include a customer ordering facility, customer support and help services, and information and decision systems for those involved in marketing and sales.
## Source[^2]
- (CRM system) A computer application used to integrate a company's information about its customers with the knowledge of how best to use this information. It essentially combines a salesforce automation system with a marketing information system
