## Source [^1]
- adj. (in neurology) describing a neuron (nerve cell) that has two processes extending in different directions from its cell body.
## Source[^2]
- adj. Having two poles, as in a bipolar cell or bipolar neuron; or more generally having two extremes or extremities, as in a bipolar adjective pair such as good-bad, frequently used in rating scales.
