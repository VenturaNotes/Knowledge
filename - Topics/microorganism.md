## Source [^1]
- (microbe) $n$. any organism too small to be visible to the naked eye. Microorganisms include bacteria, some fungi, mycoplasmas, protozoa, rickettsiae, and viruses.
## Source[^2]
- (microbe) Any organism that can be observed only with the aid of a microscope. Microorganisms include bacteria, archaea, viruses, protists (including certain algae), and fungi. See MICROBIOLOGY.
