## Source [^1]
- An issue of Treasury bills by inviting bids or tenders for a stated quantity, and accepting the bids from those offering the highest price. The actual sales are at the market-clearing price, bids at or above which are sufficient to take up the supply of bills on offer. See also TAP ISSUE.
