## Source [^1]
- $n$. A point of view or way of regarding something; specifically, the appearance of objects relative to their distance from the viewer, or the effects of distance on their appearance, including especially aerial perspective, linear perspective, and texture gradient. \[From Latin ars perspectiva the art of perspective, from perspicere, perspectum to inspect thoroughly, from per through + specere to see + -ivus indicating a tendency, inclination, or quality]
