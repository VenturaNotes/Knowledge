## Source [^1]
- (symbol: $D$) The value of the gain of an antenna in the direction of its maximum value. Equivalently it is the ratio of the antenna's maximum radiation intensity over that of an isotropic source. It can then be related to the total radiated power of the antenna so that$$D = \frac {U _ {\mathrm {m a x}}}{U _ {0}} = \frac {4 \pi U _ {\mathrm {m a x}}}{P _ {\mathrm {r a d}}}$$where $U_{\mathrm{max}}$ is the maximum radiation intensity, $U_0$ is the radiation intensity of an isotropic source, and $P_{\mathrm{rad}}$ is the total radiated power.
