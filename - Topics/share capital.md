## Source [^1]
- That part of the capital of a company received from its owners (i.e. its members or shareholders) in return for shares. Every company must commence with some share capital (a minimum of two shares). See AUTHORIZED SHARE CAPITAL; CALLED-UP SHARE CAPITAL; ISSUED SHARE CAPITAL; PAID-UP SHARE CAPITAL; RESERVE CAPITAL.
