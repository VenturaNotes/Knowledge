## Source [^1]
- A modal logic in which the modal operators express notions of time such as 'always', 'sometimes', 'strong next', 'weak next', 'next-time', 'last-time', 'interval chop', 'since', 'until', and 'while'. The logical study of reasoning about time has provided new insights and practical techniques for handling time in computations.
