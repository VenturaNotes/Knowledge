## Source [^1]
- An integer representation that uses absolute binary notation for positive values, and forms negative values by complementing the bits (converting zeros to ones and vice versa) of positive values, including all leading zeros. For example, the 10-bit representation of the number 14 is$$0000001110_2$$and the 10-bit one's-complement representation of $-14$ is$$1111110001_2$$
