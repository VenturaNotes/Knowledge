## Source [^1]
- $n$. a tissue-type plasminogen activator made by recombinant DNA technology (genetic engineering). Alteplase is used to dissolve blood clots (see FIBRINOLYTIC) in the treatment of ischaemic stroke, myocardial infarction, and pulmonary embolism. Possible side-effects include local bleeding, cerebral haemorrhage, nausea, and vomiting.
