## Synthesis
- Invisible area around a magnet
	- moving electric charge that exerts a force on other [[magnets]] or moving electric charges
## Source [^1]
- The space surrounding a magnet or a current-carrying conductor and containing magnetic flux. It may be represented by lines of force whose direction at any point is the direction of the force exerted on a small coil (a search coil) placed in the field at that point. The direction of the force is normal to the magnetic flux density at that point. It is assumed that the dimensions of the coil are sufficiently small so as not to disturb the magnetic conditions.
