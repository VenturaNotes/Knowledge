## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of the small ovoid endocrine glands, usually four in number, attached to the sides of the thyroid gland at the base of the neck. They secrete parathyroid hormone into the bloodstream. \[From Greek para beside + thyreoeides a door-shaped shield, from thyra a door + eidos form]
