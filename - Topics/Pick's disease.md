## Source [^1]
- $n$. A progressive disorder commencing in middle age, caused by predominantly frontal lobe or temporal lobe impairment, characterized by dementia with gradual personality change and deterioration of intelligence, memory, and language (1). Compare Alzheimer's disease. \[Named after the Czech neurologist Arnold Pick (1851-1924) who first described it in 1892]
