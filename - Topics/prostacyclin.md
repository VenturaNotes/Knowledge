## Source [^1]
- $n$. a derivative of prostaglandin H produced by the endothelium lining blood vessel walls. It inhibits blood clotting by preventing platelet aggregation and causes vasodilatation (compare THROMBOXANE $\mathrm{A}_{2}$ ). Epoprostenol is a pharmaceutical preparation of prostacyclin.
