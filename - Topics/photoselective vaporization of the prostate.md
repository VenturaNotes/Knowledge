## Source [^1]
- (PVP) a technique to vaporize the prostate by means of a high-energy laser, used to relieve lower urinary tract symptoms due to benign prostatic hyperplasia (see PROSTATE GLAND). It is associated with less blood loss and a shorter hospital stay than a traditional TURP (see RESECTION), but can only be used on smaller prostates.
