## Source [^1]
- Unified communications (UC) is the name given to audio-visual technologies that facilitate business communications. Some of the most popular UC technologies include:
	- Racks
	- Data centers
	- Networking resources
	- Computer equipment
	- Audio-visual equipment such as cameras, microphones, speakers, and video conferencing tools
- This equipment has one function: To support voice, video, voicemail, messaging, and content-sharing communication. Sometimes UC communications include consumer applications and devices.
