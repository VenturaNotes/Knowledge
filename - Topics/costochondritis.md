## Source [^1]
- $n$. a painful condition of the chest wall, caused by inflammation in the joints between ribs and cartilage, breastbone and cartilage, or breastbone and clavicles. It can be caused by strenuous or repetitive movements. The condition is usually shortlived and resolves without treatment. Compare Tietze's Syndrome.
