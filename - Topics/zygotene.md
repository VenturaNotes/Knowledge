## Source [^1]
- $n$. the second stage of the first prophase of meiosis, in which the homologous chromosomes form pairs (bivalents).
## Source[^2]
- The second phase of the first prophase of meiosis, in which pairing (synapsis) of homologous chromosomes takes place. Intimate contact is made between identical regions of homologues, in a process involving proteins and DNA organized to form a synaptonemal complex.
