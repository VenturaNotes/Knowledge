## Source [^1]
- Any type of impact printer in which the complete shape of each character of the repertoire is engraved or moulded onto a font carrier. The font carrier may be one of a number of forms including a moulded wheel, as in a daisywheel printer, or an etched band of metal, as in a band printer. Compare MATRIX PRINTER.
