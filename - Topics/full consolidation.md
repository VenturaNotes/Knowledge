## Source [^1]
- The method of accounting in which the whole impact of subsidiaries is incorporated into group accounts (see CONSOLIDATED FINANCIAL STATEMENTS). If a subsidiary undertaking is less than $100 \%$ owned, the percentage pertaining to the minority interest must be adjusted for. This method of consolidation is generally adopted in the UK.
