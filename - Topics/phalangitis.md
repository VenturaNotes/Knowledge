## Source [^1]
- $n$. inflammation of a finger or toe, causing swelling and pain. The condition may be caused by infection of the soft tissues, tendon sheaths, bone, or joints or by some rheumatic diseases, such as psoriatic arthritis. See also DACTYLITIS.
