## Synthesis
### Description
- A logical operator that joins two statements into a "if-then" relationship.
- Commonly represented by symbols $\to$ or $\implies$ 
### Implication rule
- $\lnot p \lor q \iff p \to q$ 
## Source [^1]
- $(\lnot [ x \in A \lor x \in B] \lor x \in C)$ = $([x \in A \lor x \in B] \to x \in C)$
