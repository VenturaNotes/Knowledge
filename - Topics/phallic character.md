## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a personality pattern determined by fixation (2) at the phallic stage, characterized by reckless, resolute, and self-assured adult personality traits, and sometimes also vanity, exhibitionism, and touchiness. Also called the phallic personality.
