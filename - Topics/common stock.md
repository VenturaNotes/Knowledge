## Source [^1]
- The equity capital of a US corporation. Holders of common stock are entitled to attend and vote at general meetings, to receive declared dividends, and to receive their share of the residual assets, if any, if the corporation is wound up. Common stock corresponds to ordinary shares in UK companies.
