## Source [^1]
- Payments of income which are not a return for the provision of current factor services. In many countries the state makes large-scale transfer payments, particularly to retired, disabled, and unemployed people. Countries also make transfer payments abroad. Some transfer payments are also made by private charities. Transfer payments are not part of the national product; as the name implies, they simply transfer spending power from one group of people to another.
