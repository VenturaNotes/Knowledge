## Source [^1]
- In traditional classifications, a class of free-living flatworms (see platyhelminthes) comprising the planarians, which occur in wet soils, fresh water, and marine environments. Their undersurface is covered with cilia, used for gliding over stones and weeds. Planarians can also swim by means of undulations of the body.
