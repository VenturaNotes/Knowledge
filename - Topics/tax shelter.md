## Source [^1]
- (tax shield) Any financial arrangement made to lower a person's or a company's tax liabilities.
## Source[^2]
- An arrangement by which part of a person's income is protected from taxes. In the UK, for example, Individual Savings Accounts enable savers to enjoy some tax-free income.
