## Source [^1]
- Parenchyma tissue that contains chloroplasts and is photosynthetic. Chlorenchyma makes up the mesophyll tissue of plant leaves and is also found in the stems of certain plant species. Compare COLLENCHYMA; SCLERENCHYMA.
