## Source [^1]
- $n$. A subdivision of the bundle of ascending nerve fibers in the posterior funiculus of the spinal cord, the part towards the center that carries information about touch and position of the leg on the same side of the body and that terminates in the gracile nucleus. Compare FASCICULUS CUNEatus. \[From Latin gracilis slender]
