## Source [^1]
- The group of symmetries of a regular $n$-sided polygon; the notation $D_{2n}$ is often used as the group has order $2n$. If $r$ denotes a rotation by $360\degree/n$ and $s$ denotes a reflection, then $r$ and $s$ are generators of $D_{2n}$. The group contains $n$ rotations $e,r,r^2,\ldots,r^{n-1}$ and $n$ reflections $s,rs,r^2s,\ldots,r^{n-1}s.$
