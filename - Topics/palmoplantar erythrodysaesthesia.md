## Source [^1]
- (hand-foot syndrome) a skin reaction marked by redness, numbness, and desquamation of the palms of the hands and soles of the feet. It can be caused by many chemotherapy drugs, particularly fluorouracil and capecitabine, and tyrosine kinase inhibitors. Treatment requires cessation of the drug.
