## Source [^1]
- $n$. Another name (used in ICD-10) for antisocial personality disorder. The diagnostic criteria are similar, with the addition of incapacity to maintain enduring relationships, though with no difficulty in establishing them, and incapacity to experience guilt or to profit from adverse experience, particularly punishment.
