## Source [^1]
- A diagram, similar to a family tree, that indicates some type of similarity between different organisms. Dendrograms can be based on phenetic or phylogenetic similarities; a cladogram shows similarities according to the system of cladistics. 
## Source[^2]
- $n$. In statistics, a branching diagram showing the clusters formed at each step of a hierarchical cluster analysis. \[From Greek dendrites of or relating to a tree, from dendron a tree + gramme a line]
