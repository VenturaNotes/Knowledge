## Source [^1]
- (velamentum) $n$. a covering membrane.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. velamina) A whitish spongy sheath of dead empty cells that surrounds the aerial roots of epiphytic plants, such as certain orchids. It absorbs any surface water on the roots.
