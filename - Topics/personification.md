## Source [^1]
- $n$. The attribution of human characteristics to non-human entities, as when the dying English television dramatist Dennis (Christopher George) Potter (1935-94) named his cancer Rupert, after a media mogul whom he hated; or a figure of speech in which a non-human entity is referred to as though it were human, as in the phrases merciful death and angry thunder.
