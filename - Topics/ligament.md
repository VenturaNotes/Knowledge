## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) a tough band of white fibrous connective tissue that links two bones together at a joint. Ligaments are inelastic but flexible; they both strengthen the joint and limit its movements to certain directions. 
- (2) a sheet of peritoneum that supports or links together abdominal organs.
## Source[^2]
- A resilient but flexible band of tissue (chiefly collagen) that holds two or more bones together at a movable joint. Ligaments restrain the movement of bones at a joint and are therefore important in preventing dislocation.
