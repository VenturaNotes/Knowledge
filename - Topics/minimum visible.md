## Source [^1]
- $n$. A measure of visual acuity in terms of the visual angle subtended by the finest hairline that can be reliably detected. Under ideal viewing conditions it is in the region of one-half of one second of arc or about $1 / 7,000$ of a degree. Compare MINIMUM SEPARABLE.
