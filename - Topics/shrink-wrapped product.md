## Source [^1]
- A software product sold as a commercial readily available item. The name is meant to indicate that the buyer obtains a complete package with no possibility of influencing the requirements for or the design of the product. These products are usually sold in large numbers, often by mail order or high-street retailers, and come complete with software, installation instructions, and user manuals.
