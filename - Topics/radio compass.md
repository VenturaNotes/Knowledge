## Source [^1]
- A navigational aid carried on board aircraft and ships. It consists of a radio receiver together with a directional antenna and is essentially a direction finder. The antenna is rotated in order to find the direction of a specific radio transmitter relative to the craft; the information is presented as the heading of the craft relative to the transmitter.
