## Source [^1]
- A compound, $\mathrm{HO}_{2} \mathrm{CCH}_{2} \mathrm{COCO}_{2} \mathrm{H}$, that plays an integral role in the Krebs cycle. The anion, oxaloacetate, reacts with the acetyl group from acetyl coenzyme A to form citrate.
