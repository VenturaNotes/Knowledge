## Source[^2]
- $n$. Loss of hearing sensitivity as a result of ageing, usually characterized by gradual loss of sensitivity to high-frequency sounds through deterioration of the organ of Corti. Also spelt presbyacousis. Also called presbyacusia or presbyacousia. See also deafness, sensorineural DEAFNESS. presbyacusic adj. \[From Greek presbys old + akousis hearing, from akouein to hear + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
