## Source [^1]
- (1) In the UK, a building society deposit account with no fixed investment period that confers a share of ownership in the society. It entitles the holder to vote at shareholders' meetings and to an allocation of free shares if the society is demutualized. 
- (2) In the USA, an account with a credit union (a non-profit making co-operative institution) that pays dividends rather than interest.
