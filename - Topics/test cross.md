## Source [^1]
- A mating (cross) made to identify hidden recessive alleles in an individual of unknown genotype. This individual is crossed with one that is homozygous for the allele being investigated (i.e. a homozygous recessive). The homozygous recessive individual may be the parent of the individual being investigated (see BACK CROSS).
