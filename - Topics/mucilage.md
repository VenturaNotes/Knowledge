## Source [^1]
- $n$. (in pharmacy) a thick aqueous solution of a gum used as a lubricant in skin preparations (see also GLYCERIN), for the production of pills, and for the suspension of insoluble substances. The most important mucilages are of acacia, tragacanth, and starch.
