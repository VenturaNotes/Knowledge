## Source [^1]
- (EVS) A vision system in which non-optical sensor data is overlaid on a display to enhance the information available to the user under conditions of poor visibility. An important use is to assist in bad-weather landings of aircraft; the EVS uses infrared video cameras, whose images are superimposed over a visible-light image.
