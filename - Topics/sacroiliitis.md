## Source [^1]
- $n$. inflammation of the sacroiliac joint. Involvement of both joints is a common feature of ankylosing spondylitis and associated rheumatic diseases, including reactive arthritis and psoriatic arthritis. The resultant low back pain and stiffness may be alleviated by rest, antiinflammatory analgesics, or biological disease-modifying antirheumatic drugs.
