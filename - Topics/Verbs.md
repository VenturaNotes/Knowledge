## Source [^1]
- Words that describe the actions or states of being of people, animals, places, or things.
- Verbs function as the root of what's called the predicate which is required (along with a subject) to form a complete sentence
	- Therefore, every sentence must include at least one verb
- Verbs include action words like
	- run
	- walk
	- write
	- sing
- Verbs include words describing states of being
	- be
	- seem
	- feel
	- sound
