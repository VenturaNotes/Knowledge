## Source [^1]
- $n$. Ill-health, or a feeling of ill-health, caused by an expectation of harm or injury from a substance or treatment that, without the expectation, would be harmless, an extreme example being voodoo death. The term was introduced in the 1990s as a counterpart of the placebo effect. See also BASKERVILLE EFFECT, SELF-FULFILLING PROPHECY. \[From Latin nocebo I shall harm, from nocere to harm]
