## Source [^1]
- Short message service (SMS) is a two-way messaging service that allows users to send messages of up to 160 characters in length. SMS is a popular means of communication because it is inexpensive and available on various devices. SMS can be used along with other services such as mobile banking, email, or social networking to provide a more robust experience to customers.
