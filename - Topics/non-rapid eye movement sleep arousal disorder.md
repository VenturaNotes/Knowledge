## Source [^1]
- n. A parasomnia introduced in DSM-5 characterized by recurrent partial awakening from sleep, usually during the first third of the night, accompanied by either sleepwalking or sleep terrors, with little or no memory of dreaming and amnesia for these episodes. Compare RAPID EYE MOVEMENT SLEEP BEHAVIOR DISORDER.
