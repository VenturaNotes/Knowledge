## Source [^1]
- $n$. a system of diagnosis and treatment based on the theory that many diseases are associated with disorders of the musculoskeletal system. Diagnosis and treatment of these disorders involve palpation, manipulation, and massage. Osteopathy provides relief for many disorders of bones and joints, especially those producing back pain. $\textemdash$osteopath $n$. $\textemdash$osteopathic adj.
