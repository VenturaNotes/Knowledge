## Source [^1]
- $n$. A cell that is a true neuron, with axon and dendrite(s), and also a true endocrine gland, secreting a hormone into the bloodstream. Such cells are found especially in the hypothalamus, secreting hormones that in turn affect secretions of the pituitary gland.
