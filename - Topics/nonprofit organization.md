## Source [^1]
- A body, typically working in the charitable or social enterprise sector, that does not seek to make a profit for itself but rather distributes any surplus accrued to further its objectives, whether internally or externally. In the US such organizations are eligible for exemption from federal income tax. See also SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP.
