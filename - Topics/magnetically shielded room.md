## Source [^1]
- (MSR) A room lined with materials, such as aluminium or alloys of nickel and iron, that prevent magnetic fields from entering or leaving its interior. Such rooms are required for the use of highly sensitive magnetic sensors in medical imaging. See also MAGNETOENCEPHALOGRAPHY.
