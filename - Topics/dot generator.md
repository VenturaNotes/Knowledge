## Source [^1]
- A test generator used with a television receiver to adjust the convergence of the picture tube. A pattern of evenly spaced dots or small squares is produced on a dark background and the dynamic focusing (see COLOR PICTURE TUBE) is adjusted until a satisfactory image is formed on the screen.
