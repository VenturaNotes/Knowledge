## Source [^1]
- (multichannel per carrier) In multiple-access systems (see DIGITAL COMMUNICATIONS), the combination of a number of channels onto a single wideband channel. An example is the multiplexing of a number of telephone channels onto a single microwave-frequency signal that is transmitted to a satellite for relay across the Atlantic.
