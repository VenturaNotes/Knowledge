## Source [^1]
- (DNI) Computer interface technology that connects directly to human nerve cells, allowing the user to control a device by thinking and to receive direct feedback bypassing their normal senses. Some proposed neural interfaces would require surgery to implant devices within the brain or near to motor nerves. Other are essentially enhanced forms of electroencephalograph. At present only very rudimentary DNIs exist, and these are mostly used as a last attempt to treat neurological damage.
