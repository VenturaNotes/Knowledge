## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug used mainly in the prevention of malaria that is resistant to chloroquine. Side-effects can include dizziness, rash, gastrointestinal problems, and psychiatric disturbances (e.g. depression, psychosis), and the drug should not be taken during pregnancy, by psychiatric patients, or with beta blockers.
