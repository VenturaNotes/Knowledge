## Source [^1]
- (CMRR) A figure of merit of a differential amplifier, defined as the ratio of the magnitude of the differential mode gain $A_{\mathrm{d}}$ to the magnitude of the common-mode gain $A_{\mathrm{cm}}$:$$\mathrm{CMMR} = \frac{|A_{\mathrm{d}}|}{|A_{\mathrm{cm}}|}$$The value of the CMRR is often given in decibels:$$\mathrm{CMMR} = 20 \log_{10} \left( \frac{|A_{\mathrm{d}}|}{|A_{\mathrm{cm}}|} \right)$$
