## Source [^1]
- The area of mathematics concerned with the application of calculus to functions of vectors; for example, the extension of scalar differential equations of motion such as $v = \frac {dx}{dt}$to the vector form $v = \frac{dr}{dt}$ where $r(t)$ is the position vector at time t.
