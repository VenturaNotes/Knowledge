## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of the dozen or so large papillae arranged in an inverted V formation near the back of the tongue, each surrounded by a ridge on its upper margin and studded near its base with taste buds, mainly responsive to bitter tastes. Compare FILOFORM PAPILLA, FOLIATE PAPILLA, FUNGIFORM PAPILLA. circumvallate papillae pl. \[From Latin circumvallatus surrounded with a wall, from circumvallare to surround with a wall, from circum around + vallum a rampart]
