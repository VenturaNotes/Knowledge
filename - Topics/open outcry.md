## Source [^1]
- Quoting prices, making offers, bids, and acceptances, and concluding transactions by word of mouth in a financial market, usually in a trading pit. See also CALLOVER; DOUBLE AUCTION.
## Source[^2]
- The system of trading in commodities, securities, or currencies by traders meeting together and calling out the trades they are offering. Open outcry is a system for trying to ensure that full information is available on an equal basis to all participants in the market. A similar function is performed in many modern markets by having offers and trades continually reported on screen in a computer network.
