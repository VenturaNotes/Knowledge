## Source [^1]
- (conidiospore) A spore of certain fungi, such as mildews and moulds, that is produced by the constriction of the tip of a specialized hypha, the conidiophore. Chains of conidia may be formed in this way; they are cut off, one at a time, from the tip of the hypha.
