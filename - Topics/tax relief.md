## Source [^1]
- A deduction from a taxable amount, usually given by statute. In the UK, income-tax reliefs are given in respect of income from tax-exempt sources (e.g. ISAs), as well as tax-deductible expenses, personal allowances, tax credits, and gift aid. See INCOME TAX ALLOWANCES.
- The reliefs against capital gains tax include an annual exemption, exemption from the proceeds of the sale of an only or a principal private residence, and entrepreneurs' relief.
- For inheritance tax there is an annual relief as well as relief in respect of gifts between spouses and gifts to political parties and charities; agricultural and business reliefs are also available.
