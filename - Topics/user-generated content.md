## Source [^1]
- (UGC) Media content that is produced by users of that medium rather than by media professionals. Although traditional media have often included some UGC (e.g. the letters page of a newspaper), the term is mainly associated with modern electronic media and embraces such phenomena as blogs, wikis, social media, and digital video (YouTube).
