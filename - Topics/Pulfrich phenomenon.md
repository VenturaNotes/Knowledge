## Source [^1]
- perception of the lateral motion of an object in the visual field as having a depth component, due to a relative difference in signal timings between the two eyes. It often occurs spontaneously in several diseases affecting the eyes, such as cataract, retrobulbar neuritis, and multiple sclerosis. \[C. Pulfrich (20th century), German physicist]
