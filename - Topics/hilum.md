## Source [^1]
- $n$. (pl. hila) a hollow situated on the surface of an organ, such as the ovary, kidney, or spleen, at which structures such as blood vessels, nerve fibers, and ducts enter or leave it.
## Source[^2]
- (pl. hila) A scar on the seed coat of a plant marking the point at which the seed was attached to the fruit wall by the funicle. It is a feature that distinguishes seeds from fruits.
