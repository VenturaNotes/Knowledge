## Source [^1]
- $n$. The upper frontal part of the cingulate gyrus, implicated in decisions between competing responses, language processing, and monitoring of performance. It shows activity when a person experiences social exclusion or distress. ACC abbrev. \[From Latin anterior in front, from ante before + cingulatus belted, from cingulus a belt, from cingere to surround or gird + cortex bark or outer layer]
