## Source [^1]
- (symbol: m) A prefix to a unit, indicating a submultiple of one thousandth, $10^{-3}$, of that unit, as in millisecond.
## Source[^2]
- prefix denoting one thousandth part.
## Source[^3]
- Symbol m. A prefix used in the metric system to denote one thousandth. For example, 0.001 meter $=1$ millimeter (mm).
## Source[^4]
- (symbol: m) A prefix to a unit, denoting a submultiple of $10^{-3}$ (i.e. 0.001) of that unit: one millimeter equals $10^{-3}$ meter.
