## Source [^1]
- The periodic variation, several times a second, of a frequency between two limits that are usually small compared to the nominal frequency. A warble tone generator consists of an oscillator whose output frequency is varied using a small variable capacitor in the tuned circuit. A warble tone is often used in a reverberation chamber in order to produce a uniform sound field containing no standing waves.
