## Source [^1]
- $n$. the surgical or operative treatment of diseases of the brain and spinal cord. This includes the management of head injuries, the relief of raised intracranial pressure and compression of the spinal cord, the eradication of infection (e.g. cerebral abscess), the control of intracranial haemorrhage, and the diagnosis and treatment of tumors. The development of neurosurgery has been supported by advances in anaesthetics, radiology, and scanning techniques.
