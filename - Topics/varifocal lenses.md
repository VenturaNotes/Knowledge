## Source [^1]
- (progressive lenses) lenses in which the power (see DIOPTRE) gradually changes from one prescription to the other and there is no dividing line on the lens between the different segments (compare MULTIFOCAL LENSES). The wearer can see clearly at any distance by raising or lowering the eyes.
