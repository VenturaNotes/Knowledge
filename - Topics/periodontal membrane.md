## Source [^1]
- (periodontal ligament) the ligament around a tooth, by which it is attached to the bone.
## Source[^2]
- The membrane of connective tissue that surrounds the root of a tooth and anchors it to its socket in the jawbone. Fibers of the periodontal membrane pass into the cement covering the root, which provides a firm attachment.
