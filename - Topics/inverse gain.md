## Source [^1]
- The gain of a bipolar junction transistor when it is connected in reverse, that is, so that the emitter acts as the collector and the collector as the emitter. The inverse gain is usually less than the gain normally obtained, due to the higher doping level of the emitter compared with the collector. This causes the emitter to have a higher injection efficiency into the base than has the collector.
