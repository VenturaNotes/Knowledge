## Source [^1]
- $n$. An altered state of consciousness resembling a form of oceanic feeling, in which a person experiences an ecstatic dissolution of the usual bonds of space and time and an attitude of wonderment and awe. The concept was introduced by the US psychologist Abraham Maslow (1908-70) in his book Religions, Values, and Peak-Experiences (1964, pp. 59-68).
