## Source [^1]
- $n$. A major dopamine pathway in the brain that includes the nigrostriatal bundle and parts of the telencephalon, portions of which are involved in experiences of pleasure and reward. See also MESOCORTICAL SYSTEM, MESOLIMBIC SYSTEM. \[From Greek mesos middle + telos end + enkephalos the brain, from en in + kephale the head]
