## Source [^1]
- (RRP) The price that a manufacturer recommends as the retail price for his product. Under the Competition Act 1998 the manufacturer has no legal power to enforce this recommendation. The RRP is also called the manufacturers' recommended price (MRP). See also RESALE PRICE MAINTENANCE.
