## Source [^1]
- Any of the raised pores in the stems of woody plants that allow gas exchange between the atmosphere and the internal tissues. The pore is formed by the cork cambium, which, at certain points, produces a loose bulky form of cork that pushes through the outer tissues to create the lenticel.
