## Source [^1]
- $n$. either of a pair of tubes, $25-30 \mathrm{~cm}$ long, that conduct urine from the pelvis of kidneys to the bladder. The walls of the ureters contain thick layers of smooth muscle, which contract to force urine into the bladder, between an outer fibrous coat and an inner mucous layer. $\textemdash$ureteral, ureteric adj.
## Source[^2]
- The duct in vertebrates that conveys urine from the kidney to the bladder.
