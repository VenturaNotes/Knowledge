## Source [^1]
- (CRP) Part of the second phase of material requirements planning, which produces daily or weekly estimates of the workload on key machines and processes in each department of a plant. This enables proactive adjustment of capacity to be made, if possible, or feedback into the master production schedule, so that adjustments can be made.
