## Source [^1]
- (pl. taxa) Any named taxonomic group of any rank in the hierarchical classification of organisms. Thus the taxa Papilionidae, Lepidoptera, Hexapoda, and Arthropoda are named examples of a family, order, class, and phylum, respectively.
