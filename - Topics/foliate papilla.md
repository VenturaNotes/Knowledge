## Source [^1]
- $n$. Any of a large number of paired oval papillae on the sides of the back part of the tongue with taste buds at their bases that respond mainly to sour tastes. Compare CIRCUMVALLATE PAPILLA, FILOFORM PAPILLA, FUNGIFORM PAPILLA. foliate papillae $p l$. \[From Latin folium a leaf + forma a shape]
