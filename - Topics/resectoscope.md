## Source [^1]
- $n$. a type of surgical instrument (an endoscope) used in resection of the prostate or in the removal of bladder tumors. The resectoscope allows continuous irrigation of the operation site during the procedure by having a fluid inlet and outlet channel. Resection is performed by an electrically activated wire loop.
