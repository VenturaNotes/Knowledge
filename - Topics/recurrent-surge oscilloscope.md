## Source [^1]
- An instrument used to investigate electrical surges. A surge generator is used in conjunction with a cathode-ray oscilloscope (CRO). The repetition rate of the surges produced by the surge generator is made to synchronize with the timebase of the CRO so that a steady picture is obtained on the screen, suitable for visual or photographic inspection.
