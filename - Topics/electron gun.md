## Source [^1]
- A device that produces an electron beam and forms an essential part of many instruments, such as cathode-ray tubes, electron microscopes, and linear accelerators, all of which need electron beams for their operation. The source of electrons may be a thermionic cathode, as shown in the diagram, or a field emitter. A series of accelerating and focusing electrodes usually produces a narrow beam of high-velocity electrons.
