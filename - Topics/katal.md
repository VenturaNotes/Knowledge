## Source [^1]
- Symbol kat. A non-SI unit of enzyme activity defined as the catalytic activity of an enzyme that increases the rate of conversion of a specified chemical reaction by $1 \mathrm{~mol} \mathrm{~s}^{-1}$ under specified assay conditions.
