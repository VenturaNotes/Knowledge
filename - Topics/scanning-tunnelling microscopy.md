## Source [^1]
- (STM) One of the operating modes of scanning-probe microscopy in which a conducting tip is used. By monitoring the tunnelling current between the tip and the surface of a device, the surface profile of the device can be mapped at atomic resolution. See also ELECTRON MICROSCOPE.
