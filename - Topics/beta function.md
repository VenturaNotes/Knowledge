## Source [^1]
- The function $B(p, q)=\int_{0}^{1} x^{p-1}(1-x)^{q-1} \mathrm{~d} x=\frac{\Gamma(p) \Gamma(q)}{\Gamma(p+q)}$ where $\Gamma(a)$ is the gamma function. For positive integers $m, n, B(m, n)=\frac{(n-1)!(m-1)!}{(m+n-1)!}$.
