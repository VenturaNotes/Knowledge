## Source [^1]
- A transaction or change (internal or external) recognized by the accounting recording system. Events are recorded as debit and credit entries. For example, when a sale is made for cash the double entry for the sales transaction would be debit bank, credit sales (see DOUBLE-ENTRY BOOK-KEEPING).
