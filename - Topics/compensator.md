## Source [^1]
- Additional equipment introduced into a system to reshape its characteristics in order to improve system performance. The process is called compensation or stabilization. When the system is compensated it is stable, has a satisfactory transient response, and has a large enough gain to ensure that the steady-state error does not exceed the specified maximum.
