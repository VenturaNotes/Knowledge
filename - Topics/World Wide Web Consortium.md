## Source [^1]
- The organization responsible for many core standards on the World Wide Web. Founded by Tim Berners-Lee in 1994, its mission is 'to lead the World Wide Web to its full potential by developing protocols and guidelines that ensure long-term growth for the Web.' Its responsibilities include CSS, Document Object Model, HTML, SOAP, XHTML, XML, XPath, XQuery, and XSL.
