## Source [^1]
- (CTA) A privately owned US organization that lobbies for public policy in the area of consumer electronics and that determines many of the industry standards in this field. In particular, the CTA now sets most of the audio and television standards adopted by manufacturers worldwide (notably those for screen resolution). It also organizes the annual Consumer Electronics Show.
