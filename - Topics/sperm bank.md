## Source [^1]
- a facility that collects, freezes, and stores human sperm for future use in artificial insemination (see CRYOPRESERVATION). Sperm is donated by men who relinquish legal rights to any future child, and donors' identities are generally unknown to recipients. In some cases, men store their sperm for their own future use if they are to undergo a medical treatment that might leave them sterile.
