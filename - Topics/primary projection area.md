## Source [^1]
- $n$. An area of the brain, such as the primary somatosensory cortex, primary visual cortex (Area V1), or primary auditory cortex, that is the first to receive information from another system before relaying it elsewhere. Compare SECONDARY PROJECTION AREA, TERTIARY PROJECTION AREA.
