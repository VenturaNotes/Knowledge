## Source [^1]
- The $n$th cyclotomic polynomial $\Phi_n(z)$ is the monic polynomial whose roots are the primitive $n$-th roots of unity. $\Phi_4(z)=z^2+1$ as $i$ and $-i$ are the primitive fourth roots of unity. When $n$ is prime $\Phi_n(z) = z^{n-1} + z^{n-2}+\ldots+z+1$
