## Source [^1]
- $n$. the furthest forward division of the brain, consisting of the diencephalon and the two cerebral hemispheres.
## Source[^2]
- (prosencephalon) One of the three sections of the brain of a vertebrate embryo. The forebrain subsequently enlarges and divides into two regions: the central diencephalon, which comprises an upper thalamus and lower hypothalamus; and the surrounding telencephalon, or cerebrum. Compare HINDBRAIN; MIDBRAIN.
