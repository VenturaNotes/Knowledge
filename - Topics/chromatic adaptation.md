## Source [^1]
- $n$. 
- (1) A form of adaptation (2) resulting from continuous exposure to light of a particular color or hue and consequent bleaching of the associated visual pigment, causing a gradual loss of saturation (1) of the perceived color and the appearance of its complementary color if a white surface is viewed. See also xanthopsia. 
- (2) More generally, an alteration of the balance of pigments in a biological membrane in response to the color and intensity of the ambient light. Also called color adaptation.
