## Source [^1]
- (ATE) Equipment used to test products, parts of products, or components without the need for human intervention at each stage. Connected to a computer or microprocessor controller, automated test equipment will undertake a series of tests on the product or component according to a programmed sequence and will analyze and record the results obtained. In a fully automated production environment ATE will supply processed results to a central results data bank.
