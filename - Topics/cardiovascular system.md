## Source [^1]
- (circulatory system) the heart together with two networks of blood vessels $\textendash$ the systemic circulation and the pulmonary circulation (see illustration). The cardiovascular system effects the circulation of blood around the body, which brings about transport of nutrients and oxygen to the tissues and the removal of waste products.
