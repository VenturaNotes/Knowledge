## Source [^1]
- $n$. any one of a group of drugs that inhibit the action of testosterone on the prostate gland by blocking androgen receptors, competing for binding sites, or decreasing androgen production. They are therefore used in the treatment of prostate cancer, which is an androgendependent tumour. Anti-androgens include abiraterone acetate, bicalutamide, cyproterone, finasteride, and flutamide.
