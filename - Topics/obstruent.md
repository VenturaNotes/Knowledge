## Source [^1]
- $n$. A consonant involving substantial obstruction of the airstream through the vocal tract. All plosives, fricatives, and affricates are obstruents; nasals (2) and approximants are not. \[From Latin obstruere to obstruct, from $o b$ in the way of + struere to pile up]
