## Source [^1]
- A theory of choice based on the premise that people anticipate regret if a wrong choice is made, and take this anticipation into account when making decisions. Regret theory is an alternative to expected utility and can explain some economic anomalies. See also BEHAVIOURAL ECONOMICS; MINIMAX REGRET.
