## Source [^1]
- a rare sex-linked recessive disorder characterized by eczema, thrombocytopenia, and deficiency in the immune response (immunodeficiency). It is caused by a decrease in the amount of Wiskott-Aldrich syndrome protein (WASP: a protein occurring in lymphocytes, platelets, and other cells) due to a mutation in the WASP gene.
