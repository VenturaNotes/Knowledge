## Source [^1]
- $n$. reduction of the oxygen concentration in the arterial blood, recognized clinically by the presence of central and peripheral cyanosis. When the partial pressure of oxygen $\left(p \mathrm{O}_{2}\right)$ falls below $8.0 \mathrm{~kPa}~(60 \mathrm{mmHg}$ ), the condition is defined as respiratory failure.
