## Source [^1]
- $n$. A collection of techniques used in ethnomethodology and other forms of qualitative research for analyzing naturally occurring verbal dialogues. It studies the structure and coherence of conversations using empirical and inductive techniques in contrast to the theoretical and deductive approach characteristic of discourse analysis. CA abbrev.
