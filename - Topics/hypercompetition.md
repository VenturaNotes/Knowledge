## Source [^1]
- A market situation in which there is an unusually high degree of competition between companies. Hypercompetition tends to arise in sectors characterized by rapid technological and structural change and low barriers to entry; as a result, no one firm can sustain its competitive advantage over time. Such situations are typical of new and emerging industries.
