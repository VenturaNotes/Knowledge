## Source [^1]
- The development of one or more species from an existing species. It occurs when sympatric or allopatric populations diverge so much from the parent population that interbreeding can no longer occur between them. See Dobzhansky-Muller model. See also DICHOPATRIC SPECIATION; PARAPATRIC SPECIATION.
