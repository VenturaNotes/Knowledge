## Source [^1]
- (1) An organized activity in which capital and labour are utilized to produce goods.
- (2) The sector of an economy that is concerned with manufacture. 
- (3) A group of firms that offer a product or class of products that are close substitutes for each other. The set of all sellers of a product or service, e.g. the car industry or the steel industry.
