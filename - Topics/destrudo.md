## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a term coined by the Italian psychoanalyst Edoardo Weiss (1889-1971) to denote the form of energy associated with Thanatos, intended as a counterpart to libido. See also MORTIDo. \[A blend of destru(ctive) + (libi)do]
