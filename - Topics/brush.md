## Source [^1]
- A conductor, often made from specially prepared carbon, that provides electrical contact with a moving conductor, usually between the rotating and stationary parts of electrical machines. The contact resistance between the brush and the moving surface is the brush contact resistance. In electrical machines this resistance usually decreases with increasing current density resulting in a roughly constant voltage drop at the contact surface.
