## Source [^1]
- n. A culture-bound syndrome found in China and southeast Asia, characterized by pathological fear of cold (frigophobia) and of wind (anemophobia), believed to produce fatigue, impotence, and death. Also called frigophobia. \[From Chinese pa fear + leng cold or the cold season]
