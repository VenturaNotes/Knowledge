## Source [^1]
- A television capable of displaying three-dimensional images. Most require the viewer to wear special glasses that filter the screen image so that each eye sees only its own image. Some have autostereoscopic displays that do not require glasses, but their quality tends to be poor. 3D TVs are high-end products, with some available at up to 4K HD resolution.
