## Source [^1]
- $n$. A sensory receptor consisting of hair cells in the basilar membrane of the organ of Corti that translate sound waves$\textemdash$pressure waves with frequencies between 16 hertz and 20,000 hertz$\textemdash$into nerve impulses. Also called a phonoreceptor.
