## Source [^1]
- (in developmental biology) A phase in the life of an organism during which it has heightened receptiveness to external stimuli that enable it to develop a certain behavior or learn a certain skill. An example is the period soon after birth when offspring learn the identity of their parents. See IMPRINTING.
