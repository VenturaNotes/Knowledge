## Source [^1]
- $n$. A synthetic anabolic steroid used in the treatment of osteoporosis and anaemia, as well as in stimulating muscle growth in undernourished or underdeveloped patients, and often used by bodybuilders and athletes. Also (more correctly) spelt oxymethylone. Formula: $\mathrm{C}_{21} \mathrm{H}_{32} \mathrm{O}_{3}$. \[From its systematic name (17$\beta$-hydr)oxymethyl(ene-17$\alpha$-methyl-3-androstan)one]
