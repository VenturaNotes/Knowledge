## Source[^2]
- adj. (in medicine) relating to the body rather than to the mind. For example, a physical sign is one that a doctor can detect when examining a patient, such as abnormal dilation of the pupils or the absence of a knee-jerk reflex (see also FUNCTIONAL DISORDER; ORGANIC DISORDER).
