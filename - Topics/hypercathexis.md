## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, additional cathexis invested in any object that is already cathected. Sigmund Freud (1856-1939) used the concept especially for explaining attention, his most detailed account of this theory appearing in 1895 in his 'Project for a Scientific Psychology' (Standard Edition, I, pp. 177-397).
