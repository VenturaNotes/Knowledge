## Source [^1]
- $n$. a blind-ended lymphatic vessel that extends into a villus of the small intestine. Digested fats are absorbed into the lacteals.
## Source[^2]
- A minute blind-ended lymph vessel that occurs in each villus of the small intestine. Digested fats are absorbed into the lacteals (see CHYLE) and transported to the bloodstream through the thoracic duct.
