## Source [^1]
- $n$. a developmental disorder in which a child's ability to learn arithmetical facts and to process and manipulate numbers is significantly below normal for his or her age. Like dyslexia, with which it is associated, it is not caused by intellectual impairment.
## Source[^2]
- n. Impairment in ability to do arithmetic. See also SPECIFIC DISORDER OF ARITHMETIC SKILLS, SPECIFIC LEARNING DISORDER. Compare acalculia. \[From Greek dys- bad or abnormal + Latin calculare to count, from calculus a stone, diminutive of calx limestone + -ia indicating a condition or quality]
