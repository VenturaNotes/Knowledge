## Source [^1]
- A nonlinear amplifier whose response describes the mathematical logarithmic function. Computing operations such as multiplication, division, and the taking of powers or roots may all be performed using logarithmic amplifiers. They also provide a convenient method of compressing signals termed logarithmic compression.
