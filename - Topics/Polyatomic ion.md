### Oxoanions with 4 Variations
| Symbol      | Name         | Symbol | Name | Symbol | Name | 
| ----------- | ------------ | ------ | ---- | ------ | ---- |
| $ClO^{-}$   | Hypochlorite |  $BrO^{-}$      |  Hypobromite    |  $IO^{-}$      | hypoiodite     |
| $ClO_2^{-}$ | Chlorite     |  $BrO_2^{-}$      | Bromite     |   $IO_2^{-}$     |  iodite    |
| $ClO_3^{-}$ | Chlorate     |   $BrO_3^{-}$     |   Bromate   |  $IO_3^{-}$      |  iodate    |
| $ClO_4^{-}$ | Perchlorate  |  $BrO_4^{-}$      |  Perbromate    |   $IO_4^{-}$     |  periodate    |
- 4 variations exist for chlorine, bromine, and iodine
	- Bromine oxoanions [^2]
	- Iodine oxoanions [^3]
