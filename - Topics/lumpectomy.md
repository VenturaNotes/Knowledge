## Source [^1]
- $n$. an operation for breast cancer in which the tumor and surrounding breast tissue are removed: muscles, skin, and lymph nodes are left intact (compare MASTECTOMY). The procedure, usually followed by radiation, is indicated for patients with a tumour less than 2 cm in diameter and who have no metastases to local lymph nodes or to distant organs.
