## Source [^1]
- (credit-reference agency) An organization that gives a credit reference on businesses or persons. They specialize in collating information regarding debtors, bankruptcies, and court judgments in order to provide a widely based service to their clients. See also ANCILLARY CREDIT BUSINESS; CREDIT RATING.
