## Source [^1]
- $n$. the length of axon covered with a myelin sheath. Internodes are separated by nodes of Ranvier, where the sheath is absent.
## Source[^2]
- (1) (in botany) The part of a plant stem between two nodes. 
- (2) (in neurology) The myelinated region of a nerve fiber between two nodes of Ranvier. See MYElin SHEATH.
