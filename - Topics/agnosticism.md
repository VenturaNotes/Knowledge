## Source [^1]
- $n$. A belief that nothing is or can be known about God. More loosely, a belief that the existence of God cannot be known with certainty, but this sense is avoided in careful usage. agnostic adj. n. (Of or pertaining to) one who espouses agnosticism. Compare ATHEISM, DEISM, PANTHEISM, theism. \[Coined in 1869 by the English biologist Thomas Henry Huxley (1825-95), from Greek $a$ - without + gnostos known + -ismos indicating a state or condition]
