## Source [^1]
- A rebate offered by a supplier of goods or services to customers on the understanding that further goods and services are purchased from the same supplier. The rebate is usually paid periodically, after the supplier has seen that he has the customer's continued support. Some shipping companies offer a deferred rebate to shippers.
