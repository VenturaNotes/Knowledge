## Source [^1]
- $n$. One of the monocular cues of visual depth perception, depending on the fact that movement of the head produces relatively large apparent displacement of nearby objects in the opposite direction and relatively small displacement of distant objects in the same direction. Also called motion parallax or movement parallax. See also MOTION PERSPECTIVE, PARALLAX, REDUCTION SCREEN. Compare BINOCULAR PARALLAX.
