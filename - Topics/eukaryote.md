## Source [^1]
- (eucaryote) An organism consisting of cells in which the genetic material is contained within a distinct nucleus. All organisms except bacteria and archaea are eukaryotes. See EUKARYA. Compare PROKARYOTE.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. An organism composed of cells with well-defined nuclei (1) containing genetic material. All organisms except bacteria, cyanobacteria (blue-green algae), and viruses are eukaryotes. Also spelt eucaryote. Compare PROKARYOTE. \[From Greek eu well or good + karyon a kernel]
