## Source [^1]
- $n$. a drug that mimics the action of calcium in the body by activating the calcium-sensing cell receptors and thus reduces the level of parathyroid hormone. It is used in the treatment of secondary hyperparathyroidism in advanced kidney failure and also for reducing the level of hypercalcaemia in parathyroid carcinoma. It is taken orally. Trade name: Mimpara.
