## Source [^1]
- $n$. Substance-related and addictive disorders in DSM-5 associated specifically with drinking alcohol, including alcohol abuse, alcohol dependence, Korsakoff's psychosis, and a range of alcohol-induced disorders. DSM-5 distinguished alcohol use disorder, alcohol intoxication, and alcohol withdrawal as separate disorders. See also SUBSTANCE-INDUCED DISORDERS.
