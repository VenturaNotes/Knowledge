## Source [^1]
- The relationship between the linear and angular speeds of a cylinder or sphere when it is rolling on a plane surface without slipping. If $v$ is the speed of the centre of mass of the cylinder or sphere, with radius $r$, and $\omega$ is the angular speed of the rotation, then $v = r\omega$
