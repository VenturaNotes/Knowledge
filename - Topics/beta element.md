## Source [^1]
- $n$. In psychoanalysis, a term introduced by the Indian-born British psychoanalyst Wilfred R(uprecht) Bion (1897-1979) to denote any of the raw elements of emotion that are transformed by the alpha function into alpha elements suitable for mental 'digestion'.
