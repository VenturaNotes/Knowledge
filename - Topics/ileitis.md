## Source [^1]
- $n$. inflammation of the ileum (small intestine). It may be caused by Crohn's disease, tuberculosis, the bacterium Yersinia enterocolitica, or typhoid or it may occur in association with ulcerative colitis (when it is known as backwash ileitis).
