## Source [^1]
- adj. Of or relating to the management of resources or the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services. In psychoanalysis, of or relating to the circulation and distribution of cathexis or instinctual energy within the mental apparatus. See also HYDRAULIC THEORY, METAPSYCHOLOGY, PRINCIPLE OF CONSTANCY, QUOTA OF AFFECT. \[From Greek oikonomia household management, from oikos a house + nemein to manage]
