## Source [^1]
- (1) Any of various practices in which a company inflates the value of its assets or exaggerates its profits as a pretext for issuing an unjustified amount of shares. Fraudulent practices of this kind were particularly associated with the US railway boom of the late 19th century. 
- (2) A derogatory term for the dilution of equity in a company.
