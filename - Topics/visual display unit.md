## Source [^1]
- (visual display terminal, VDT) A device that consists of a display, keyboard, and computer connection.
- VDT is another term for visual display unit
## Source[^2]
- (VDU) A display device used with a computer that displays information in the form of characters and line drawings on the screen of a cathode-ray tube. A VDU is most often associated with a keyboard and/or mouse and/or touchscreen and/or light pen, which allow the information in the display to be altered or new data to be input, and a printer for a permanent record of the display. See also TERMINAL.
