## Source [^1]
- A compound in which there are fewer electrons forming the chemical bonds than required in normal electron-pair bonds. Such compounds use multicentre bonds. An electron-precise compound is a compound that has exactly the number of valence electrons required to write a Lewis structure with normal covalent bonds. See Borane.
