## Source [^1]
- A phenomenon in which the finite sampling used when calculating a discrete Fourier transform of some signal results in spurious side frequencies being reported that do not correspond to those frequencies present in the signal. Various techniques that adjust the sampling window size and transform the windowed signal can be used to alleviate the problem.
