## Source [^1]
- an X-ray appearance of the spine in which there is squaring of the vertebrae and vertical syndesmophytes that span adjacent vertebrae, so that the spine resembles a stick of bamboo. This results in fusion of the spine with a very limited range of motion. It is seen in ankylosing spondylitis.
