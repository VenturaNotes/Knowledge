## Source [^1]
- n. an artificial radioactive isotope that has a half-life of about four hours and decays into the radioactive gas krypton-81m, emitting radiations as it does so. It provides a useful source of krypton-81m for use in ventilation-perfusion scanning. Symbol: Rb-81.
