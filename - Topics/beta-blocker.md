## Source [^1]
- $n$. A drug such as propranolol that inhibits the action of neurons that respond to adrenalin (epinephrine) by blocking their (beta-) adrenergic receptors, and thus subdues the activity of the heart; used as a medication in the treatment of cardiac arrhythmias, hypertension, migraine, tremor caused by anxiety, akathisia caused by neuroleptic medication, social anxiety disorder, panic disorder, and withdrawal from alcohol and other substances; also used by musicians and actors to suppress the effects of performance anxiety and by athletes as a performance enhancer to moderate excessive arousal.
