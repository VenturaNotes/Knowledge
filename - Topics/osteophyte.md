## Source [^1]
- $n$. a projection of bone, usually shaped like a rose thorn, that occurs at sites of cartilage degeneration or destruction near joints and intervertebral discs. Osteophyte formation is an X-ray sign of osteoarthritis but is not a cause of symptoms in itself.
