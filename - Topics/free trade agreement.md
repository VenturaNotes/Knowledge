## Source [^1]
- An agreement to abolish tariffs between two or more countries. Notable examples are the agreement to set up the European Free Trade Association (EFTA) in 1960 and the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA), concluded in 1994 between the USA, Canada, and Mexico.
