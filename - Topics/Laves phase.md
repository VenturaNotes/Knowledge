## Source [^1]
- A type of intermetallic compound that occurs for compounds of the type $AB_2$; an example is $MgZn_2$, in which the coordination around A is $12B + 4A$. This structure was suggested by Fritz Laves (1906-78) in 1934. The Laves phase is a particular case of the Frank-Kasper phase.
