## Source [^1]
- adj. Of or relating to a relatively uncommon type of synapse (1) between the dendrite of one neuron and the dendrite of another. It does not generate nerve impulses but alters the receptivity of the target neuron. Compare AXOAXONIC, AXODENDRITIC, AXOSOMATIC. D-D abbrev.
