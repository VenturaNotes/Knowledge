## Source [^1]
- (adipocytokine) Any of a group of cytokines that are secreted primarily by adipose tissue. They include leptin, tumour necrosis factor, interleukin 6, and adiponectin; they have roles in regulating a range of bodily functions, including energy metabolism, inflammation, cardiovascular function, and fetal growth.
