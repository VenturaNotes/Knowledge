## Source [^1]
- $n$. For light of a particular hue, the range of light intensities from its absolute threshold to the lowest intensity at which its hue can be detected; analogously, for a pure tone of a particular frequency, the range of amplitudes from its absolute threshold to the lowest intensity at which its pitch can be detected.
