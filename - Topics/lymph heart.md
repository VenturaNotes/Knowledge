## Source [^1]
- Any of a series of muscular pumping structures that occur in the major lymphatic vessels of certain lower vertebrates and maintain a flow of lymph around the body. Many animals (including all mammals) do not possess lymph hearts; in these the flow of lymph is maintained by a series of muscle contractions and valves (see LYMPHATIC SYSTEM).
