## Source [^1]
- a psychiatric disorder in infants and young children resulting from institutionalization, poor parenting, emotional neglect, or child abuse. Affected children may be withdrawn or aggressive, and fearful or attention-seeking and indiscriminately friendly. Treatment requires the provision of stable caring adults as parents over a long period of time.
