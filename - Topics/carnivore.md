## Source [^1]
- An animal that eats meat, especially a member of the order Carnivora (e.g. tigers, wolves). Carnivores are specialized by having strong powerful jaws and well-developed canine teeth. They may be predators or carrion eaters. See also CONSUMER. Compare HERBIVORE; OMNIVORE.
