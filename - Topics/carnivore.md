## Source [^1]
- An animal that eats meat, especially a member of the order Carnivora (e.g. tigers, wolves). Carnivores are specialized by having strong powerful jaws and well-developed canine teeth. They may be predators or carrion eaters. See also CONSUMER. Compare HERBIVORE; OMNIVORE.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. Any animal of the order Carnivora of flesh-eating mammals, including dogs, cats, bears, raccoons, hyenas, civets, and weasels. Homo sapiens is not a carnivore but an omnivore. See also predator. Compare herbivore, omnivore. carnivorous adj. Flesh-eating. \[From Latin caro, carnis flesh + vorare to devour]
