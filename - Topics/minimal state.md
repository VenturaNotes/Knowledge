## Source [^1]
- A government whose intervention in the economy is just sufficient to sustain organized economic activity. The responsibilities of a minimal state would include provision of policing, a judiciary, and defence of the nation. These interventions are necessary to uphold property rights, enforce contract laws, and defend the gains from trade.
