## Source [^1]
- $n$. In the terminology introduced by the English physicist and mathematician Sir Isaac Newton (1642-1727) in a paper to the Royal Society in 1675, and elaborated in his book Opticks in 1704, any light that can be broken down into constituent colors. Compare PURE LIGHT.
