## Source [^1]
- adj. describing the sex that produces two different kinds of gamete, which carry different sex chromosomes, and that therefore determines the sex of the offspring. In humans men are the heterogametic sex: the sperm cells carry either an X or a Y chromosome. Compare HOMOGAMETIC.
