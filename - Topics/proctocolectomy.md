## Source [^1]
- $n$. a surgical operation in which the rectum and colon are removed. In panproctocolectomy the entire rectum and colon are removed, necessitating either a permanent opening of the ileum (see ILEOSTOMY) or the construction of an ileal pouch. This is usually performed for ulcerative colitis.
