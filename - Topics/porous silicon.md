## Source [^1]
- A network of silicon wires, each with a thickness of a few nanometers, made by dissolving a bulk silicon wafer in an electrochemical cell containing hydrogen fluoride solution. This porous structure significantly enhances the surface area of the silicon. By varying the ratio of the air to silicon, the wavelength of the light emitted from the porous silicon can be controlled.
