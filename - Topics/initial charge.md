## Source [^1]
- The charge paid to the managers of a unit trust by an investor when units are first purchased. For most trusts the initial charge ranges between $5 \%$ and $6 \%$, as laid down in the trust deed. Money-market unit trusts and exempt unit trusts have lower initial charges and in some cases no initial charge.
