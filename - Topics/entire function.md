## Source [^1]
- A complex-valued function that is holomorphic everywhere in the complex plane. Polynomials, exponentials, and trigonometric and hyperbolic functions are all examples, while the function $f:z \to 1/z$, which is not holomorphic at zero (but is meromorphic), is not an entire function.
