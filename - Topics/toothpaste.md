## Source [^1]
- $n$. a paste used for cleaning the teeth. It contains a fine abrasive and a suitable flavoring to promote use. Most toothpastes contain fluoride salts, which help to prevent dental caries. Some contain antimicrobials, to counteract dental plaque, and whitening agents.
