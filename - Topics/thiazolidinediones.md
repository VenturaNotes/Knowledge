## Source [^1]
- pl. n. a group of oral hypoglycaemic drugs, including pioglitazone, used in the treatment of type 2 diabetes mellitus. They act by reducing resistance of the body to its own insulin and may be taken alone or in combination with metformin or a sulphonylurea. Thiazolidinediones should not be used in patients with heart failure or bladder cancer or with a history of these.
