## Source [^1]
- A fund in which the money is invested in tangible or corporate assets, such as property or shares, rather than being treated as savings loaned to a bank or other institution. Asset-backed funds can be expected to grow with inflation in a way that bank savings cannot. See also EQUITY-LINKED POLICY; UNIT-LINKED POLICY.
