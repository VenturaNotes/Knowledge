## Source [^1]
- The state in which all the economic resources of a country (and more specifically, its labour force) are fully utilized.
## Source[^2]
- A situation where the labour market has reached a state of equilibrium with the property that those in the active labour force who are willing and able to work at prevailing wage rates are able to find work. In this situation there is no cyclical unemployment or demand-deficiency unemployment but only frictional unemployment.
