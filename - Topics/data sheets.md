## Source [^1]
- A means by which manufacturers of electronic devices and components convey information about their products to potential customers, such as circuit designers. The data sheet will contain details including physical dimensions, pin connections, absolute device ratings, and basic performance data. Some data sheets also include typical applications and possible circuit configurations.
