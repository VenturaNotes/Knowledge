## Source [^1]
- (sigmoidectomy) surgical removal of the sigmoid colon using either an open approach (see LAPAROTOMY) or laparoscopy. It is performed for tumours, severe diverticular disease, or for an abnormally long sigmoid colon that has become twisted (see VOLVULUS).
