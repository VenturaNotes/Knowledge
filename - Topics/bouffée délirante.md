## Source [^1]
- $n$. A culture-bound syndrome of Francophone West Africa and Haiti, characterized by sudden outbursts of violent behavior, accompanied by confusion, psychomotor agitation, and sometimes also paranoid ideation and hallucinations. It is sometimes interpreted as a brief psychotic disorder. \[From French bouffée explosion + délirante raving]
