## Source [^1]
- $n$. Drostanolone propionate, a fast-acting, orally administered anabolic androgenic steroid used by athletes to build muscle strength without increasing weight, and occasionally used in the treatment of breast cancer. Formula: $\mathrm{C}_{23} \mathrm{H}_{36} \mathrm{O}_{3}$.
