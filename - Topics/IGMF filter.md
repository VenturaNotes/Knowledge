## Source [^1]
- (infinite-gain multiple-feedback filter) A configuration of filter that is used to construct low-pass, high-pass, and band-pass filters and is similar to the Sallen-Key filter but requires one less component. It has good stability and low output impedance and inverts signals in the pass-band (see INVERSE FILTER).
