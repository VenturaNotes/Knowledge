## Source [^1]
- $n$. Loss of vision of one nasal field (the right field of the left eye or the left field of the right eye) resulting from damage to the lateral region of the optic chiasm. Also called nasal hemianopsia. See also hemiANOPIA. \[So called because the affected area lies on the side nearest the nose]
