## Source [^1]
- A smart multi-function printer (SMFP), besides printing, faxing, and scanning, also helps organizations reduce their reliance on paper and hard copies. Thus, it catalyzes faster digital transformation while simultaneously encouraging environmental sustainability. It can also perform advanced tasks such as creating text-searchable documents and translating them.
