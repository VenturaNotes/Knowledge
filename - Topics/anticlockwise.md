## Source [^1]
- Movement in the opposite direction to that which the hands of a clock normally take. In compass terms, $\mathrm{N} \rightarrow \mathrm{W} \rightarrow \mathrm{S} \rightarrow \mathrm{E}$ is anticlockwise. In polar coordinates, or when orienting curves, this is taken to be the positive direction.
