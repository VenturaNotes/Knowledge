## Source [^1]
- (1) (spoilage) The amount of material lost as part of a production process. Acceptable levels of waste, known as a normal loss, are part of the cost of production and as such are allowed for in the product costs. 
- (2) Any process or activity that does not add value. See Shingo's Seven Wastes.
