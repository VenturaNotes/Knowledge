## Source [^1]
- (periplasmic space) The zone between the cytoplasmic membrane and the outer membrane in Gram-negative bacteria. It contains a thin layer of peptidoglycan, has a gellike consistency, and contains various types of proteins concerned with the cell's metabolism. In Gram-positive bacteria the corresponding space is much narrower.
