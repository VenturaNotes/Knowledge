## Source [^1]
- (ethanedial) A low melting-point yellow crystalline solid, CHOCHO, m.p. 15$\degree$C. It is a dialdehyde. It gives off a green vapor when heated and burns with a violet-colored flame. Glyoxal is made by the catalytic oxidation of ethanediol and is used to harden the gelatin employed for photographic emulsions.
