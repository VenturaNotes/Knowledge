## Source [^1]
- A device that analyses and displays the total radiofrequency signals received at an antenna. The signals are usually displayed on the screen of a cathode-ray tube and some indication of the modulation and field strength at the transmitted carrier frequency can be obtained from the height and spread of the trace.
