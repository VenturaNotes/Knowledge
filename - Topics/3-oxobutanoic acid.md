## Source [^1]
- (acetoacetic acid) A colorless syrupy liquid, $CH_3COCH_2COOH$. It is an unstable compound, decomposing into propanone and carbon dioxide. The acid can be prepared from its ester, ethyl 3-oxobutanoate.
