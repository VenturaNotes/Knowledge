## Source [^1]
- $n$. The summated electrical activity of large numbers of neurons in the retina, recorded from the front of the eyes, analogous to the cochlear microphonic or the EEG. ERG abbrev. \[From Greek elektron amber (in which electricity was first observed) + English retina, probably from Latin rete a net + Greek gramme a line]
