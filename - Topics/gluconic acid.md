## Source [^1]
- An optically active hydroxycarboxylic acid, $\mathrm{CH}_{2}(\mathrm{OH})(\mathrm{CHOH})_{4} \mathrm{COOH}$. It is the carboxylic acid corresponding to the aldose sugar glucose, and can be made by the action of certain moulds.
