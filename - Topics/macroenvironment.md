## Source [^1]
- The wider environment that creates the forces that shape every business and non-profit enterprise. The physical environment, social and cultural forces, demographic make-up, economic climate, prevailing political beliefs, and current legal infrastructure are components of the macroenvironment. Generally, most companies have no control over these factors. Compare MICROENVIRONMENT.
