## Source [^1]
- $n$. a member of a family of proteins $\textendash$ keratins $\textendash$ found in the cytoplasm of epithelial tissues and the cancers arising in them (carcinomas). Any given carcinoma has its unique pattern of cytokeratins, which can be identified on histochemical analysis of a specimen and can help in the diagnosis of metastatic carcinoma when the primary site of metastasis is unknown.
