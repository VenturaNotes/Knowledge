## Source [^1]
- $n$. exposure of the genitals to another person, as a sexually deviant act. The word is often broadened to mean public flaunting of any quality of the individual.
## Source[^2]
- $n$. 
- (1) A paraphilia characterized by recurrent, intense sexual fantasies, urges, or behavior involving exposure of one's sexual organs to unsuspecting strangers. See also exhibitionistic disorder. 
- (2) Extravagant, theatrical, or boasting behavior designed to draw attention to oneself. \[From Latin exhibere to display, from ex- out + habere to have]
