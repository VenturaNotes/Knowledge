## Source [^1]
- The total value that is overdue after failing to make one or more required payments on a financial contract. ‘In arrears’ refers to the state of being behind in fulfilling obligations. The term is applied to contracts with periodic payments such as loans and mortgages, rent, and child support.
## Source[^2]
- A liability that has not been settled by the due date. For example, cumulative preference shares entitle the shareholders to receive an annual fixed dividend. If this is not paid, the dividend is said to be in arrears and this fact must be disclosed in the notes to the financial statements.
