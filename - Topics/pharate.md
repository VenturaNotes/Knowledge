## Source [^1]
- 'Cloaked': describing a larva or adult when inside the cuticle of the previous developmental stage. For example, a newly metamorphosed adult insect may remain 'cloaked' for some hours or even days within the pupal case until it receives the appropriate environmental cue that triggers emergence.
