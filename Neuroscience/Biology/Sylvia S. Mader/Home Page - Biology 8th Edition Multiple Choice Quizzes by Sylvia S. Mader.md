## Chapter 1
- (1) [[Living things]] respond to stimuli, reproduce, and have adaptations
- (2) The smallest unit of matter that can enter into chemical combination is an [[atom]]
- (3) The smallest living unit is a [[cell]]
- (4) Similar cells with a similar function describes a [[tissue]]
- (5) All of the gray squirrels in a temperate deciduous forest are referred to as a [[Population (Biology)|population]]
- (6) Atoms do not contain molecules
- (7) Unicellular organisms can respond to their environment
- (8) The ultimate source of energy for nearly all living things is the [[sun]]
- (9) [[Homeostasis]] is the maintenance of internal conditions within certain limits
- (10) The ability to respond
	- ensures survival of the organism
	- allows the organism to carry on its daily activities
- (11) Homeostasis is maintained by
	- the nervous system
	- hormones
- (12) Plants are not the only ones able to capture solar energy and carry on photosynthesis
- (13) The process that transforms solar energy into chemical energy in the bonds of organic molecules is called photosynthesis
- (14) All cells have a copy of the hereditary material, DNA
- (15) The instructions for the organization and metabolism of living things are encoded in the genes
- (16) Some organisms reproduce by simply splitting in two
- (17) In most multicellular organisms, reproduction involves the pairing of a sperm from one partner and an egg from the other partner
- (18) Penguins are adapted to an aquatic existence in the Antarctic
- (19) Adaptations of penguins
	- Flat feet
	- A bill adapted to eating small shellfish
	- Stubby flattened wings
	- Not an adaptation
		- forelimbs proportioned for flying
- (20) [[Natural selection]] results in genetic changes in a species' member
- (21) Organisms become better suited to a particular environment through the process of natural selection
- (22) The characteristics of living things suggests that living things descended from several different ancestors is a false statement
- (23) The great diversity of life forms is the result of adaptations to different environments
- (24) A [[species]] is defined as a group of interbreeding individuals
- (25) Communities are not static and unchanging
- (26) An [[ecosystem]] is comprised of the physical environment and interacting populations
- (27) [[Energy]] flows through an ecosystem and is eventually converted to heat
- (28) The cycling of nutrients through ecosystems depends on
	- decomposition of organisms after they die
	- respiration
	- photosynthesis
- (29) One of the most complex ecosystems are tropical rain forests
- (30) Mining does not account for about 60% of tropical deforestation
- (31) The discipline of identifying and classifying organisms according to certain rules is called taxonomy
- (32) In the [[binomial system]], the first word of the name is [[genus]]
- (33) Similar [[Genus|genera]] are placed into families
- (34) The domain [[Archaea]] and [[Bacteria]] contains prokaryotic unicellular organisms
- (35) Organisms in the Domain [[Eukarya]] have a membrane bound nucleus
- (36) The most inclusive group of organisms is a [[domain (Biology)|domain]]
