## Source [^1]
1. System Settings $\to$ Spotlight
2. Scroll down and click Search privacy (or Spotlight privacy) button
3. Drag folder you want to index again to the list of locations that Spotlight is prevented from searching
4. Wait a few seconds. Then click the remove button `-` to remove it from the list so it can be reindexed.
5. Click Done. Spotlight will reindex contents of disk or folder. It might take some time, and you might see an indexing progress indicator at the top of the Spotlight window
