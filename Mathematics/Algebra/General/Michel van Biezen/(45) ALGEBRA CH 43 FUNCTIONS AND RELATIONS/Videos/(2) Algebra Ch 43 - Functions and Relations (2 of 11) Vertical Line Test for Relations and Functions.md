	- The vertical line test is taking a vertical line and moving it across the x-axis.
	- If at any point the curve crosses the vertical line at more than 1 spot, it is not a function, it is a relation
	- The square root of something is defined as having to be positive by definition
	- Every function can be a relation (sort of like a subset)
	- From a practical perspective, there isn't much of a difference between functions and relations
