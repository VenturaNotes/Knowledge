- Discriminant
	- D > 0
		- 2 Real Solutions
	- D = 0
		- 1 Real Solution
	- D < 0
		- 2 Imaginary Solutions
- Discriminant Formula
	- D = $b^2 - 4ac$
		- For quadratic equations in standard form $ax^2 + bx + c$
