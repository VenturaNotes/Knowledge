- An equation has 3 parts
	- An equation indicates that the left side is equal to the right side
	- ![[Screenshot 2023-01-10 at 11.14.28 PM.png]]
- A linear equation
	- The variable in the equation is raised to the first power (exponent = 1)
	- x = $x^1$
- Solving an equation means finding the value(s) of the unknown variable that will make the left side equal to the right side.
	- We can find the solution by guessing or by following a set of steps
- Quadratic equation
	- 0 = $x^2 + 5x + 6$ 
		- Because the equation is raised to the 2nd power
