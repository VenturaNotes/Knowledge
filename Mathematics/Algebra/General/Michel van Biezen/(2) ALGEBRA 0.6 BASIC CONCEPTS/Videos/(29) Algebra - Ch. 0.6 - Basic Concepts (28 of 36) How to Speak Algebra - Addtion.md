- Represent as an algebraic expression
	- (1) The sum of an unknown number and 16
		- x + 16
	- (2) 6 more than a number
		- x + 6
	- (3) A number increased by 13
		- x + 13
	- (4) A number added to 4 times the number
		- x + 4x
		- The word "the" implies that it's the same number
	- (5) Two more than 5 times the number
		- 2 + 5x
