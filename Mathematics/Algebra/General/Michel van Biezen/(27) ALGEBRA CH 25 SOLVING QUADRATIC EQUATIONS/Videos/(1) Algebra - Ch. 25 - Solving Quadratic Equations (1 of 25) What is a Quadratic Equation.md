	- An equation
		- Has a left side, an equal sign, and a right side
		- Solving the equation means finding the value of the variable (x in the example) that makes the left side of the equation equal to the right side
	- A quadratic equation
		- Has at least one term where the variable is raised to the second (but no higher) power
