	- Partial Sum Formula for Geometric Series
		- $S_n = \frac {a_1(1-R^n)}{1-R}$
	- Nth term formula for Geometric Series
		- $a_n = a_1R^{n-1}$
	- Infinity formula for geometric series
		- $S_\infty = \infty$  if R > 1
		- $S_\infty = \frac {a_1}{1-R}$
