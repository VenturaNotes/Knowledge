- A polynomial is an algebraic expression with 2 or more terms
	- Monomial (not a polynomial)
		- $3x^2$
		- "A monomial is also a type of polynomial. But a polynomial is not always a monomial." [^1]
	- Binomial
		- $3x^2 - 4x$
	- Trinomial
		- $3x^2 - 4x + 5$
	- Polynomial of 4 terms
		- $4x^3 - 2x^2 + x - 2$
	- Term
		- Part of an expression that consists 1 or more factors
		- A factor is something that is multiplied with another factor
		- $4x^3$ is a factor
			- 4 is a factor
			- $x^3$ is a factor
			- Could see above as 4xxx
				- as 4 factors
				- Your choice for the way you want to look at it
		- Terms are separated from other terms by "+" or "-" signs
