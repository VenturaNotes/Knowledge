	- $3x^2 - 2x + 4 + \frac {5}{x-2}$ 
		- The $3x^2 - 2x + 4$ is the quotient
		- $\frac {5}{x-2}$ is the remainder
	- This is how synthetic division was derived from long division
		- For (5), we prefer to add rather than subtract which is why we take the root of the denominator.
