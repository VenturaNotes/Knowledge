- A number or symbol placed above another number or symbol that indicates the power the number or symbol at the bottom is raised to.
- The number at the bottom is called the <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">base</mark>
- The number at the top is called the exponent
- $5^3$
	- Indicates how many times that number is multiplied by itself or how many factors of that number there are.
	- It has 5 raised to the 3rd power
- $3^n$ 
	- Means that there is n factors. We just don't know how many n is.
