	- $2000 is invested at 8% compounded quarterly. How much time will it take to double the investment?
		- $A(t) = P(1 + \frac {r}{n})^{nt}$
			- A(t): Amount you end up with
			- P: Invest this amount of Principal
			- r: rate
			- n: number of compounds per year
			- t: years
