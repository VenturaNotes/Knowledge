	- Related Conditionals - Statements based on a given conditional
		- Given p: It is raining, q: I wear a jacket
			- Conditional: p $\rightarrow$ q
				- If it is raining, then I wear a jacket
			- Converse: q $\rightarrow$ p
				- If I wear a jacket, then it is raining
			- Inverse: ~p $\rightarrow$ ~q
				- If it is not raining, then I do not wear a jacket
			- Contrapositive: ~q $\rightarrow$ ~p
				- If I do not wear a jacket, then it is not raining
	- Logically Equivalent - Same Truth values
		- The Conditional and Contrapositive are logically equivalent
		- The Converse and Inverse are logically equivalent
