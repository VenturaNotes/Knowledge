	- Postulate/Axiom - A true statement describing geometric term relations
	- Postulates
		- Two Point Postulate
			- P.1) Through any two points, there is exactly one line
		- Line Point Postulate
			- P.2) A line contains at least two points.
		- Line Intersection Postulate
			- P.3) If two lines intersect, then their intersection is exactly one point.
	- Intersection - A point at which one line meets or crosses another line
