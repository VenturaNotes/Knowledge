	- Adjacent Angles
		- Common Vertex
		- Common side
		- Do not share interior points
	- Vertical Angles
		- 2 angles formed by intersecting lines, but are not adjacent 
			- A and B, B and C, C and D, D and A are all adjacent angles
		- A and C are vertical angles
		- B and D are vertical angles
		- Vertical angles are congruent
	- Linear Pair - 2 adjacent angles formed by opposite rays where the opposite rays are the noncommon sides
