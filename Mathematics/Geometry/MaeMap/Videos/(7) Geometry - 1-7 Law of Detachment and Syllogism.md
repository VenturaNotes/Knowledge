	- Forms of Deductive Reasoning
		- [[Law of Detachment]]: $[(p\rightarrow q) \land p] \rightarrow q$
			- If p $\rightarrow$ q and p is true, then q is also true
			- It is always true
		- [[Law of Syllogism]]: $[(p\rightarrow q) \land (q \rightarrow r)] \rightarrow (p \rightarrow r)$
			- If p $\rightarrow$ q and q $\rightarrow$ r are true, then p $\rightarrow$ r is also true.
			- It is always true
