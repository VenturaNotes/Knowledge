	- [[Complementary Angles]]
		- Angles whose measures add up to 90$\degree$
	- [[Supplementary Angles]]
		- Angles whose measures add up to 180$\degree$
	- Not required that the angles are not adjacent
	- Memory Trick
		- Since "C" appears before "S" in the alphabet, we can associate "Complementary" with the lower sum and "Supplementary" with the higher sum
