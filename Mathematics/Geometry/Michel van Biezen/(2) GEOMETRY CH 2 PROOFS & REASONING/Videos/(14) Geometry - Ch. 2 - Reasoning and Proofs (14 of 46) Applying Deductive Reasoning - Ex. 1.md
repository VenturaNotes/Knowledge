	- If x is an odd number, which expression represents an even number?
	- Add (or subtract)
		- Odd + Odd = Even
		- Odd + Even = Odd
		- Even + Even = Even
		- (Adding Odd $\rightarrow$ Change)
		- (Adding Even $\rightarrow$ No Change)
	- Multiplying or dividing
		- Odd $\times$ Odd = Odd
		- Odd $\times$ Even = Even
		- Even $\times$ Even = Even
		- Even $\times$ Odd = Even
