- Say whether the triangle is right, acute or obtuse
- Reminder
	- Right: $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$
		- Triangle has 90 degree angle
	- Acute: $a^2 + b^2 > c^2
		- Has all 3 of interior angles less than 90 degrees
	- Obtuse $a^2 + b^2 < c^2$ 
		- One of the interior angles is greater than 90 degrees
