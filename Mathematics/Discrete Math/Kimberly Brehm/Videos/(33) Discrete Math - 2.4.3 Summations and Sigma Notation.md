- Sigma Notation
	- To express the sum of the terms of the sequence $a_n = \{a_m, a_{m+1}, ... a_n\}$, we write $$\sum^n_{i=m} a_i = \sum_{m \leq i \leq n} a_i$$ which represents $a_m + a_{m+1} + a_{m+2} + ... + a_n$
