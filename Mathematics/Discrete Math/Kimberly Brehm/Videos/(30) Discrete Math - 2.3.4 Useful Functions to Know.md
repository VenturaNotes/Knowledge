- Floor Function f(x) = $\lfloor x\rfloor$ 
	- Largest integer less than or equal to x.
- Ceiling Function f(x) = $\lceil x \rceil$
	- Smallest integer greater than or equal to x

- Factorial Function
	- f: $\mathbb{N} \implies \mathbb{Z}^+$ denoted by f(n) = n! is the product of the first n positive integers when n is a non-negative integer
	- Stirlings Formula
