	- Proof by contradiction
	- Recall: [[Contradiction]] is an argument of the form:
		- $\lnot p$ $\to c$ 
			- Therefore, p.
	- Proof by contradiction
		- (1) Suppose $\lnot$p is true
		- (2) Get a contradiction like 0=1
		- (3) Therefore, p is true
