	- [[Universal-Conditionals]]: $P(x) \implies Q(x)$ means 
		- $\forall x \in D, P(x) \implies Q(x)$
	- Example
		- "If x is the POTUS, then x is a US citizen"
			- "Every POTUS is a US citizen"
		- Saying "if x is the POTUS" is saying for all POTUS
