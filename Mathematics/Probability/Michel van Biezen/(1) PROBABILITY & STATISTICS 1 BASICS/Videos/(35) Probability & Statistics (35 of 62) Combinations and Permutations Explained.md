		- order matters
		- How many ways can 4 letters (n) occupy 3 spaces? (k)
			- n possible letters with k possible spaces
		- $_nP_k = n(n-1)(n-2)...(n-k+1)$
	- [[Combination|Combinations]]
		- Order does not matter
		- How many combinations of 3 letters (k)
		- $_nC_k = \frac {n!}{(n-k)!k!}$
