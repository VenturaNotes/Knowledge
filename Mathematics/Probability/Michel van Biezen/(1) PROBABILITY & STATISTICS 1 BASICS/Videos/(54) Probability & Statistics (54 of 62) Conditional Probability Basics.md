	- P(B|A) = Probability that B will occur provided A has occurred
	- P(B|A) = $\frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(A)}$ 
		- Dividing by event to find probability since we know P(A) has occurred
		- Can be rewritten as $P(A \cap B) = P(A)*P(B|A)$ 
	- [[conditional probability]]
