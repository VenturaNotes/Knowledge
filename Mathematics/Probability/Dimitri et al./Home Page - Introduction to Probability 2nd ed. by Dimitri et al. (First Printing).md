## Preface to First Edition
- “The book covers the fundamentals of probability theory ([[probabilistic mod­els,]] discrete and [[continuous random variables]], [[multiple random variables]], and [[limit theorems]])” ([Bertsekas and Tsitsiklis, 2008, p. 4](zotero://select/library/items/7I2D3L6T)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/MLTZNTRI?page=4&annotation=E3VMACIP))
- Chapter 4 develops: advanced view of conditions, [[sums of random variables]], [[least squares estimation]], [[bivariate normal distribution]]
- Chapters 5 and 6 go into detail about [[Bernoulli process]], [[Poisson process]], and [[Markov process]]
- Main text divided in 3 categories: Theoretical problems, Problems in the text, Supplementary problems
	- Supplementary problems only have solutions for course instructors
