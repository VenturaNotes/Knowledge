	- [[Partition|Partitions]]
		- n $\ge$ 1 distinct items; r $\ge$ 1 persons give $n_i$ items to person $i$
			- Here $n_1,...,n_r$ are given nonnegative integers
			- with $n_1$ + ... + $n_r$ = n
		- Ordering n items: n!
			- Deal $n_i$ to each person $i$, and then order
	- [[Multinomial coefficient]] (generalizes the binomial coefficient)
		- Number of partitions = $\frac {n!}{n_1!n_2!...n_r!}$
