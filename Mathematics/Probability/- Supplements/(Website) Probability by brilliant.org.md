- a number that represents the likelihood of an uncertain event
- Always between 0 and 1, inclusive
- Larger the probability, more likely the event will happen
- Probability of 0 means event is impossible, will never happen
- Probability of 1 means that event is assured, will always happen
- Examples
	- Probability deals with quantifying problems with uncertain results
		- Understanding the probability of a defect can help a manufacturing company handle defects more intelligently
- Interpretations of probability
	- Objectivist Probability
		- Deals with experiments that can be repeated many times in order to do analysis on the outcomes
	- Subjectivist Probability
