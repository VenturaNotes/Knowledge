	- What will be the change in the period of the pendulum if the length of the pendulum is changed from 1.00m to 1.01m?
	- [[Period]] equation
		- $P = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{L}{g}}$ 
			- L = length of pendulum
			- g = acceleration due to gravity
