	- Combinations
		- Imagine you're visiting a zoo with six animals, and I ask you to record the first three animals you see.
			- Tiger, Lion, Monkey, Zebra, Walrus, Snake
		- With combinations, order does not matter.
	- Combinations formula
		- $_nC_r$
			- n: Total # of objects
			- r: # of objects you're picking
	- Will have 20 possible combinations of there different animals instead of 120 permutations of animals
