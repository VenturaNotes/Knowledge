- Describing Data
	- Measures of Central Tendency:
		- Mean: The "Average" of a distribution
		- Median: The middle score of a distribution.
		- Mode: The most frequence score(s) in a distribution
	- Measures of Dispersion (measures of how "spread out" a data set is around the mean):
		- Variance
		- Standard Deviation
