	- Statistics is the science of collecting, classifying, presenting, and interpreting Data
		- Collecting: Questions asked, sample group picked, choice of geometric location, etc.
		- Classifying: How the data is grouped
		- Presenting: How the data is presented, type of graphs used, scaling of the graphs
		- Interpreting: With or without bias, predetermined result, motive
			- Statistics can often be misused here
		- Descriptive Statistics: "Data speaks for itself" or unbiased representation
		- Inferential Statistics: "Technique of interpreting the numbers resulting from the descriptive techniques"
