	- [[Present value of an annuity]]
	- The annual interest rate on a credit card is 13.99%. If a payment of $100.00 is made each month, how many months will it take to pay off an unpaid balance of $2,489.45? Assume that no new purchases are made with the credit card.
	- Formula: $P=\frac{R(1-(1+\frac rn)^{-nt})}{\frac rn}$
		- P = loan amount
		- R = periodic payment (monthly payment in this case)
		- r = interest rate
		- n = number of compounding period
		- t = time (years)
