	- [[Linear Transformation|linear transformations]]: 
		- Definition: A linear transformation T: $\mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}^m$ is a transformation such that
			- Respects scalar multiplication & vector addition
				- (1) T(c$\vec{x})$ = cT($\vec{x})$
				- (2) T($\vec{x} + \vec{y}$) = $T(\vec{x}) + T(\vec{y})$ 
	- Can add vectors and multiply scalars on vectors
	- Rotation is a linear transformation
