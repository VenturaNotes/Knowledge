	- When are compound statements true?
	- Conjunction statements ($p \land q$)
		- True when both simple statements are true
	- Disjunction statements (p $\lor$ q)
		- True when either simple statement is true, or when they are both true
	- Negation of a simple statement $\lnot p$
		- True when the simple statement is false
