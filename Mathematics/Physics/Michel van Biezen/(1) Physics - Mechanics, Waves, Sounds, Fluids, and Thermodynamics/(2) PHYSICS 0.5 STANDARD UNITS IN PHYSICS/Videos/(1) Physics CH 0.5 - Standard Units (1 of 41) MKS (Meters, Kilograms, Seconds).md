	- MKS system (metric system as its known)
	- [[Length]]
		- Meter m
		- Defined as 1m = $\frac {1}{10,000,000}$ ($\frac 14$ the circumference of the earth at the equator 1km = 1000m) 
	- [[Mass]]
		- Kilogram kg
		- 1kg = Mass of 1 liter of water at $4\degree C$ ($39.2\degree F$) 
		- 1g = $\frac {1}{1000}kg$
		- Surprising that grams is not the standard unit of mass
	- [[Time]]
		- Second s (sec)
		- 1 solar day = 86,400 seconds
	- [[Measurement prefixes]]
		- Centi
			- cm = $\frac {1}{100}m$
		- Millil
			- mm = $\frac {1}{1000}m$
		- Micro
			- $\mu m$ = $1 \times 10^{-6}m$
			- millionth
		- Nano
			- nm = $1 \times 10^{-9}m$
			- billionth
		- Pico
			- pm = $1 \times 10^{-12}m$
			- trillionth
