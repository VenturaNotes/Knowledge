## Index Laws
- $a^m \times a^n = a^{m + n}$ 
- $a^m \div a^n = a^{m - n}$ 

### Power of a power
- $(2^3)^5$ 
	- $= 2^3 \times 2^3 \times 2^3 \times 2^3 \times 2^3$
	- $= 2^{15}$ 
- $2^3$ 
	- = $2 \times 2 \times 2$ 
- $(a^m)^n = a^{m \times n}$
