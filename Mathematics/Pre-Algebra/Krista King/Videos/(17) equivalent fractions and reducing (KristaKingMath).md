- Find the equivalent fractions
- Going from $\frac {5}{10}$ to $\frac 12$ is called reducing fractions
	- To do this, look for common factor between the numerator and the denominator and then divide both the numerator and denominator by that common factor
	- Can find the greatest common factor between 2 terms
