- Rewrite the decimal number
	- 1.333333... = $1.\overline{3}$ 
		- Conventional way is to draw a line over the smallest number of repeating values as you can
	- 454.545454... = $454.\overline {54}$ 
		- Condense a decimal
	- $6.\overline {786}$ = 6.786786786...
		- Expand decimal by writing out a repeating sequence.
	- $14.\overline{32}$ = 14.323232...
